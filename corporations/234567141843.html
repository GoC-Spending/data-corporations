Company Profile


Tristar's origins date back to 1971 with contracts to provide specialty coatings to both de Havilland and Canadair, two pioneers in Canada's aerospace industry.

The Company currently manufactures a full range of specialty coatings not only for the aerospace but for the transportation, mining, agricultural, industrial, commercial, and architectural markets.. Specific chemistries include zinc rich primers as well as polyurethane, ISO free, epoxy and fluoropolymer topcoats. Products also include a specialty Color Shift coating in 18 distinctively different metallic formulations which create a swirl of different or changing colors when you look at them from different angles.

Formulations are not only engineered to the requirements of the industry but can be custom tailored to the unique demands of the Customer.

Tristar is dedicated to the highest quality of Customer Service of warmth, friendliness, individual pride and Company spirit. Our employees are provided the same concern, respect and caring attitude within the organization that they are expected to share externally with every Tristar Customer. Our goal is to provide a quality paint system aligning with the needs of each of our Customers.

PRIMERS - Solvent based
PRIMERS  SOLVENT-BASED

Starpoxy470 High Solids Epoxy Primer (2K)
Highly competitive workhorse for a broad range of applications providing exceptional corrosion resistance and excellent adhesion properties.
 Low V0C compliance at either the 2.1 or 2.8 pounds per UG formulation.
 Can be top coated within 30 to 60 minutes.
 Fast Dry / 8 hour pot life
 High build and strontium chromate formulations available


Starpoxy 440 High Solids Epoxy Primer (2K)
Ideal primer for large substrate surfaces with excellent spraying characteristics boasting no overspray. Result is a smooth finish. Minimum one hour dry time before topcoat application.
 Exceptional corrosion resistance and superlative adhesion to steel and aluminum compared to other primers
 Low 2.1 pounds per UG VOC compliance
 Ideal for higher film builds
 Available in chromate-free gray, aluminum or zinc rich formulation
 Easy 1:1 mix ratio with 2 hour pot life
 No Induction time


Starpoxy 430/480 Anticorrosive Epoxy Primer (2K)
Similar workhorse to Starpoxy 470 with comparable properties and durability but lower solids with corresponding higher VOCs.
 Very fast dry times
 Exceptionally forgiving to apply
 High build formulation as well as conventional build for smoother finish
 Available in Zinc Rich formulation


Starpoxy 420 Chromate Epoxy Primer (2K)
Strontium chromate formulation is well known for exceptional value and corrosion resistance at low film build on aluminum and steel substrate.
 Exceptional adhesion to aluminum
 1:1 easy mix ratio (15 to 30 min. induction time)


Stardry 300 Phenolic Primer (1K)
Offers strong corrosion resistance and adhesion properties at exceptional value.
 Excellent spraying characteristics in both reduced and non-reduced formulations
 Fast drying
 Frequently used as a sandwich coat on repaint applications to prevent wrinkling of the pre-existing coating
 Low 2.8 lbs per UG VOC compliant formulation available.


Starbake 320 Baking Primer (1K)
Designed for general OEM industrial applications as an all-purpose alkyd melamine baking primer.
 Full cure in 10 min. at 300° F
 Combined primer/topcoat film can be baked at once by allowing the primer to flash off for 3 min. prior to topcoat application



Product Name:
TOPCOATS - Waterborne
TOPCOATS  WATERBORNE

Proteus 770 Waterborne PUD Topcoat (1K)
Designed as a premium polyurethane acrylic dispersion when 2K products are not practical/cost effective.
 Superior performance in terms of adhesion, weathering corrosion and chemical resistance,
 Low VOC (0.5 lbs/UG)
 Fast drying (dry to touch 15 min., dry hard 55 min.)
 DTM formulation available.


Proteus 940 Waterborne Polyurethane (2K)
Highly superior and compliant low VOC polyurethane offering long-term durability with extreme chemical, moisture, and UV resistance.
 Very tough coating to withstand mechanical abuse
 Fast dry ( Dry to touch, 20 to 40 min depending on the airflow)
 Excellent anti-graffiti coating (600+ double MEK rubs)
 Exceptional resistance to dirt build up. Dirt basically sheds from the surface or is readily washed off with normal rain on outdoor installations


Proteus 910 Waterborne Floor Coating (2K)
Premium floor coating with long-term durability against indoor traffic and chemical abuse.
 Available as either a smooth or textured surface with fine, medium or coarse aggregate option
 Aggregate is a very tough but light synthetic grain which accommodates spray application in fine texture, or alternatively, roller application in any texture. Regardless of application technique, the final result is a highly uniform appearance.
 Dry to touch: 2 to 3 hours; Dry through: 8 hours
 Extreme chemical resistance (600 double MEK rubs)
 Very hard (9H) with excellent impact resistance (80 inch pounds direct and reverse) without texture added


Proteus 960 Waterborne Epoxy Topcoat (2K)
Highly durable tough finish for interior industrial applications with exceptional chemical/solvent resistance
 Virtually zero VOC (0.003lbs/UG or 0.31gms/l) against the LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) program.
 Fast dry (dry through less than two hours)
 Can be force dried for 20-30 min using IR lamps to achieve full cure in 4 to 10 hours.
 Excellent anti-graffiti coating. Extreme chemical and moisture protection (400+ double MEK rubs)
 Hard (2H+) against abrasion or mechanical abuse
 Excellent adhesion to a variety of substrate.
