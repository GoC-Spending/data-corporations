Company Profile


World Communications provides translation, communications, social media and language services. We have a comprehensive service that solves all of your business needs with world class communications and the 4 Pan American languages, English, French, Spanish and Portuguese.

We are a step ahead of any competitor in every field that we specialize in. Communications, social-media, translation and language have something in common. We blend them all together and leverage our deep understanding of Pan-American cultures to your advantage. This standardizes the way you speak and communicate person to person, business to business, and the way you interact with communities around your business. If you do business in Canada, either in English or French, in Latin America or the US, in English, Spanish, French or Portuguese, you can solidify internal and external communications with our professional team.

Reinforce your objectives, increase and solidify your presence while trading, connecting, traveling or conducting business in Spanish, Portuguese, French and English speaking countries.

We offer best practices in communication in: Branding and reputation, Social media strategies, Internal communications within your offices in the Americas, Investor relations and public relations, Corporate social reports, Business plans, Legal and contracts, Internal and external surveys, Marketing and Market research, Bulletins, letters, memos, policies and procedures, Media advisory, press releases, articles, white papers, reports, Web copy, marketing writing, advertising and sales copy, Investigative research and advice on current affairs

Service Name:
Translation and Language Services
We provide high quality translation and interpretation services. In our global context and in an international business environment, culture and language play a key role in the success of a business. We comprehend the importance of cultural context and have developed high standards of communication. We recognize the intricacies and nuances of each language we handle.

We are a team of knowledgeable and creative individuals that, on a daily basis, deal in a global environment that is focused on the Americas. We provide intelligent, clear, and up-to-date text conversion from one language to another.

SPANISH, FRENCH, PORTUGUESE AND ENGLISH LANGUAGES

We have created highly sophisticated, interactive, and fun learning environments in several of the most important companies in Canada and the US, including major banks, mining companies, technology, entertainment, travel, food, consulting and specialized industries. We develop your Spanish, French and Portuguese language skills to allow you to meaningfully communicate with your business partners in Latin America. Our courses are customized to suit your company’s strategic needs.

Our programs are based on a selection of the best worldwide language instruction standards. We incorporate the most current, cutting-edge Spanish, French and Portuguese teaching material teaching material from recognized academic institutions in Canada.

Service Name:
Social Media
SOCIAL MEDIA AND MARKETING

We develop social media and marketing strategies to allow you to better engage your customers with your company, products, programs, and services. We can effectively leverage social media platforms, innovative strategies, and our knowledge of internal and external communications, to connect your company to the world. Our international creative team specializes in knowing the regional cultures, trends, and languages of the Americas, and can help you to better communicate with your clients.

Awareness of culture, language, diversity, and regional trends plays a crucial role in a rapidly developing world. We offer best practices in electronic communications, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, video, music and copyright in English, French, Spanish and Portuguese
