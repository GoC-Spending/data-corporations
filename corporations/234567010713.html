Company Profile


Tri-Y Enterprises Ltd. (Tri-Y Technologies Inc. or Tri-Y Environmental Research Institute) is an environmental high-tech research institution based in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. The institution specializes in advanced environmental technologies, especially waste water treatment and energy saving. It is recognized as one of the leading specialists in the treatment of industrial and municipal waste water, and energy saving technologies for buildings and houses.

Mission of the institution is to provide customized and cost-effective solutions to customers and to dedicate the institution resources to the creation of a clean environment that will ensure the perpetual existence of future generations on earth.

To fulfill this objective, the institution has been actively participating in research & development in collaboration with the National Research Council Canada and the University of British Columbia. The institution's significant contribution to the advancement of technologies has earned itself a remarkable reputation.

The institution has a team of technical professionals who possess expertise and experience in the field of environment technology, water treatment, energy saving, renewable energy, mechanical and chemical engineering, research and development.

The institution's waste water treatment technologies and services can benefit a variety of industries such as pulp and paper mills, leathering, printing and dyeing factories, hotels and restaurants, oil refineries, as well as the municipal water treatment plants.

The institution's energy saving technologies including Temperature Control Material (TCM) and High thermal Resistance Panels (HRP) used in buildings and houses can significantly save heating and cooling energy.

Please visit our websites: www.tri-y.com and www.watersolutionsinc.com for more information.

Technology profile

* High efficiency flocculant P30:

Mainly used in wastewater treatment systems such as settlement, flotation, filtration, etc. The function of P30 is to encapsulate, flocculate, adsorb and fixate the dirty particles in waste water.

* BMR water treatment technology:

BMR is a series of waste water treatment equipment, which are designed to remove pollutants from waste water fast and efficiently.

BMR is based on conventional biochemical treatment. It applies our proprietary Biological Micro-entrapment technology to strengthen the process and enhance the efficiency of the treatment. BMR technology can be used in a wide range of applications, such as waste water from restaurants, hotels and residential areas etc. After the treatment, the rate of COD removal can reach a high level of 90%, and COD, BOD, SS, pH can reach the discharge standard. BMR has the advantages of high efficiency, easy operating, low operating and maintenance costs, less occupation of room.

* Temperature Control Materials (TCM) used for saving of heating and cooling energy in buildings and houses.

* High Resistance Panels (HRP) provides high thermal resistance (High R) of insulation on walls of buildings and houses to save heating and cooling energy.

Please visit our websites: www.tri-y.com and www.watersolutionsinc.com for more information.
