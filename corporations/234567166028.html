Engineering Support

Construction/Project Management
• Constructability
• Risk Management
• Manage safety, quality, production to the highest levels
• Manage the TRIF safety record
• Ensure turnaround of files and records are on time
• Manage the performance factor “PF”
• Daily/Weekly Costs and Status Reporting (Ensures that the actual work progress during execution is monitored and dynamically updated in the schedule on a daily basis)
• Schedule (Development and updating to reflect actual) Primavera (P6), MS Project.
• Detailed execution plan
• Coordinate Tasks
• Coordinate material/procurement
• Coordinate and manage all sub-contractors
• Mobilization onto site in a timely manner
• Develop effective relationships with clients & project management
• Environmental safety and management change requirements review
• Incorporate safety, environmental and rescue into planned jobs
• Ensures development of electronic schedules for fieldwork, provide technical feedback on job packages provided
• Monitor quality of contractor performance and safety performance
• Develops effective relationships with the site stakeholders; Maintenance and Operations, Supervision, Field Service Coordinators, Contract Administrators, Project Managers, by communicating planned scope, using dynamic scheduling to track progress and supporting job completion
• Complete experienced onsite support staff including; Site Superintendent, Coordinators, Safety Advisors, Quality Control Inspectors (CWB Level 2) and Admin. Assistant (LEM’s) / Document Controller

Safety
• Daily Toolbox meeting
• Onsite support
• Field observations, field engagement & coaching
• JHA’s
• FLHA audits, review
• ONEC orientations (as required)
• Gas monitor bump testing / calibration / management (as required when applicable)
• Project HSE performance tracking (daily statistics)
• Incident investigation and reporting
• Weekly safety meetings
• Training and orientation for maintenance and contractor personnel
• DNA Testing (at client request)

QA-QC
• Preparation and obtaining approvals for Inspection Test Plans (ITPs) and regulatory documents
(AB-40, AB-519, etc.)
• Review welding requirements – welding process, WPS, welder tickets
• Material and welding consumable verification and control
• Ensure proper WPS and welders with appropriate qualifications are being employed
• Interface with client and regulatory inspectors and engineering
• Interpret appropriate Code of Construction requirements related to the project
• Onsite QA support to ensure compliance to Code of Construction
• Dimensional and weld fit-up check
• Pressure equipment, structural welding and tank (API650, API653) inspection
• Reviewing torqueing and tensioning parameters and witness the process
• Coordinate NDE, PWHT, hydrostatic and pneumatic testing and witness
• Review NDE and PHWT records from subcontractors
• Coordinating closure weld process and inspections required
• Monitor quality performance of subcontractors
• Record and log RFIs, NCRs, weld progress, repairs, NDE and PWHT
• Redline drawings, weld mapping, bolt torque mapping and as-built drawings
• Turnover documentation and Regulatory documentation (AB-81, AB-83, AB-40, etc.)

Field/Shop Construction
• Onsite Pre-Turnaround Activities (as required)
• De-commissioning/Start up

o Mechanical support
