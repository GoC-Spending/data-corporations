Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
FRONTIERSMAN Bear Attack Deterrent 225 gm and 325 gm
ISO Certified. Environmentally Safe, Frontiersman Bear Attack Deterrent contains .857% capsaicin (pepper). This level of capsaicin will not permanently injure the user. Higher levels will damage eyes in the event of blow-back. No ozone depletion. Available in two sizes, 225 gram and 325 gram

Product Name:
SABRE Dog Repellent 22 gram and 50 gram
50% capsaicin. ISO Certified
Fires approximately 25 - 1 second bursts at 3 meters. Fires 70%
longer than other Dog Attack Deterrents.

Product Name:
Kodiak Bear Safety Kit
Kodiak Bear Safety Kit is made of durable material with binding around edges. Designed for quick access; drop down cover exposes contents which are each held securely within kit when exposed. Easily pull selected item from kit for immediate use! Includes FRONTIERSMAN "Environmentally Friendly" Bear Repellent
02-C Centre Fire Pen Launcher, 6 Bear Bangers (there is room in the kit for an additional box of 6 flares or 6 bearbangers or flares, Flip Top Nylon Holster, Nylon Shoulder Strap, Signal Horn

Product Name:
Staying Safe in Bear Country Video
The primary video in the Safety in Bear Country video series. A major educational tool for anyone living, traveling, or working in black and grizzly bear country in North America. It includes the consensus opinion of leading experts on bear behaviour and its relevance to human safety. Viewers will develop a better understanding of bear behaviour and how this knowledge can help them to minimize the chance of bear encounters and bear attacks. The video stresses that a much greater degree of co-existence with bears is possible if people understand and apply a few safety principles.


Product Name:
Working in Bear Country Video
The first 20-minute module in the Safety in Bear Country video series comes packaged with the 50-minute primary video Staying Safe in Bear Country. This module provides additional detailed information on working safely in bear country. It is not a stand-alone educational tool; it is essential to be familiar with the content of the primary video before viewing Working in Bear Country. The module is especially relevant to industry managers and supervisors responsible for the safety of workers. This program is divided into the following sections: 1) Field Safety, including (a) employee responsibilities and (b) helicopter support 2) Camp Safety, including (a) location and design and (b) attractant management 3) Detection Systems 4) Bear Deterrents 5) Firearms 6) Bear Response Planning.


Product Name:
15mm Bearbangers
15 mm cartridge with audible effect and exploding flare insert. These flash bang cartridges explode with a loud bang after traveling 40 meters (131 ft.). Used for animal control and signaling. Also known as Bird Bombs, Bear Bombs, Noise Bombs, Bird Bangers, Crackers and Twin Shot. Available in centre fire or rim fire.
Signal flares and safety flare
Packaged 6 to a box. Weight. approx. 12 grams. Height range of 40 meters (131 ft.) with 0.8-1.0 second time delay. Noise level 115db.


Product Name:
15mm Flares (red, white, green)
Packaged 6 to a box. They each weigh about 10 grams. The flares are used for distress (red); locator (white); ok (green) signaling and will produce a plume when launched. Please visit our website for technical data and video at http://www.macecanada.com/canada/wilderness1/Signal_Cartridges1.htm


Product Name:
Signal Kits (Pen Launcher Kits)
A variety of centre fire kits depending on the type of launcher. The kits include pen launcher, bear bangers, flares.

Product Name:
Pen Launchers
A large selection of center fire pen launchers for launching bear bangers and flares including push button, dual pin, pull-type, twin shot, snap-on triple head and more..

Product Name:
Critter Gitter Animal Motion/Heat Sensor
Using passive infrared, body heat and motion detection, Critter Gitter detects animals moving into its protected area. It then makes ear piercing sounds (and flashing lights) to send them fleeing. Once the animal has cleared the area Critter Gitter automatically resets itself. This detector changes its sound and light patterns with each intrusion and protects a diagonal area of up to 40' (depending on the size of the animal). It comes complete with a 9-volt alkaline battery and a protective weather bag.

Product Name:
Bear Bells
Wilderness hiking can have hazards like any other sport. One of these hazards is bears. To avoid a close encounters with bears, attach a bell to your backpack, walking stick and/or shoe. The bells are the best means of letting the bears know you are hiking along the trail and give the animal time to leave the area. Basically, you don't want to surprise a bear! Kodiak bear bells are made with quality webbing and velcro to easily attach to a pack, walking stick and/or shoe.

Product Name:
12 Gauge Comet Flares
12 gauge Red Comet Flares. Ideal where long range capabilities are required. Packaged 5 per box. Project 55+m (180 ft). Produce a sparkling tail which aids in determining direction of travel. The comet flares have a high candle power and are visible for long distances. A great product when venturing into the backcountry!
Use with open choke shot gun only!

Product Name:
12 Gauge Bearbangers
12 gauge Bearbangers. Projection distance before detonation is approximately 60 meters (200'). Time lapse from firing to detonation is approximately 3-5 seconds. Exploding with a loud bang at end of travel these cartridges are ideal where long range capabilities are required. Use with open choke shotgun only! Also known as Cracker Shells, Bangers, Shot Tell, Schreckpatronen, Shell Crackers and Twin Shot. Packaged 5 per box.
Use with open choke shotgun only!

Product Name:
15 mm Whistles/Screamers
15 mm illuminating cartridge. With a loud, pronounced whistling, these shells produce a loud screeching noise through complete flight. In low light they have a pronounced visual effect. Packaged 6 to a box. Available in centre fire and rim fire. With a range of 76m to 91m (250 ft to 300'+ feet), these unique pyrotechnics produce an unusual, alarming sound creating an immediate fear reaction in birds and animals. Also known as Racket Bombs, Crackers, Whistle Bombs, Screamers-Sirens and Twin Shot.

Service Name:
Non-Lethal Bear Management Training
Chief Instructor Dan LeGrandeur specializes in the training of bear awareness and safety in addition to non-lethal methods of preventing human/bear conflicts. Training courses vary from non-lethal bear management training for law enforcement and wildlife managers to safety orientated training for working or recreating in bear country.

Dan's non-lethal bear management training is endorsed by the Canadian Bear Alliance and the Humane Society of the United States. Previous clients include the RCMP, B.C. Search and Rescue, Resort Municipality of Whistler, B.C. Conservation Officer Service, Halliburton Energy Services, Suncor, Weldwood of Canada, Ministry of Transportation-Prince George, Ministry of Natural Resources-Manitoba, Suncor Energy, Alberta Paper Products and The Alberta Trapper Association.



As a former B.C. Conservation Officer of 12 years, Dan gained considerable experience in managing human/bear conflicts. Dan was a member of Whistler's Black Bear Task Team and in 1999 implemented Canada's first non/lethal bear management program in Whistler.

He is also the chief training instructor for the Canadian Bear Alliance and Humane Society of The United States' non-lethal bear management program, providing both in classroom and in the field training to law enforcement officers in Canada and the U.S.
pepper spray sprays survival kits
Having worked in a wide range of environments from the urban areas of North and West Vancouver to the coastal and rural areas of south-western B.C., Dan has gained extensive experience dealing with a wide variety of conflict situations (farming/ranching, urban areas, remote camps) involving both blacks and grizzlies.

His knowledge in all aspects of human/bear conflicts further includes educational training (creating Bear Smart communities, bear safety, etc.) trapping, relocation, and tranquilizing. Dan is also a qualified firearms instructor and has considerable experience conducting training courses and public speaking.

In addition to providing training, Kodiak Security manufactures and distributes the non-lethal environmentally friendly bear protection kit for law enforcement, industry and personal use. Personal Bear Protection Kits and replacement deterrents are also available
