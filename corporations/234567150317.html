material stainless steel

shape round

specification
wire diameter:0.23mm,0.25mm,0.28mm
thickness:100mm, 150mm
diameter :300mm to 5200mm

Application
Used in gas-liquid separation device in chemical,petroleum, tower manufacturing,pressure vessels and other industries.

How it works
When vapor and entrained liquid droplets pass through a demister , the vapor moves freely through the mesh pad but the liquid droplets, due to their greater inertia, cannot make the necessary sharp turns and as a result are thrown into contact with the wire surfaces and briefly held there. As more droplets enter the pad and collect on the wires, they grow in size, run down the wire to the bottom surface of the mesh separator and fall from the unit. Overhead vapor is now free of entrained liquid

How it works
When vapor and entrained liquid droplets pass through a demister , the vapor moves freely through the mesh pad but the liquid droplets, due to their greater inertia, cannot make the necessary sharp turns and as a result are thrown into contact with the wire surfaces and briefly held there.
