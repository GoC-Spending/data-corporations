Company Profile


For 25 years, Raymark has been empowering retailers with world-class enterprise software solutions and services to grow customer loyalty, improve associate productivity, optimize stock turns and increase sales. Raymarks integrated, end-to-end suite of retail technology solutions operate in real-time and provide retailers with everything they need for store operations, customer-centric retailing, planning and inventory management, reporting and analytics. To learn more, visit www.raymark.com/about

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Raymark Retail Enterprise Software for Store Operations
In todays omni-channel marketplace, your stores need to become a true destination and provide amazing customer experience. Whether youre looking for a highly-configurable, robust Point of Sale solution with E-Payment or the freedom to service customers anywhere with Mobile Point of Sale, Raymark has the retail software solutions to run your store operations in real-time, all the time. Raymark Store Associate Portal is the all-in-one tool for your sales associates, putting every web application on website they need at their fingertips.

Product Name:
Raymark Retail Enterprise Software for Customer-Centric Retail
Todays customer is more demanding than ever. Raymark empowers your store associates and marketing team with the retail software solutions they need to provide unparalleled customer experience and service, building relationships before, during and after every sale. Arm your store associates with the knowledge of the enterprise at the point of customer decision with Raymark Clienteling. Back at head office, your marketing team will be empowered to craft relevant, targeted communications to the right customer segments with Raymark Retail CRM.


Product Name:
Raymark Retail Enterprise Software for Planning and Inventory Management
Always have the right products in right place, at the right price, in the right quantity, and in the right sizes and colors. Simple, right? Our centralized, real-time solutions for Planning and Inventory Management enable your merchandisers and operations team to make data-driven decisions at the most opportune times. We support you through the whole product lifecycle, with integrated solutions for Merchandising, Replenishment, Assortment Planning, Purchasing and Warehousing, and Planning and OTB.

Product Name:
Raymark Mosaic Point of Sale
Raymark Mosaic Point of Sale is a complete retail point of sale solution that provides extensive cross-platform mobile point of sale functionality. Raymark's real-time capabilities make the solution a must-have for up-to-the second access to omni-channel product and customer information.

Technology profile

Ready for todays retail, Raymark offers retailers highly-sophisticated technology tailored to their specific business requirements. Raymark's real-time, centralized Xpert-Series empowers retailers through integrated, flexible applications including POS, inventory management, automated replenishment, mobile solutions, forecasting and OTB, assortment planning, CRM, reporting, data analytics and BI, providing the tools required for enlightened decision-making. Its ability to handle various types of retail business models, coupled with world-ready functionality, are the reasons so many top retailers choose to partner with Raymark.
