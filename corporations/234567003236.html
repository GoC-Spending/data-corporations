Company Profile


More than just a business advisor, PentA+ acts as a proactive business partner. The PentA+ five service offerings include:

1) Conducts comprehensive due diligence including technical, business evaluations and external reviews.
2) Provides mentoring and guidance on financing, growth and business development as hands-on entrepreneur in residence or interim executive
3) Brings value added governance & adds diversity to Board profile as Advisor or Independent Corporate Director
4) Acts as catalyzer for strategic alliances, channel partners and funding
5) Links funding organizations, investors , VCs, and potential business partners & clients and initiates exit strategies through Mergers & Acquisitions
