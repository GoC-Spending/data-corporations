Company Profile


SOLITEK Systems Inc. (Solitek.ca) has been at your service since 1992. Our highly qualified personnel are available to you to perform network support; security audits, needs analysis, network design and implementation, and performance enhancement.

We use inormation technology to help with your technical problems but also to help business processes. So whether it's hands-on network upgrades or help in designing a project plan or security policies for your network, we can help. We have in-depth experience in both Microsoft and Novell operating systems, and will provide you with a secure and powerful network.

We certainly are aware of the IT shortage in the current marketplace so we take pride in providing a single-point of contact to take care of all your networking needs, so you don't have to worry about looking for IT personnel with varied skillsets to handle different types of problems. We can manage all of it for you.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Network Services
Network Support

Our FlexSupport contract provides you with the type of service you need when you need it. FlexSupport consists of a prepaid block of time that can be used at your discretion for emergency on-site or telephone service (less than 4 hours response time), as well as non-urgent service. FlexSupport also includes custom-tailored scheduled maintenance service to ensure your network is maintained at its best and to keep down-time to a minimum.

We can be your single point of contact to handle all of your computer needs from a basic network installation to a large, multi-server network using a full load of today's common solutions such as email, firewalls, spam solutions, antivirus, wired and wireless networking, remote connections, VPN, high speed internet, intranet, and more. We have seen that there is a shortage of people who can troubleshoot the many problems that pop up on a day-to-day basis to keep networks running to the highest up-time possible. Too many networks are getting bogged down with viruses, spyware and spam and its really causing a productivity problem. This is where we can help.

Network problems are elusive and often leave you in a no-mans land between vendors. We will take charge of the problems and get the job done for you.

Our network specialists maintain top-level Microsoft and Novell certifications.

Whether it's hands-on network upgrades or help in designing a project plan or policies for your network, we can help. We have in-depth experience in Microsoft and Novell operating systems, and can integrate these in a mixed environment.

Security is our concern

As network security issues become more and more complex, it is difficult to know whether the security policy is being maintained. With high-speed internet access, companies are exposed 24 hours a day, seven days a week. This makes the job of monitoring and reporting a major resource commitment. This means security must be treated as an ongoing process involving technology, people and policies. Security is a process that requires input from the entire organization to be effective.

There are lots of glossy ads describing how wonderful a certain security product is. The problem is that in real life scenarios security systems such as firewalls, remote VPN and Intrusion Detection systems are often setup at installation and forgotten about. As time passes, features are turned on and off, configurations are modified and security is compromised.

We can do a comprehensive security audit and setup ongoing maintenance to ensure that security levels are up to your standards. The ongoing maintenance will ensure that the latest patches are installed and newest vulnerabilities are addressed.

General Consulting

Our expert services are available on a daily rate basis for network support or implementation.

We are also available on a consultation basis, ensuring that you get the proper information for your informatics decision-making. We put ourselves in your shoes, so that you get the most bang out of your Informatics budget.

We can help you along any phase of a project, from the Project Planning to the Deliverables, and provide the required resources in any step in between. In any project we place serious considerations on Security and Disaster Recovery so that your company is not placed at risk or unnecessary exposure at any time.

We can also provide you with an accurate picture of the performance of your system. Our specialist will provide on-the-spot corrective action or advice on minor problems and provide you with a detailed plan of action, should your system need more work.
