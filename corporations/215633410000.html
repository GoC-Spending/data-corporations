Company Profile


RWDI is an international specialty consulting engineering firm that uses a variety of engineering, computing, and scientific capabilities to help designers create comfortable environments and high performance buildings and structures.

Since first offering its services in 1972, RWDI has evolved from a tiny local firm, created to help mitigate snowdrifting problems in farm lanes, to a worldwide participant in the proof of concept process for the world’s most outstanding structures and complex masterplans.

Always adding to and improving its extensive services, RWDI has worked tirelessly to give its thousands of loyal clients the answers they need to reach their design goals. More than a test lab, this firm has developed a collaborative, holistic approach to the integration of developments into their environments.

Unparalleled experience gained from thousands of projects in every climate is not our only offering. RWDI’s talented staff, working from offices in several countries, is a multi-disciplinary team of scientists, engineers, meteorologists, technologists and modellers capable of dealing with the most complicated design issues.

“Making complicated issues simple” is more than a phrase we use: it is our mission!

This diverse staff works in a mentoring, learning environment in which RWDI encourages its members to participate in continuing education, publish technical papers and provide leadership in professional organizations to advance the knowledge of our various technical disciplines.

As a company dedicated to delivering knowledge-based solutions and strategies to our clients in architecture, engineering, masterplanning and property development, we focus on continuous improvement, state-of-the-art systems and procedures, accountability, and attention to clients that exceeds their expectations.

We aim to be accessible and responsive. Indeed it is our vision to deliver an unrivalled client experience. We look forward to working with you!

Service Name:
Wind Loads & Effects
RWDI’s wind-engineering consultants provide expert advice for the design of structures affected by wind. The consulting expertise of RWDI’s engineers and scientists is based on decades of invaluable experience working on many of the world’s landmark structures using physical modeling and computational techniques. Local wind loads are assessed for component and cladding design.

Aerodynamic stability and structural wind loads are assessed for the design of main wind force resisting systems to mitigate predicted wind-induced motions and other potential issues.
