Company Profile


Our company in Canada and our sister concerns in North America,
Middle -East and Asia have been a pioneer in the packaging
industry since last 32 years. We provide a complete line of
customized solutions to the Food Packaging Industry, the Second
hand clothing Industry, the Furniture Industry and Automotive
Industry. We provide packaging solutions that increase productivity and lower packaging costs. We are more
than a manufacturer,we are a partner.

As a manufacturing house, KT Canada's focus is customer
application specific and solution oriented. Our manufacturing
facilities are located in the state of Maharashtra, the business
capital of India.

We have the ability to produce yarn and weave our own
material, thereby enabling us to manufacture any construction or
colour.

OUR PRODUCTS
Our products include (PP) fabrics covering a
wide range of specifications and uses. Products can be designed
for virtually any application. Many colours,constructions and
physical properties are available.

NURSERY GROUND COVER AND WINTER PROTECTION
Our nursery ground cover made from 100% UV stabilized
polypropylene fabric is tough enough to resist tears and
punctures. The specially designed fabric is porous, allowing
water permeability while retarding weed growth. While its
lightweight and easy to install, it can withstand the abuse of a
small truck traffic.

NURSERY SHADE CLOTH
A woven polypropylene fabric manufactured to resist tearing and
puncturing will withstand the toughest tests of rain, sunlight
and wind, making it very cost efficient. Our shade cloth
is offered in varying %s to accurately control the amount of
sunlight admitted to plants. Also multi purpose shade cloth
available Patios,Truck tops and privacy.

AGRICULTURAL CURTAINS are ideal for use on poultry houses,hog
houses and green house.extrusion coated polyethylene are
durable and resist puncturing.
POULTRY HOUSE CURTAINS available in any length .The panels may
be seamed togather, taped and or grommeted to fit specific
needs.
BURLAP PRODUCTS: Pecan, Peanut, Rip Rap, Parts, Potato
WEED SCREEN: Smother weeds in lawn and garden High UV, Durable
long lasting, easy to install, porous
SILT FENCE for soil erosion is a woven polypropylene fabric
engineered specifically to stop erosion at onstruction sites.
We also have custom printing,extrusion coated bags, custom size
polywoven bags and much,much , more......



Other products are Bulk Bags/Bale Wrap/ Bags(Feed, Sand, Rice,
Grain). We offer first quality custom printing up to four
colours.

We also make mesh bags- Citrus, Onion, Cabbage, Peanut, Pecan,
Clam, Oyster, Crayfish, Baler Extruded Net, Header.

Our other products include Chritsmas Tree Disposable Bags, Drop
Cloth,Laundry Bags, Lumber Covers,Flexible Suction pipes,
Plastic Shopping bags, Sand bags/Sacks, Shade net, Suit covers,
Vegetable bags.

Product Name:
Non Woven Fabric
In industry, the possibilities for non woven fabrics are
virtually
limitless. In most cases, however, the non-woven fabric must
meet
highly specific need. Such needs can vary enormously, depending
on the product, the market and the customer. This calls for
close
collaboration between researchers and marketing experts. All of
which is good news for KT Canada Corp. customers since our sales
professionals are extremely knowledgeable in evaluating and
identifying these specific needs... and the creativity of our
research and development group has no limits.

KT Canada Corp. nonwoven fabrics are important components in a
number of
industries notably apparel, absorbant, sporting goods, steel
mills, electrical appliances and transportation.

The vast majority of our industrial products are developed in
partnership with our customers, and sold to them on exclusive
terms. And we are proud to say the attention we give to their
needs provides enormous, long term benefits for all concerned.

Product Name:
Landscaping Fabric
PP Spunbond nonwoven have a variety of uses in the agriculture
sector. It is commonly used as seeding covers for crops and
garden plants, to increase crop yield. Lightweight and delicate
in texture, they encourage growth in a greenhouse like fashion
while keeping pestat bay. The Material provides substantial
protection against frost and damage from extreme weather. A
further conservation throughthe preservation of soil moisture.
Spunbond nonwoven are agriculturally very economical.

Technology profile

KT specializes in supplying polypropylene spunbond nonwoven
fabric for the local as well as the international market. PP
Spunbond nonwoven fabric is manufactured on the latest state of
the art German technology "RECOFIL II" Double Beam plant from
Reifenhauser - the world's leader in the field of spunbond
nonwoven manufacturing technology. The Plant has highly
qualified and experienced team of engineers and techicials in
Production, Engineering and Quality Assurance departments.
