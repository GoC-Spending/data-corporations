Company Profile


MEDIA & REDAKTICA Pro offers highly efficient and professional services in writing, research, editing, translation, media relations and training.

MEDIA & REDAKTICA Pros mission is to conceive and improve texts of all kinds and in all sectors of activity; whether they are corporate, promotional, advertising or event-related, technical, scientific or journalistic.

Benefit from our expertise in research, editing, corporate writing, Web writing and writing indexation, journalism and in popularization of technical and scientific subjects!

Entrust us with the translation of your documents from English
to French and from Spanish to French: a MEDIA & REDAKTICA Pro specialty.

Take full advantage of our know-how in media relations!
MEDIA & REDAKTICA Pro will identify for you the best
communication strategy to adopt, compose all of your texts and elaborate a report on media coverage results
which guarantees your optimum visibility.

Have access to a warm and confident voice in French for the fulfillment of your various business needs.

Let us carry out your mandates and take advantage of a customized, reliable and price-competitive service!
