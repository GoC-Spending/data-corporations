Company Profile


We design and manufacture scientific instrumentation
for electro-optics and optical spectroscopy (UV-VIS-NIR) with
applications to the medical, environmental and analytical
chemistry fields.

We also produce an off-the-shelf line, manufacture OEM instruments as well as develop prototypes and custom made
instruments.

In addition, we offer a unique line of modular
instruments for fluorescence, Fourier transform, time
decay, spectrophotometry and spectroradiometry. This line of modular instruments has been designed especially for research and academic communities, enabling the
integration of state-of-the-art instrumentation for both standard and unique applications.

Product Name:
Remote emission sensing device.
Sciencetech has studied the development of an instrument which will remotely monitor exhaust emissions from moving vehicles. An assessment of available technologies for remote measurement of trace gases, and of smoke opacity, was carried out. Typical exhaust emission levels from in-use vehicles were analysed based on current and future emission regulations. A conceptual design was developed for an instrument which can observe the exhaust plumes of passing vehicles form the roadside and assess the emission levels of specific pollutants. The prototype instrument is capable of detecting carbon monoxide (CO), unburned hydrocarbons (HC), nitrogen oxides (NOx) and particulates (diesel smoke, measured as optical opacity).

Product Name:

Service Name:
Custom Design And Manufacture Of Scientific Instrumentation
Service Name:
Custom required instruments
Our high talented team of scientists and engineers possess the
diverse capabilities required to design instruments specific to
customer requirements by using a unique "Optical Building Block"
concept. Sciencetech use a variety of components, including
monochromators, light sources, power supplies, detector, and
accessories, to produce a wide variety of spectroscopic systems.
This concept allows the arrangement of components in
configurations that best fit customer needs for particular
experimental requirements. We also modify any equipment and
design any unique optical and electronics required to best meet
those needs. Sciencetech also produces an excellent line of off-
the-self instruments.

Service Name:
Research and Development
New product development, small to medium quantity manufacturing
facilities, and a line of optical instruments for analytical
research with application in medicine, materials research,
industrial processes, and space science.

Strategic alliances:

Due to the large areas of developments and a consistent growing

joint ventures in other countries, will continue to expand our
horizons and allow us to better serve multinational corporations.
