Company Profile


PEICE is a leading provider of short (1 to 5 days duration) courses to the upstream, midstream and downstream sectors of the petroleum industry, offering hundreds of courses throughout the year in Houston, Calgary, Denver and London.

Our course subject matter spans technical engineering and geoscience, business/commercial, leadership, communications and management training, ranging from basic awareness to skilled application level.

Cross-training, introductory courses for junior staff, and technical awareness training for both technical and non-technical personnel are particular specialities of PEICE.

At the more advanced technical level, PEICE offers courses on the application of the latest techniques and technologies.

Our instructors are among the petroleum industry’s most respected and knowledgeable in their field.
PEICE has trained over 40,000 oil & gas professionals since its inception in 1998. In 2013, PEICE became part of the RPS Group, a leading consultancy to the petroleum industry globally.

Under the umbrella of RPS Training, PEICE and its affiliate Nautilus World, provide the oil & gas industry with a comprehensive response to its training and development needs.
