Company Profile


Knudsen Engineering Limited, a Canadian owned and operated firm,
specializes in the design and manufacture of high performance
digital systems for marine applications. For over thirty five years
Knudsen products have been used by research institutions, port
authorities, navies, and hydrographic agencies as well as civil
engineering, dredging, and survey companies. The team includes
experts in underwater acoustics, digital signal processing,
electronic design, systems engineering, image display, data
acquisition, and transducer technology.


The Knudsen echosounders are used in over 60 countries
worldwide for precision measurement of water depths for
hydrographic survey, dredging, ship navigation, defence, and
scientific applications. The Company offers total "survey"
solutions and through OEM agreements is able to provide
customers with a complete survey package including echosounder,
GPS, heave sensor and datalogger.


Geographic Marketing Activity: U.S.; The United Kingdom; The
Netherlands; France; Belgium; Spain; Portugal; Poland;
Phillippines; Singapore; Malaysia; Vietnam; Korea; China;
Australia; Columbia; Argentina.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
SONAR & ECHO SOUNDING EQUIPMENT
Product Name:
320M Marine Echosounder
The 320M Marine Echosounder was developed for conventional
open-water survey, scientific and navigation applications. A
flexible architecture allows ready integration to other modern
survey tools including GPS, heave compensators, and
dataloggers. Available in single or dual frequency models, the
320M represents the current state of the art in echosounder
technology and includes unique capabilities such as built-in SCSI
interface, field programmable software, and a standard Windows
interface. The 320M was the chosen system for Canada's twelve
new minesweeper vessels, and is also used by well-known
institutes such as the Canadian Hydrographic Service, U.S. Army
Corps, and the Royal Dutch Geological Institute of Geology.

The new 320B portable echosounder is a ``black box`` version of
the 320M marine system. It has the functionality of the 320M
minus the printer and front control panel. It is designed to plug
into a personal computer and uses a Windows interface instead of
the front control panel. The 320B comes standard with the same
features as offered in the 320M, including a built-in SCSI
interface, field programmable software, and standard drivers to
other survey tools such as GPS, heave sensors, and dataloggers.
