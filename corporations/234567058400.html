Company Profile


MultiShred provides a permanent solution to secure your confidentiality on any media. Classified information is every organizations most critical asset. Unfortunately, its also your greatest liability. Paper and old IT Media assets are a burden on your security, storage and bottom line. MultiShred provides your organization with efficient and reliable value added shredding services that are approved by the Government of Canada.

MultiShred can improve your business and security integrity. Whichever product or service you choose, MultiShred can provide you with secure products/services to securely handle all of your shredding requirements. Our equipment is capable of destroying highly sensitive and confidential information to ensure the reputation of your organization.

Secure state if the art services for the destruction of classified assets and recycling services to complement your specific requirements.

Providing destruction services for over a decade. Multishred provides a permanent solution for the destruction of IT media, proprietary assets and documents. Environmentally safe shredding services and recycling programs.

Unique applications:

Your IT staff can spend more time securing your business IT and reducing vulnerability with our classified asset destruction services. Key / Major clients:

MultiShred provides innovative shredding services as well as product solutions that many business sectors rely on for destroying their classified assets. Our innovative solutions are being implemented by an increasing number of clients.
