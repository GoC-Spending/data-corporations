Company Profile


Specializing in interactive digital signage for touch screens or non-touch.

Free online content management system (CMS) for digital signage. MAPTmedia provides custom and easy self-managed online content solutions. Get started quickly with immediate availability, web-enabled from your browser - even your smartphone browser - our free online tool lets you manage, update and create the right solutions for your digital signage needs.

With free built-in tools for managing your network, creating mapping applications, interactive directories, donor recognition, or just simple display signage, our content management system has the tools you need to get started. If there's a customized solution that you require - whether it's database integration or interface designs - let us know and our team of developers and designers will bring your unique ideas to reality.

Success stories:

SFU Surrey contracted us to implement a large 46" touchscreen interactive wayfinding system capable of guiding students to their classes. Incorporated current events as a scrolling RSS feed, animated pathfinding, large numeric keypad for easy destination search, associated images of destination with their class search, multiple levels of 3D maps, minimalist display in eye-popping graphics. Both free-standing kiosk and in-wall mount utilized for two installations.

SFU Vancouver - We built wayfinding software for this system and worked with the administration as they designed the kiosk themselves. It turned out great! This particular wayfinding system integrates with their existing EBMS database software so that there is no duplication of data. All they do is update the source data and it is reflected in their wayfinding! Students, faculty, and visitors alike can search for events and rooms over four buildings spread out across beautiful downtown Vancouver, British Columbia. All 3D models developed in house.

HealthEast, Minnesota - An 18 touch-screen video-on-demand installation. One of our clients asked us to build a content and video-on-demand system for St. John's Hospital, one of Minnesota's HealthEast care facilities. Users of the touch-screens in this hospital are able to select video from a list of topics that would allow for more information about various health issues. The 18 touch-screens are updated online by the clients with our CMS (content management system) tool, allowing them to instantly update images, text, and video how they please. Clients have the ability to manage content, turning their display signage into dynamic display signage. Main content area to the left is updated by clients as can the bottom ticker feed information area. Buttons and logo on the right can be updated as well.

Surrey Memorial Hospital Foundation - website and kiosk touchscreen signage - The Surrey Memorial Hospital Foundation approached us to develop an exploded view of the Jim Pattison Outpatient Centre to be displayed on their website for a fundraising campaign. They wanted to physically show the spaces that potential donors would be contributing their donations to. This system's key features included an exploded view map showing major locations tied to donors, donor recognition and information, donation subscription form, instant updating of all text and media through the use of our online CMS (Content Management System) tool. In addition to the website, they also asked us to build a touch-screen donor kiosk. This kiosk displays the names of donors who have contributed to the Jim Pattison Outpatient Centre. The donor kiosk has a number of features: an exploded view map showing major locations tied to donor contributions, donor recognition and information, needs of the Foundation, ways to donate, and information about the clinics housed within the centre. All media content is instantly updated with our online CMS (Content Management System) tool by the client.




Testimonial:

On behalf of the Surrey campus of SFU, I’m pleased to provide a letter of reference for the principals of MAPTmedia, Gordon Manson and Matt Jeffrey.

I have worked with MAPTmedia since April 2008. The building we occupy is an award-winning design by Bing Thom Architects, but it is asymmetrical in shape and structure and can be confusing to navigate. SFU needed to have an easy-to-read campus map.

MAPTmedia, with their technical expertise, were able to translate our needs and basic floor plans into a sophisticated, yet easy-to-use directory. The directory includes room search functions, room information including photos, and current events on campus that are automatically updated from an existing database. The screen graphics and system performance perfectly reflects our high tech culture and environment. The system is robust and adaptable. MAPTmedia anticipated future needs for the campus and developed project phases so that SFU will be able to add enhancements and new modules as the campus grows.

In addition to providing the software platform, they researched hardware, including touch screen TV, PC controller and millwork or cabinetry to create a free-standing kiosk. This offers a fullservice solution for the client, successfully matching software and hardware.

Throughout the development of the interactive wayfinding system, MAPTmedia organized the project according to standard project management protocols. They set targets, schedules and budgets for SFU as the client to review and accept, from Pre-design and Needs Analysis, Schematic Concept and Design, Design Development to Testing and Implementation.

SFU would be pleased to host prospective clients at the Surrey Campus to demonstrate the MAPTmedia wayfinding system, or contact me directly for further information.

Yours truly
Marie Schneider, CFM
Facilities Manager
Simon Fraser University Surrey

Email: marie_schneider@sfu.ca
