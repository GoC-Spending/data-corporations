Company Profile


Vision Wheelchair Seating Systems Inc. is a dynamic and innovative Canadian owned company that
specializes in seating systems for wheelchairs.

Our products reflect our motto of `MAKE A DIFFERENCE'.
All our products have 30 months ``NO PROBLEM`` warranty.

We currently export to the USA, Japan, Israel and the UAE; we are interested in exporting to South America, Europe, the Middle East and Asia.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
DRY N COOL COVERS SET
It is two, internal & external, incontinent covers. The
covers are made with a combination of fabric, laminated film
and foam. The fabric creates a seamless seating surface.
The film ensures that the covers are waterproof. The foam
makes the covers breathable and absorbs fluids. The covers
are designed to disperse fluids from the skin. Both covers
are machine washable and dryable.


Product Name:
VITAL CUSHION
Gentley contoured, duel density cushion. Latex coated for a
better foam protection. Bevelled bottom for better adjustment to
the sling shape. It has a breathable cover that contains natural
fibres, and disperses away the natural perspiration. The VITAL
cushion is a comfort that promotes self propelling.


Product Name:
VITAL SPECIAL EDITION CUSHION
Maintains level seating surface for hammocked sling. This
cushion incorporates all the features of the VITAL cushion
with the edition of a rigid insert glued to the bottom of the
cushion, it also comes with the breathable cover.


Product Name:
SUPER VITAL CUSHION
This cushion incorporates all the features of the VITAL
cushion with the incontinent covers set that keeps the user DRY
N COOL.


Product Name:
VITAL DROP-SEAT
This drop-seat incorporates all the features of the VITAL
cushion with a set of 4 drop clips which designed to grip round
or square w/c rails, regardless of their diameter.


Product Name:
SUPER VITAL DROP-SEAT
It has all the features of the VITAL Drop Seat, and the set of
incontinent covers; DRY N COOL.

VerySOFT Cushion/Drop Seats

* Available in standard and custom sizes


Product Name:
VerySOFT VIVE Cushions/Drop Seats
 Designed in response to the Least Restraint/No Restraint policies
 Available as a regular depth leg trough - VIVE
or with DLT (deep leg troughs)  VIVE.DLT
 This Deep contour cushion promotes proper body positioning and postural stability

 High resiliency foam base cushion is used to prevent bottoming-out

* For category five wheelchairs
* Complete back is coated in memory foam to provide extreme support and comfort
* Inflatable lumber & lateral supports (with out of site out of mind bulbs to limit maintenance)
* Removable, machine washable and machine dryable external cover
* Available in standard and custom sizes/shapes
