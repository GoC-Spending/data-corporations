Company Profile


Marina Garrison Consulting offers Project Management and Business Analysis services in the Okanagan, British Columbia.

At Marina Garrison Consulting, we understand that software development, customization and implementation are the most challenging and critical undertakings your company will face in its lifetime. You must be assured that the job is done right from the start in a time frame that won't impair the day-to-day operations of your business.

We understand the unique issues surrounding implementation and have the product knowledge, technical expertise, and resources to ensure that your business is up and running as quickly as possible.

We'll help you not only implement your project, but also develop and attain business objectives and maximize your return on investment.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Project Management Consulting
We offer a complete range of project management and technical implementation services, encompassing all phases of the project lifecycle.

We use a process improvement framework based on the Software Engineering Body of Knowledge (SWEBOK) and the Project Management Body of Knowledge (PMBOK).

Project Management
Project success or failure is often determined during the first 20% of a project. Our project planning assistance will assure that your whole project is done right. Specific tasks, time frames, and resources are identified and a workable project plan is developed to meet your needs.

Project Recovery
About 75% of all software projects are delivered significantly late or cancelled, and the risk of problems grows as a projects size increases. Most projects are too critical to be cancelled. We can help rescue these projects using techniques accumulated through our vast project experience.

Service Name:
Business Analysis
Business Analysis & Solutions
No two projects are the same. For this reason, the actual requirements a project must meet are different for every client. We work together with you to analyze your business situation and to recommend solutions that fit your company. New systems are reviewed to insure that they accurately reflects the needs of the users.

Requirements Management
Most surveys list incomplete and changing requirements as the top causes of project failure. Our requirements workshops and change management processes can help you develop high quality requirements that provide a strong foundation for your project.

User Training & Documentation
As experienced trainers, our consultants can put together a training course that is customized to suit the needs of the users, including user manuals and on-line help.

High Performance Designs
Our knowledge of software architecture and design principles coupled with our experience solving many challenging design problems can be brought to bear on your project. Experienced in object-oriented design, we use UML and other appropriate notations and methodologies to capture document designs that allow for straight-forward implementation and future flexibility.

Testing
Our consultants are experienced in the full range of unit, integration, system and acceptance testing activities. Our clients typically utilize this expertise to assist with the development of testing philosophies, the production of user and technical test specifications and the implementation of formal test stages.
