Company Profile


Products:
Concentrators; water heaters; fume, liquid waste and solid waste incinerators (oxidizers); gas and oil burners; Snowmelters; vaporizers; air and gas heaters.

Company Description:
Trecan was established in 1962 as the Canadian subsidiary of Thermal Research, USA. In 1986 a controlling interest was purchased by MBB Mechanical Services. In 1993 MBB merged with Trecan to form MBB-Trecan. In 1996 Trecan was sold to one of the owners of MBB and renamed Trecan Combustion Limited.

Trecan specializes in the design, manufacture, installation and commissioning industrial combustion and incineration equipment and systems. The product range includes incineration systems for: medical waste; pharmaceutical waste; industrial solid waste; industrial liquid waste and fumes and waste gases; animal crematoriums; high efficiency industrial burners; Snowmelters; fired heaters; and submerged combustion
equipment. Incineration systems include waste heat recovery and flue gas scrubbers to meet local air quality regulations. The above product range was generally fairly well mixed up until the late 1980's and early 1990's. During this transition period, the demand for Snowmelters started to increase, and since the early 1990's, Snowmelters have become our main product, with stationary Snowmelter melting capacities from 20 ton/hour up to 1200 ton/hour, and portable Snowmelters ranging in melting capacity from 20 ton/hour up to 500 ton/hour.

Geographic Marketing Areas:
The firm's traditional market prior to the late 1980's has been 80% Canada, 15% USA and 5% other exports. From the late 1990's when Snowmelters became Trecan's main product, the geographic marketing area started to shift to the USA, with roughly 55% of sales, 40% in Canada, and 5% outside North America. In the last five to six years, the mix has changed to roughly 33% USA, 32% Canada, 33% Russia, and 2% China. As of late 2010, Trecan Snowmelters are working in the larger cities and airports in the snow belt of North America, and the main cities in Russia and China.

Key words:
Incinerators, oxidizers, solid waste, biomedical waste, liquid waste, fumes, VOCs, crematoriums, Snowmelters (snow melter), direct contact water heaters.

Manufacturer of Snowmelters; gas and oil burners, incinerators for medical, pathological , animal remains, general solid waste; liquid wastes; fume wastes; air heaters and gas heaters; inert gas generators; vaporizers; evaporators; direct contact water heaters.

Product Name:
Manufacturer of Snowmelters (Industrial Snow Melter)
Snowmelters are used at airports, city streets, parking lots, parking structures, and anywhere snow removal and dumping costs and environmental considerations require an economical and environmentally friendly solution.
Industrial Snowmelters use the submerged combustion direct contact process resulting in extremely high (95-98%) thermal transfer efficiencies resulting in lower fuel usage, lower CO2 emissions, and higher economical savings compared to other types of snow removal and assisted snow melting. Depending on the customer requirements and fuel type availability, Snowmelters utilize Natural Gas, LPG, Diesel, jet fuel, and could use hydrogen in the future when readily available.

Industrial submerged combustion water bath vaporizers are used to take cryogenic liquids and change to the gaseous state (high volume vaporization and regasification). Cryogenic liquids to gases include oxygen, nitrogen, propane, ethylene, ammonia and LNG, etc.

Product Name:
Manufacturer of Controlled Air Incinerators
The controlled air system usually has a two-stage system to limit toxic gas emissions by better temperature control. An environmental control system such as a scrubber and automatic loader complete the system. The only variation is the sizes of the units; units can handle volumes ranging from 25 kg to 1 ton/hour.

Product Name:
Manufacturer of Inert Gas Generators
Industrial inert gas generators are available in eight models with capacities from 2,000 to 60,000 scfh. Fired on either distillate fuel or gas and using the submerged combustion process, the system is reliable and extremely responsive. Inert atmospheres can be produced in just 30 seconds!

Service Name:
Manufacturer of Diesel fuel tanks to CSA B620 TC 406
Diesel fuel tanks designed and manufactured per Standard CSA B620 TC 406
Highway Tank and TC Portable Tanks for diesel generators, pumps, heating systems, etc.
Fuel tanks can be designed for single and multi-axle trailers and oil field/mining skids.
