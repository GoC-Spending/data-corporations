Company Profile


Vidir Carpet and Vinyl Carousels features include
sleek streamlined design, safety engineered, and equipped with
our user friendly patented loading mechanism, and mechanical
patented collapsible re-roll core. They are the best in the
marketplace. That is attested to by Home Depot, Eagle,
Revelstoke, Totem, RONA, Armstrong, Mannington, and others who
are specifying our Carousels. Since 1987 we have supplied more
than 2,000 carpet and vinyl handling machines worldwide.
Our Carpet Carousel equipped with our Deluxe Cut-N-Roll Table
makes our cutting and re-rolling system is the best in the
industry. When the operator chooses the carpet he/she wants,
the carpet gets pulled out over the table, and unto the roll up
table. From there it becomes a breeze. Press a button to
activate the Cut-N-Roll machine, and the carpet is measured and
rolled up in one pass. When the desired length has been reached,
the electronic counter shuts the machine down. Press another
button and the carpet is cut at the desired length. Re-activate
the Cut-N-Roll table and shrink wrap the carpet before loading.
The whole process is a one person operation, and the carpet
never touches the floor! Loading is a very simple procedure with
the aid of our patented loading bars. The power of the motor is
used to put the roll of flooring onto the machine. If the
optional transport dolly is purchased the roll will go directly
from the warehouse to the machine, never touching the floor.
This system makes it very easy to keep the rack full.
Other significant features on our larger carousels include:
more power through dual three phase motors, more frame supports
and a master electrical switch and timed keypad access switch.
Our certified structural engineering consultant reviews all
changes on our machines, to assure you that they continue to be
the safest, most dependable machines available today.
I look forward to helping you maximize productivity in your
plant. Please call me for any further information you may need.
Call me at 1800-210-0141 when you need more info
Others products we manufacture include dual cradle carpet
cutting machines, shelving carousels (installed at Hunter
Douglas plants Atlanta & Frostburg) and shrink wrap machines
