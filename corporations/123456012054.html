Company Profile


NJM Packaging offers its customers the benefits of one-stop shopping for all
their labeling and packaging equipment needs, including turnkey
line integration services. NJM has over 100,000 square
feet of production facilities incorporating research and
development, engineering, manufacturing, assembly and testing,
and parts supply functions.

QUALITY
In an effort to best serve our customers, NJM focuses on two
primary philosophies: 1) a strong dedication to developing long-
term relationships with our customers regarding their labeling
and packaging needs; and 2) a desire to provide the highest
quality products and services possible. These guiding principles
are applied in all facets of our day-to-day business. At
NJM Packaging, we believe quality is what sets us apart.

RELIABILITY
NJM's network of qualified sales and service professionals
serve all markets with thousands of successful installations to
our credit. Our client list is impressive: Glaxo Wellcome, Eli
Lilly, Wyeth-Ayerst, Merck Frosst, Proctor & Gamble and L'Oreal,
to name a few. We are also proud to have played a part in the
continuing growth of many small and medium-sized organizations
in all types of industries around the globe. We understand the
unique needs of each customer and strive to develop cost-
effective, quality equipment solutions to each customer's unique
requirements. The versatality of our customer base, the types of
markets we serve, and the types of applications we have
installed enable our customers to trust and rely on us for their
labeling and packaging equipment needs.

EXPERIENCE
Established in 1915, New Jersey Machine has been a recognized,
well-respected leader in manufacturing quality labeling and
packaging systems. CLI has been a leading packaging equipment
manufacturer, distributor, and provider of complete line
integration services since 1967. With NJM Packaging's combined years
of experience, our customers can trust that our recommendations
will help them implement the best, smoothest-running packaging
line possible for their applications--not only now but as their
needs grow and change in the future. Our diverse experience and
breadth of products enables our customers to gain from the best
available technology for their particular needs, including
custom engineering, without having to deal with a multitude of
vendors. Our experience saves our customers time and money.

SERVICE
NJM Packaging is committed to customer satisfaction and the long-term
relationship we strive to maintain with every customer. In
addition to offering standard warranties for our equipment, we
also offer extended warranties and preventative maintenance
programs. Our parts department inventories thousands of
different items to assure fast, efficient fulfillment of all
part orders, minimizing downtime and loss of productivity. Our
qualified, factory-trained service technicians ensure smooth
installation, provide on-site training and ongoing support to
ensure our equipment performs to our customers' standards and
requirements. At NJM Packaging we believe service is our #1 product.

Product Name:
TURNKEY LINE INTEGRATION SERVICES
NJM/CLI's diverse knowledge, experience and breadth of product
enable our customers to profit from well-designed lines
consisting of the best, most appropriate technology for their
particular needs without having to deal with a multitude of
vendors. One source means quicker, smoother installations;
simplified paperwork; comprehensive validation documentation;
and better after-sale service, support, and accountability. Our
total packaging solutions lead to significant bottom line
savings for our customers.
Whether you have a tablet, solid or liquid filling application,
a tube filling or blister packaging application, or other
packaging needs, NJM/CLI has all the pieces of the packaging
line puzzle. Our portfolio of products includes just about any
type of labeling and packaging equipment you may need--from
bottle to pallet bar code labeling equipment. Every aspect of
the line--from design, layout, manufacturing, assembly, testing,
documentation, and shipping--is carefully coordinated by our
team of sales, engineering, and manufacturing professionals. A
project manager is assigned to each project to oversee and track
your order's progress through our system to ensure that you
receive it accurately and on time. Our qualified, factory-
trained service technicians ensure smooth installation, provide
on-site training and ongoing support to ensure your equipment
performs to peak efficiency for many years to come.


Product Name:
EQUIPMENT OPTIONS AND ACCESSORIES
To complement our broad equipment portfolio, we offer an equally
impressive variety of options and accessories which can be
installed on your equipment or integrated into your packaging
line to maximize your investment and increase productivity. The
latest inspection, verification, and line monitoring technology,
including bar code readers, vision systems, emergency stop,
reject devices, centralized PLC controls, and full security
systems, can be added. NJM/CLI can also provide imprinters,
thermal transfer printers, conduits for smooth, safe
coordination of electrical and air supplies, cap elevators,
special container handling systems, hardware, software--whatever
you need.

Service Name:
COMPREHENSIVE VALIDATION SUPPORT SERVICES
A concern often voiced by our customers is how to comply and
adhere with FDA regulations and validation protocols. NJM/CLI's
validation support team is available to assist your in-house
validation team with the development of comprehensive validation
test protocols customized to your specific equipment, internal
documentation format and functional requirements. We specialize
in supplying the support documentation required to write the
IQ/OQ (Installation and Operational Qualification). Complete
validation packages can also be supplied. Our experience as
equipment manufacturers and designers provides us the ability to
help you produce a validation protocol faster and more
accurately. Fast turn around on validation protocols reduces
start up time, frees internal validation staff for other tasks
and saves money. The result is a seamless validation package for
the entire line, implemented in a minimal amount of time.

Technology profile

NJM Packaging is committed to maintaining its leadership position in
the labeling and packaging equipment industry through investing
in an ongoing, dedicated new product research and development
program. Our well-known brands (i.e. Pony, Thorobred, and
Mustang labelers and Unicap cappers) are a testament to the
strength of this strategy which has brought us to the enviable
competitive position we are in today. NJM is committed to
the technological innovation and new product development to
maximize our customers' productivity.
