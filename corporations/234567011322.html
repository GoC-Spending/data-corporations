Company Profile


SOMOS Consulting Group Ltd. is a dynamic management consulting and training company operating in Ottawa, Toronto, Calgary and Edmonton, Canada. Since its inception in 1991, SOMOS team of experienced Senior Management Consulting Professionals have provided management solutions that address the needs of technology and business based organizations. Our management consulting and training services enable organizations to achieve an industry leadership position in organizational efficiency and management best practices. SOMOS has Canadian and International consulting experience in information technology, telecommunications, microelectronics, computer software development, life sciences, pharmaceutical and the financial and government sectors.

SOMOS has planned and delivered hundreds of major projects in various areas, including: information system development and implementation; project management control system design and implementation; systems integration; new product development; and business process improvement. With its Professional management, business, engineering, and software development consultants, SOMOS is uniquely positioned to serve a wide variety of organizations and needs by providing a diverse range of services.

In September of 2010 SOMOS became the first North American organization to be awarded the designation of Accredited Consulting Organization (ACO) for the PRINCE2 project management methodology. SOMOS provides PRINCE2 consulting and, in association with Advantage Learning Ltd. of the UK, offers PRINCE2 and related methodologies training across Canada.

SOMOS has helped many people and organizations in acquiring the knowledge, skills and tools they need to effectively manage and deliver efficient, practical results for todays complex world. SOMOS is a Project Management Institute (PMI) Global Registered Education Provider (REP) and our best practices educational programs and professional services are based on the industry-standard PMBOK framework developed by the Project Management Institute.
