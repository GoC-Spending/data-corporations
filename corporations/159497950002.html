Company Profile


SED Systems is a Canadian advanced technology company specializing in satellite communications engineering and custom RF and electronics manufacturing.

SED has extensive expertise in communications, satellite test and control, defense systems engineering and in custom electronic system manufacturing. We have proven professional engineering and manufacturing capabilities, a strong renewing technological base, and an international reputation for excellence.

SED has been a successful team member on numerous national and international projects. As a result of 50 years experience, SED has the project management skills, systems engineering capability, integrated logistics support, and product assurance procedures in place to meet the highest project standards.

FACILITIES & EQUIPMENT:

SED operations are located in a 11,600 square meter (125,000 square foot) custom built facility in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan.

The facility includes 4,000 square meters (43,000 square feet) of manufacturing and integration space, and a 2000 pound force vibration table. The building is fully secure.

The Canadian Space Agency's Telemetry, Tracking, and Command station for RADARSAT is located adjacent to SED's facility.

A partial list of SED's customers includes Boeing, Hughes, Intelsat, Inmarsat, the Canadian Space Agency, the European Space Agency, General Dynamics Canada, General Dynamics Land Systems, DRS, SEI, Echostar, Sirius/XM Radio, the Canadian Department of National Defence, Kidde Aerospace & Defense, Blackberry

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Satellite Gateway Systems
Since 1990, SED has been providing satellite gateways to
telecommunications service providers, in both civilian and
military markets. Providing the interface between satellite and
terrestrial communications networks, these systems support
voice, fax and data services and are applicable to geostationary
and low/medium earth-orbiting satellites in global or regional
satellite communications networks.

Product Name:
Satellite In-Orbit Test Equipment
SED has been providing world class satellite test technology
since the late 1970s. We now supply a broad range of products to
support satellite testing, communications payload monitoring,
carrier monitoring and remote earth station verification
testing. We also provide expert assistance in satellite test
campaigns.

Our In-orbit Test Systems are used after launch of a
communications satellite to verify payload performance meets
specifications. Our systems use swept techniques and optimized
algorithms, resulting in faster, more accurate measurements.



Product Name:
Satellite Spectrum Monitoring Systems
The Satellite Spectrum Monitoring System (SSMS) is used to
monitor communications traffic through satellites.

The SSMS is capable of measuring a number of parameters and
characteristics of the traffic, displaying the information, and
may be remotely controlled and operated from any location.

Now operating in five continents, these systems provide
automated monitoring of satellite traffic in order to
effectively manage the use of communications payload resources.
In addition to the standard configuration, these systems are
also available in a transportable version, facilitating
verification and monitoring of spot beam satellite systems.



Product Name:
Satellite Command and Control Systems
The communications link between mission control facilities and
the spacecraft, our Telemetry Tracking and Command Systems are a
vital part in the management of space resources. We are
providing systems for applications ranging from satellite
communications and remote sensing to the control and monitoring
of deep space instruments.

We can also include a variety of satellite control functions
such as command management and flight dynamics by integrating
proven off-the-shelf software packages into the system.

Product Name:
Satellite Feeder-link Stations
Our Feeder Link Stations transmit audio and data to orbiting
satellites which in turn, broadcast the signals to large
regional audiences. Whether for television or digital radio
applications, SED's up-link systems provide flexible, reliable
service to the broadcast industry.




Product Name:
Network Management Systems
Network Management Systems provide network-level support and
control of satellite communications systems, including VSAT
hubs, Network Control Stations and Network Operations Systems.
SED has been providing network management systems for over 10
years.

Unique applications:

In-orbit Test Systems


Today's modern satellite communications payloads
rely on advanced digital signal processing
techniques that offer extreme flexibility in
signal routing through the satellite network.
SED's IOT systems are equally flexible, able to
validate performance in all operational
configurations, regardless of frequency and power
level changes.


New generation low- and medium-earth orbit
satellites can be in view of a ground station for
only minutes at a time. Numerous complicated
measurements must be completed during these
limited times while compensating for factors
associated with satellite movement relative to
the ground station. SED's IOT systems have been
used successfully to test these new generation
satellites.


SED's IOT systems have pioneered multi-carrier
measurement techniques, where a large number of
signals can be transmitted to the satellite at
the same time. This technique more closely
approximates the normal traffic the satellite
will see when put into service and permits the
measurement of many parameters at the same time,
further reducing test durations to get the
satellite into service sooner.


SED's test systems support a central control site
that can access measurement systems at remote
sites around the world over low bandwidth links.
This reduces the need to have trained staff
located at the remote sites and allows for a
concentration of expertise at the customer's main
facility.


SED was the first to deliver systems where a
signal transmitted from one earth station was
received and measured by another earth station
thousands of miles away.


SED continues to evolve its IOT product line to
meet future challenges associated with evolving
satellite communications programs that use higher
frequencies and more bandwidth to provide more
flexible service applications to a growing global
user base.

Satellite Gateways and Network Systems

The cornerstone of our success is the flexibility
of implementation. Designed to be easily
configured for varying traffic levels and unique
customer requirements, our systems can be easily
expanded as traffic grows. And, because of the
modular nature of the software, modifications to
add new services are simplified.

Success Stories
In-orbit Test Systems

SED is a world leader in communication satellite
testing, having delivered In-orbit Test Systems
to the leading satellite manufacturers and
service providers around the world. These
customers depend on SED's expertise to
demonstrate that the heart of their multi-billion
dollar communications system is operating as
required. Despite competition from companies many
times its size, SED is enjoying increasing market
acceptance of its IOT systems. We are providing
systems to some of the largest satellite
manufacturers and operators around the world,
customers who are leading the way with new
generation commercial satellite programs. A
testament to the quality and utility of these
systems is the repeat business SED enjoys from
these customers as their program requirements
evolve to serve new global communications needs.

Satellite Gateways and Network Systems

The proliferation of new satellite communications
services for global and regional markets has
dramatically increased the demand for reliable
network inter-connectivity. SED has met the
demand with several innovative system solutions.

We are a leader in the supply of Access Control
and Signaling Equipment (ACSE) for Inmarsat M and
B services. SED was the first company in the
world to be awarded a contract for the supply of
an M/B ACSE and the first to have systems
carrying traffic in all four ocean regions. SED
has since provided M/B gateways to British
Telecom, Telstra (Australia) Singapore Telecom,
INDOSAT, and VSNL (India). We continue to work
closely with them to include new services such as
Mini-M and mailbox services, as they are defined
and implemented by Inmarsat.

Our Network Systems provide network-level support
and control of satellite communications systems.
Our Mobility Management systems provide a global
registry for all mobile terminals in Inmarsat's
global satellite communications network the
network to improve access efficiency for users.
Our Network Coordination Systems manage
Inmarsat's satellite resources by allocating
channels between the mobile terminals and the
land earth stations, ensuring that precious
network resources are utilized efficiently, to
improve performance for the user and
profitability for the operator.
