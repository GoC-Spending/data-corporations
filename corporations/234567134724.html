Company Profile


Kentz is a global engineering specialist solutions provider with over 10,000 employees operating in 26 countries worldwide. It has provided specialist Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) services, Construction, and Technical Support Services to clients in the energy and resources sectors for more than 90 years. Kentz is listed on the AIM market of the London Stock Exchange (symbol: KENZ
