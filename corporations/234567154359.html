Company Profile


Zenith Media Canada is a Montreal, Quebec based marketing company specializing in turn-key solutions for small businesses.

They have packages that allow any company to get online with a domain name, cloud web hosting, design, emails and business card printing.

Zenith Media Canada has been offering service since 2000 under the name Sphere Telecommunications and has since rebranded as Zenith Media Canada in March of 2014.

Zenith Media Canada has its primary client website located at www.zenithmedia.ca, their network division at www.zenithmedia.net, non-profit division at www.zenithmedia.org. They also own www.zenithmedia.me that is currently being used by end users.

Based on recent interviews, Zenith Media Canada plans to re-launch www.spherehosting.ca as part of a 99c hosting service for non commercial use. (Dec 2nd 2014)

Zenith Media Canada uses local partners to expand their service, they have partnered up with Toronto Based OpenSRS in 2009 to offer domain name names and security services.

They have recently (March 2014) partnered with Stripe the API driven credit card processing gateway to offer integrated credit card processing for all their clients.

Zenith Media has partnered with VCCS Telecom (Montreal, Quebec) in an effort to offer VOIP telephone service to their larger business customers and drive their turn-key solutions to the next level.

They also bost partnerships with CloudFlare using their Railgun technology, SAJE the Montreal based local business development center and a few others in an ever evolving race to offer cutting edge technology using Canada based companies.

Social Media links to follow their progress.

www.zenithmedia.ca/facebook, www.zenithmedia.ca/yelp, www.zenithmedia.ca/youtube, www.zenithmedia.ca/linkedin
