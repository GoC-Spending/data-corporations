Company Profile


Reliability Screening Solutions provides a range of screening services designed to assist employers and other agencies with a need to know the background of individuals. Our clients include federal government agencies, volunteer organizations, corporations, lawyers and consultants who specialize in assisting Immigration and Pardons applicants.

Reliability Screening Solutions provides RCMP Certified Criminal Records Checks via electronic fingerprint submission and Canadian Police Certificates, including both CPIC and Premium CPIC checks, via name and date of birth.

The RCMP has certified our equipment and systems ensuring that they have appropriate image quality and are compliant with Real Time Identification (RTID) standards for fingerprints that are submitted electronically to the CCRTIS national repository of fingerprints and criminal record information.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Fingerprint Submission Systems
Reliability Screening Solutions Inc. is an RCMP certified vendor of elctronic fingerprint submission system. Our equipment and systems has been tested to ensure that they have appropriate image quality and are compliant with Real Time Identification (RTID) standards for fingerprints that are submitted electronically to the CCRTIS national repository of fingerprints and criminal record information. As a prerequisite to undergoing CCRTIS vendor certification, our devices have been certified for compliance with the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) Integrated Automated Fingerprint Identification System (IAFIS) Image Quality Specifications (IQS). Our products that have been certified by CCRTIS may be used by authorized agencies to access and benefit from the efficiency of RTID.

Service Name:
Electronic Fingerprinting
Relability Screening Solutions Inc. is able to capture and submit electronic fingerprint from your own place of business. To conduct a certified criminal record check, CCRTIS requires a full set of fingerprints, including both rolled and flat impressions of all ten fingers. There is no subsitute for a Certified Criminal Records Check, it is the only way that you can be sure that the individual you are dealing with is really who they claim to be and that they do not have a criminal record.

CCRTIS maintains the national repository of fingerprint and criminal record information and is mandated to provide direct operational support to the Canadian law enforcement, criminal justice and public security communities, as well as international partners such as the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) and Interpol for criminal, civil and immigration purposes. CCRTIS is the sole provider of criminal record verifications for civil and criminal court purposes as well as the security screening environment for all levels of government and the general public.

CCRTIS clients submitting electronic fingerprints now receive responses within 72 hours (3 business days) for the majority of civil clearance fingerprint requests that do not link to a criminal file. The goal of CCRTIS is to respond to 85% of civil requests (those that do not hit a criminal file) submitted electronically within 72 hours. If a criminal record is encountered during the verification process, the turnaround time could exceed 120 days.

Reliability Screening Solutions service are available from 6:30 am to 9:30 pm seven days per week, excluding statutory holidays. To book an appointment for a Customer Service Representative call us at 613-216-1888, send a fax to 613-482-5090 or email us at Service@ReliabilityScreening.com.

Service Name:
Criminal Background Checks (CPIC)
Canadian police certificate (record check by name and date of birth only, not fingerprint based) can be performed. To conduct a Police Records Check, the Canadian Police Information Centre (CPIC) database, operated by the Royal Canadian Mounted Police (RCMP), is searched for relevant criminal record information and/or the existence of a CPIC record. The CPIC database is a compilation of police information and court decisions. Information collected in the CPIC database includes individuals who:

have a criminal record for any Criminal Code or other Federal Statute offence and/or
have been judged not criminally responsible for an offence because of mental disorder and/or
have federal and/or provincial charges pending and/or
are on probation or subject to a Prohibition Order.
The voluntary organization must have your written permission to request a PRC. Since 2001, as a result of amendments to the Criminal Records Act, criminal records of pardoned sex offenders can be made available to organizations that work with vulnerable people. Your written consent is required to search the CPIC database for pardoned sexual offences. Depending on the juristiction, we also work with local police agencies to access their own databases, which contain information about every complaint they receive. Examples of complaints include:
local disturbances
offences contrary to provincial statutes (such as traffic violations or liquor-related violations)
abuse of children
allegations of offences where charges were not laid.

Reliability Screening Solutions services are available from 6:30 am to 9:30 pm seven days per week, excluding statutory holidays. To book an appointment for a Customer Service Representative call us at 613-216-1888, send a fax to 613-482-5090 or email us at Service@ReliabilityScreening.com.


Service Name:
International Fingerprint Card Conversion
We accept ink fingerprint cards from virtually every country in the world and convert them to RCMP certified electronic files. We then submit these files to the RCMP and search their criminal records database. Results that typically take 120 days to complete can be completed in as little as 3 - 4 days. If you are looking for an expedited service we can even have the RCMP embossed and certified criminal history search results returned to our Ottawa office and couriered to clients in any country. Our services are used by individuals qualifying to live and work in Canada as well as by Canadians who are now working abroad and need to provide a certified criminal history to their employer. If you are immigrating to Canada or require a work visa we can help, email us at Service@ReliabilityScreening.ca or call us at 613-216-1888.

Service Name:
Embassy Authentication/Legalization of Documents
If the country receiving your criminal history check requires the documents to be authenticated by their embassy or consulate in Canada we are able to provide this service for all Canadian jurisdictions by hand delivering documents directly to the Ottawa embassy for the receiving country. Our services have standard pricing for the first document authenticated by Department of Foreign Affairs and International Trade (DFAIT) as well as a number of different foreign government embassies. We also have discounted pricing for any additional documents that need to be authenticated. For example, teaching english as a second language (ESL) generally requires authentication of both the certified original RCMP criminal history search results, as well as academic qualifications. Authentication of a notarized copy of the diploma requires it to be presented with the original diploma and a sealed envelope of original transcripts, however applicants can often be completed using a certification of degree letter from a Canadian university. Please note that embassy authentication services often require us to present a copy of the applicant’s passport with the documents being authenticated.

Service Name:
Premium CPIC Name Check
The CPIC Name Check is a query of the Identification Data Bank by an authorized Canadian police service using a person’s name and date of birth to identify the possible criminal convictions. These checks, however, omit review of other data banks that could provide valuable screening information. Reliability Screening Solutions' Premium CPIC Name Check provides a thorough review of other data banks including those which identify the following:

- Outstanding entries, such as charges and warrants, judicial orders, peace bonds, probation and prohibition.

- Absolute and Conditional discharges.

- Criminal Charges resulting in dispositions including but not limited to: cases where charges have been Withdrawn or Dismissed, and cases where the individual has been found Not Criminally Responsible by Reason of Mental Disorder as listed on local indices.

-Negative police contacts including, but not limited to: theft, weapons, sex offences or violent, harmful and threatening behavior.

Service Name:
Declaration of Criminal Record
CPIC Name Checks are fast but criminal information can only be confirmed, not disclosed using our service. Therefore, you are aware of an existing criminal record you should use our declaration of criminal record option. There are three possible outcomes of a Declaration of Criminal Record application:

Negative – no association could be made with a criminal record.

Incomplete – there is a possible match to a criminal record that is in addition to or different from the declared record and that fingerprints should be submitted to certify that the match is or is not correct.

Confirmed – This is when an individual declares an existing criminal record and we have been able to confirm that this declaration is a complete and accurate record of information held in the data bank.
