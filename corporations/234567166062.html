Company Profile


LED Light Bar is the Canadian and USA supplier of Earthtrack industrial LED lights built tough and made to last even under the harshest conditions. Whether you are deep in the northern mines, running heavy construction site equipment or working long nights to harvest your crops, LED Light Bars has LED lighting solutions for your worksite needs.

After the workday is done, it’s time to hit the trails in the off-road truck or Quad, and let the high-powered LED Lights from Earthtrack Group light the way for play! We offer an extensive line up of LED lighting options for your personal vehicle or off-road toys. Standard headlights may be fine for urban areas, but when you hit bad weather or remote road conditions count on LED Light Bar driving lights to lead the way for maximum visibility.

"If these lights are designed first for the tough environment of the Australian Mining sector, what does that mean for your off road adventures?" It means you've picked the right light. It is built to be tough.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
LED Light Bar
LED Light Bars are designed for recreational vehicles to be able to see well at night.

Product Name:
Commander LED Light Bar
The Commander 400W dual row 40" Xtreme LED light bar you can light up the night with ease.

With 80 x 5 watt CREE LED’s this high Intensity light bar illuminates a 90 degree angle with a 8 degree central beam making nighttime driving safer than its even been. Its strong, high impact die-cast aluminum housing and IP 68 waterproof rating make it fit for even the most extreme environment.

The commander also comes in 20", 30", and 50" sizes to help work the size you need.

Product Name:
Eclipse LED Light Bar
With 80 x 5 Cree LED’s the 400W Eclipse light bar is an option for those looking for a curved light bar with a bit more power. The 2 rows of LED’s allow for more power without making the light bar too wide.

The 400W Eclipse light bar, with CREE LED’s, is a great option for medium sized vehicles which are used for night driving. The attractive curve design and dual row of the Eclipse range also provides a wider pane of illumination.

The Eclipse Curved LED Light Bar also comes in 12", 20", 30" and 50" options.

Product Name:
LED Round Headlights
When you were a kid you had to be home by dark. Not anymore. LED Light Bar offers Earthtrack’s 7" LED round headlights so you can keep playing well into the night. Whether off road or on the highway, it doesn't matter. These 7" Round LED Headlights are DOT approved and will help light up any road. Get outside and enjoy the dark.

Product Name:
Nitro LED Driving Lights
The Nitro 70W LED driving light (image not exactly as shown) features 7 high output 10W Cree LEDÕs producing an exceptional 13,300 lumen per pair. With a polycarbonate lens, acrylic lens cover, tough alloy die-cast housing and stainless steel fittings, these lights are well suited to highway or off road driving.

Product Name:
Enforcer LED Work Light
The all new Enforcer 60W LED work light offers the very latest in high powered LED technology; Ideal for use on heavy duty mining and construction vehicles. With its superbly compact design, the Enforcer offers 145 degrees of adjustment ensuring a solution to the most demanding application.

The Enforcer features revolutionary high output optics which produce optimum light levels and premium visibility in even the darkest mines. When you need a tough and versatile light for your heavy equipment, the Enforcer is the answer.

Electronic Thermal Management (ETM) monitors the heat output of the LEDs and makes use of integrated Pulse Width Modulation circuitry in order to maintain safe operating temperatures which prolong the LED life span to its full 50,000 hours.

Integrated Pulse Width Modulation (PWM) dimming circuitry is used by the Enforcer to manage heat levels. PWM works by flashing the LED at a frequency faster than the human eye can detect, reducing the time that the LED is operating while still delivering 100% light output.



Product Name:
Work Buddy LED Work Light
The 50W LED Work Buddy is an extremely versatile light with countless applications. The light has a handle for adjustment to your work area, and produces 2,800 lumen while drawing only 1.84 amps. With a super wide, even spread of light, it is ideal for use on service trucks and rear decks on trailers and pick-ups. These lights are multi-volt and can be used in all industries.

Product Name:
Duravision HD LED Work Light
This 5 X 10W Cree LED work light offers the very latest, high powered LED technology, and is ideal for use on heavy duty mining vehicles. Superbly compact and exceptionally tough, it offers a substantial 145 degrees of adjustment, ensuring a solution to the most demanding applications. This model is available with Heavy Duty Mount, Standard Mount and Side Mount attachment options.

Product Name:
Work Buddy 45 W LED Work Light
The 45W LED Work Buddy Rectangle work light is an extremely versatile LED light with countless applications. The light produces 2,300 lumen while drawing only 1.64 amps. With a super wide, even spread of light, it is ideal for use on service trucks and rear decks on trailers and commercial vehicles. These lights are multi-volt and can be used in all industries.
