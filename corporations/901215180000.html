Company Profile


Presagis is an industry leader in commercial-off-the-shelf (COTS) modeling, simulation, and embedded display graphics software. We help customers in the Aerospace, Defense, and Automotive industries to create, train, simulate, and visualize.

Our Technology
At Presagis, we build customer value through continual innovation and the rapid advancement of technology. We are committed to providing easy-to-use integrated solutions that meet the rigorous demands of our customers' complex applications. Our world-class solutions are used by industry leaders, including Boeing , Lockheed Martin , Airbus, SAAB , BAE Systems , DaimlerChrysler, Volkswagen, CAE, Thales, Northrop Grumman, and Vivendi Games.

With core technology built on industry standards, Presagis is creating the world's first truly unified simulation toolset. We offer our customers an unmatched range of solutions for efficiently developing tailored visualization, simulation, and embedded applications.

Our History
Presagis was formed following CAE's acquisition of three industry-leading companies: Engenuity Technologies, MultiGen-Paradigm, and TERREX. By integrating the powerful products created by these companies, Presagis is extending its knowledge base and is bringing innovative and integrated solutions to customers. The OpenFlight, VAPS, and TerraPage standards, as well as the HLA communications standard, are long standing legacies of these companies and will continue to be the foundation for the Presagis product portfolio.

Our Commitment
Presagis is committed to excellent customer care. Our commitment to our customers is present in how we develop software, in the product range we offer, in the services we provide, and in how quickly we respond to customer feedback.

By developing the most robust, feature rich, flexible COTS software that helps our customers meet their objectives, Presagis continually strives for excellence in customer satisfaction.

Our Name
"Presagis" (Pre - say - gis) is derived from the word "presage", which means to forecast or predict, and speaks to our core products that help our customers anticipate and prepare for the future. Presagis is a visionary leader that provides advanced COTS solutions for simulation, modeling, and embedded applications.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
VAPS XT
VAPS XT is a next generation PC-based software tool for the rapid development of dynamic, interactive, real-time graphical HMIs. Offering greater freedom and limitless design possibilities, VAPS XT is ideally suited for data display, simulation applications, and the development of embedded systems.

Product Name:
VAPS
VAPS is a software tool suite that has become the de-facto standard for rapid prototyping, designing, testing, and deploying HMIs. VAPS enables the development of dynamic, interactive, real-time graphical HMIs for complex applications such as the displays and controls found in the cockpit of an aircraft or the instrumentation of an automobile. VAPS is used by teams of engineers and users to jointly design and test the look, feel, functionality, and behavior of a particular device or group of devices.

Product Name:
FlightSIM/HeliSIM
FlightSIM and HeliSIM are the industry standard toolkits for high-fidelity, PC-based, flight simulations. From building and evaluating simulators, training equipment, and cockpits to developing part-task trainers, FlightSIM and HeliSIM are the smart solutions.

The open architecture of FlightSIM/HeliSIM also allows for seamless integration with STAGE for computer-generated forces, with VAPS for interactive controls and graphical interfaces, and with 3rd party partner technologies, including viewers and other high fidelity models.


Product Name:
STAGE
Designed for building and animating synthetic environments containing both moving and stationary entities in real-time, STAGE is:
The best-in-class COTS software solution for real-time scenario generation
The ideal solution for the training and evaluation of individuals and teams operating in a tactical environment
The right choice for analyzing tactical scenarios and/or systems in a simulated environment
Perfectly suited for the simulation of real or synthetic systems for prototyping purposes, including real-time simulations of scenarios containing a large number of entities.
STAGE allows you to simulate any combination of civil and military traffic, including planes, vehicles, ships, weapons, and sensors.


Product Name:
Creator
Creator is the modeling & simulation industry's leading software for creating 3D models optimized for real-time simulation. Creator provides immersive control over the entire modeling process. The polygon-based authoring system quickly generates optimized objects, characters, and areas-of-interest for a wide range of simulation applications.

Product Name:
Terra Vista
Terra Vista is a terrain modeling software tool for developing sophisticated terrain databases. In addition to providing the highest level of correlation in more formats than any other terrain database product in the industry, Terra Vista also ensures support for all major SAF/CGF formats, including OneSAF, CTDB, and JSAF.

Product Name:
Vega Prime
Vega Prime is a visualization toolkit for the real-time 3D development and deployment of simulation applications. Ideal for both high-performance and low-cost hardware platforms, Vega Primes flexible plug-in architecture facilitates the rapid design and prototyping of real-time 3D applications by utilizing the most sophisticated technology available.
