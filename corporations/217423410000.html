Company Profile


Since 1957, our company has manufactured high quality equipment
for the bakery, confectionery and other food industries.

We specialise in manufacturing:

- Industrial ovens
- Proofers
- Bowl lift
- Horizontal cake slicer
- Adjustable sheet pan depositor
- Roasting oven
- Automatic muffin batter depositor
- Mixers
- Automatic lines for making pies.
- And many more equipment

It is with great pride that we present our products.

Always looking to offer you equipment which meets your high
standards of quality in regards to materials, design and
construction. We take all the necessary steps in order to
satisfy your expectations. We manufacture custom-made equipment
to suit your specific needs.

We are sure that our products and our experience will be
profitable in the bakery industry in all the world.

Product Name:
Picard Model T Revolving Oven
Top quality baking thanks to our revolutionary burners (direct
fire)and our efficient heat exchanger (indirect fire). A truly
heavy-duty oven with minimum maintenance. Heat exchanger and fully
insulated walls and top. Built-in steam system. Stainless steel
construction. Available with 4, 6, 8 or 10 shelves and capacity
of up to 80 baking trays. 100% glass door, automatic start and
stop system for shelves, mechanical steam evacuation system
available as options.

Product Name:
Flametech Rotisserie PR Oven
Model Flametech PR unique dual rotation system allows you to
prepare the tastiest and most tender roasted chicken. Powerful
(125,000 btu) gas or propane jets. Refractory bricks line the
combustion chamber. Electronic digital thermostat. Forward and
reverse rotation. Wide door opening. The door slides inside the
front panel to save space and prevent prevent accidental burns.
Oven is mounted on solid wheels. Stainless steel front, interior, and
exterior side panels. Stainless steel pan on bottom retains
chicken grease. Capacity of 12 skewers holding up to 60 chickens
Of 3.3 lbs. (1.5 kg.)automatic pneumatic door opening system
with foot switch also available as an option. 36 chicken model available also.

Product Name:
Picard LP-200 Stone Conveyor oven
Model LP-200 can reach high temperatures. Ideal for products
which need a short baking time. Designed for simultaneous
operation with both gas (bottom) and electricity (top). Maximum
temperature of 525 degrees F at the bottom, 575 degrees F at the
top. Baking compartment, front, back and top panels made of
Stainless steel. Aluminum sides. Six inches of insulation in
walls and ceiling. Removable panels for easy access to mechanical
systems. Panoramic window. Baking belt made of natural granite
stone.

Product Name:
Picard Reversible Sheeter
Models SM-520s and SM-630. Handy adjustable roller gap setting.
End-stop device for desired thickness. Convenient stop-and-go
rocking lever. Scientifically calculated synchronization speed
For extremely delicate types of dough. Protective safety guard
on each side of rollers. Fold up conveyors for compact storage.
Reel for rolling up sheeted dough. Flour reservoir.

Model SM-630 only: two catch pans-one for each end. Retractable
swivel casters ensure stability during operation and allow for
easy mobility when machine must be removed.

Product Name:
Picard Final Proofer
Heat and humidity automatically controlled by separate
electronic thermostats. The operator may select desired relative
humidity (as high a 90% rh)in any temperature range up to 120
degrees F. Master power switch, latches and hinges are heavy
duty die cast. Aluminum construction except for front which is
all stainless steel. Walls and ceilings are constructed of
sandwich aluminum panels insulated with moisture resistant and
flame retardant polystyrene. Extruded stainless steel frame.
Bumper guards made of aluminum. Safety inside escape latch.
Lighting. The air conditioner is constructed of stainless steel
and aluminum. Wide range of models available.

Product Name:
Picard Horizontal Cake Slicer
Model LP-450 makes perfect slices for all kinds of cakes by
combining multiple cutting heads. Adjustable blade (height).
Thickness of bottom slice must be at least 1/2 inch. Following
slice may be as thin as 1/16 of an inch. Variable-speed belt. Dc
drive. Safety guards. Adjustable legs provided. Available in
varying conveyor lengths depending on the number of cutting
heads that best suit your needs.


Product Name:
Picard Semi-Automatic Bun Divider And Rounder
Model SM-11-36 divides and rounds soft and hard dough evenly.
Production capacity of up to 6,000 pieces/hour depending on
operator efficiency and production flow. Easy weight adjustment.
Motor overload protection. Easy cleaning. Three lightweight,
sanitary plastic rounding plates are included.

Product Name:
Picard Bowl Lifter
Model LP-280 for hobart bowls. Hydraulic system able to lift up
bowls 73 inches from floor. 12-volt rechargeable, heavy duty
battery. Charge indicator. Removable control box. Only one
operator required. Made for 140, 80 and 60-quart bowls.

Product Name:
Picard Water Cooling Unit
Model LP-460, 1-hp compressor, 240 volts, 1 phase. Water goes in
with city pressure and comes out refrigerated and under pressure.
It can be connected directly to a water meter; made of stainless
steel.

Product Name:
Picard Spiral Mixer With Fixed Bowl
Solid and sturdy construction. Practically maintenance-free.
Entirely belt-driven transmission which results in quiet
operating cycles. Central tool spiral arm, safety guard and bowl
are 100% stainless steel (safety guards for mixers SM-25 and
SM-50). Overload protection. Two timers: one for each speed
(except for SM-25). Safety guard with limit switch. Limit switch
ensures that mixer will not run if safety guard is not properly
engaged. Two stationary casters in rear, one swivel caster in
front for easy mobility. Four stop locks to secure mixer in
place when mixing dough. Great reliability.


Product Name:
Picard Spitfire Revolving Pizza Oven
The newly designed Spitfire is a piece of art in itself. Sleek European design suggests high technology coupled with unadulterated clean design.
The Spitfire oven is a rotating oven that is revolutionizing the history of revolving ovens.
It has a large capacity with a state of the art burner that uses only 200000 BTU at full capacity! It has one of the smallest footprints in the industry, 43 Sq Ft. and because its thermal insulation is so effective, it can be installed at 1" clearances against combustible surfaces from the side and back. The Spitfire has access panels that enable cleaning the interior of the over with a vacuum from the exterior of the oven.

Versatility is what its all about today and this oven gives you just that.
Models are available for Natural gas, Propane or Electric.
