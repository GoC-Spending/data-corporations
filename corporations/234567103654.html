Brief Introduction
****Ferroalloys Corporation Limited (JFC)is reformed from the former ****Ferroalloy Works which was one of the 156 major projects during the First-Five period, JFC owns the sole national-grade technology center among the domestic ferroalloy enterprises, and it is the president of the Ferroalloy Branch of Chinese Metal Association. JFC is mainly engaged in ferroalloys producing, researching, developing, designing, equipment manufacturing, installing, technical service and import and export business, The annual capacity is 350,000tons and it owns 34 various furnaces, As the Chinese ferroalloys leader, JFC has become the largest ferroalloy producer and supplier with the ability of production and operating, scientific research, project design new products development and domestic and foreign trades, and also the largest special ferroalloys base.

The Ha-Da Brand ferroalloys of JFC are all excellent products, among them, 75% Ferrosilicon, Ferromolybdenum. Low Carbon Ferrochrome, Silicon Manganese Award with the National Silver Prize, JFC has set up the complete quality control system and has passed the ISO9001-2000 in June, 2002, The Quality Inspection Center of JFC owns the most advanced inspection equipments in domestic ferroalloy enterprise, and it has been confirmed the National Ferroalloys Import and Export Inspection Laboratory by CIQ, it is also the base for national ferroalloy samples.

Unique applications:

Nitrogen Ferrochrome with Cr 60% N 3%min, 8%min
Silicon Manganese with.Low Carbon And Low Phosphor (C:0.05%/0.08%/0.1%,P:0.05%/0.08%/0.1%)
Extro-Low Carbon ferrochrome
