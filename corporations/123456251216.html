Company Profile


Since 1980, SIMCO Chemicals Inc. is manufacturing and distributing a wide range of chemical products. Through the years, SIMCO has developed very competitive prices in glycols such as Propylene Glycol Tech or USP and the Ethylene Glycol Tech. A certificate of Authorization has been issued by the Canadian Food Agency Inspection for our product SIMFROST Propylene Glycol USP under the number S-796.

Dedicated to live in a better environment, we have developed a biodegradable geothermic product that meets the LEED certification. GEOTHERM meets and exceeds the expectations of mechanical engineers. Plumbers and well drillers are also adopting this new formula to their great satisfaction.

SIMCO Chemicals Inc. is also manufacturing cleaning products for the general, heating, cooling & refrigeration companies. We also serve automotive body & repair shops, printers, silk screen and paint businesses and are distributing various products for water treatment industry. Our concrete sealers and penetrating oils 'Protek' are well known in the market.

Preparation of your various formulas and blends under private labelling is one of our specialties. Delivery by tank truck for several products is available.

SIMCO Chemical Products is holding a Certificate of Authorization by the Ministry of Environment and we are insured against environmental risks. We are a member of the 'Charte québecoise de la Qualité Totale'.

Our clients are our business partners, they can rely on us for great competitive prices and a quick & efficient service.
