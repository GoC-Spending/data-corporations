Company Profile


Since 1922, Trudell Medical has established a history of innovation developing ground-breaking new products that enhance aerosol drug delivery while improving the quality of life for Asthma and Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease patients worldwide. TMI also produces sophisticated adjunctive devices for respiratory disease management, actuation indicators and counters, veterinary devices and numerous products for research applications.

TMI offers a wide range of aerosol delivery devices ranging from its flagship AeroChamber* Brand of Valved Holding Chamber (VHC) line of products to custom-designed devices and systems for the global pharmaceutical industry.

TMI products are supported by over 300 in vivo and in vitro peer reviewed articles published and recognized in journals such as the New England Journal of Medicine, Journal of Pediatrics, Lancet, Archives of Childhood Diseases, Pediatric Pulmonology and CHEST.

TMI is registered to the ISO 13485:2003 Standard, which is specific for medical device manufacturers. In addition, all of our products are manufactured in an ISO Class 8 Cleanroom Manufacturing Environment, at our facilities in London, Ontario.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
AeroChamber® Brand of Valved Holding Chamber
The Aerochamber® Brand of VHC is a medical accessory device used with metered dose inhalers (MDI) which help with coordination while decreasing side effects.


Product Name:
TruZone® Peak Flow Meter
TruZone® Peak Flow Meter (PFM) provides easy to use, reliable readings of a patients Peak Expiratory Flow. The patented logarithmic scale combined with ColorZone tapes simplifies use by providing easy identification of individual target zones.

Used daily, TruZone® PFM helps patients become active partners in monitoring their condition, provides an accurate early warning system, and can save trips to the hospital emergency room and healthcare costs.

Product Name:
AEROECLIPSE® II Breath Actuated Nebulizer
The AEROECLIPSE® II BAN creates aerosol only in response to the inspiratory maneuver for dose assurance, high efficiency and safer healthcare environments.

Product Name:
AeroPEP Plus* Valved Holding Chamber
AeroPEP Plus* Valved Holding Chamber is the first combination valved holding chamber and positive expiratory pressure (PEP) device, Offering patients efficient aerosol drug delivery and PEP therapy in one easy-to-use device.

Product Name:
AeroTrach Plus* Valved Holding Chamber
AeroTrach Plus* Valved Holding Chamber is specifically designed for spontaneously breathing tracheostomy patients with standard 15mm tracheostomy tube connections.

Product Name:
AeroProbe* Catheter Technology
The AeroProbe* Catheter Technology was developed to facilitate the delivery of aerosolized drugs deep into the lungs, with a high degree of efficiency and control. Due to its unique benefits, the nozzle technology originally developed for the catheter is now being utilized for other internal and external nebulization applications. In addition, the AeroProbe* Catheter Technology is currently in pre-clinical trials for delivery of anti-cancer and gene therapies via bronchoscope.

Product Name:
AeroKat* Feline Aerosol Chamber
Approximately one in 100 cats has asthma. The AeroKat* Feline Aerosol Chamber (FAC) was developed by TMI to aid cats with feline asthma while significantly reducing the risk of side effects caused by traditional oral treatments.

Product Name:
AeroDawg* Canine Aerosol Chamber
The AeroDawg* Canine Aerosol Chamber (CAC) was developed by TMI to provide an effective solution to treat canine chronic bronchitis. Chronic bronchitis is a serious disease in dogs and can be fatal due to secondary complications. The AeroDawg* CAC is available in two sizes and accommodates all breeds, weight and ages of dogs.

Product Name:
AeroMask* Equine System
Since 1990, AeroMask* Equine System (ES) has been used and recommended by dozens of leading university-based equine medical centres around the world. The AeroMask* ES facilitates delivery of medical aerosols to the lungs and small airways of horses with respiratory disease. Delivery is achieved via pressurized metered dose inhalers, wet aerosol nebulizers and ultrasonic devices.

Service Name:
