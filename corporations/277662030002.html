Technology profile

TS Manufacturing is capable of designing, building and installing all of the sawmill, mining and biomass equipment it manufactures. We have in-house engineers for the entire design process or consultations. Our 65,000 sq. ft. Lindsay plant manufactures all our equipment with full in-house, welding, machine shop and CNC services.
