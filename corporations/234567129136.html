Company Profile


Trust Management Services Inc. is an Alberta, Canada registered corporation, with offices located in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. In business since the year 2000 with distinct divisions revolving around our primary business of financial structuring for project investment funding from the Private Sector to private enterprises and all levels of Government - within our North American reach and definately around the world!

Trust Management Services Inc. Structures Solutions for Project Funding

We have the ability to structure private investment funding and apply these customized structures to see projects become successful realities. Whether you are at the early stages of project conception, have already attained feasibility studies, even reviewed tenders and bids and stand ready to break ground to begin construction, but NEED funding - we are here to assist.

Our professional consultants, investment bankers and industry suppliers can move any project forward, provided there is a collateral base provided that is bankable. In order to streamline the process, Trust Management Services Inc. has created turnkey systems that provide a step by step course of action, which are not lengthy nor complicated. The completion of our systematic process culminates into private investment funds being made available so the project(s) can proceed almost immediately.

Our project related associations involve us in projects throughout all sectors of development including all types of infrastructures including. but not limited to: transportation, water and sewer requirements, gas & utility distribution, housing needs, urban & rural development planning, roads, airports, resource industry development in oil & gas, mining, electrical power plants, commodity structured funding, and numerous other aspects of project investment funding. We invite your inquiry and request that you make submission through our procedures outlined on our website: www.trust-management-services.com After you have read applicable areas of interest that pertain to you, as a Private Company or a Government entity or a Private contractor awarded a Government contract supported by a Government guarantee, we can assist.

Trust Management Services Inc. customizes and develops private financial investment structures and our associations and representations with various private entities include: individuals of wealth, trusts, foundations and other private fund sources, help us to provide private investment funds under unique financial structuring terms and conditions for larger projects. We work primarily with:

*PRIVATE PROJECT INVESTMENT FUNDING*
*GOVERNMENT PUBLIC-PRIVATE-PARTNERSHIP (PPP) INVESTMENT FUNDING*
*PARTNERING SUPPLIERS AND CONTRACTORS*

Our project value for investment funding arrangements begins with a minimum of reasonable proportion. Higher project values, and/or an accumulation of projects to reach higher values, even into the many Millions or Billions of Dollars; are very much part of the financial structures we customize and develop for private investment funds use.

All projects are evaluated based on the overall amount requirement, the construction period, a monthly draw down schedule and capabilities of repayment - either via Government guarantee extended into taxation payment recoveries OR a private enterprise project venture that generates its income capable to repay. Our loans are ammortized for extended periods, carry terms and conditions very favourable, with terrific rates!

We specialize not only in providing private investment funding, but also in implementing a system of delivery of those funds to meet the project development milestones for the various suppliers and contractors using third party engineering supervision and international accounting firm scrutiny.

PARTNERING SUPPLIERS AND CONTRACTORS

The suppliers and contractors that are involved in the work on the projects where placement of our investment funds are used, are expected to work under a code of ethics monitored through our independent supervisory management teams. We invite and introduce new technologies through our supplier/contractor registration process and suggest to our project supervisory engineers that these new technologies be considered, if applicable, to any specific project development.

Contact us, request our funding application through our shopping cart to arrange further attention to discuss your particular project funding requirements through our website:
www.trust-management-services.com
