Company Profile


Mecan-Hydro is a manufacturer of equipment for mini hydro sites.
The company offers over 25 years of experience in the power
generation field.

- Our engineering department design on client`s specifications
- We manufactures as per our client`s needs
- We supervise installation on site
- We use the Canadian Welding Bureau
- Homologation for ISO 9002 is planned for September 1998
- We just started selling products in the U.S.A.
- We do on site preventive maintenance

Today MECAN-HYDRO offers your an indispensable source of
equipment and accessories for your future hydro-electric
installations. A full line of products, adapted to your needs,
have been developed to permit optimization of your installations.
Contact us for further information on our product offering.

Products offering :
- crest gates
- head gates
- outlet valves
- coffer dam systems
- stacking gate systems
- manufacturing of steel trash rack
- manufacturing of synthetic intake trash rack
- automatic intake trash rack
- manual intake trash rack cleaners
- water treatment systems
- design and manufacturing of handling equipment

Product Name:
Automatic Water Intake Cleaner
Automatic Water Intake Cleaner - The solution for your rake
problems

FEATURES :
- Prevents blockage of your water intake
- Minimum space requirement
- Design built to meet your site conditions
- Ease of installation on most existing sites
- Design to withstand North American climate

To increase your installation efficiency...

At last, a solution to your longstanding water intake rake
cleaning problems. MECAN-HYDRO's automatic scraper jaw permit
dislodging and removal of aquatic plant matter, wood and other
assorted debris. The jaw is mounted on a telescoping arm that
allows bi-directional scooping will sufficient force to displace
most installation all refuse is removed from the site, to avoid a
return of same debris.

BENEFITS
- Permit cleaning without shut down
- Increase equipment efficiency
- Optimizes energy production
- Facilitates your site maintenance
- Increase equipment longevity
- Reduces risk of accidents
- Improves profitability

OPTIONS
- Automated systems
- Operator interface (MMI)
- Remote control
- P Input
- Refuse conveyer systems
- Operator protective housing
- Articulated telescoping arm
- Special other


Product Name:
Crest Gates
Crest Gates
CHARACTERISTICS :
- Allows water flow at the top of the gate
- Lowers the rate of flow
- Ease of installation
- Permits optimum opening
- Can open without use of energy
- Minimizes civil engineering costs

For increased production from existing installations

The suitability for multiple applications of crest gate is making
their use more and more popular. The crest gate's main feature
is the surface water evacuation. This permits a water fall
effect that eases environmental impact and favours fisheries.
Used as high level control, the waterfall disperses part of the
water's kinetic energy improving the performance of the outlets.

BENEFITS :
- Permits the evacuation of floating debris and prevents the
accumulation of surface ice.
- Offers a point of interest for both tourists and visitors
- Ease of maintenance
- Adapts well to fish ladder systems
- Eliminates debris preventing the closure of conventional gates.

OPTIONS :
- Electric actuator
- Manual actuator
- Hydraulic actuator
- Implantation engineering
- Emergency opening control
- Installation supervision
- Installation
- After sales service


Product Name:
Stacking Gate
Stacking Gate

CHARACTERISTICS :
- Water tight
- Ease of utilization
- Requires low capacity handling equipment
- Easy to store
- Economical

For increased production from existing installations

The stacking gate system is ideal as a temporary dry out. The
modular construction adaptation to different uses on the site,

BENEFITS :

- Mechanical interlocking system ensures water seal efficiency
between stacking gates
- Low maintenance

Product Name:
Synthetic Racks
Synthetic Racks

CHARACTERISTICS :
- Environmentally friendly
- Better hydraulic profile than conventional steel
- Adjustable spacers
- Fixation system overrides problems associated with linear
expansion
- Low thermal transfer
- Field adjustable mechanical assembly
- Chemical resistance similar to that of glass
- High resistance similar to mechanical impact
- Excellent mechanical memory
- Abrasion resistant
- Lighter than conventional steel
- Excellent friction co-efficient

For increased production from existing installations

MECAN HYDRO's synthetic grates help eliminate many water flow
problems. Whether in an industrial or hydro electric
application, this new technology can be adapted to suit your
specific needs. It's innovative conception offers and increased
mechanical structure of the synthetic rake to counter operating
conditions.

BENEFITS :
- Minimizes icing on the grate
- Minimal adhesion of aquatic faunae and flora
- Ease of maintenance
- Lower friction loss than conventional metal grates
- Adjustable spacing of the grate elements through the use of
removable spacers

OPTIONS :
