Company Profile


Luff Industries Ltd manufactures a complete line of CEMA quality
conveyor products which are used in a variety of applications.
Luff provides conveyor components of exceptional quality and
innovation to a broad range of industires such as Food
Processing, Mining, Forestry, Recycling and Agriculture.

Luff Industries prides itself on sustainable advantages such as,
delivery times which are unsurpassed in the industry, an
impeccable level of service and product knowledge, patented
technology, quality assurance programs, and a warranty
guarantee. We continually strive to improve our methods of
operation and customer service by implementation of management
initiatives such as ISO 9001 Quality System, Strategic
Alliances, Safety Programs, and the provision of Safe Working
Environments. Luff is a member of the Conveyor Equipment
Manufacturers Assoc. (CEMA), the Power Transmission Distributors
Assoc. (PTDA), and the Canadian Assoc. of Mining & Services for
Export (CAMESE).

Luff Industries has exported across North America as well as
Mexico, Chili and New Zealand. Luff Industries welcomes all
inquiries world wide. Our order desks are computer linked to
both design and production control. WE can advise you on an
idler assembly selection for your application, or help you with
modifications to our standard design to fit your specific needs.
Luff products are also available through a host of distributors
throughout North America.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
CONVEYOR PULLEYS
Luff Industries manufactres standard heavy duty, mine duty and
engineered class drum and wing style pulleys. Diameters range
from 4" to 84" diameters and accommodate any belt width, with
any style of hubs, including 'QD', 'XT', or ringfedder, etc.
With a full service machine shop and lagging department, we can
supply all pulley accessories, shafting of any diameter, and
lagging ranging from SBR, Holz slide lagging, to ceramic. Past
jobs have included spiral drums and wings, spiked and knurled
drums, trunions, as well as stainless steel food grade drums and
wings.


Product Name:
BELT CONVEYOR IDLERS
Luff Industries manufactures a CEMA B, C, D, E, and F Idler
Assembly in 4, 5, 6, and 7 inch diameter rolls, for all belt
widths. Idler styles include equal roll and unequal roll
troughing assemblies, in steel and impact, offset centre roll,
trough training, flat impact, flat carrying, rubber disc return,
plain steel return, feeder impact live shaft, plain return live
shaft, disc return live shaft, disc and steel return training,
disc and steel 10 degree V-returns, and retrofits. Retrofits
may be necessary when specific requirements are needed to
accommodate the roller fitting an existing frame, eliminating
the need for frame replacement. Various types listed are
offered in 20, 35, and 45 degree troughing assemblies.


Product Name:
BELT BRUSH CLEANERS
Luff Industries manufactures a standard 10" diameter brush with
3" long bristles. Other brush diameters and bristle lengths can
be accommodated. All are avialable in nylon, poly propylene and
natural fibers. A belt brush asembly is supplied with brush
wear adjustments, hanger brackets, shaft, pillow block bearings,
motor (TEFC), brush speed adjustment, cross members, belt guard
and the brush of your choice.


Product Name:
TAKE-UP FRAMES
Luff Industries manufactures 3 styles of take-up frames. Regular
duty, heavy duty (for split pillow block housings), and side
mount take-up frames are manufactured to any take-up length
desired, with bearing sizes starting and 1-3/16" to 5-5/16".
Custom frames can be built to suit special applications
including an optional anti-seize coil rod. The coil rod resists
corrosion and material build-up in harsh environments such as
mining and quarry applications.


Product Name:
CONVEYOR ACCESSORIES
Luff Industries offers all sizes of shafting in all types of
materials, all types of bearings, hubs and bushings, laggings,
return roller guards, urethane coatings, urethane anti-lock
shields, special machining, and CNC work.


Service Name:
ENGINEERING SERVICES
Luff Industries can aid in the design of complete conveyor
systems, or individual pulleys. With finite element analysis
life and load ratings can be predicted under pre-determined
conditions. Other programs such as Auto Cad Drafting and a
variety of conveyor design programs are employed for engineering
designs. These programs dictate CEMA rating of the idlers, as
well as which pulley and shaft sizes are suitable for the
desired application.

Technology profile

Luff Industries uses Polycap technology in their conveyor-belt
roller assembly (US Patent No.: 6,234,293 B1). The roller
assembly is secured inside a conveyor-belt roller and onto an
inner shaft. The roller assembly has a stationary anti-lock
shield which protects the rotating exterior surface from coming
into contact with the machinery, or outside elements such as
debris. A dust shield is also positioned aft of the inti-lock
shield to prevent the penetration of small particles and dust
into the inner workings of the conveyor-belt roller assembly.
The roller assembly also has deep rings in the load-bearing
portion for thermal expansion and contraction as well as heat
dissipation. Bridging ribs are positioned across the deep rings
to greatly increase the strength, and threfore load bearing
capacity of the roller asssembly. A tapered labyrinth seal is
used so that it can be press locked into position making
installation quicker and easier. The main load-bearing
component and tapered labyrinth seal can be fabricated, when
necessary to meet D level standards of the Conveyor Equipment
Manufacturers Assoc. (CEMA), from a high strength, heat
resistant, 30% glass filled polypropylene material. It can
resist up to 305 F / 152 C degrees. This conveyor-belt roller
assembly is ideal for use in carrying heavy loads in potentially
hazardous environments
