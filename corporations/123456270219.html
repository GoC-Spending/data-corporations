Company Profile


SYSPRO is a enterprise resource planning (ERP) solution that provides complete control over the planning and management of all facets of business, including accounting, operations, manufacturing and distribution, in a variety of industries.

SYSPRO has always specialized in delivering software specifically for mid-size manufacturing and distribution businesses. Every component of the business, including financials, sales, CRM, inventory, operations and the supply chain, is controlled through SYSPRO. This offers unparalleled visibility, agility and control with an extremely attractive price performance ratio. This clear business focus as well as differential functionality delivered around four Super Vertical industries; Food, Medical Devices, Machinery and Equipment and Electronics, is the reason SYSPRO has been able to maintain the highest levels of customer satisfaction for more than 30 years.

Our mission is to continually develop remarkable software that simplifies operational effectiveness and keeps customers in control of their businesses at a price mid-size companies can afford.

SYSPRO Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)

SYSPRO is a fully integrated business software solution that provides complete control over the planning and management of all facets of business including accounting, manufacturing and wholesale distribution. The software extends control throughout the enterprise. It is a comprehensive solution that delivers outstanding value based on exceptional price / performance and functionality to assist companies in achieving greater efficiency. SYSPRO truly optimizes the operations management capabilities of manufacturers and distributors.

SYSPRO e.net solutions

Utilizing the best technologies available, SYSPRO e.net solutions and Microsoft .NET provide real value-added solutions to meet specific customer needs. SYSPRO e.net solutions opens the door to developing unique solutions for customers using SYSPRO ERP software. In addition, you can maximize business-to-business trading and leverage wireless connectivity.

SYSPRO Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

SYSPROs comprehensive CRM solution enables sales, marketing and customer support personnel to work collaboratively within the enterprise to achieve total customer satisfaction. Customer correspondence, status, vendor relationships, marketing campaigns, service issues, product returns, warranty and repair histories, quote and sales order information are monitored and recorded. This provides a 360 degree view of all internal and external business relationships.

SYSPRO Advanced Planning & Scheduling (APS)

SYSPRO APS is a low-cost, dedicated supply chain solution that gives the factory manager comprehensive, flexible and real-time control in a dynamic manufacturing environment. It enables a company to maximize resources and increase available manufacturing capacity without incurring additional investments in machines or manpower.

Relevant information at the point of manufacture is available in real-time to a factory manager, enabling dynamic shifting of workloads down to the machine level to increase the plants overall throughput and execution. A live picture of the shop floor helps effectively determine actual lead times, instantly answer what-if questions and feed an order into the production schedule in seconds.

Success stories:

Abbotsford Concrete is a multi-site supplier of concrete products. The company needed better visibility
into their inventory and improved customer service capabilities. Abbotsford Concrete also required the ability to calculate costs in real-time.

Arctic Manufacturing- Arctic Manufacturing is a private company situated in Prince George, British Columbia. Like most companies in the global marketplace, Arctic relies on efficient business processes to maintain its competitive edge in difficult times. Having an appropriate enterprise resource planning (ERP) system has helped integrate its key operations, and synchronize, plan and optimize its available resources. Using SYSPRO offers a wealth of real-time information that has greatly enhanced the insight of company decision makers.

Atlantic Tube and Steel- Implementing SYSPRO has enabled AT&S to fully automate its production floors. That allows us to look at the shop floor in terms of reducing man-hours. In addition, with SYSPRO we no longer need our manual production reporting process. In the past, while a mill operator was supposed to be watching the seam, or adjusting the heat source, he would sometimes have his back to the mill while he prepared his reports. These days, the whole production process, from input to output, is all recorded and reported by SYSPRO. Not only does that eliminate a ton of paperwork, it provides us with the ability to get information more efficiently, so we can analyze the data on a timely basis.

BioVectra- Existing accounting package no longer met the expanding companys needs, Increasing regulations required tight control of inventory, production, inventory, quality control and data. Enabled better decisions based on more widely available information.

WeighPack Systems- SYSPRO provides an integrated manufacturing and distribution solution, as well as real-time inventory and financial information, across multiple warehouses and corporate entities.

Natural Factors Nutritional Products- As a manufacturer of naturally-sourced nutraceuticals and supplements, Natural Factors places enormous emphasis on quality control. Natural Factors began automating its systems in 1992 and needed total supply chain management and financial tracking capabilities.

Circa Enterprises- Circa Enterprises Inc. is a multi-site manufacturer of surge protection devices for the telecommunications industry. The manufacturing is 50% make to order and 50% engineer to order. Circa needed comprehensive manufacturing planning and control to see where the organization stood from an
operational perspective and to make informed decisions about improvements for key manufacturing indicators.

Seon Design- To improve product assembly, supply chain management and post-sales tracking to meet
evolving needs during a time of rapid product class expansion.
