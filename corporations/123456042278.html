Company Profile


Manasc Isaac is an award-winning, 46-person integrated architectural, engineering and interior design practice, focused on outstanding sustainable buildings.

Dedicated to a client-centered planning, design and construction approach to architecture, Manasc Isaac has a reputation for architecturally stunning and technically outstanding buildings. The firm is is a recognized Canadian leader in sustainable design.

Technology profile

For an optimum of contemporary efficiency, our office is fully equipped with computer-assisted architectural design systems. With the use of three-dimensional drawings, we enable our clients to see their future buildings in a realistic way.

At Manasc Isaac, we make building design decisions based on established, positive experience and add to this experience by keeping abreast of continually evolving developments in building technology. Our team's wealth of knowledge, experience and expertise are always at the service of our clients as we share with them the exciting, creative adventure that is architecture.

Distribution of employees:

Manasc Isaac Architects' Head Office is located in downtown Edmonton, Alberta at 10225 - 100 Avenue. Our Calgary-based office, Reimagine Interiors by Manasc Isaac, is located in downtown Calgary at 414 Memorial Drive NE in downtown Calgary, Alberta.

In Edmonton and Calgary, in addition to our skilled support staff, we employ 46 people including:

7 Registered Architects;
14 Architectural Graduates;
4 Professional Engineers;
1 Engineer in Training;
1 Registered Landscape Architect;
5 Technologists;
2 Interior Designers; and
1 Graphic Designer.

Of our 46 employees, 14 are LEED Accredited Professionals.

Testimonial:

This is a building that works. The Slave Lake Government Centre and Library is a first-class, well planned, sustainable building that will support the people who use it and work there by enabling efficient delivery of key services. - Honourable Ray Danyluk, Minister of Infrastructure, Government of Alberta

The building is the Town centerpieceThe ceilings in the council chamber and the courtrooms are quite stunningLibrary enrolment has increased and it has become a magnet for residents of all ages. - Mayor Karina Pillay-Kinnee, Town of Slave Lake

"As the selected firm in our RFP process, Manasc Isaac immediately understood the issues and
challenges of the project and articulated a clear set of resolutions. Not only did this firm provide
a beautiful and functional design they had the skill set and depth to help steer the project
through the sensitivities of the community consultations and city planning and zoning processes." - Marianne Stewart, Vice President, Alberta Health Services (Edmonton Zone)

"Manasc Isaac Architects worked closely with our team, their Engineering team, and our Construction Manager to create a delightful, sustainable and functional "re-imagined" corporate centre for Servus. The design process was enjoyable, and the construction process was collaborative. The Servus staff is enjoying their new space, and the organization is benefiting from the co-location of our staff. We are confident that Manasc Isaac could successfully complete any large office building renovation, or new construction project, with the same focus on cost-effective, sustainable and delightful workspaces." - Brian Los, Senior Manager, Servus Credit Union

"Overall, the consultant performed very well, especially during construction. The consultant worked quite well as part of the project team and provided answers and clarifications quickly." - Kee King, Public Works and Government Services Canada

"Throughout the design process and during the construction of the facility, Manasc Isaac Architects have been professional and responsive to the needs of St. John Ambulance. They are a team of leading and innovative architects with an intellectual energy that places them on the forefront of energy, environmental and efficient design." - David J. Hook, Executive Director & CEO, St. John Ambulance Provincial Headquarters in Edmonton, AB

It certainly will be their home, their place of learning; it will reflect the fundamental values of our people. - Phyllis Cardinal, Former Principal, Amiskwaciy Academy

"Our design-build team was successful in a large part due to your enthusiasm for excellence in design and construction...Your ability to design to an approved budget provided the Town with the price assurance they required to proceed with the project." - Scott A. Matheson, R.E.T., G.S.C., Director Business Development, Alberta, PCL Construction Management Inc. (regarding Morinville Community Cultural Centre)

Marshall Lee Construction has been a general contractor in Alberta for 38 years and has worked with numerous design teams. We found your team members to have worked with professionalism, expertise and in cooperation with all parties. We will in the future highly recommend your firm for commercial and institutional projects." - Larry McCargar, Vice President, Marshall Lee Construction (regarding Memorial Composite High School Renovation and Addition project in Stony Plain, AB)
