Company Profile


INTRODUCTION TO WOOD BAY CONSULTING GROUP

PROFESSIONAL SERVICES

Experience, vision and a long-standing commitment to our clients
have enabled Wood Bay to design and build on the principle that
``anything is possible`` in the recreation/theme park and
entertainment industry.

Today this dedicated group of professional architects, landscape
architects, engineers and market/financial staff, work out of
offices in Western Canada and the United States (head office in
Edmonton). Wood Bay has grown with a commitment to professional
development and design excellence. Our unique combination of
technical and artistic talents combined with the application of
state-of-the-art technology makes it possible to transform the
most brilliant idea into a realistic design solution.

The comprehensive range of professional services Wood Bay offers
is a direct result of our commitment to the industry and
approach
to blending creative design with operational requirements. Wood
Bay staff have turned concept into reality in themed
entertainment projects from the deserts of Egypt to the Old West
landscape of Wyoming and the Rocky Mountains of Canada. Wood
Say's considerable experience encompasses a broad range of
projects in such areas as theme park conceptual and detailed
design, hotel and resort plans, marketing and financial
forecasting, and management strategies.

The integrity of Wood Bay Consulting Group is evident today not
only in the strength of its creative concepts and designs, but
in
its consistent and enduring commitment to its clients.

greensIRON Applications & Benefits

- Allows for an increase in the height of cut while maintaining
desired bowling green speed.

- Reduces recovery time and inconvenience to players following
top dressing and aerification of greens.

- Ensures longer life to mower blades and bedknives by reducing
mowing frequency.

- Smooths surface of clay tennis courts to ensure even bounce.

- Ensures consistent bounce in tennis balls from court to court.

- Removes morning dew quickly and effectively.

- Establishes tournament paced greens without having to increase
cutting frequency or decrease cutting height.

- Used in major tournaments throughout Canada and the United
States.


GreensIRON® 5000 BENEFITS TO GREENS MAINTENANCE PROGRAM

1- Rolling increases pace and surface smoothness, significantly
improving the turf Surface for bowlers. it produces a consistent
green speed within each rink and from rink to rink.

2- Rolling allows a superintendent to mow at 3 to 4 mm heights in
contrast to having to risk scalping the turf to obtain desired
results.


3- Rolling after aerifying can close a 112' tyne hole to 114' at
the surface immediately improving bowling conditions.

4- Rolling with the use of the greensIRON 5000 Turf Broom
attachment assists moving top dressing into irregular hallows.
evens and smooths the surface, and places the green back into
good playing condition quickly. Minimizes the amount of top
dressing picked up and lost in mower catchers.

5- Rolling after heavy rain assists to firm thatch layer and
increase pace.

6- Rolling on alternate days in early spring (initial growth
period) and in the fail reduces labour costs, maintenance to
reels and increases mower lifespan.

7- Rolling greens that have two or more species of grass (e.g.,
bent, poa) will minimize the inconsistency of green speed 1 grain
caused by differences in turf growth rates.
