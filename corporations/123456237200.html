Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Site Access
Ensuring that contractors working on your property are trained according to relevant legislation and your site-specific procedures are vital for meeting training requirements and creating a safe work environment.

NORCAT provides turn-key training, site access and security solutions for industry. With the swipe of a card, NORCATs Site Access System and allows the individual access to the site and the security RFID system allows for tracking of vehicles, property and contractors while on property.

Site Access Kiosk features include:
Training tracked through site access cards
Training expiration reports
Allows for employer updating and contact notification
Location requirements
Instructions for non-compliancy

RFID features include:
Vehicle, individual and asset tagging
Currently on property and company traffic reports
Controlled mechanical entry
Emergency vehicle tagging and auto-entry
Restricted individual notification


Service Name:
E-Learning
With NORCATs Internet-based safety courses, your classroom is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Employee training has become easier than ever, while maintaining the highest level of quality and record management.

Employees can instantly take advantage of the features and availability of eLearning through the reliability of NORCAT. NORCAT offers a host of occupational health and safety courses.

Visit www.norcattraining.com for more info


Service Name:
Contractor Orientation
In co-operation with Ontario mining companies, NORCAT has developed a common Contractor Orientation Program.

The two-hour program focuses on:
1.The safety rules and proper working practices for construction workers, and
2.The general rules of conduct and work etiquette required of contractors working on company properties.

NORCAT's Computer-Based Training materials ensure that the quality of the training program remains consistent.

NORCAT's central database offers clients easy access to accurate training documentation.

A successfully trained participant receives a NORCAT Contractor Pass. The NORCAT security pass allows the holder access to properties of participating companies.


Service Name:
Health and Safety Resource
The Health and Safety Resource Centre provides expertise in health, safety and environment issues ranging from industrial hygiene consulting services to instructor led training and information sharing.

Training Offered:
Workplace health and safety training
Generic & site-specific WHMIS (ongoing)
Spills response (site-specific)
Chemical toxicology
Air monitoring for Joint Health and Safety Committee (JHSC) members
Site specific hazard awareness training for certified JHSC members
Confined space awareness
Confined space entry
Respirator use & care (including self-contained breathing apparatus)
Specific hazard awareness training (PCB handling, Asbestos awareness)
Transportation of dangerous goods for drivers
Transportation of dangerous goods for shippers
Transportation of dangerous goods for receivers
General laboratory safety
Office safety awareness


Service Name:
Underground Centre
The NORCAT Underground Centre provides clients with a range of training and prototype development opportunities that are not available elsewhere in Canada. The former Falconbridge Fecunis Adit Mine in Onaping, Ontario serves as the perfect location for NORCATs 4-week Common Core Training program and for product testing in an actual underground environment.

The layout is ideal for equipment and vehicles, accessible from two surface level entrances (Adits) in the side of the landscape, as opposed to drilling down into the earth. Higher elevations in the mine provide natural ventilation and drainage, with no need for water pumps or hoists.

The Underground Centre has been used for promotional photography of mining tools, equipment and machinery in an underground environment, as well as a filming location for TV and music videos.


Service Name:
Common Core Training
NORCAT's Common Core Training is delivered at NORCAT's Underground Centre in Onaping, Ontario. Courses include Basic Underground Hard Rock Miner, Basic Underground Hard Rock Miner Service Type, Surface Miner, First Line Underground Mine Supervisors and Generic Supervisors.

Service Name:
Custom Training
NORCAT offers custom solutions for the diverse needs of our clients. We can customize your in-house training and deliver it in a variety of formats, including online eLearning, instructor-based classroom learning, and practical hands-on instruction.

Our qualified personnel will work with you to develop company- or site-specific training to fulfil your particular training needs. This includes program development, script writing, graphic design, multimedia features, testing, assessment of practical hands-on skills, and end user technical support. We can also develop support materials, including instructor manuals, student handbooks, and health and safety posters.

Whatever the format, NORCAT will deliver a consistent quality of training that will give your personnel the knowledge they require to perform their jobs safely.


Service Name:
NORCAT Innovation Centre- Mentorship & Advisory Services
Starting or building an innovative business is not easy. Engaging with the right advisors and mentors that have been there, done that can be easy. To assist our entrepreneurial ventures and small / medium enterprises, NORCAT has engaged an exceptional group of community and business leaders that are committed to supporting you on the nerve-racking, exciting, stressful, and challenging path of building and growing your business. This is the starting point of engaging with NORCAT  finding and connecting you with the right mentors to work with you to understand your business goals and how best to get there.

Service Name:
NORCAT Innovation Centre- Entrepreneur Education
Entrepreneurship can be learned. But the key, according to the Kauffman Foundation, is to provide education at teachable moments  when you are thinking about starting a business or ready to scale it. What entrepreneurs need isnt the type of abstract course they teach in business schools, but practical, relevant knowledge you can apply to your business.

This is what NORCAT offers  an array of entrepreneurship educational programs and online resources to help you answer your complex questions  at your own time, at your own pace, and in a format that works best for you. Visit www.norcat.org for more info.

Service Name:
NORCAT Innovation Centre- Market Research
Through NORCAT's partnership with the MaRS Discovery District, NORCAT has preferred access to leading international market research resources and a team of sector research specialists in information and communications technology, clean technology, advanced materials and engineering, energy and resources, life sciences and healthcare, and social innovation. To access this exceptional resource, potential customers must engage with their NORCAT mentor and work together to identify and formulate questions that need to be resolved.

Service Name:
NORCAT Innovation Centre- Talent & HR Services
NORCAT provides dynamic HR and talent services that bring together companies looking to grow and people looking for interesting opportunities. Our focus is connecting these two parties for roles aligned to innovation, commercialization, or entrepreneurship.

Finding and engaging the right people at the right time to be part of your team or involved with your company is the single most important decision that will determine your path forward. Understanding how to recruit, retain, compensate, and motivate prospective or existing talent can be overwhelming and time-consuming  thats where we can help.


Service Name:
NORCAT Innovation Centre- Software Development
In todays age, it is difficult to imagine an innovative, scalable business growing and exploiting new markets without leveraging technology to make that happen. NORCATs software development team can assist you in not only understanding how technology can help you expedite achievement towards your goals, but we can also help you with the doing - the technical architecture, the programming, or simply helping you build your website. We can do both using our in-house team of experts and / or through our network of software development partners throughout the province.

Service Name:
NORCAT Innovation Centre- Product Design, Prototyping & Testing
Speed to market is imperative and because of this competition to get products to market more quickly, many companies use rapid prototyping techniques to build parts for testing and analysis. Furthermore, as innovation-driven companies know all too well, customers and investors often want to see something to give them a better understanding of the product and how it functions to address the complex global market opportunity that it is designed to address. Our one-stop shop product design, prototyping, and testing group can assist in the following ways:
Product technical design, engineering, and development
Custom mechanical, electrical, and control system fabrication and assembly
Product testing services for harsh and extreme environments
3D printing, CNC machining, and rapid-prototyping
General mentorship and advisory services including new market opportunity evaluation and product management

In addition, our state-of-the-art NORCAT Underground Mine Testing Centre offers resources and equipment to support our clients interested in testing their technologies in an operating mine environment.


Service Name:
NORCAT Innovation Centre- Capital Services
There is no denying the importance of capital to help you start and accelerate the growth of your business. However, understanding the amount, the type, and the timing for capital is imperative. NORCAT will help you navigate and understand the complex capital landscape and how the nuances, stage, and sector of your business will determine the most relevant and realistic capital opportunities for you.

Given the vastness and complexity of the global capital landscape, be it dilutive or non-dilutive capital, we encourage you to do your own research and connect with your mentors to learn about the different funding vehicles in our community and beyond. We have outlined some of the key sources of capital in the NORCAT ecosystem.


Service Name:
NORCAT Innovation Centre- Facilities & Event Services
The NORCAT Centre is a 70,000 square foot modern architecture marvel. Built in 2008, our centre includes fully equipped office, lab and shop facilities, prototyping services, training and development rooms, event venues and meeting places, retail services, and flexible, co-location, incubator space that brings together entrepreneurs to work alongside other like-minded entrepreneurs to share ideas, enhance networks, and discuss challenges and opportunities.
THE NORCAT COMMONS:

The NORCAT Commons is a creative and dynamic place designed for entrepreneurs to work together and share ideas to accelerate the growth of their scalable and sustainable businesses. Being a member of the NORCAT Commons means you have a flexible month-to-month, co-location working space that brings you together with other like-minded entrepreneurs to share ideas, enhance your network, and discuss challenges and opportunities to help you expedite the growth of your business. To join this group and have an address in one of Canadas leading innovation centres, you must first connect and engage with a NORCAT Mentor.
THE NORCAT INCUBATOR:

As you continue to grow and scale your business, you will undoubtedly need to graduate to more formal, dedicated, longer-term office and / or shop space to accommodate your evolving staff and business needs. Similar to the NORCAT Commons, the NORCAT Incubator is dedicated space, but offers more privacy for clients that are still keen to participate and work with others in our vibrant ecosystem.
THE NORCAT LONG-TERM RESIDENCY PROGRAM:

The NORCAT Long-Term Residency Program is designed for our larger, flagship anchor tenants that play an active role in our innovation, commercialization, and entrepreneurial ecosystem. These tenants often require greater than 5,000 square feet of space and are engaged on a multi-year contract. If your business is interested in being in our ecosystem, having close proximity and access to other high quality entrepreneurial endeavours, and committed to contributing to a dynamic community, then reach out to us and will we schedule a follow up meeting with you.
