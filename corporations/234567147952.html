Company Profile


Founded in 2004, Summit Healthcare Management provides client-centered recovery solutions and litigation support for those who have suffered devastating injuries. Our objective is to develop a personalized treatment plan shaped and directed by our clients' goals. Our services include a roster of in-community treatment providers (Occupational Therapy, Social Work, SLP, Psychology, Dietitian), specialists for treatment direction and TORT, catastrophic case management, and litigation support. We are driven by a proactive, empathetic rehabilitation philosophy and work in close collaboration with our clients, their family, medical specialists, family doctor and lawyer. We believe that a collaborative approach ensures optimal recovery results for our clients and supports the lawyers efforts in a settlement reflective of our clients' injuries, pain, suffering and future needs.

Our Services:
1. In-Community Treatment Services
2. Specialist Assessments (AB & Tort)
3. Catastrophic Case Management
4. Litigation Support

Service Name:
In-Community Treatment
Our providers work with our clients where they are needed; within their home, work, community and school. For those providers that work out of their office, we'll find a location that is convenient for our clients. Regardless of the services required, Summit takes a "case management" approach for each client. Our full time clinical director, Dr. Rebecca Dworkin, oversees all client files and supports our treatment providers. Whether a client requires comprehensive rehabilitation management or a specific service, we work to ensure the best service possible is provided while remaining cost effective. We are committed to an approach that emphasizes compassion, integrity and advocacy and we'll work to ensure the funding available to the client is used to his or her best benefit.

Service Name:
Catastrophic Case Management
Catastrophic case management services are available and funded through insurance (SABS) when an individuals injuries have been deemed catastrophic by his or her insurance. Case Management is a comprehensive process that supports the client and his or her family through advocacy, assessment, planning, communication, education, resource management, and service facilitation.

Duties of a Case Manager include:
Designing a care plan
Education on the complexities of the injuries and possible sequelae
Investigating available funding through private insurance, OHIP or community programs
Collecting and recording information and directions provided by medical specialists and treatment providers
Monitoring for changes in the clients needs and any barriers to recovery
Revising the plan of care as indicated
Facilitating communication among the client, family, doctors, hospitals, insurance companies and other parties
Advocate and support for client & family
Coordinate care
Facilitate communication
Track outcomes
Facilitate recommendations

The goal of the case manager is to create a collaborative community around the client, manage the stress that often falls on the client and his or her family, and most importantly, develop a pro-active treatment plan based on the clients goals. At Summit Healthcare Management our case management services are characterized by advocacy, communication, resource management and the promotion of quality and cost-effective interventions.

Service Name:
Litigation Support
At Summit Healthcare Management our goal is simple...to support the physical and emotional recovery of our clients while providing litigation support to the lawyer. We work to ensure our clients' lawyers have up-to-date information and the documentation necessary to support litigation efforts. Our team is driven by a proactive, empathetic rehabilitation philosophy and a belief that a collaborative approach with our clients, their family, their family doctor and their lawyer will support the best possible outcome.

Cost-effective services
