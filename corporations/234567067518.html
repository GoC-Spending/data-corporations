Company Profile


Kirk Marketing is a privately held direct marketing services provider based in Vancouver BC. Kirk helps clients design, plan, and execute direct mail marketing campaigns that reach prospects, grow customers, and deliver measureable results. The company has been helping clients with their direct marketing projects for almost 60 years and understands that customers must receive value and practical solutions for their marketing communications.

Kirk Integrated Marketing Services Ltd. operates out of a 55,000 square foot production facility with in-house printing, data services, variable imaging and mailing services that cater to a variety of industry sectors.

The companys production unit is structured into three departments; printing, data and mailing services. Each department operates as a self-sustaining unit within the organization. Each department has an internal coordinator to ensure continuity of production and schedules. Overall coordination is handled by a dedicated account team that manages creative development, strategy, and planning in order to ensure that all components of a project mesh smoothly when bringing campaigns to market.

The organization has a highly skilled labour force, a professional team of full time programmers, and an experienced management team. Mechanized letter shop operations feature the most up to date equipment and are supported by a full time maintenance technician. Critical processes have redundancy to protect service levels. The operation has a quality assurance program in place and is managed as a visual work place with a 5 S program that ensures the highest quality standards.

Service Name:
Data Management and Processing
Data Department

Kirk Marketings Data Department is one of our biggest assets. The equipment is one component of the department, quality workstations, servers and networking infrastructure. All current software packages and programming tools give Kirk data the tools needed to deal with all client data needs.

While it is extremely important to have the right tools to do the job, the most critical component is the people. Kirk has assembled a team of professionals who are passionate about quality and accuracy. With a variety of different skills, they are a very strong team with a very solid Can Do attitude. As well as dealing with all client data work, they wrote and maintain Kirks Docket System, which keeps track of all production and inventory activity in real time. With five full time programmers and a host of operators, our data division is absolutely world class.


Service Name:
Laser Printing - Black and White, and Colour
Variable Imaging

Kirk Marketing is home to one of the highest capacity laser printing facilities in the city. Utilizing the latest in Xerox imaging equipment, Kirks three 14x17 format printers combine to give a throughput of over 25,000 per hour, 24 hours per day 7 days per week. Redundancy in equipment and 24/7 service on all printers mean that delays due to downtime are virtually non-existent. As well, all machine operators are extremely well trained and follow stringent quality control procedures to ensure that every job is output quickly and accurately.

Remaining on the cutting edge of technology, Kirk Marketing understands the importance of variable messaging to target your clients. Using our Xerox DocuColour, we print variable messaging, including variable graphics, in colour. Using criteria that you select from your client database, we aid in communicating to your clients more specifically and directly, increasing your return on investment.


Service Name:
International Mail Preparation
Mailing Services

Kirk Marketings 40,000 square foot mailing division is the biggest of its kind in BC. It is also one of the most organized and productive. The shop boasts a full compliment of equipment including folders, inserters, inkjets and labelers. Machine repairs and maintenance are done in house by our own technician, so downtime is always minimal. All machine operators are extremely well trained and receive ongoing training as equipment and requirements change.

As well as machine work, Kirk has an extensive manual-processing department, which handles thousands of pieces of mail and hand assembly projects daily.

Stringent production tracking and documentation processes ensure that all jobs are completed correctly and on time.


Service Name:
Fulfillment, Inventory Management and Warehousing
Inventory Management and Fulfillment

Kirk Marketings 40,000 square foot warehouse and plant area allows us ample space to warehouse and manage both paper inventory, and other products and materials. Using our internally created docket system, we track each piece of material on a real-time basis. This includes verification procedures at the time of receipt, production and shipping. We have the option to manage reorder points, provide regular reporting and manual verifications.

We regularly perform pick and pack, order processing and kit assembly. Our fulfillment area includes a high-security section for those products requiring additional security.


Service Name:
Offset Printing
Kirk Integrated Marketing offers a range of offset printing services, including four colour printing.

Service Name:
Marketing Services
Kirk Marketings combination of data analytics, creative development, and production expertise offers a unique advantage to clients in the Vancouver and international market who are looking for a full service direct marketing partner that is accountable for measurable results.

At Kirk Marketing, the scale of our operation and expertise provides excellent direct marketing services and the benefits that accrue from leveraging expertise and volumes. Having access to a vast array of business and consumer lists, we can streamline any direct mail campaign by focusing on a target audience. Through list sourcing and development, we can acquire sales and marketing, telemarketing, opt in e-mails, leads, direct mail, business to business (B2B), business to consumer (B2C) and charity lists.

Kirk Marketing also takes care of all planning, design, printing and mailing services required during a direct mail campaign. Our services in database management, pre-press, colour printing,variable printing, mail piece design and mail processing (like binding and folding, sortation, labeling, etc.) are perfect to help manage any direct mail campaign from start to finish. With capabilities extending beyond Vancouver and North America, clients receive the most cost effective domestic and international direct mail and mailing services.
