Company Profile


Kevllar develops responsive, custom web site designs for mobile and desktop platforms and proudly services businesses in the Greater Toronto Area. Kevllar specializes in supporting small businesses and health professionals to promote their presence online. Our services range from mobile, tablet and desktop web experiences to branding, logo design, print collateral and web applications. Count on Kevllar to deliver end-to-end ideation, creation and management of your online brand and presence.

Service Name:
Web Design
Kevllar develops responsive, custom web site designs for mobile, tablet and desktop platforms. Kevllar specializes in supporting small to medium sized businesses. We also work with health professionals to promote their professional practices online. We understand how to elevate your brand and increase your ranking in the competitive online economy.

Service Name:
Web Development
Anything is possible. That is our mantra. Work with Kevllar and to achieve your grandest vision. We develop web experiences with the latest technologies and adhere to the latest web standards to ensure that your project not only shines but is absolutely bulletproof. Web technologies are constantly changing so you can count on Kevllar to stay ahead of the technology curve so you can focus on your business.

Service Name:
Branding
From logo ideation, print collateral production, online and offline brand management we offer end-to-end development of your company brand. Kevllar will help you to elevate your existing brand or work with you to develop your very own brand identity. We are designers with a deep understanding of how brands affect your customers and how to stay "top of mind" in this competitive landscape.

Service Name:
Print and Design Collateral
From traditional paper collaterals to online web marketing Kevllar has your business covered. We have 15 years of experience in the design industry creating, Brochures, Logos, Business cards, Letterheads, Annual Reports, Posters, Flyers and Retail Signage. All key components in the effort to elevate your brand. Don't trust your brand related materials to just any design company.

Service Name:
Custom Logo Design
We can help you to reimagine your existing branding or we can work with you to develop your unique company identity and branding. Your logo identifies your company and establishes your uniqueness from your competitors. The design of your logo cascades into the rest of your marketing material and also inspires the culture of your organization. Being such an important part of your business identity make sure that your logo is in the right hands.

Service Name:
eCommerce Website Development
Are you meeting the needs of your customers? We can equip your website with the tools to sell and market to potential buyers. Your website is the ultimate marketing and sales vehicle because it never rests and is selling 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Your website can sell products while you sleep. We can help you to reach those customers.

Service Name:
Web Strategy (Consultant Services)
In today's online landscape having just a website is almost not enough. Your website is only part of the equation. Your business needs to deliver on all aspects of marketing to deliver results in this competitive online economy. Kevllar has the industry experience to elevate your brand to stand out from your competitors. We leverage Social Media campaigns, on-site and offsite Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Email Marketing, Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and website analytics to drive traffic and sales to your website.
