Company Profile


We specialize in programming to assist people of all ages, organizations and communities develop and deepen their ability to deal with personal, family, organizational and community adversity. Our clients use our services to assist them learn how to safely and effectively deal with challenges and adversity. We offer an easy to understand model, simplified common language approach, powerful yet easy to use skills, audio programing and it's all done using the research proven and very powerful realistic optimism approach.

Organizations benefit with reduced turnover, higher levels of employee engagement, the potential of significant lower chronic illness costs and higher levels of morale to name just a few of the powerful benefits. Clients have included Fortune Five Hundred, Canadian Business 500, Government of Singapore, and dozens of associations from Bermuda to Singapore.

People, families, schools and communities benefit as they find that there are lowered levels of negative behavior and higher levels of successful outcomes. People once they learn the process and the skills are more able to deal with lifes inevitable obstacles and significant challenges. Then how to move forward with added courage and conviction and make a difference in their lives. Clients have included Foster Parents, CanGrands.com, Schools, School Boards, Child and Family groups and Associations. We have clients from The United Kingdom, North America, New Zealand, Bermuda and Australia.

Languages
Currently the majority of our programming is in only in English. However we look forward to having our programming translated into French and Spanish in the intermediate future with other languages being considered.

We have done some work with large (1,000's) to small (dozens) live audiences with our work translated into French, Spanish and American Sign Language in North America. Plus with NGO's we have had some of our printed programming translated into French, Spanish and Creole to assist children and families , students and schools lessen anxiety and lower stress of the students, parents, teachers and support staff.

Don't hesitate to contact us if you have any questions. Thank you.

Service Name:
Imagine Yourself... energy management for life©
Imagine Yourself...
Energy Management for Life©

So what is this program all about?
It is a skills based program that reduces negative behaviour and increases positive behaviour in children, youth, adults and families. It is also being used in classrooms, group homes, organizations and communities.

We help children, youth, adults and families practice skills that create positive rational, emotional and physical states. This assists them gain a greater sense of control to more effectively deal with past, current or future situations with more ease. In the process it allows them to gain a greater sense of inner control and confidence that can be applied and used in many other situations.

What are the outcomes
- recognise and manage our own energy (responses to stress)
- recognise energy (four states of behaviour) in others
- establish positive understanding with themselves and others
- handle challenging situations effectively
- develop their capacity

Parents, Granparents, Foster Parents, Teachers and Professionals tell us they've been very pleased at the changes many of the children and youth experience having taken this program.

Would you like have us present in your town or to your group, association or school?

Please contact us through our business manager at:
AR@ResiliencyForLife.com

Imagine Yourself Positive behaviour Ages 3+
A program to assist children learn key skills to self regulate. Ages three to seven.

We've designed, developed and delivered many programs to assist organizations with staff and group resiliency issues. We've developed live, online, and video programming. How can we assist you? Plus we offer an online resiliency evaluation that is very cost effective with over 130,000 people having used it.

We assist parents, professionals and volunteers help children, youth, adults and seniors develop and deepen their resiliency to better deal with their adversity issues such as:
- Behaviour / Behavior issues for children youth and seniors
- Chronic illness for all ages
- Job loss for youth and adults
- Job / Career search for youth adults
- Retirement
- Productivity
- Dealing with stress and anxiety after a serious or series of high impact negative events.
