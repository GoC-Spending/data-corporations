Company Profile


Tek Gear is a privately held company, founded in 1993, focused
on providing emerging market technologies to specialized
industrial, medical and military markets. Tek Gear has grown to
offer the largest selection of wearable computing products in
the world.Whether you are looking for a display, computers, I/0
devices, or custom wearables we have it all.

Tek Gear has also partnered with many of the world's top
wearable companies and suppliers so finding a commercial off-
the-shelf solution is easy. We carry most everything in stock
so delivery, in most cases, is next day. If you don't find
anything you like, Tek Gear also offers custom hardware design
services to meet your needs. If it's off the rack or tailor
made, Tek Gear can deliver.

Product Name:
M1 Personal Viewer
M1 Personal Viewer is the ideal solution for critical
applications requiring hands-free operation and viewing of real
time results without obstructing the user's view of their
environment.

The M1 provides a high-resolution (320 x 240) gray scale display
in an affordable package to meet the demands of today's
industrial portable display needs.

Consisting of a head mounted display and a belt pack drive
electronics assembly, the M1 can be connected to any standard
VGA or NTSC video. A standard 15 pin D-Sub VGA connector
provides end user integration with existing computer systems.
The unit automatically selects either the VGA or NTSC mode based
upon the signal input.

The M1's durable yet comfortable design along with its low 2
Watt power consumption makes it exceptional for extended field
use. The M1 is fully occluding for flexible viewing for any
application.


Product Name:
M2 Personal Viewer
The M2 is a high end portable viewing device. Targeted at
mission critical display applications, the M2 is unparalleled in
performance.

Providing an ultra high-resolution (True 800 x 600) full color
display, the M2 is an excellent CRT replacement. It is lighter,
smaller and draws much less power. It is always right where you
need it - in your field of view - leaving your hands free.

The M2 is deliverable in many form factors. The base model
utilizes a universal form factor suitable for almost every
application. Its primary function is to act as a lead-off
technology demonstration unit for OEM applications. The M2
lowers your development risk, by allowing you to quickly and
inexpensively integrate a microdisplay solution into your
designs.


By utilizing our Flexi-COTS(tm) component system, the time and
cost to market of new custom designs is much lower than was
previously possible. Tek Gear is committed to assist OEMs in
order to help them achieve their microdisplay requirments with
very little development risk


Product Name:
Twiddler


The Twiddler is a pocket-sized mouse pointer plus a full-
function keyboard in a single unit that fits neatly in either
right or left hand. It plugs into both keyboard and serial ports
on IBM-compatible PC's and works on DOS, Microsoft Windows
3.X/95/NT, Unix, and Palm Pilot operating systems.

Product Name:
WristPC Keyboard
The WristPCThe WristPC is a rugged QWERTY keyboard with a
standard PC keyboard interface. The housing is a black anodized
aluminum. Completely sealed, it can operate in the rain and
other harsh environments.

A curved back provides a secure and comfortable placement on the
wrist. The keyboard layout is optimized to provide alphanumeric
entry. Carefully positioned arrow keys ease menu-oriented tasks.

The WristPC keyboard comes with an optional wrist strap to
provide the capability of attaching it to your wrist.
