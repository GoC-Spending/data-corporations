Company Profile


LATIN ACCESS was founded in 1993, initially with a view to the
opportunities and intercultural communication challenges that
the North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA) presented to
Canadians, Mexicans and Americans. Our intention was to provide
them the necessary skills to communicate, understand and deal
with each other effectively as people, and to negotiate in a
successful fashion.
LATIN ACCESS name symbolizes a ``bridge`` to
facilitate effective communication between English speakers and
their international counterparts of "Latin" origin (Italian,
Portuguese, Spanish and French). However, due to globalization,
we quickly started to deliver our services in many other
languages such as Polish, German, Japanese, Russian and Arabic
to meet our clients' needs. Throughout the past 20 years, we have successfully rendered our certified multilingual services
to professionals, governments, corporate sector officials, and people at-large involved in international affairs
from a variety of industries and countries in more than 60 languages including Mandarin, Cantonese, Korean, Farsi, Brazilian-Portuguese, Spanish, Swedish, Dutch, Norwegian, Ukranian, Czech, Hebrew, Amharic, Mi'kmaq, French, Greek, Russian, Icelandic, and many more!
The term ACCESS symbolizes our interest to assist our clients in establishing key contacts and penetrating international markets
by utilizing our Certified Multilingual Verbal and Written Communication Services and International Marketing Consulting Network.

LATIN ACCESS was launched with the World Potato Congress held in
Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island, Canada in 1993, where we
catered Certified Simultaneous & Consecutive Interpretation Services
into several languages and also provided Translation,
International Social Etiquette & Business Protocol Consulting as
well as bilingual Hostessing Services to the organizers,
exhibitors and participants in this event.

LATIN ACCESS services emphasize human relations and effective
communication as the most important elements for doing business
with people from a variety of social, business and ethnic
cultures. These elements allow business, trade and professional
commitments to become fruitful.

We are proud of having contributed to the success of an
array of initiatives undertaken by our clientele by supplying
them with key support services and sound intercultural advice.

Our clients have been pleased with the quality and outcome of
our services, and the strict confidentiality with which we
conduct our business.

Our team consists of Certified Multilingual Translators and Interpreters; International Marketers; Social Etiquette & Business Protocol
Consultants; professionals in Public Relations for International Events, Multimedia Voice-Over, and Customized Language Training.

We are also pleased to offer our expertise in diverse writing, editing and proof-reading services invaluable to achieve effective written and verbal communications such as motivational speeches, corporate profiles, international marketing campaigns et al. We operate under the premise "`Language is the reflection of the
attitudes, values and customs of a culture``; and do our best to contribute to the enhancement of understanding, cooperation and trade in the world through our certified multilingual services.

We enjoy assisting our clients in designing culturally-sensitive
oral & written communications that will translate into
successful social, professional and commercial dealings. Our
main interests are to meet our clients' wants, needs and
expectations and to continue to develop innovative value-added
services that keep-up with new technologies and international
marketing trends.

We are ready to deliver our services at the location most
convenient to you: Canada, Latin America, the Caribbean, Asia,
or elsewhere in the World!

Technology profile

We deliver our written certified multilingual services in a hard copy duly certified; or on any electronic format required by our valued clientele: Windows, PDF, Excel, Power Point et al.
Notarized multilingual translations are also available.

Our customized seminars in intercultural communication and
language training programs are currently being delivered at our
clients' premises or in our office.

Our certified consecutive interpreters are available to escort our clients on their trips overseas, and to also assist them via video or teleconferences; long-distance telephone calls made on their behalf.

Our certified conference (simultaneous) multilingual interpretation services are successfully delivered at a venue convenient to our valued clientele.

Our network of experienced international marketing consultants assist our clients to effectively penetrate a myriad of sectors and industries and to make key business contacts in their target foreign markets.

We have also assisted Canadian government and private sector organizations during the job description design, interviewing process and selection of candidates to positions requiring an array of linguistic skills.

Management of human resources from diverse ethnic cultures is another value-added service we have capitalized on since 1998.

Unique applications:

Our certified multilingual services are a reflection of the respect and sensitivity we have towards all corporate and ethnic cultures. They are consistently delivered within a frame of accuracy and strict confidentiality.

Key / Major clients:

Since 1993, we have render our confidential services to an array of embassies, consulates, Federal an Provincial Governments and Agencies (all sectors), Supreme Court of Canada, Royal Canadian Mounted Police, universities, community colleges, exporters/importers from diverse industries, anyone involved in foreign affairs or requiring certified multilingual or international marketing services.

Success stories:

We have successfully supplied our certified multilingual services in more than 60 languages to date. Our schedule is neither Monday through Friday, nor 9 to 5, but rather, we abide by our clients' needs.
On December 31, 1999 at 5 pm, we got a telephone call from one of our clients. He needed immediate assistance to get his shipment of non-perishable goods through customs in an Arabic-speaking country. He could not communicate in Arabic at all and he had several forms to fill out and fax back ASAP.

Latin Access facilitated the process via its intercultural communication services in Arabic and English, happily finishing the project one minute before the new milenium arrived!
