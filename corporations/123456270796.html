Company Profile


Since our inception in 1995, KLJ Computer Solutions, Inc. has understood that information is the key to maintaining a successful and proactive organization. This knowledge is integrated into each of our applications. Our corporate mission is simple - To use our knowledge and expertise to develop solutions that will empower organizations to effectively manage information at a level that will ultimately increase the efficiencies of their operations. Our client-based mentality allows us to build solutions to fit the client - not the other way around.

KLJ strives to move beyond the traditional client-supplier relationship. We want to be your partner. Your trusted source
of quality work and excellent service - time, after time, after time. This approach has attracted an impressive clientele that transcends all industries and is not limited by geographic boundaries. KLJ applications can be found running locally,
nationally, and internationally within call centres, academic institutions, professional associations, public utilities, government agencies, and a wide variety of small, medium and large sized businesses.

Our ability to understand the unique needs of each organization and provide custom solutions to our clients is what makes us successful. IBM Canada, the College of Physicians & Surgeons of NS, Royal & SunAlliance Insurance Company of Canada, Anderson Consulting, American Airlines, and MTT have all benefited from KLJ's innovative solutions. Isn't it time you did?

KLJ Computer Solutions, Inc. is the proud recipient of C.E.E.D's 1999 Entrepreneurship & Technology Award and NovaKnowledge's 1998 Smart Young Leader Award.

A member of ITANS, Halifax Metropolitan Chamber of Commerce, NovaKnowledge, & the Project Management Institute.

Specializing in Information Management Solutions
APPLICATION DEVELOPMENT

KLJ's Information Management Systems are customized to respond to
the needs of today and the business concerns of tomorrow. Our
solutions transform by-product data into powerful sources of
actionable information. Ensuring that the right information is
captured, stored and reported upon is fundamental to the success
of any enterprise. KLJ's applications are built along-side the
client to ensure that the broader needs and objectives of the
organization are met.

Our extensive experience has broadened our design focus towards
creating user-friendly, flexible and powerful contact management,
conference management, membership tracking, customer-relationship
management and communications applications. Always maintaining
the needs of the client, these applications enable the seamless
integration with many existing software programs.

In addition to building full turnkey solutions from the ground
up, KLJ offers expertise in -

- Needs Assessment
- Data Conversion, Cleaning & Manipulation
- Database Conversion & Enhancement
- Web-enabled Database Applications
- Database Redesign
- Training
- Database Integration
- Broadcast E-Mail & Fax Communications

The capacity of KLJ's role in any given project is determined by
the client's needs whether it be day-to-day consulting, needs
assessment or full solution design, development, implementation,
and training.

DATA SERVICE & TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS FOR MARKET RESEARCH & CALL
CENTERS

The objective of KLJ's innovative technology and data services is
to increase effectiveness and maximize resources of market
research and call center operations. This is achieved through
the seamless automation of manual and time-consuming processes
that would otherwise occupy the valuable time of center staff.
With KLJ's solutions in place, center management and staff may
allocate their time to more pressing tasks.

KLJ's growing line of Data Service & Technology Solutions
currently includes CATI Programming, Data Manipulation &
Cleaning, Sample Generation, CATISpell, SampleManager, and
customized AutoReporting and AutoFaxing tools.

A few organizations that have benefited from these services
include IBM Canada, American Airlines, AVCO Financial Services,
Mitel Corporation, and the NS Dept. of Housing & Municipal
Affairs.

Technology profile

Call Centre Services:

KLJ has developed an expertise in call centre services- an
expertise directly attributable to our extensive project
experience and the formation of strategic partnerships and
alliances with organizations from within all key segments of the
industry- call centre software developers, market research houses
and call centres, alike. Following are some of the
innovative tools we've developed to aid in call centre
operations:

Auto Reporting Tools
One great innovation that KLJ offers the Call Centre Industry, is
its line of Auto Reporting Tools. KLJ has developed a number of
applications for organizations all over Canada that are capable
of generating reports with thousands of pages of detailed
statistics on a monthly basis.

These Reporting Tools allow organizations that conduct monthly
tracking studies to obtain very detailed monthly, or even daily
reports, as required. All reports are professionally desktop
published and often include charts and graphs where necessary.

AutoFaxing Tools
One of KLJ's real-time products for the Call Centre Industry is
the AutoFaxing Application. The AutoFax is designed to tie into
the CATI system to allow faxes to be sent out to individuals from
directly within the call script.

All faxes are fully desktop published and allow for any necessary
logos, fonts, etc. The AutoFax can display all survey information
(including open-ended comments) as well as any imported data.

Call Handling Interfaces
Another innovative product KLJ has added to the Call Centre tools
it develops is the Call Handling Interface. A Call Handling
Interface is a database application designed specifically to
enable the user(s) to effectively handle a multitude of possible
incoming call scenarios.

For example, one of our custom-designed interfaces is responsible
for handling an average of more than 30,000 incoming calls on a
monthly basis and allows for the detailed tracking and reporting
of all information collected on a daily basis. In addition, the
system forwards the user the ability to search through over
500,000 records containing valuable customer information at any
given time.

Database Application Development:

Each and every database application that KLJ designs is
customized to fit the client's specific needs and data structure.
It is the responsibility of our development team to assess the
needs of, and advise, the client on what options may be available
to them. Once basic specifications are agreed upon, the KLJ
design team uses their expertise and creativity to build an
application that will be sound, effective and, most importantly,
user-friendly for the client.
