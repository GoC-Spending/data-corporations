Company Profile


Since 1970, TPM (P.M. transformers) manufactures medium voltage
class current transformers for protection and metering purposes.
These units are made for indoor or outdoor application in
switchgear and distribution systems. We specialize in
custom-built replacement transformers for refurbished
powerhouses. When custom built current
transformers are involved, the cost of installation is reduced
because they fit to the main bus or mounting plate without any
undue changes.

TPM has put the accent on custom built
replacement transformers for refurbished powerhouses. The
primary advantage of the TPM units, is the flexibility of instal-
lation on the main bus or mounting plate without undue changes,
especially when split-phase current transformers are in place.
Though using these types of transformers improve the efficiency
of the protection in the generator and prolonging its lifetime
expectancy by indicating if their is one single coil in fault
inside the generator.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
CURRENT TRANSFORMERS
Medium voltage class current transformers for protection and
metering purposes. Indoor and outdoor applications in either
switchgear or distribution systems. Insulation class:
0,6 kV up to 50 kV.

Product Name:
TRANSFORMERS SLIT-PHASE
Allows you to have a constant diagnostic on every single coil in
the stator of your generator. It will detect any coils coming
short. Custom-built manufactured according to engineering
specifications.

Product Name:
TRANSFORMERS FOR PROTECTION
Current transformers
Indoor: split-phase, line and load side breakers, differential
and auxiliary, substations circuit breakers
Outdoor: station breakers, neutral

Product Name:
AUXILIARY TRANSFORMERS
Insulation class : 0,6 kV

Product Name:
ZERO-SEQUENCE TRANSFORMERS
Product Name:
DIFFERENTIAL TRANSFORMERS
Allows the controllers to be aware of the flow of current between
the total output of the generator, the switchgear and the entry
of the substation distribution. If one of these areas does not
match the flow of current, the protection relay will trip.


Product Name:
TRANSFORMERS TOROIDALS
CURRENT TRANSFORMERS WINDOW TYPE OR BUSHING TYPE FOR INDOOR OR OUTDOOR, INSULATION CLASS 0.6 KV TO 50 KV, IN OIL ENVIRONNEMENT.

Product Name:
MEDIUM VOLTAGE CURRENT TRANSFORMERS
The precision classes for transformers used for protection varies
from C10 to C1600. For metering purposes the precision varies
from 1.2B0.1 to 0.3B1.8 with one or two secondary outputs of 5
amps and a maximum ratio of 15,000/5 amps with or without multi-
ratio. It should be noted that the secondary output may be of
any value while still maintaining the standard codes.
