Company Profile


Welcome to STYL&TECH Design & Build.

Founded in 2003, STYL&TECH offer state of the art product design and build services, expert in industrial design, mechanical engineering, prototyping, custom part fabrication, pattern and mold design & fabrication, 3D reverse engineering and 3D metrology for to international clients. Our tireless passion for research and innovation still drives the concept works of our designers and engineers.

We are looking forward to team up with you to develop better products, merging styling, design and engineering under proven production criteria. Together, we will bring better, innovative products to the peoples.

STYL&TECH is a product design & build firm which integrates DESIGN, ENGINEERING, TOOLING, 3D MEASUREMENT, REVERSE ENGINEERING, PATTERN & MOULD TOOLING, parts fabrications and PROTOTYPING to accelerate our customers products market introduction in automobile, transportation recreational and commercial

We used CATIA, Siemens NX, FARO 3D Laser SCAN Arm, SURPHASER 25 HSX 3D Hemispherical Laser Scanner and INNOVMETRIC Polyworks software.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
engineering services
The company use advanced sofware to offer services in the design, engineering and prototyping stages such as:

- Digital styling and human factors
- Digital concept modeling and 3D simulation as FEA
- Digital product architechture
- Digital comparative material analysis
- Prototype build-up and validation
- Reverse engineering and laser scan inspection


Service Name:
Product development solutions
The company use advanced sofware to offer services in the design, engineering and prototyping stages such as:

- Digital styling and human factors
- Digital concept modeling
- Digital product architechture
- Digital comparative material analysis
- Prototype build-up and validation
- Reverse engineering and laser scan inspection


Service Name:
Reverse Engineering
3D CAD reverse engineering by using 3D LASER SCANNERS as SURPHASER & FARO SCAN ARM with INNOVMETRIC POLYWORKS software. We making reverse engineering for parts, small and large products as car-bus-truck-train-plane, also mining equipments, plant manufacturing, 3D architecture building and bridge.

Service Name:
3D MEASUREMENT LASER SCANNING & PROBING
3D measurement and color map report sevices by using 3D SCANNER as SURPHASER & FARO SCAN ARM with INNOVMETRIC Polyworks software. We measure parts, complete products, manufacturing plant, mining equipments, internal and external building for measurement or reverse engineering.

Service Name:
Custom Part Fabrication
From your product we have the ability to design and build your product features from the3D design or 3D scanning. We fabricate tools like pattern and mold and we fabricate the part and making the assembly of your features or product.


Service Name:
CMM Inspection
With Faro Laser ScanArm V2, Surphaser  Laser Scanners we will scan from jewels-sized parts to production plants.

Compliance report are generated quickly for part, structures or assemblies, with precision up to .0032" for the Faro Scanarm and .0012" on probing with the 8ft. Platinium arm. Application are almost unlimited, ranging from car damage inspection to plant compliance.


Service Name:
Industrial Engineering
From the final 3D CAD part data, we develop and procure industrial grade patterns, injection molds or production jigs-fixtures. We can recommend moldmakers, industrial suppliers, or any product specific to industrial engineering need. Upon request, we will inspect the final molds with our industrial quality 3D laser scanners.

We can also create production plant in 3D CAD, for industrial engineering performance assessment, based on large scale cloud data scanned on-site or 2D layout information.


Service Name:
Product Design
With the intimate knowledge of your product, we are in position to care for all the stages of the prototype design and fabrication: CNC milling, rapid prototyping, part trimming and fitting, fasteners and brackets locations, dry fit and assembly check up to the final painted prototype.

We perform inpections at the critical steps of the prototype, validating the accuracy of parts or assemblies.


Service Name:
Product Engineering
Our engineering design team follows the design in context philosophy where all parts are developped first in 3D to build a virtual product assembly. 3D mechanical engineering simulations and assembly studies are then performed to identify and eliminate the product flaws.

Depending, FEA and CFD engineering analysis will be used to assess further the design engineering performances. 2D drafting is then executed to communicate technical information to prototype part suppliers.


Service Name:
Product Development Services
Product Design, Product Engineering, Industrial Design, Mechanical Engineering, Reverse Engineering, 3D Scanning and Probing Metrology Inspection, Project Management
