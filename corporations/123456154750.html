Product Name:
KleinPort - Port Management Information System
The KleinPort solution is a modular port management information system application initially released in 2007. The application has since been substantially enhanced and improved and is currently being used by a number of ports around the world. The system has been used to manage port vessel traffic and business operations and supports an interface to VTS for commercial ports. It also functions as the MIS component for regional Vessel Traffic Management Information Systems.

The system is capable of managing data for multiple ports from a single user interface and database with access being controlled through user privileges. A number of customers are using KleinPort to manage vessel traffic and business operations across multiple ports.

The application modules are:

Visit Management
Find all the information related to a visit is in one, easy to access location. Minimize phone interruptions and faxes with Web-initiated visit requests. Manage vessel scheduling, berth planning, conflict resolution, waypoint and route management, VTS/AIS integration and document management. Reduce data entry time and deal with conflicts proactively, saving time and effort later.

Resource Management
Plan, schedule, request and allocate various resources such as Pilots, Tugs, Linesman and Port equipment. Ensures resources are available at the planning stage thereby reducing delays later. Efficiently monitor and utilize service providers and Port facilities.

Cargo Management
Electronically import cargo manifests, dangerous goods handling, errors and omission processing, commodity code mapping. Accurate cargo data imported electronically reduces data entry time and ensures accurate billing and reporting.

Billing Management
Reduce your cost and effort to collect revenue with rate table based tariffs, multiple inputs for a single tariff, customer or vessel contract management, automated billing processes and Accounts Receivables.

Tariffs are easy to update, complex tariffs are automatically handled reducing billing errors and calculation time. Contract management is automatic, reducing the time to generate appropriate discounts, special rates and manage minimum guarantees. All invoicing is automatic and operational data and financial data are kept synchronized.

Property Management
Make it easier to manage your properties. Manage details of agreements, tenants, produce rent rolls, expiration notifications. Rent invoices are automatically created reducing preparation time and ensuring accuracy. Ports can specify rent increases to be automatically applied. Lease renewal dates are flagged ensuring all charges are billed accurately and on time.

Mobile Management
Automatically receive job assignments. Capture pertinent data and submit worksheets when a job is completed from a mobile device. Delays are reduced. Inputting data remotely speeds up the billing process.

System Management
Manage the system the way that best suits your organization. These system tools let you control user privileges, security, operational workflow, interfaces to 3rd party systems and the reporting and messaging functions of the system. Messages and alerts can be sent automatically based upon specified events, greatly improving communications. Key data changes are recorded automatically, providing an easily accessible audit trail. The system comes with a robust report generator, making it simpler for you to get the information you need to run your business.


Product Name:
KleinPilot - Pilot Management Information System
The KleinPilot solution is a modular pilot dispatch and billing system application, currently being used by a number of pilotage organizations around the world.

The system is capable of managing data for multiple locations from a single user interface and database with access being controlled through user privileges.

The application modules are:

Dispatch Management
Reduce your data entry time, deal with conflict resolutions up front saving time and effort later on and ensure the right pilot is dispatched to the right job. Features include customer-initiated job requests via the Web; optimized pilot dispatching based on pre-defined rules, qualifications; rotation and schedule management; intelligent pilot fatigue ranking for optimized dispatching ; capture Pilot activities, time logs, and services from the Pilot’s mobile device; tidal window determination; automation of recurring schedules; interface with external systems.

Billing Management
Speed up your billing process with automatic invoice generation based on your tariffs and rules. Tariffs are easy to update, complex tariffs are automatically handled reducing billing errors and calculation time, and all invoicing is automatic. Operational data and financial data are kept in synchronization. Features include rate-table based tariffs, multiple inputs for a single tariff, automated billing processes and Accounts Receivables; available General Ledger systems interface.

Pilot Mobile Management
Give your Pilots mobile access to the information they need on the go. Pilot delays are reduced and the billing process is accelerated. Automatically send and receive job assignments and updates from a mobile device. Pilots can record ship defects and remarks with their device. They can exchange messages with the pilotage office, acknowledge assignments and critical messages. Alarms are reset automatically based on updated job start time.

System Management
Manage the system the way that best suits your organization. These system tools let you control user privileges, security, operational workflow, interfaces to 3rd party systems and the reporting and messaging functions of the system. Messages and alerts can be sent automatically based upon specified events, greatly improving communications. Key data changes are recorded automatically, providing an easily accessible audit trail. The system comes with a robust report generator, making it simpler for you to get the information you need to run your business.

Product Name:
Maritime Traffic Mgmt Solutions
Klein Systems Group is a member of the Maritime Traffic Management group of the Saab Group of companies and represents the group in Canada.

http://www.saabgroup.com/en/Civil-security/Maritime-Transportation-and-Port-Security/?tab=150

The Marine Traffic Management product area comprises 5 product segments addressing various needs of ports, pilots, governments and other groups involved in the management and/or supervision of vessel, cargo and passenger activities.

The product segments are:
• Vessel Traffic Systems: provided by Saab Netherlands BV
• Hydrography and Precision Navigation software provided by QPS
• Port Management and Pilot Management Software provided by Klein Systems Group
• Precision Navigation systems (Portable Pilot Units) – provided by AD Navigation
• AIS Transponder, Base stations and networks – provided by Saab TransponderTech

Klein Systems and MTM can offer a single source supplier for integrated solutions such as port management and VTS (KleinPort and V3000), and AIS (KleinPort or V3000 and Transpondertech base stations).

Service Name:
PMIS Project Services
Since 1985, KSG has successfully implemented software solutions in the maritime community on a worldwide basis. As a result, KSG has gathered considerable expertise and knowledge that we can share with our customers. KSG is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company.

KSG has perfected the implementation process and provides the following services to ensure a well-executed project and assure you that we are there to meet your ongoing needs:

Business Process Consulting

KSG offers in-depth consulting services on maritime port, pilotage and VTMIS business processes. Our staff has years of experience with best practises in ports around the world and can provide you with expert recommendations on how to make your business processes as efficient and cost effective as possible.

Requirements Analysis

Any IT project has to start with a detailed collection and documentation of the organization’s specific requirements and an analysis of those requirements against the available IT solutions. KSG provides a full range of consulting services to assist our customers in this process. For qualified prospects, KSG offers a free on-site, no obligation Requirements Analysis. Click here for more information.

System Design

Based on the results of the Requirements Analysis, the KSG technical team develops a detailed customer specific system design for each of its project implementations. The system design documents the application specific configuration which will be applied to each of the system modules to ensure that the solution functionality meets a customer’s unique operational needs.

Project Management

One of the key reasons for KSG’s success is a robust Project Management Lifecycle that is executed in a logical and staged sequence. The result is an ability to consistently meet project deadlines and deliver the required functionality.

Quality Assurance Testing

Each customer’s system configuration is subjected to a standardized quality assurance process and testing regiment to ensure that it meets the customer’s requirements. All product releases are run through a Quality Assurance process using the customer’s specific configured solution to ensure the release does not adversely affect the customer’s operations. These releases may contain corrective, preventive, perfective and adaptive modifications as per ISO/IEC 14764 standards:

On Site Deployment

A KSG team attends each deployment on site to install the solution database and application, address any technical issues and ensure that the application is functioning according to specification.

On Site Training

KSG provides project specific end-user and system administrator training services including instructional material. These services are delivered on-site and/or via remote video conference depending on the project needs. As a part of the project plan, a formal training plan and a training schedule will be submitted to the customer.

Product Support and Maintenance

All KSG solutions include a comprehensive Product Support and Maintenance program including regular product releases tested against each customer’s specific configured solution.

Post installation support is a key factor in a successful long-term implementation of the KSG solution. The customer’s configured solutions are supported by qualified in-house support and maintenance specialists, backed up by our highly skilled Product Development group. Support is available by phone, e-mail and/or an online issue tracking system and can be available 24 hours/day/365 days a year.

KSG’s customers are automatically enrolled in KSG’s Customer Advisory Group (CAG) and Technical User Group (TUG) to provide them with the opportunity to provide first hand input into the future direction of the product.
