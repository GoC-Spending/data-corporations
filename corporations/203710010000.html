Company Profile


MDA is a global communications and information company providing operational solutions to commercial and government organizations worldwide. MDA's business is focused on markets and customers with strong repeat business potential, primarily in the Communications sector and the Surveillance and Intelligence sector. In addition, the Company conducts a significant amount of advanced technology development. MDA is active in two principal markets: Communications: MDA offers space-based solutions for cost-efficient global delivery of direct-to-home television, satellite radio, broadband internet, and mobile communications. Surveillance and Intelligence: MDA offers end-to-end solutions to monitor and manage changes and activities worldwide. MDA's established global customer base is served by more than 4,800 employees operating from 13 locations in the United States, Canada, and internationally.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAV)
MDA operates high reliability, long-endurance UAV services providing real-time multi-sensor surveillance to support critical operations.

Product Name:
Airborne Radar
MDA's airborne systems capabilities have been at the leading edge of operational and experimental surveillance programs for more than three decades.

MDA delivers operational airborne imaging systems based on synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR). These systems are used for wide area surveillance as well as target tracking, in both maritime and inland applications. Our systems operate at a long stand-off range and provide strip maps as well as spotlight modes. Moving target detection and tracking are accomplished using advanced, radar signal and data processing algorithms and architectures.


Product Name:
Command & Control Systems
MDA has proven, operational products as well as a deep technology expertise base in the C4ISR (Command, Control, Computing, Communications, Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance) domain. MDA has decades of experience gained from delivering complex defense, surveillance and security capabilities to domestic and international government and commercial customers. MDAs strengths lie in systems with a multi-mission capability in which multiple payloads, multiple sensor modes and sophisticated processing are required to meet the diverse information needs of numerous customers.

Product Name:
Naval Systems
Naval Combat Operator Trainer

MDAs Naval Combat Operator Trainer (NCOT) is a synthetic training environment that brings realistic virtual recreations of naval war ship combat operations into the classroom, to certify combat operators and officers and minimize reliance on fleet resources.

Meeting Navy requirements for a system that is cost-effective to build, maintain, and expand, NCOT has become a core training resource. Expandable to suit trainee loads in times of need, and challenging trainees with realistic mission scenarios, the system has been designed to minimize instructor support requirements.

Product Name:
Soldier Systems
Ferret

MDAs experience in acoustic technology has been extended to the battlefield with Ferret, the worlds most accurate acoustic small arms
detection system that alerts warfighters to the source of small arms fire. Designed and manufactured by MDA in conjunction with Defence Research and Development Canada, the system instantly detects small arms fire and locates its source. Ferret maintains a digital log of all small arms fire events for complete who-shot-first post analysis investigations. Employing state-of-the-art COTS/MOTS technology to deliver optimal performance at a competitive price, Ferrets false alarm rate is an order of magnitude lower than any other system currently available.


Product Name:
Earth- and Space-based Robotics
MDA is a world leader with long-term heritage in space robotics as the provider of robotics for ISS: Canadarm and Dextre. Currently developing next-generation space automation capabilities for planetary exploration and on-orbit satellite servicing and repair, and terrestrial applications that include microsurgery, nuclear facility servicing and others.

Product Name:
Satellite Subsystems
For prime contractors of commercial and defence communication satellites worldwide, MDA is the world's largest independent commercial supplier of communication satellite antennas across C, Ku, Ka, L, and UHF bands. MDA also provides advanced RF, Power Electronics, and Digital Solutions for satellite providers. In selected cases, MDA offers complete payload solutions to emerging satellite prime contractors.

Product Name:
Communication Satellites
Through its subsidiary SSL, MDA is a premier supplier of geostationary satellites to satellite fleet operators, and an industry leader in advanced high throughput satellites for broadband Internet services.

Service Name:
Space Missions
MDA is a leading supplier of satellite missions, subsystems and ground segments. We provide end-to-end engineering, development, delivery and support to customers who require real-time intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance information from space based sensors. MDA has engineered and successfully launched various satellites and built ground stations on all continents including the Antarctic. Depending on the customers mission and goals, we offer tailored products including custom satellites and constellations of small satellites, data services from commercial satellites, prescheduled satellite sensor tasking to tasking on demand, fixed and mobile ground stations owned and operated by the customer, or leased and operated by MDA.

Service Name:
Satellite Ground Systems
MDA is a leading supplier of satellite missions, subsystems and ground segments. We provide end-to-end engineering, development, delivery and support to customers who require real-time intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance information from space based sensors. MDA has engineered and successfully launched various satellites and built ground stations on all continents including the Antarctic. Depending on the customers mission and goals, we offer tailored products including custom satellites and constellations of small satellites, data services from commercial satellites, prescheduled satellite sensor tasking to tasking on demand, fixed and mobile ground stations owned and operated by the customer, or leased and operated by MDA.

Service Name:
Aviation Information Systems
For customers who operate or who plan, regulate or enable the operations of aircraft fleets, MDA provides a range of systems and services that improve the efficiency and safety of flight operations. For over thirty years, MDA has been building complex aviation systems and today we are leveraging this expertise to provide both system and service solutions to help our customers be successful. Our solutions range from safety-critical information such as terrain and obstacle data, instrument flight procedures, high-resolution airport maps and aeronautical charts to aeronautical information management (AIM) systems for flight procedure design, aeronautical charting, NOTAM management, preflight briefing, and aircraft performance calculation. We provide our customers with two classes of solutions: turnkey operational system solutions and geospatial information services.

Service Name:
Land and Property Systems
For defence and security customers who collect and manage surveillance & intelligence of land, MDA provides a range of embedded surveillance and intelligence systems and services that generate critical strategic and tactical actionable intelligence information. Our solutions leverage space, airborne, ground, and cyber sources coupled with sophisticated, knowledge-based processing to provide land decision makers with clear information that enables them to plan and manage their defence and security operations, and to make rapid decisions in response to changing situational assessments. We provide our customers with two classes of solutions: turnkey operational system solutions and geospatial information services.

Service Name:
Maritime Information Services
For defence and security customers who have a mandate to secure maritime environments, MDA provides a range of embedded surveillance and intelligence systems and services that detect, classify, identify and track potential maritime threats. Our solutions leverage space, airborne, surface, and cyber sources coupled with sophisticated, knowledge-based processing to provide maritime decision makers with clear information that enables them to make rapid decisions to respond to critical security threats. We provide our customers with two classes of solutions: turnkey operational system solutions and geospatial information services.

Service Name:
Energy and Mining
For customers that own, manage, support or regulate energy and mining operations, MDA provides a range of embedded surveillance and business intelligence services that improve the operational efficiency and safety of oil, gas and mining operations. Leveraging our global space-based and airborne surveillance capabilities, MDA uses sophisticated processing and deep domain expertise to reliably generate monitoring information that makes daily exploration, production and delivery safer and more efficient. Our information solutions cover both land and maritime regions and are designed to seemlessly embed into a customer's business operations with little or no modification. We provide our customers with two classes of solutions: turnkey operational system solutions and geospatial information services.
