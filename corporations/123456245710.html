Company Profile


Woodbridge Foam Corporation - Kitchener designs and manufacturers custom moulded foam plastic (EPP, EPS, PEPP, ARCEL, Piocelan) products used as automotive components, protective packaging (consumer electronics, pharmaceutical, etc), material handling, and construction products. WFC is ISO/TS16949 and ISO14001 registered.

Product Name:
Automotive Components
Woodbridge Foam Corporation Kitchener (WFC) designs and manufacturers custom moulded foam plastic (EPP, EPS, PEPP, ARCEL, Piocelan) products used as automotive components such as interior auto components designed to cushion vehicle occupants in the event of a crash, as required by Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards. Also supply components for headliner and trunk/cargo systems.

In addition to automotive components WFC supplies products used as protective packaging (consumer electronics, pharmaceutical, etc), material handling, and construction products. WFC is ISO/TS16949 and ISO14001 registered.


Product Name:
Protective Packaging
Woodbridge Foam Corporation - Kitchener (WFC) designs and manufacturers custom moulded foam plastic (EPP, EPS, PEPP, ARCEL, Piocelan) products used as protective packaging (consumer electronics, pharmaceutical, etc). Products can protect a broad range of fragile and temperature sensitive products during transport and storage.

In addition to protective packaging components WFC supplies products used as automotive components, material handling, and construction products. WFC is ISO/TS16949 and ISO14001 registered.


Product Name:
Construction ICF
Woodbridge Foam Corporation - Kitchener (WFC) designs and manufacturers custom moulded foam plastic (EPS  expanded polystyrene) products used in Insulated Concrete Forms (ICF) utilized in the home and commercial construction industry.

In addition to ICFs, WFC supplies products (made of EPP, PEPP, ARCEL) used as automotive components, protective packaging (consumer electronics, pharmaceutical, etc), material handling products. WFC is ISO/TS16949 and ISO14001 registered


Product Name:
Material Handling Dunnage
Woodbridge Foam Corporation - Kitchener (WFC) designs and manufacturers custom moulded foam plastic (EPP, EPS, PEPP, ARCEL, Piocelan) products used as material handling / dunnage.

In addition to material handling / dunnage, WFC supplies products used in automotive components, protective packaging (consumer electronics, pharmaceutical, etc), and construction products. WFC is ISO/TS16949 and ISO14001 registered

Technology profile
