Company Profile


Tetranex Solutions Inc. is a Calgary-based company dedicated to the delivery of high quality industrial automation, electrical and instrumentation solutions and services to our clients. As a privately held company, our team understands the importance of forming and maintaining great relationships with our customers by delivering outstanding products and services

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Controls Engineering
Tetranex Solutions is a ‘vendor neutral’ solution to the engineering & design of your control system. Each project provides unique challenges to a control system and we will integrate the components that best meet these challenges. A properly designed system can also provide unique opportunities for your project which can provide dramatic cost savings, improved safety & reliability, and improved integration with other systems in your organization.

Service Name:
Control System Configuration & Integration
Even a properly designed facility is still at the mercy of the control system programming. Quality system programming can maximize production, reduce facility downtime, enhance safety and ease the day to day operations. Tetranex Solutions has experience with a broad range of programmable logic controllers (PLC), distributed control systems (DCS), remote telemetry units (RTU) and human-machine interfaces (HMI). We also have broad experience with integrating these system components together to best suite your projects needs. Tetranex Solutions has developed industry proven programming methodologies which are constantly updated to take into account the best features of each integrated system and avoid the weaknesses and pitfalls.

Service Name:
Digital Infrastructure
Whether you refer to it as your “Operational Technology” (OT) or your “Industrial IT” (IIT), Tetranex has the skills necessary to design, implement and maintain the digital infrastructure around your Industrial Control System (ICS). The network hardware integrated into the ICS is continually growing in complexity to meet the demands of performance, cyber security and the quest for the “Internet of Things” (IoT). This infrastructure now requires a high degree of IT skills as well as a heightened awareness of the unique demands and requirements related to ICS hardware and software. Our innovative Digital Infrastructure discipline is trained and certified to handle your network, systems, virtualization and security.

Service Name:
Electrical Engineering
Tetranex Solutions is a vendor neutral solution to the engineering & design of your electrical system. Each project provides unique challenges and we strive to integrate the equipment and protection systems that best meet these challenges. A properly designed power system can also provide unique opportunities for your project in terms of reduced capital cost, increased operational efficiency, and improved safety & reliability. The existing technology used in the design and manufacturing of electrical equipment is rapidly evolving. Tetranex Solutions is dedicated to the continual evaluation of these industry related changes to determine opportunities for implementing more modern and proven approaches. These factors and others play key roles in designing and implementing any industrial electrical system.

Service Name:
Electrical Design & Drafting
Working closely with all disciplines on a given project, internal and external, the electrical design and drafting group is seamlessly integrated as a key member of the team through the complete project execution cycle. We do complete drafting services for all Electrical, Instrumentation and Control drawings.

Service Name:
Instrumentation Specification & Design
With constructibility as the driver, our extensive experience in instrumentation design, specification and construction achieves minimised time and improved operational efficiency at site. Our model is based on ensuring continuity through engineering to onsite construction support, the complete project cycle. Our instrumentation team brings their years of field experience to your design and ensures your facility instrumentation is done right the first time.

Service Name:
Field Services
Tetranex, we value, as do our clients, our ability to provide strong field service support, whether the project is in the stages of pre-commissioning and commissioning, start-up, or where there is a need for ongoing support or maintenance and operation. Safety is our main priority, keeping our field techs up to date on all required industry training and certifications as part of our safety policy, as well as exceeding client specific safety requirements. Our projects don’t end with issued for construction drawings and work packages, they end when your facility is up and running as per the design. We follow our design to the field across all of our disciplines.
