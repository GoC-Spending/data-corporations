Company Profile


Pelican Products, Inc. is the global leader in design and manufacture of both high-performance protective case solutions and advanced portable lighting systems. Their products are used by professionals in the most demanding markets including fire safety, law enforcement, defence / military, aerospace, entertainment, industrial and consumer. Pelican products are designed and built to last a lifetime. The company operates in 19 countries, with 28 offices and seven manufacturing facilities across the globe.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Pelican Protector Case
Pelican Cases are the toughest on the market and are virtually indestructible. With over 500 models to choose from, they are ready to protect sensitive equipment of all sizes. Our cases are fully stackable, lockable, dustproof, crushproof, and have an industry leading guarantee of excellence.

Product Name:
Pelican-Hardigg Cases
Pelican-Hardigg Cases are shockproof, watertight, dustproof, heat and chemical-resistant roto-moulded containers. Pelican-Hardigg Cases meet a number of stringent military requirements including: MIL-STD-810, MIL-STD-2073, ASTM 4169, DC18, Def Stan 81-41 Level J, STANAG 4340 and ATA300 specifications.


Product Name:
Pelican Storm Case
Engineered for extreme, military and industrial applications, Pelican Storm Cases are built from Pelicans patented HPX® high performance resin which makes them lightweight, and virtually indestructible. With 26 models to choose from, all Pelican Storm Cases feature the companys unique and durable Press & Pull latches, which open smoothlyyet hold fast when the case is taking a beating.

Product Name:
Pelican-Hardigg Advanced Case Solutions
Custom cases created by Pelican-Hardigg Advanced Case Solutions (ACS) provide Mission Critical confidence using a four-stage process of analysis, design, testing and manufacturing. From technical prototypes to sensitive military electronics  ACS has the experience and resources to guarantee performance. When failure is not an option, an Authentic ACS solution is the answer.

Product Name:
Pelican Remote Area Lighting
Pelican Products Remote Area Lighting Systems (RALS) can provide dependable, powerful and portable light to maintenance bays or bases in the middle of nowhere by harnessing the efficiency and dependability of light emitting diode (LED) Technology. More importantly, these systems provide convenient and ecologically responsible alternatives to wasteful generator powered lights.


Product Name:
Pelican Advanced Lighting Tools
Tactical or patrol, Pelican has you covered. Pelican Products manufactures handheld and firearm-mountable lights that are lightweight with no-slip bodies for a sure grip. Push button switches are easy to operate, even when wearing thick gloves. Smaller models make the ideal backup to the larger, primary patrol lights.


Product Name:
Pelican-Hardigg Specialty Products
Pelican-Hardigg Specialty Products are well engineered portable workspace solutions. These include portable desks that set up in minutes, storage chests with metal drawers and dividers, or lockable footlockers and trunk lockers for protecting personal gear.


Product Name:
Pelican-Hardigg Mobile Military Cases
Pelican-Hardigg Mobile Military Cases keep your sensitive and vital equipment safe from moisture, dust, sand, salt and even impact. Safe inside, your gear is protected against drop damage while the rib design maximizes stacking and stability en route. When you open a Pelican-Hardigg Military case, be assured you&#700;ll find what you packed inside arrives fully operational.
