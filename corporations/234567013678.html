Company Profile


Userful Corporation is a world leader in zero client software & cloud management solutions, with millions of users in over 100 countries. The foremost expert in Linux shared computing, Userful has more than 10 years experience in multiseat solutions, and has specialized expertise in large scale deployments. In the ICT for Education sector, Userful is the software powering the world's largest digital inclusion effort in Brazil. The company's software which turns 1 computer into 20, lowers desktop computing costs, improves manageability, and reduces both electricity consumption and e-waste. Userful's software is sold through a partner network of resellers and OEMs worldwide. Privately held and founded in 1999, Userful launched their flagship product in 2002, and has since sold close to one million seats.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Userful MultiSeat
Userful MultiSeat software turns 1 ordinary PC into 20. Just install the software, add monitors, keyboards and mice - and a single computer can support up to 20+ independent users all at the same time. Userful MultiSeat is a simple approach to desktop virtualization that offers each user the features of a full PC including high performance video with significant cost savings over traditional desktop computing. Save up to 80% on hardware cost, 70% on support and maintenance costs, 90% on electricity use, and 80% on electronic waste as compared to a traditional PC-per-user solution. The cost of computers can be recovered through electricity cost savings alone. Save on required infrastructure (power plugs, cabling, etc.) by a factor of 20.

Service Name:
Userful Desktop
Userful Desktop is a complete public computing solution that lets anyone manage, monitor, and multiply PCs. It takes the headache out of public computing by providing a safe, secure, and virus-free setting for clients that includes over 40 applications supporting over 30 languages. A simple remote management web-site makes it easy to control and customize PCs - saving time and money. In a recent survey, Userful customers reported saving an average of 54% on desktop computer acquisition and operating costs over their previous solutions.

Success stories:

Worlds Largest Virtual Desktop Deployment  The Brazilian Ministry of Education Deploys 356,800 Linux Stations with Userful MultiSeat

When the Brazilian government set out to expand computer access in its schools, they had a broad and important set of goals. Based on the knowledge that improved computer access in K-12 schools and post-secondary institutions leads to a more skilled workforce, a narrower digital divide, a more diversified economy, and wider participation among all citizens in the knowledge-based economy, the government set a plan in motion. For the long term, to have an entire workforce educated in using information and communication technology, and within the next five years, to make computer access a part of everyday life for a growing generation of Brazilian children.

To make these goals feasible, the Brazilian Ministry of Education needed to deploy the most cost-effective desktops possible in their computer labs. ThinNetworks (A Userful Partner) and Daruma proposed a low-cost, high performance Linux desktop solution that allows five students to work at the same time on a single desktop Linux computer, with the PC-sharing hardware and software costing 60% less than a traditional PC-per-seat solution. Each student has their own monitor, keyboard and mouse with a full desktop experience and access to all the necessary educational applications.

The Brazilian Ministry of Education faced many daunting challenges. They needed to deploy and manage desktops in multiple educational sectors spread across a wide geographic area; maximize their IT budget for computers, software, maintenance and support; reduce software licensing costs; reduce the expense and downtime of battling computer threats such as viruses, malware and hackers; minimize the amount of electricity used and reduce e-waste and pollution.

In addition to these challenges which are faced by all school districts around the world, the Brazilians faced unique infrastructure challenges. The deployments included remote schools in indigenous villages where infrastructure is minimal, electricity is unreliable, there is little physical space, and roads are impassable by cars.

Userful, ThinNetworks, and Daruma came together to provide a low-cost, high performance computing solution: one high performance computer powering five independent stations. Daruma provided the PCs, ThinNetworks provided the video cards, audio hubs and the heart of the project--Userful MultiSeat software. Compared to a traditional PC-per-seat solution, The Brazilian Ministry of Education is saving 60% in up-front costs, 80% in annual power savings, additional savings in ongoing administration and support costs, and five years down the road only 1/5th the number of computers will need to be upgraded.

The initial pilot project has the students of rural Brazil enjoying 18,750 new stations in over 3,800 new computer labs. The Ministry of Education has greatly reduced their technical costs for years to come. They used to spend R$18,000 on each traditional computer lab; the new computer labs using Userful MultiSeat can be deployed for as little as R$6,000. Following up on the success of this pilot, ThinNetworks,and other strategic partners are deploying hundreds of thousands more Userful MultiSeat Linux desktops for Brazilian schools. Other countries are following Brazils lead and are taking advantage of Userfuls ability to turn one computer into twenty.
