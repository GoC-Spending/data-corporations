BUSINESS CONSULTANT

Michael Renwick has worked for 40 years for processing, food manufacturing and marketing firms and has held senior management positions in many areas including managing director, executive director and vice president responsibilities including overall management, international marketing and sales management, strategic planning, new product research and development, corporate awareness and brand building programs and new product launches.

Mr. Renwick holds an Honors Bachelor of Science Degree in Marine Biology (1972) and a Masters in Business Administration (1976) both from the University of British Columbia, Canada.

He has broad credentials in the biological, technical and business aspects of the fisheries and agri-food resources of
British Columbia, Canada and globally. He has parallel experience with a broad range of industrial, manufacturing and consumer product industries domestically and internationally.

He is qualified to manage businesses, divisions, departments and multi-stakeholder industry associations. He is adept at providing evaluation, strategic input and bottom-line direction to a broad range of businesses and in crafting new strategies and action plans to deliver fast, productive and value added results.

Past clients include the United Nations/FAO, the Federal Government of Canada (Western Diversification, Transport and
Industry Canada), the Provincial Government of British Columbia (Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food), the Bank of
Montreal, Deloitte & Touche Management Consultants, and a wide range of business associations and private companies.

Among the services Mr. Renwick provides are:

- overall business management and bottom line responsibilities to owners/shareholders
- operations, marketing and sales management of industry Associations and companies
- strategic and business plan assessment and preparation
- assessment, development and management of industry association strategic marketing programs
- project management and R&D project management
- new business start-up guidance
- marketing & communication plan evaluation and development
- industry and corporate competitive sector analysis
- assessment of new product trends, new product development, and program implementation and launch programs
- internet, web site and e-commerce assessments and development in business-to-business settings.


Renwick and Associates conducts work primarily on a contract basis with costs dependent on project complexity, duration and
location. Short term projects are billed on a day-rate basis.
