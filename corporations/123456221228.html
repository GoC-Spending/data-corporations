Company Profile


newterra is a leading provider of decentralized water and wastewater treatment solutions to the industrial and municipal markets. With its suite of patented and proprietary technologies, newterra offers a broad range of solutions to the worlds most demanding industrial end users including mining, oil & gas, and food & beverage companies. newterras capabilities  from design & engineering to manufacturing in its own facilities  allow the company to maintain full control over the quality of its systems while achieving on-time delivery.

With operations in Canada, the United States, Germany, and the UK, newterra can address its customers needs on a global basis. newterra is a distinguished recipient of Deloittes 50 Best Managed Companies for the past six consecutive years. For more information, visit www.newterra.com

Product Name:
Watewater & Sewage Treatment, Groundwater Remediation and Process Water Treament
newterra is at the forefront of MBR technology and its use in a wide range of wastewater treatment applications. Membrane Bioreactors combine conventional activated sludge (CAS) treatment with a membrane liquid-solid separation process. Our MBR products are treating sewage to near-potable quality in thousands of systems worldwide. These include remote work camps for resource industries, decentralized systems for municipalities, resorts, and industrial and agricultural applications.newterra MBR systems utilize our patented MicroClear membranes  making them suitable for a wide range of treatment applications, including:

Sewage, Greywater & Municipal Wastewater Treatment Plants
Remote camps for resource industries (mining, forestry, oil & gas)
Small municipalities
Subdivisions without access to sewage support network
Individual homes
Hotels, resorts, golf & country clubs



top of page

Technology profile

MicroClear Membrane

The core of newterra MBR wastewater treatment systems is our patented MicroClear membrane. These units are submerged directly into the wastewater and filtrate is drawn through and out of the membrane by a slight negative pressure. Solids, bacteria, parasites and viruses remain in the bioreactor to be digested by bacteria or settle as sludge. newterra MBRs eliminate the need for the final stages of conventional wastewater treatment plants, including clarification and fltration.

Advantages
Chemical-free self-cleaning with backpulse capability for reduced maintenance
High efficiency design minimizes space requirements
Effective barrier against solids, organic pollutants and microorganisms
Consistent plate spacing for optimized self-cleaning
Air scouring earns an oxygen credit in the aeration tank up to 40% versus other MBR systems
Reduce sludge yield by 60-80% versus conventional systems




Smart technology.
Sustainable solutions for every market.

Today, protecting our natural elements is a responsibility of doing business. Thats why youll find newterra solutions in virtually every market sector. Our systems have become integral to the operations of our clients. newterra technology is reducing the environmental impact of resource exploration and extraction in remote areas. Were helping manufacturers recycle and conserve the water they use in their processes. And our air instrumentation is giving government and industry the tools they need for emissions monitoring and air quality management. At newterra, we help companies become partners with the planet for a sustainable future.

Mining

newterra solutions for the mining industry include innovative and reliable processes for sewage and greywater treatment at remote work camps, as well as mine source and process water. Our chemical-free membrane bioreactor (MBR) systems using MicroClear filtration technology meet and exceed the strictest regulatory requirements, including those for surface discharge. Equally important, newterra self-contained treatment systems are scalable  so they can adapt to changes in your camp populations. And theyve proven themselves in the extreme conditions of some of the most remote areas on the planet. Learn more

newterra also provides ambient air monitoring and test equipment to ensure safe operating and working conditions

Oil & Gas

Domestic oil and gas exploration requires effective sewage water treatment systems to serve the needs of work camp populations. newterra is there with our MicroClear MBR systems that facilitate reuse or discharge  even in environmentally sensitive areas. Learn more

newterra groundwater and soil solutions provide effective site remediation for retail service stations, refineries, pipelines and manufactured gas plants. Learn more

And our air monitoring and treatment equipment help the industry maintain compliance with air quality regulations.

Municipal & Development

Urban planners and developers are recognizing the cost advantages of decentralized sewage treatment. newterras robust, compact MBR systems are helping subdivisions, resorts, hotels, and golf courses meet increasingly stringent discharge requirements in a fraction of the space of conventional treatment approaches Learn more

Managing landfill sites is an ongoing challenge for municipalities. newterra engineers customized systems for extracting landfill gas and removing and treating leachate to prevent groundwater contamination.

Industry

newterra is well qualified to address the diverse needs of manufacturers for process water recycling. We can supply independent modes of water treatment such as pH adjustment, solids removal/filtration, and oil/water separation using ultrafiltration, chemical treatment, and our Pump & Treat or Flow & Plug systems. Learn more

Our environmental support also includes an extensive selection of precision equipment for continuous emission monitoring (CEM) and gas analysis. Learn more

newterra has considerable experience providing effective solutions for the control of highly toxic, corrosive, and odor-causing gas emissions.

Construction

Construction sites requiring subsurface excavation often require dewatering to prevent groundwater intrusion. newterra sells and rents water treatment systems capable of dealing with high amounts of sediment, as well as the many contaminants that can be found in extracted groundwater. These can include volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and metals such as iron, manganese and lead.
