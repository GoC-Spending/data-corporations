Company Profile


North America's largest full line manufacturer of leaf springs
and agricultural tillage components. Products are built to OEM,
ISO, and military quality standards. Operating since 1924 in
Calgary, Alberta, Canada. Currently exporting to five continents,
Standen's develops state of the art solutions to your suspension
and tillage problems. ISO 9001 certified.

Market profile

Alliances:

Strategic alliances:

Established in 1924, Standen's is North America's leading full
line manufacturer of Leaf springs for motor vehicles and tillage
tools for the agricultural implement industry. Standen's
supplies OEM, aftermarket, and miltary customers on five
continents. Standen's is ISO-9001 certified.
Other products include Trailer axles and suspension components,
stabilizer bars, threaded rods and U-bolts, tillage tools,
cultivator shanks and agricultural equipment as well as formed,
forged, and heat treated alloy steel products.
