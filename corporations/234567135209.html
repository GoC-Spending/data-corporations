Company Profile


OSI Network Solutions is an IT Support firm located in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. OSI Network Solutions provides a broad range of computer networking services and solutions for many industries, from new businesses to large multi-office organizations. Whether you'd like us to proactively manage your computer network or to assist your existing onsite staff, OSI Network Solutions can help.
Give us a call today for a free evaluation and audit of your infrastructure.
Services we offer include:
-IT Helpdesk Computer Support Services.
-Managed IT Services.
-On-Demand IT Services.
-LAN/WAN Support & Design.
-Server & Desktop Support including Linux.
-Disaster Recovery.
-Information Security and Intrusion prevention.
-VPN services for multi-office locations.
-Online Remote Data Backup Services.
Our vendors and partners: Microsoft, Citrix, FortiNet, SonicWall, WatchGuard, VMWare, HP, Google.

Visit our website for more information at http://www.osins.ca

Service Name:
Remote Online Data Backup
OSI Network Solutions highly recommends Secure Online backups for anyone that wants to backup their data safely and securely. When you need to, you can retrieve data quickly and easily.

At OSI Network Solutions we have developed an online backup solution that removes the cost and the headaches of backing up your data. Taking the pressure off you and providing a failsafe solution to ensure your data is secure.
Using a broadband Internet connection we can securely store your data in our data-center on a daily basis. Using the latest encryption technologies you can be sure that this is a secure transfer and your data cannot be read.
By installing a small application on your file server the files are encrypted and then sent across the Internet using a broadband connection (your existing one). They are then stored on our servers securely and updated daily to ensure you have an up to date back up.

Below is a list of challenges faced by traditional backup methods including tape, CD/DVD or portable hard disks:

1. They require significant manual intervention.
2. Arrangements must be made to store media off-site.
3. They offer limited security in the form of data encryption.

Our system provides you with a number of benefits:

1. Data is encrypted and transferred off-site to our locations.
2. Your data is available from anywhere in the world 24/7.
3. Straightforward backup of multiple computers, servers or laptops.
4. No hardware or software licenses to buy and maintain.
5. No staff intervention is required.
6. No limit to the amount of storage available to you.
7. Integrity of stored data is guaranteed.
8. Bare metal restore option for critical servers.

When considering the true cost to your business of alternative systems, you must factor in the cost of media, staff time, hardware, hardware maintenance and replacement, software licences and the provision of off-site storage for your media. We believe that our system offers a more robust and cost-effective solution to such traditional systems.
