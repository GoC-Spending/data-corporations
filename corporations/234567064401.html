Company Profile


We distribute janitorial cleaning supplies & equipment, safety supplies, hotel supplies, and wash bay supplies Canada-Wide to industrial sites, cleaning contractors, institutions, government and commercial customers.

We are proud to offer the most extensive line of certified, highest quality environmental-friendly cleaning products, recycled & biodegradable paper products and tissues, environmental wash bay degreasers and recycled & biodegradable plastic bag products. We have partnered with leading Canadian manufacturers and are members of the Dissan Canada Buying Group. Our leading product lines offered include our newest family of Bitumen Remover Degreaser products in liquid and gel formats. the INO Solutions Bio & Eko Environmental product lines, as well as the Dustbane Canada Environmental product line. Our products are produced in Canada and exceed Canadian and international environmental standards.

We intend on helping industries to make a difference by reducing their environmental footprint and also helping industry to realize cost savings. Let us help you by using our highly effective products that are safer and not as harmful to the environment or workers. We can help you reduce wastage and save money.

We are located in Metro-Edmonton and Fort McMurray, Alberta, and with sales offices in British Columbia and Saskatchewan. We ship from warehouses in Vancouver, Edmonton, Calgary, Saskatoon, Toronto and Montreal.

Product Name:
Environmental General Purpose Degreasers
ENVIROSOL:

A concentrated, non-corrosive heavy duty degreaser and pressure wash detergent formulated for a wide variety of industrial cleaning applications. This unique formula contains a proprietary blend of highly effective, biodegradable surfactants, solvents and wetting agents, making it very effective when removing oil and grease from most surfaces. Envirosol rinses very easy, leaving surfaces residue-free.

GR-2000:

A concentrated, low odor, non-corrosive natural solvent-based degreaser that provides high strength cleaning and degreasing power. This product is a mixture of synthetic hydrocarbons and a proprietary blend of wetting agents. GR-2000 is ideal for removing heavy mechanical greases and oils found on industrial equipment, as well as for use in parts washers and dip tanks. It will not damage paint or decals and does not require any special handling requirements.
