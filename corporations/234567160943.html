Company Profile


Fraserwoods Fabrication & Machining Ltd. is Western Canada’s premier service and repair facility for truck mounted and industrial service vacuum/pressure blowers. We are the market leader in the repair, service, sales and warranty of PD blowers, vacuum pumps, gearboxes and rotary air lock feeders. Our passion for rotating equipment and the quality repairs we perform have been at the core of our business model since 2001. Endorsed by leading OEMs Hibon, Howden Roots, Fruitland Manufacturing, National Vacuum Equipment (NVE) and Moro Pumps, we provide the highest quality products and services to our customers and industry.

With over fifteen years of commitment to quality, our operation represents the cohesive integration of rotating equipment service, uniform methods, best practices, and focused customer service. Fraserwoods has earned a reputation for delivering quality products to a wide scope of industries in North America. That legacy continues today with top quality workmanship and putting the needs of our customers first. Our team is committed to continuous improvement, research and development, as well as best practices and techniques. It is our goal to continue adding cutting edge technologies and processes that improve efficiency and rotating equipment reliability.

Fraserwoods is Recognized as:
• Hibon PD Blower Distributor, Repair, Service and Warranty Center – Alberta, Saskatchewan, BC and Manitoba
• Howden Roots PD Blower Distributor, Parts, Service and Warranty Repair Center – Alberta, Saskatchewan, BC and Manitoba
