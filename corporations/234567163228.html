Hardwood Flooring in Toronto

Hardwood flooring is gaining tons of popularity these days, and for good reason. It provides a solid platform for any floor, yet it evokes a stylish/classic feeling that can bring nostalgic feelings to even the latest generation. The aesthetically pleasing look of a nicely done hardwood floor can fill your house with a charm that commands attention. There are many benefits to choosing hardwood flooring Toronto, so grab a coffee, sit back, and continue reading about its benefits:

Maintenance

Hardwood floors are ideal for any room in the house except for the bathroom and laundry room due to the inherent moisture levels of these rooms. You may also want to consider any pets that will be living in the space that will have hardwood flooring. Their nails have the potential to scratch the flooring, so keeping them trim on a regular basis is something you will have to keep in mind with hardwood flooring. Other than these two things, cleaning a hardwood floor is markedly easier than cleaning a carpeted floor. There is no worry about buildup of dust or allergens, making it a great hypo-allergenic for those who deal with allergies on a daily basis.

Solid Hardwood

This type of hardwood is milled from individual pieces of 100% wood, and can be installed in most rooms except areas that carry more moisture such as the basement, bathrooms, and laundry room. The moisture in these rooms can cause the wood to expand and shrink, due to the way that the wood reacts to moisture changes over time. Moisture also creates the potential for bacterial and mold growth if not maintained, so it is best to keep this type of flooring in dryer areas of the house. Other than that, they offer the most natural look for your floors and carry the added benefit of not needing any extra chemical adhesives that engineered hardwood requires.

Engineered Hardwood

Engineered hardwood was designed to improve the stability of the floor. It offers greater stability since layers of the hardwood are fixated together with a strong adhesive using a cross-grain construction model. Since it is a man-made product, the engineered hardwood can be made into ideal parameters including thickness, size, strength, and grades that make them extremely versatile for almost any home application. Visit your local hardwood flooring Toronto store for more details about the pros and cons between solid and engineered hardwood.

Style

The colors to choose from run from a spectrum of light yellow-brown to black which is determined by the tree species and how it was finished. The style of hardwood is another thing you can get creative with. You can elect to choose a modern style with a bold and shiny finish that matches the contemporary design of an apartment, or a more classic look that adds age and antiqueness to any room looking to bring back memories of the good old days. Finishes of the hardwood function to add a protective surface and give the hardwood a glossy stain that can bring out the rich details of the wood itself. Hardness is another factor to consider. Every type of hardwood flooring has varying levels of durability with respect to the species and finish it has. The harder the species (red oak for example), the better its ability to withstand wear and tear.

Summary

Hardwood flooring is an excellent option for most rooms in the house as it adds good durability, cleanliness, and a modern look to your living area. Keep the above points in mind when deciding on the hardwood flooring Toronto location you will be shopping from, and you will not go astray!

Carpet Flooring in Toronto

The best carpet flooring job is essential to making your house a comfortable, safe and luxurious place for your family to live. A carpet is the best choice when the goal is to get flooring that provides warmth, sound absorption, and unbeatable comfort. Just like any type of flooring, there are many options when it comes to carpet flooring Toronto, which means there are various things to consider before you find the perfect carpet. These things include style, maintenance, padding, budget, pattern choice, and types of floors (room vs stairs).

Style

The flexibility that carpet flooring offers you is through a virtually unlimited number of styles available since they are based on how the manufacturer designs them. Choosing the right style can help you dictate the type of environment you want to set for your room. You can go even further by using textured or decorative elements that can give the room an extra emphasis on design. Earthy tones can give a natural feel to the room, different carpet textures provide more or less density to the fabric which is normally influenced by its use, and patterns show your personality.

Maintenance

The type of carpet that you choose will also determine the type of maintenance that will be required for it. As wonderful as carpets are, they have the potential to pick up dirt and allergens and are more likely to require extensive cleaning for stains. Consider who will be living in the house when thinking about maintenance. Children and pets may warrant a carpet that is easier to clean (stain-resistant), while a higher-traffic area of the house will need a higher-density carpet that can resist wear and tear more effectively. Well-maintained carpets are longer-lasting, cleaner, and visually pleasing, so it is important to keep that in the back of your mind when thinking about making a purchase.

Padding

Padding is the component that is placed between the carpet and the floor to add strength, support and extra cushioning to the carpet. Although you will never see the padding if your carpet flooring Toronto job was done right, it is something that can wear and tear easily if it is made out of an insufficient material. The foaming material of the padding helps conceal any imperfections that may be in the floor underneath it. The material also acts as an effective insulator that can better control the temperature of the house, perfect for those long Canadian winters. Finally, good padding offers the added benefit of sound absorption which is helpful in eliminating any noise that can come from the rooms around you.

Safety

Safety in the home is one of the main reasons customers elect to choose carpet flooring Toronto over any other type of flooring. Considering the fall risk of the older adults, or the unpredictability of children running around the house, your best bet is to choose flooring that can not only cushion a fall, but provide extra grip that can help prevent a fall from happening in the first place. If safety is on your mind, then a good quality carpet should be too.

Summary

Carpets offer comfort, insulation, and overall safety that is unmatched by any other type of flooring. If your goal is to provide a safe and comfortable environment for you or your family, then look no further than a good quality carpet flooring job which can add that warm, cozy feeling to a place you call “home”.

Laminate Flooring in Toronto

Laminate Flooring is an excellent choice for any room minus some restrictions related to moisture/humidity and the type of wear and tear that the flooring will be exposed to. Laminate flooring in general is a very tough and durable option, but not all laminate floors are created equal as you will find out when you this article and visit any laminate flooring Toronto stores.

The Abrasion Class Rating

The abrasion class rating is a measure of a laminate floor’s resistance to abrasion, impact, staining, burns, and swellings/indents across its surface, including the edges which are usually the most vulnerable part of the surface to swelling. This rating system is a great guideline for any first-time buyer of laminate flooring as it relates to the expected traffic and use of the floor. The ratings range from AC1 to AC5. The ratings of AC1, AC2, and AC3 relate to moderate, general, and heavy residential use respectively. These are guidelines you will general think about for a home setting where the only traffic will be coming from family and guests, and differ based on the furniture and footwear that will be used in the room. Ratings of AC4 and AC5 are reserved for commercial settings, but you can think about these ratings for your home if you are looking for extra protection.

Warranty

A good laminate flooring Toronto store will explain the guidelines for return and exchange policies beforehand, but it is always good to be prepared just in case you aren’t given all the details. There are generally two warranties you need to consider, and they are the manufacturer’s warranty and the retailer’s warranty. Just keep in mind that it is highly unlikely that any retailer will allow you to return laminate flooring that has already been installed, so be sure that you have the color and style you desire before you install it so you do not run into this problem. If there were any damages upon delivery of the laminate flooring, the retailer should rectify this issue by either guaranteeing a refund or providing a full replacement. In terms of returning the flooring before installation, getting a full refund depends on the retailer themselves as well as whether any shipping on their end was involved.

Selection Process

You will need to determine: how much flooring you will need, the overall cost, financing options available, and if you can take samples to compare against the room that you will be furnishing. Doing laminate flooring can be a costly project, and financing can make it a more affordable option which is something that many laminate flooring Toronto stores will offer. Keep in mind that companies can offer better pricing for larger orders, so planning flooring for multiple rooms at once is usually the best idea to cut overall costs. Good stores will also offer sample books which contain a collection of popular laminate flooring options that are available to choose from. Lastly, do not refrain from shopping around. Prices can vary substantially depending on the type of job you need, so keeping your options open can get you the best prices.
