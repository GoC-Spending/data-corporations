Technology profile

Founded by translation professionals over ten years ago, Terminotix Inc. is a company specializing in computer-aided translation (CAT). Terminotix aims to help linguistic services departments and translation firms boost the performance of coordinators, translators, editors and terminologists through effective CAT solutions.

Over the last few years, linguistic services departments in numerous public and private sector organizations and businesses have increased their productivity and quality with the help of Terminotix.

Today, Terminotix works closely with demanding, high profile clients, such as the General Assembly of the United Nations, UNECA, Merck, UPS, Wyeth, the Canada Mortgage and Housing Corporation, Canada Post, Costco, IKEA, Environment Canada, the Bank of Canada and Exxon Mobil.

With its effective products and experienced staff, Terminotix provides clients with solutions tailored to their specific needs and existing software, backed by reliable technical support.

Terminotix draws on the broad experience of its sister company, Société Gamma Inc., a translation firm known for its expertise.

Terminotix offers the following products:

· LogiTermWeba complete Web CAT solution for businesses
· LogiTerm Corporatea complete Windows CAT solution for businesses
· LogiTerm Proa complete CAT solution for freelancers
· AlignFactoryan automated alignment tool that creates data for translation memories and full-text search software
· SynchroTerman automated tool for bilingual term extraction
· TransSearcha trilingual concordance with over 400 million words
