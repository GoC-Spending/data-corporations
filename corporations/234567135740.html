Company Profile


SA Associates Canada Inc ( SAA ) is a Non Governmental Educational & Immigration Consultancy Organization, operating in the field of career development at home and abroad for its local skilled professional personalities, students and experienced workers which was founded at first in Bangladesh in 1998 by the heads of the Foreign Department of several leading education & migration establishments in Canada in order to increase and simplify the admission of students from foreign shores to them (educational establishments). The rectors of major universities and other professorial staff of educational establishments are in the advisory board of SAA. Besides so many top ranking Employment Agencies and Immigration Lawyer are also honorary adviser of this company. During the past fifteen years, over 1200 students studying through the SAA in various fields of Canada, Australia, USA, UK and Switzerland Universities. And almost 300 families has been migrated in Canada, Australia, Denmark and New Zealand thorugh SA Associates premium Immigration Services. And over 100 skilled worker has changed their life Work Permit Visa cooperation by SA Associates.

SA Associates Canada Inc is officially registered Canadian Company by Ministry of Consumer and Business Services ( OCN 1726863 ), tax payer of Ministry of Finance in Ontario, Canada and BN holder of Canada Revenue Agency ( 85***-0189-RC00** ). SAA is the honorary member of Canada Bangladesh Chamber of Commerce and ISO 9001:2008. SA Associates is also registered company in Bangladesh Dhaka Mayors office; associate member of CACUSS ( Canadian Association of College & University Student Service ), Canada India Education Council ( iCARE certificate holder, Canada ), ICEF in Germany, NAFSA, IAPBC ( International Association of Professional Business Consultant, USA ), IECA ( Independent Educational Consultant Association, USA ), WEBA , AIC ( Association of Independent Consultant in Canada ) and approved by the Ministry of Education in Bangladesh including DCCI ( Dhaka Chamber of Commerce ). They are the official member & Representative of CSIC, QISAN in UK & NCDA ( National Career Development Association in USA). Associates Member of Association of Certified Fraud Examiner in USA and Toronto chapter; Member of ACTE ( Association of Certified Travel Executive ) and OneWorld- World Travel Explorer. They operate through a world wide network of highly qualified specialists and professionals who are involved in the field of education and migration consultation with over 9 official representatives and over 20 marketing agents around the world. All Consultants are CCEA, IATC certified by ICEF, CSP- Canadian Specialist, VSP-Vancouver Specialist, NYC-Specialist, BritAgent, Oneworld trained and OTT airlines certificate holders. ( OTT means Online Travel Training ).

Principles
The hallmarks of their work with and support of colleagues and students include:

Respect for each persons dignity;
Fairness and honesty
Trust and fairness
Advocacy on behalf of student needs and interests
Moreover, the SAA strives for quality continuous evaluation, improvement and innovation in its work.

SA ASSOCIATES CANADA INC

SAA is staffed by a highly qualified team of professionals whose main goal is to help foreign students adapt to the lifestyle in CANADA. The working language of SAA is Russian, English, French and Hindi are mainly used for correspondence purposes and implemented in the day to day work for special cases.

Additional activities of the SAA and its goals.

Handling admission for foreign candidates to educational establishments in Canada, USA, Australia, UK and Switzerland and the surrounding region.
Promotion of bilateral International cooperation with educational establishments in other countries in order to implement new educational projects and exchange programs.
Implementing and executing new educational programs which are focused on the needs of the 22nd century in order to upgrade educational standards and academic levels in any field.
Funding and overseeing of joint research projects.
Organization and the utilization of new teaching methods (distant learning programs, teaching in languages other than English ).
Exchange programs, for students and professionals.
The maintenance of a Human Resource Database of professionals and job oriented courses for multinational companies, which are drafted as per their demands.
The design & execution of programs in order to upgrade the qualifications of people who have a basic degree.
Taking part in foreign projects & grant funds as coordinators to help and develop scientific research institutes in Canada.
Bilateral exchange of knowledge across regions.
Immigration Program for Skilled Professionals in various sector with full cooperation and support information provided by Canada, Denmark, New Zealand & Australia Immigration Department.

Specialist for Express Entry to manage Job Offer from Canadian Employers in-cooperation with ESDC to get LMIA. SA Associates has top 10 Employers Agreement to recruit the skilled workers from Bangladesh for Immigration purpose. They are operating total total 4 offices In Bangladesh ( Dhanmondi, Gulshan, Uttara & Chittagong ) and one in Canada ( Toronto ).
