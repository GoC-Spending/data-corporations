Company Profile


Oracast has been developing websites, web applications, and online marketing strategies since 2003, however the founding partners (who are ex-IBM'ers) have been creating websites since 1999. We believe that every website should either help you make money, by generating leads and sales, or help you save money by automating your administrative business processes such as project management, invoice tracking, and any process that will help your business become more efficient.

What makes us unique in a highly competitive industry with literally zero barriers to entry, is our extensive experience, skill sets and capabilities. We have delivered custom solutions to many different industries including Oil & Gas,
Government, Law/Legal, Retail, Aviation, and Education just to name a few. Our solutions range from marketing websites that generate brand exposure, leads and sales, to mobile apps and large-scale websites that run entire companies.

Services  

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Pay-Per-Click Advertising
To build a successful Pay-Per-Click (PPC) campaign you need to optimize the factors that influence your search rankings. These include landing page design, keyword performance and advertising relevance. Oracast specializes in helping our clients build, run, maintain and measure their PPC campaigns in today's highly competitive internet advertising market.

We are a Google certified AdWords Partner, which means we know what we're doing and Google trusts us.

Service Name:
Search Engine Optimization
What good is your website if nobody finds it? In order to do business effectively on the web, you need to constantly refine and optimize your online marketing strategy, website navigation, and page content. Search Engine Optimization will help your website appear higher up in search engines for keywords relevant to your business. Our SEO experts conduct powerful SEO Audits and implementations that will make sure your website stays ahead of your competition.

Service Name:
Web Design
Not all websites are created equal. That's why we focus on building websites that work. First we identify your goals: do you want to generate leads? make online sales? improve your brand's online visibility? Then, with your objectives in mind, we deliver an engaging website that is visually appealing to your audience, easy to use, and provides your business with the goal-driven website it needs.

Our web design clients typically see 30 percent to 120 percent improvements in key operating metrics such as lead conversions, traffic, and bounce rates (to name a few). Imagine what we can do for your next web design.

Service Name:
Web Applications / Cloud Solutions
We have created custom web applications of all sizes and in many different industries. Prior to working with us, many of our customers managed their operations using spreadsheets and others were still manual. Today, they can't imagine doing business without their custom web application.

After working with Oracast, one of our clients saw operational training costs reduced by 80%; saving 1.25 million per year. Our web application clients typically see 40 percent to 150 percent improvements in key operating metrics such as profit margins, costs savings, and operational efficiency. That's just one example!

Service Name:
Content Management Systems - CMS
Our CMS experts work with systems like Wordpress, Drupal, and Joomla (to name a few) that provide our customers with the simple, secure, and reliable CMS platform they need. We have seen custom CMS platforms come and go over the years and recommend using a well-known CMS like the platforms mentioned above. This is because most custom CMS platforms have a limited shelf-life or the provider disappears; leaving you with the painful task of migrating to a better supported platform. Why take the risk? If you're in the market for a simple and reliable content management system, or if you currently have a CMS platform and would like to improve it, give us a call  our experts and standing by ready to help!

Service Name:
E-Commerce / Shopping Carts
E-commerce can get complicated so it's important that you hire an experienced company that knows what they're doing. That's us. We have successfully implemented many e-commerce shopping carts over the years, shopping carts of all sizes, in both Canada and the United States.

Service Name:
Mobile App Development
Do you need an app for that? We can help. Our mobile app development capabilities span from simple apps to very sophisticated apps that integrate with web applications, cloud solutions, and other 3rd party systems. We also write mobile apps using a single code base that can deploy to multiple platforms such as iOS, Android, etc. What this means to you is that you can have a powerful mobile app that spans multiple devices for much less money. Contact us today and get an app for that.
