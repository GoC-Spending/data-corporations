corporations achieve a competitive advantage in a complex and
difficult business environment.
Member of CHRP
Founding Director of YESS Young Entrepreneur Success Strategies
STARBOND can help your company meet the management challenges you
face. We provide the following services:

Analysis, Identification and Summary of Organizational Needs

Coaching and Follow up to Improve Performance

Development of Strategic Training Plans

Presentation of Training and Development Seminars

Today everyone can purchase the same technology, they can
implement similar procedures, but what gives a company its'
competitive advantage is its' people. Every successful
corporation has recognized that training its' employees is a
critical factor in business success.

We train, coach, and facilitate employee development to achieve
greater corporate effectiveness in corporations in Canada.

active in training, and human resource development for the past

the advanced technology and the fashion industries. She has been
active in developing training strategy, customizing training
models, coordinating, developing and delivering sessions.
Ms. Starkman received her Masters degree, which specialized in
Adult and Community Education from the University of Calgary.

Organizations are under increasing pressure to design training
programs that achieve results. This practical workshop offers a
step by step approach to understanding adult learning principles
and their application to the design and delivery of effective

NEEDS ANALYSIS
NEEDS ANALYSIS
STARBOND does a careful evaluation of your current state of
affairs by going through a step by step process which helps
determine how to adapt or develop a program to meet your needs.
The process may include the following front-end analysis
strategies:

- Reviewing your strategic plan
- Linking your plan to your strategy
- Determining the most appropriate assessment strategies
- Developing training needs assessment worksheet or questionnaire
- Coordinating employee surveys
- Facilitating focus groups to determine needs not met
- Interviewing staff to pin point gaps of actual and desired
performance levels
- Surveying staff clients or suppliers to determine key concerns
and issues
- Reviewing services provided by the competition
- Designing customer or employee feedback systems
- Determining long range and short range goals
- Analysing and reporting research results

Service Name:
MAKING BETTER BUSINESS PRESENTATIONS
MAKING BETTER BUSINESS PRESENTATIONS:
A Practical Guide for Success
Jessica Starkman
STARBOND CONSULTING

Research has shown that people would rather die than make a
public presentation. Research has also proven that presentation
skills are a significant factor in winning over or influencing
audiences. This program provides you with a practical guide to
help you when planning and preparing for business and technical
presentations. The content of this customized one-day workshop
can cover the following topics:

· Self-Analysis; am I the bore I think I am?
· Factors to consider before you begin; what am I getting into?
· Purpose of the presentation; sell, tell, defend, support,
report?
· Analysing your audience; never talk to a naked audience
· Organizing your presentation; tell them what you are going to
tell them, tell them, and tell them what you told them
· Using visual aids; and other tools that provide an impact
· Rehearsing your presentation; practice produces precision
· Making the presentation; comparing this to life and death
· Dealing with anxiety; overcoming stage fright
· Answering questions; consider a question as a compliment
· Practising your presentation
