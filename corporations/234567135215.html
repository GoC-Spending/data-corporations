Company Profile


We are a Business Development firm helping companies start or grow in new markets.

Our experienced consultants will help you with your market analysis, marketing plans, business plans, trade missions, prospecting, and funding proposals.

Your organization will benefit from one-on-one consulting, team facilitation, professional development training, or leave it all to us to develop and implement.

Sector information

Key / Major clients:

Business Development Bank of Canada (BDC) and the Nova Scotia Department of Labour and Advanced Education. The principal consultant, Bonnie O'Toole, had a 14 year employment history with Bell Aliant as a Senior Business Planner and Strategist in the departments of Marketing, Customer Experience, Contact Centres, and Service Delivery.

Success stories:

Our principal consultant facilitates the Marketing Booster Program in Nova Scotia for the BDC which encompasses in-class training, a marketing diagnostic, and one on one consulting to improve the marketing success of small businesses. As well she helps small business owners with their individual needs through personalized consulting.

Sponsored by the NS Dept. of Labour and Advanced Education, Workplace Training Program, she facilitates workplace training programs in: Marketing, Financial Management, Communications, Starting a Successful Small Business (Foundations for Success), and Essential Skills for Small Business.

An actionable Marketing Plan was created for an emerging business to help a small business grow 120% year over year.

A comprehensive funding proposal was created to apply for government (DFAIT) export development support.

A business plan was created for a business moving to Nova Scotia to get funding from NSBI, ACOA and Innovacorp to better understand the business environment in Nova Scotia.
