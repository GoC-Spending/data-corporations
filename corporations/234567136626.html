Company Profile


Silicone batteries embraces the breakthrough of the silicate compound electrolyte that has a smaller resistance of 2 mili-ohm, twelve to thirteen times smaller than that of the lead-acid battery of 25 mili-ohm and greater on the maintenance free. The charging and discharge ability of the silicone battery is greatly improved. The silicone battery has the capability to be discharged at 30C in 8 seconds with no damage to the battery. Furthermore, there is no or little temperature rise during usage of the silicone battery. The silicone battery has a extremely strong cold and heat temperature resistance, it not only works normally at -50C but works normal at +70C. Silicone batteries have no sulfate and no corrosion meaning a longer lifespan and are widely used by the Military, Solar industry, Telecommunications (UPS), Electric Vehicles (EV), Hospitals and Airports because of the high dependable performance, long life and no battery memory. The silicone battery has the capability to be recharged at a 150amp in 22 minutes with no or little visible temperature rise.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Silicone Rechargeable Power Batteries

Silicone Batteries are a high performance battery in whole over that of the lead-acid battery. For over 140 years people only wished for a "Green" battery that is environmentally friendly with a higher capacity, higher performance, easy-to-use and keeps our air and sky clearer.

Today that dream is a reality with the birth of the Silicone Rechargeable Power Battery, meaning that an epochal power storage technology revolution has just begun on a global scale.

Silicone Rechargeable Batteries Exceed Environmental Standards:

(a) Silicone batteries exceed international storage capacity standards by 1.75 times
(b) Silicone batteries exceed international recharge acceptance capacity standards by 2.68 times
(c) High current recharge 0.8°C - 1.0°C
(d) High current discharge at 30°C in 8 seconds with no damage to the battery
(e) Working temperatures: -50°C to +70°C
(f) Long life-span of up to 10 years under normal working conditions
(g) Full charge can be maintained for up to 18 months when stored under normal conditions
(h) No recharge memory and is Maintenance Free (tightly sealed)
(i) No acid mist emission when charging or discharging
(j) No pollution from the electrolyte
(k) Over 400 times for Full Recharges (lead-acid battery is 200 times)
(l) Functions normally at 6,000 meters beneath the sea
(n) Eco-friendly electrolyte can be used as a high grade fertilizer after the life-cycle, the rest of the housing recycled

Technology profile

Silicone Batteries has no sulfate to corrode electrodes and joints, meaning a longer battery life, no internal battery memory, making the silicone battery dependable in harsh temperatures of -50c to +70c. In the 1990's Edison (Greenball) Testing Laboratory in Southern California, stated, the Silicone Battery's performance is much higher than that of the Lead-acid battery in whole.

Silicone Battery Certifications

Certified ISO-9001 and ISO-14001 also certified "Green" non-hazardous materials, by Green Star Environmental Protection Agency.

Issued by China National Rechargeable Batteries Quality Control Center: Certification for tightly sealed silicon power batteries used in electric cars, Certificate for silicone power batteries used for the purpose of start-up.

Issued by the Information industry Ministry of the PRC: Certificates for network access and license for telecommunications device network access in respect of the GYM-1000(below 2V/Ah) telecommunication release valve silicone power batteries; Certificates for network access and license for telecommunications device network access in respect of the 6-GYM-100 (below 12V/42Ah, 60Ah, 100Ah) silicone power batteries

Issued by the electric facilities & instrument quality inspection & testing Center of the Electric Ministry of China: Certificate for release valve tightly sealed silicone power batteries

Issued by the Railway Ministry of China: Certificate for silicone power batteries used in electric locomotives

Issued by the PLA General Intelligence of China: Network access License for national defense telecommunication facilities in respect of the GYM series fixed & tightly sealed silicone power batteries

Issued by the Factor 5701 of the PLA of China: Certificate for 6,000 meters deep-sea battery performance testing, in respect of the 12V38Ah Silicone Power batteries

Issued by the PLA Air-force Aero material 4th Station Equipment institute: Certificate for 14V182Ah (10HR) Aeronautical Silicone Power Batteries

Edison International Company (Southern California) Green-ball Battery Test-Performance Characterization of: The EV1242 Battery in EV Applications, Project Report No.TC-01-0147-Tr01, Electric Vehicle Technical Center

Success stories:

Breaking away from the technological limits of the lead-acid batteries the "Green" certified Silicone Rechargeable Power Batteries exceed the lead-acid battery in whole.

Silicone battery technology is drawing a lot of attention from the international community, rightfully so, the lead-acid battery industry has met their match with the silicone battery. In all testing done by Edison laboratories the silicone battery is the first and only pollution free battery that has no impacts to the environment.

Testimonial:

Soltron Corporation praises silicone batteries for their high performance in solar applications and for other power and storage solutions, we have tested the battery in Arctic temperatures with the results being, the battery worked at -60C including wind shields, Quote: We have never seen a battery work in such cold temperatures, the silicone battery has won our confidence as the best over all battery the world has seen. The battery worked in solar applications so far for 3 years, when we used the lead-acid battery, we had to change the battery 3 times in the same period of time, and the high cost of maintenance to change the batteries.
