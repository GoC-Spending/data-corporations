Company Profile


Western Alfalfa Milling Co. Ltd. is a world leader in the production of certified organic alfalfa pellets for organic fertilizer, soil amendments, and animal feed. Our fertilizer, Alfalfa Green, will contribute to the lushness of lawns, golf courses, municipal green spaces and landscaping, abundance in vegetable gardens, flower beds, even indoor plants all year round. Use in reclamation projects to increase soil aeration, moisture retention, organic matter, and decompact clay soils to improve re-vegetation success after industrial use. On the remediation side, Alfalfa Green is used to boost microbe levels, facilitating hydrocarbon breakdown through a bioremediation process. Alfalfa Green can also be used for feed for virtually any livestock or poultry ration to enhance yolk colouration and are a great source of bypass protein in ruminant diets.

Unique applications:

Use Alfalfa Green as a soil amendment to breakdown hydrocarbons rather than 'dig and dumps'. Rather than bringing in new topsoil and dealing with the associated weed issues, use Alfalfa Green to improve your existing soil to a ''near topsoil'' state.

Success stories:

Alfalfa Green has been used as a soil amendment by major oil and gas companies as a way to reclaim and remediate their affected soils. On the reclamation side, Alfalfa Green boosts soil organic matter, aeration, and moisture retention capabilities promoting a more productive soil. On the remediation side, Alfalfa Green enhances soil microbe populations, acting as a bioremediation agent for hydrocarbon breakdown.

Alfalfa Green is also used by municipalities as a certified organic fertilizer product. As a natural option it is used on flowers, sports fields, green spaces, and more. Alfalfa Green works as above to improve your soil structure. This results in a thicker grass with improved root development, which helps to choke out weed development.

28 July 2010


Testimonial by Shannon Wanner, Urban Forestry/Green Space Technician for the City of Estevan, Saskatchewan

In April 2010 the City of Estevan purchased certified organic Alfalfa Green manufactured by Western Alfalfa Milling Co. Ltd. (WAMCO). We bought the product primarily for use in flower pots throughout the city.

In an average year the City of Estevan plants approximately 10 000 flowers in flower beds and pots. This year I was looking for a more convenient organic fertilizer option. I went with Alfalfa Green and applied it to our flower beds and pots at a rate of one cup per foot of weep line circumference. The pellets were applied in early May at time of planting and were re-applied one month later in June and again in July. The Alfalfa Green was easy to handle and apply and was more convenient than some inorganic fertilizers Ive previously used which had to be mixed with water prior to applying. Most importantly, I have peace of mind knowing that these organic pellets do not pose a health threat to Estevans citizens, children, or pets as they walk through our parks; nor have they been detrimental to the health of any of my staff members who applied them.

In July I observed that the flowers seem to be doing better than in previous years. The blooms, leaves, and foliage appear larger and the colouring perhaps a bit deeper and more vibrant than normal. The only change to our program this year is the addition of WAMCOs Alfalfa Green fertilizer.

Overall I like Alfalfa Green and will use it again in our flower program next year.



Shannon Wanner, City of Estevan
