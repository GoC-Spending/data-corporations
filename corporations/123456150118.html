Company Profile


SciCorp International Corp. Is a closely held Canadian corporation
founded in 1989 to research, manufacture and market a complete
innovative line of environmentally sound wastewater and waste
treatment products. Traded globally under the BIOLOGIC
trademark SciCorp's unique, patented natural nutrient
formulations solve a complete range of wastewater and waste
treatment problems.

SciCorp's principal and operations directors are located at
their head office in Barrie, Ontario, Canada while maintaining
additional district sales offices and warehousing in the United
States and Mexico. In 1993 SciCorp opened a technical sales
office in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia to accommodate increased Asian
presence and offer its distributors technical support on product
applications unique to the Asian environment.
Since not all biological systems function equally, BIOLOGIC
brand products have been introduced in blends specifically
formulated to stimulate activity in various areas of the
application including Wastewater Treatment, Sludge Treatment,
Composting/Landfill odour control, Septic Systems, Aquaculture
ponds, Portable Toilets and Grease Traps. To fully accommodate
the demand of these diversified markets SciCorp customarily
elects to licence distributors for one or all its BIOLOGIC line
of products with defined territories, which generally encompass
a countries boundary.

Product Name:
BIOLOGIC SR2 - Wastewater Treatment
BIOLOGIC SR2 is an industrial strength liquid formulation of
micronutrients for water and wastewater treatment. SR2
accelerates biological activity, growth and metabolism of
exisiting facultative anaerobes to increase biological treatment
efficiency and eliminate common treatment problems. SR2 can be
used to elimiante odor, reduce sludge volume, improve effluent
quality, reduce aeration requirements, improve treatment
efficiency, reduce oil, fat and scum formation, and increase co-
generation performance. Potential SR2 applications include
anaerobic/aerobic digesters, oxidation ponds, sewage treatment
lagoons, large holding tanks, RBC's, trickling filters, lakes, &
ponds.

Product Name:
BIOLOGIC (TM) brand concentrated micronutrient formulations.
SciCorp Systems Inc. developed and markets the next generation of biological waste and wastewater treatment through the manufacturing of its patented micronutrient formulations. Developed in co-operation with Canada's National Research Council the BIOLOGIC (TM) Industrial line of products have become common place in numerous internationally recognized composting operations and wastewater treatment projects.

Traded globally under the BIOLOGIC (TM) trademark SciCorp's unique, natural nutrient formulations solve a complete range of wastewater and waste treatment problems. All of SciCorp's BIOLOGIC (TM) brand of products are based on technology involving the stimulation of non-pathogenic indigenous bacteria within natural biological systems utilizing natural nutrients.

With the addition of BIOLOGIC (TM) products to biological systems, the biological pathways are no longer inhibited due to the lack of essential nutrients and as a result biological activity, growth and metabolism is substantially increased. By stimulating these specific micro-organisms, tremendous benefits in waste and wastewater treatment can be achieved including a considerable increase in treatment efficiency and odor control along with substantial operational cost savings.

SciCorp's BIOLOGIC (TM) formulation has been introduced in blends specifically formulated to stimulate activity in various areas of application including: Composting, landfill and transfer stations to control odors before they are generated and increase the rate of decomposition. Wastewater treatment lagoons, canals, holding tanks and septics to increase treatment efficiencies, extend the operational life of the system and eliminate odors.

Product Name:
Treatment with BIOLOGIC .
BIOLOGIC (TM) products are a group of biological waste system additives containing a proprietary blend of organic and inorganic nutrients, all of which are biodegradable and completely non-toxic, in a form readily available to microorganisms. The relative concentrations and proportions of the various components contained within the BIOLOGIC (TM) products have been explicitly designed to stimulate a specific group of microorganisms called facultative anaerobes.

BIOLOGIC (TM) products do not contain bacteria or enzymes, but rather are designed to stimulate existing bacteria found in biological systems. With the addition of BIOLOGIC (TM) products to a waste handling/treatment facility, the biological pathways are no longer inhibited due to the lack of essential nutrients and as a result biological activity, growth and metabolism is substantially increased.

By stimulating these specific microorganisms, tremendous benefits in waste treatment can be achieved including a considerable increase in treatment efficiency along with no capital costs. In many evolving countries BIOLOGIC (TM) can offer immediate treatment of waste or wastewater.

Biologic products are used in the following ways:
Biological purification of wastewater;
Food wastewater treatment;
Inefficient performance of a wastewater treatment facility;
Odour control in composting, landfill and from sewage sludge;
Pulp and paper wastewater treatment;
Treatment of domestic municipal sewage;
Treatment of domestic sewage.

Technology profile

The biological treatment of waste and wastewater harnesses the
natural power of living microorganisms to provide treatment and
stabilization of waste prior to its release to the environment.
Like all living organisms, the microbial populations of these
biological systems have minimum nutrient and micronutrient
requirements for optimal growth and reproduction (called growth
factors). Insufficient concentrations of these nutrients can
often limit treatment efficiency and create problems throughout
the treatment process.

BIOLOGIC® micronutrient formulations can help prevent these
problems and increase biological treatment efficiency by
providing the essential growth factors required by the existing
microbial population for optimal growth and performance.

Because individual species of microorganisms react differently
and have different sensitivities to these various growth
factors, within a mixed microbial population, individual species
with desirable qualities can be purposefully stimulated while
simultaneously suppressing bacteria with negative or undesirable
qualities.

BIOLOGIC® products contain many of the required growth factors
and other essential nutrients in a form readily available to
stimulate desirable, non-pathogenic, naturally occurring
facultative (non-odor producing) anaerobes.

With the addition of BIOLOGIC®, the biological pathways are no
longer inhibited due to the lack of essential nutrients and as a
result biological activity, growth and metabolism of facultative
anaerobes is significantly increased, providing a dramatic
increase in the performance of biological treatment systems and
effective odor control.
