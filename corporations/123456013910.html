Company Profile


Plastics of Ottawa is a privately owned corporation with over 28
years of experience in design, manufacture and installation of
everything from custom display cases to illuminated exterior
signs.

We custom fabricate interior and exterior signage. Directory, doors code signs, cutom acrylic fabrication, custom cut vinyl and plexiglass, borchure holders, fiberglass repair products

In between we sell fibreglass products to build and repair boats,
canoes and windsurfers. We carry a full line of 3M adhesives
(industrial). Skylights are another product we supply with many
styles (from curbmount to pyramid) and variations in lense
colours (clear, white and Bronze).

Our showroom and plant are located just south of the Queensway
(Hwy. 417) at Bank Street and our store hours are Monday to
Friday from 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM and Saturdays from 8:30 - NOON.

We have supplied our products to customers from Jamaica, the
United States and all across Canada, so feel free to try us with
your requirements for any of the above and expect quality and
service to be #1. Our diversity and commitment to customer
service have kept us in the forefront in our industry for years.
Thank you for your interest and please read on to see in more
detail how we may help your on your future requirements.


CUSTOM FABRICATION

Full line of custom work is available. Free estimates on your designs, or tell us your requirements and we will design it for you. We work with such materials as acrylic, Implex, Lexan, polyethylene, polypropylene, Kydex, foam core and Octolux.

Some of the items fabricated are menu holders, brochure holders, display cases (museum quality), holding tanks, ducting, Slatzwall shelving, food bins, door handles and art bases to name a few. Both prototypes and productions runs are available.

Visit our store , fax your design or call for a sales representative to visit your site.

EXTERIOR SIGNS

We also offer a full line to exterior signage including pylons, channel letter, facias and ground signs.

- Materials used include aluminum, plexi, Lexan, steel and wood.
- Each of our signs include a full 1 year warranty on all parts, labour and workmanship. We use Allanson ballasts H/O fluorescent lamps. All of our signs are CSA approved.
- As part of our full survice policy at Plastics of Ottawa, we offer a complete CAD service, experienced sales team that work closely with the client to ensure that the client receives the most effective sign possible. We have a full knowledge of municipal sign by - laws and procedures required to install correctly sized and located signs.
- We also service existing signs that are in need of new ballasts, lamps, sections of neon, wiring or transformers.

INTERIOR SIGNAGE

We supply and manufacture complete lines of interior signage finding signs i.e.: directory boards and directional signs including door and desk nameplates.
- For your reception area or entrance we manufacture custom cutout letters or logos. These can be manufactured from a myriad of materials some of which are acrylic, octolux, brass, aluminum,
etc. If cutouts are not appropriate for your logo we can reproduce it by silk-screening, computer cut vinyl or data imaging.
- We stock all building code required signage, such as " In Case of Fire Signs ", stairwell ID and emergency exit signs.


FIBERGLASS MATERIAL

Plastics of Ottawa is your distributor for polyester resin, mats, cloths, gelcoat, acetone, mold releases, brushes, fiberglass cleaners and 3M 5200 marine sealant.
- The complete line of " WEST SYSTEM " epoxy products are available : epoxy resin, hardeners, additives, reinforcing materials, tools and supplies.

SKYLIGHTS

Plastics of Ottawa are distributors for Artistic Skylight Domes, manufacturers of custom skylights from 14 1/2 " x 14 1/2 " up to any size and in all shapes. Acrylic domes with aluminum frames encased in vinyl are available and provide exceptional energy performance. Artisitic skylights are manufactured in conformance with the Canadian Government Specifications CGSB 63 - GP - 14. Both frames and domes are available in any colour as well as single, double or triple glazing and offer a 2 year warranty.

SERVICE ? YES WE HAVE IT !

Plastics of Ottawa offers cut - while - you - wait service for straight cut items in stocked materials (acrylic, Lexan, Octolux, foamed PVC, clear acrylic tubing and rod).
- We can also provide boat windows, protective sheets for tables and corners, cutting boards, liners for children's slides, lenses for lights, etc.

Product Name:
PHOTLUMINESCENT SIGNAGE (NEOLITE)
NEOLITE Emergency Directional Signage during power failures.

NEOLITE Safety Products are one of the brightest Glow in the Dark
products available today. Brighter than other products because
NEOLITE materials perform more efficiently over a broader
spectrum of activating light sources.

NEOLITE Safety Products last longer and are easy to clean because
they have a protective finish. They are made of ultraviolet
stabilized plastic and offer greater impact and weather
resistance than other products. No cracking, edge splitting of
delamination because NEOLITE products are constructed of layers
which are thermo-hermatically sealed. NEOLITE materials can take
repeated flexing and in the case of films even creasing, without
losing their physical and luminous properties. They do not
depend only on their substrate for their strength as do many
other luminous materials. The ability of NEOLITE materials to
function over a broad temperature range adds a new dimension to
their use.

NEOLITE films are pre-shrunk to reduce variations due to weather,
temperature and aging. Self-adhesive materials have a new
specially developed permanent adhesive system for longer lasting
adhesion. NEOLITE rigid materials incorporate high-impact
modifiers against cracking and shattering of edges when cut.
This not only produces finished products of high quality, but
also makes handling easier during conditioning and application of
all types of surfaces. NEOLITE materials will not support
combustion when the flame source is removed. NEOLITE materials
have undergone extensive quality control testing.
