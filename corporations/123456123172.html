Company Profile


PBR Laboratories Inc. (PBR), a privately held company
incorporated in the Province of Alberta in 1984, owns and
operates a bioanalytical, analytical chemistry and research laboratory. PBR offers a
full range of microbiological, analytical chemistry and genetic toxicology services applicable to food, natural health products, food and water-pathogen screening, indoor air mold identification, environmental monitoring for chemical and microbiological contaminants, water quality testing, drug safety evaluation, product safety and efficacy evaluations, soil fertility augmentation and biomonitoring for human exposure to DNA damaging, mutagenic and carcinogenic agents.

The company undertakes contract research projects in its area of specialization and has an active research and development program of its own. Our clients include corporations from the pharmaceutical, chemical, food, herbal and nutraceutical, agriculture, petrochemical, health care, environmental biotechnology and other knowledge-based industries, as well as government departments concerned with human health, food safety and the environment.

PBR is an approved bioanalytical laboratory certified as Containment Level 2 (CL2) by CFIA (Canadian Food Inspection Agency) and Public Health Agency canada with accreditation from Standards Council of Canada for ISO/IEC 17025 (CAN-P-4E). PBR is in compliance with OECD-GLP (Good Laboratory Practice of the Organisation for Economic Cooperation and Development) Guidelines and holds Establishment Licence from Health Canada.

PBR has highly qualified and experienced scientific management
team and a dedicated technical staff operating within a network consisting of a Scientific Advisory Board and consultants with recognized expertise. The Board of Directors consists of well known business and community leaders with a strong scientific background.

PBR's core business includes analytical services and applied
research in four major areas of scientific and technical
expertise: a) Genetic and cellular toxicology, b)Biochemical
and immunoassay technologies, c) Environmental and food Microbiology and d)analytical chemistry.

PBR's scientific staff includes Ph.D's with specialisation in microbiology, chemistry, genetics, toxicology and immunology. Equipped with a modern laboratory and highly trained technical staff, PBR offers product safety and efficacy evaluations; assessment of environmental and occupational exposure to toxic substances, and detection and identification of: cyanobacterial toxins in water; mycotoxins in foods and feeds, and bacteria/molds in food, feed, indoor air and water. PBR' s R & D program is directed towards the development of diagnostic kits for environmental toxins, and microbial products for bioremediation.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
MICROBIAL PRODUCTS
The research and devlopment of microbial products (bacterial and
fungal cultures) for the remediation of soil and water
contaminants,and treatment of industrial effluents.

Service Name:
GENETIC TOXICOLOGY TESTING
This service is applied to pre-clinical testing of pharmaceutical
products, medical devices, veterinary drugs, as well as
agricultural and industrial chemicals as required under the
Canadian Drug Act, Pesticide Act, and New Substances Notification
Regulation.These tests, which include a battery of integrated
assays, determine the cytotoxic, mutagenic, and carcinogenic
potential of chemicals. The tests are also used for screening
antimutagenic/anticarcinogenic compounds in natural products and
nutraceuticals. In vitro genotoxicity assay including the Comet assay and Pig-a gene mutation assay are also used for biomonitoring for environmental and life style exposure to mutagens and carcinogens.

Service Name:
TOXIN MONITORING SERVICES
Detection and identification services for Cyanobacterial and
Diarrhetic Shellfish Poisoning toxins in freshwater, marine
samples, aquaculture, and blue-green algae food products

Service Name:
ANALYTICAL MICROBIOLOGY
PBR offers research and testing services applicable to
microbiological quality of indoor air, food, and water,
sterility audits of food manufacturing and processing
facilities, and product safety and efficacy evaluation.

Service Name:
BIOREMEDIATION
We provide comprehensive laboratory support for the application
and monitoring of biotreatment products and processes for the
clean-up of contaminated sites.

Service Name:
PRE-CLINICAL LABORATORY SERVICES
Laboratory testing services are offered to pharmaceutical and
health care industries and clinical trial investigators.

Service Name:
BIOCHEMICAL ANALYTICAL SERVICES
These services are applicable to the identification of
mycotoxins and bacterial toxins in food, dairy and feed products.
We utilize DNA probe and PCR technologies for the identification
of bacterial and fungal pathogens in food, water, air and other
environmental samples.

Service Name:
REGULATORY COMPLIANCE CONSULTING
We assist clients in preparing for product registration
requirements in Canada and compliance to the New Substances
Notification Regulation.

Service Name:
PRDUCT SAFETY AND EFFICACY
Screen products for sterility (bioburden),
germicidal/antibacterial/antifungal activities. Evaluate
products for carcinogenicity and anticarcinogenicity. Efficacy
of nutraceuticals and functional foods.

Service Name:
Probiotics-Identification and characterisation
Provide analysis of probiotics through identification of components and testing for susceptibility to various antibiotics.

Service Name:
ANALYTICAL CHEMISTRY SERVICES
Analytical chemistry services for environmental monitoring for chemical (hydrocarbons, heavy metals, etc.) contaminants, soil fertility augmentation through macro and micro nutrient analyses and other physical and chemical parameters.

Service Name:
NUTRITIONAL LABELING SERVICES
Nutritional labeling analysis of prepared food products is provided for all required parameters for nutritional regulatory compliance.
