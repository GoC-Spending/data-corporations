Company Profile


Nicky Fried works with entrepreneurs and internal teams to develop business ideas. We use a process called graphic facilitation which involves large scale imagery to lead groups and individuals towards a common goal. It is quite literally drawing out conversations so participants can see and understand their discussion as it progresses, and so reach consensus more quickly.

Graphic facilitation is ideal for the complexity inherent in group discussions. It reflects back multiple perspectives, focusing people on the issues rather than personalities. It helps us make connections between thoughts, provides a way to store information, describes a complex flow of activity, energizes a group, helps maintain focus, and provides a framework for thinking.

Benefits of graphic facilitation:
 It boosts learning and retention - 88% of people are visual and kinesthetic learners
 It makes the group thinking explicit  everyone can see it
 Captures and holds emerging thinking so people can quickly find the patterns and connections, which leads to improved decision making
 Increases engagement - People see their contributions to the discussion
 People focus on the issues rather than on personalities
 Increases clarity
 Enhances creativity
 Decreases meeting time
 Creates a graphic summary
 Fosters collaboration
 Its fun!

When to use graphic facilitation
 Strategic Planning
 Innovation & Problem Solving
 Re-Organization & Shifting Paradigms
 Change process
 Project launch and lessons learned
 Team Building
