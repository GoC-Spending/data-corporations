Company Profile


TRILINKS - Building our Nations Wireless Telecommunications Networks –Safely, Efficiently and On-time.
We are General Contractors that Design, Construct, Implement, Optimize and maintain our Nations Wireless Networks and Tower infrastructure.
Since 1999, our skilled teams have constructed new wireless sites in all Canadian Provinces both in urban and rugged remote settings. We have delivered hundreds of fully commissioned Microwave links and thousands of Technology Upgrades. With regional service shops and fully equipped crews, we work alongside our customer to support their network infrastructure needs.
Extensive resources, size and industry experience enables our success through a superior level of technical training, safety, quality and direct customer focus.
As an industry leader we create value for our Stakeholders - Clients, Shareholders and Business Partners. New Technology change creates Opportunities, Passion and Innovation Drives us forward.


Planning, Design & Engineering
Project and Construction Management, Tower Inspections, Mapping and Analysis, Tower and Foundation Designs, Rooftop Cell Site Designs, Tower Reinforcement Engineering, Structural Detailing and Site Audits, Geotechnical, Civil, Electrical and Mechanical Engineering.


Construction & Installation
Site Preparation, Foundations and Roadways, Tower Erection, Structural Steel and Accessories, Line, Antenna and Radio Systems, Equipment Shelter, Electrical, HVAC, Rectifier/ DC Power Systems, Battery and Back-up Generators, All Physical Layer 1 Networking Hardware.


Towers, Accessories and Custom Steel
Custom and Pre-Engineered Towers: Guy, Self Support, Monopole, Micropole, Tripole, CSA S37-13 Compliant Structures. Site Concealment Products, Portable, Rapid Deploy and Temporary Towers. Antenna Mounts, Rooftop Mounts, Transmission Line Supports, Cabling and Hardware, Ladders and Fall Arrest Systems, Grounding & Lighting Systems.


Network, Technology & Systems Upgrades
Engineer, Furnish & Install Industry Leading Original Equipment Manufacturer’s Products and Systems to the Standards and Specifications of our Valued Clients. We have Experienced, Trained Certified and Trusted Field Staff which Provide Installation, Testing and Commissioning Services to Support the Continuous Evolution of Wireless Networks.


Site Facilities & Network Infrastructure Maintenance
Annual Site Inspections & Maintenance Programs. Repair, Upgrade and Replacement of Failed Components. Preventative and Corrective Maintenance and Troubleshooting. Minor/ Major Capital Improvement Projects to Extend Life Cycle providing Operational Cost Savings. Technical Advice, Alternate Solutions, Project Planning, Administration and Reporting.


Emergency Call-out Support
24/7/365 Around the Clock Support through Master Service Agreements. Guaranteed Service Level Agreement Response Times. Single Point Direct Network Control Centre Dispatch. Aerial and Radio Technicians on Standby. Site Access Protocol knowledge and Network Fluency. Exceeding Key Performance Indicators, Objectives, Measures and Deliverables. Established and Proven Emergency Support Programs On-Going.
