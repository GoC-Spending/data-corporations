Successful system implementations require the seamless integration of people, data and systems. Before any existing systems are changed, management needs to understand their critical business metrics, the best source for accumulating related data, and who needs to be empowered with what data to drive operational efficiencies into daily business practice.

GCI deploys cloud computing solutions for clients with staff residing in different physical locations, to provide them with efficient and effective IT infrastructure.

To date GCI has empowered energy companies, retail organizations, engineering firms, modularized residential construction in production line environments and mining companies that harvest precious metal and mineral resources while establishing humanitarian initiatives for locals to benefit from their natural resources.

Product Name:
Strategic Business Software
Stakeholder Cost Reporting (SCR) facilitates system user innovation from the point of data capture and validation, to the reporting of data and creation of business intelligence by:
 providing meaningful information to different levels of the organization with the same data. Tactical data, which is typically more granular in nature, is used at the operational level to drive operational efficiency, is simultaneously mapped to strategic data, which is used at the executive level to support long term corporate planning.
 enabling a digital document strategy to minimize costs associated with manual paper records, systems, human resources and professional audit services.
 creating a blue sky view of programs and projects as well as immediate access to detail including the originating source document.
 establishing business process for corporate metrics to be captured and reported. The process is well documented and easy to maintain by people and requires little training. The process also creates a segregation of duties, authorization accountability, continuous audit trails and automatic tracking reports for individuals required to monitor and process tasks.
 impacting the ERP implementation strategy, by allowing smaller organizational units to adopt a Best of Breed  (BOB) application strategy, while improving value of the data provided to the ERP system. SCR facilitates BOB by ensuring that islands of data have a single source of data that is shared by all systems, including the data requirements of the corporate ERP system. ERP implementation ROI is improved by having ERP functionality at the core of the organization where change is minimal, while the BOB approach is implemented in the peripheral Business Units where change is rapid and continuous.
 supporting a flat BU organizational structure with powerful reporting features to create a transparent team environment. When transparency is used with a well-defined business process, team politics are minimized and people tend to focus on their specific duties, which improves the performance of the whole team.
 facilitating cost allocation techniques that are developed and consistently applied to deliver on the unique reporting requirements of every client stakeholder.

Technology profile

Gci has specialized in providing ERP solutions to small and medium size companies by using Stakeholder Cost Reporting for tracking critical corporate metrics, transparent reporting to key stakeholders and sharing fininacial data with other vertical applications in the organization.

The following is a summary of how internally developed products are used to manage operations and projects. The appeal of these products from an international perspective is that they are based on remote access technologies and therefore very portable to, and locally supportable in all locations around the world.

Stakeholder Cost Reporting is a great tool for those organizations that need:
· quick access to accurate data and related specific documents
· more detail than typically available in conventional accounting systems
· to analyze trends on specific expenditures / suppliers,
· a document management strategy to maintain and archive both financial and related technical data
· to efficiently work with commonly used technologies such as spreadsheets and word processing
· to proactively manage relationships with senior management, shareholders, joint venture partners.....

These products are ideal for corporations wishing to benefit from powerful Document and Data Management features that perform well in Project Management environments.
