Company Profile


Urban Aesthetics is a full service decorating and design company providing design solutions. We offer in-home consultations perfect for any homeowner that seeks assistance with colour & interior design dilemmas, and who is looking for an objective opinion and design guidance. Urban Aesthetics is based out of Vancouver, BC and we come to your home!

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Interior Design Projects
Urban Aesthetics is a full service interior design and interior decorating company, specializing in quality custom home interiors. We offer everything from design concepts and space planning to full interior decorating services. We also offer home decorating & design consulting on what to do with your exterior spaces including paint colour and façade material selection. Our typical projects range from single room makeover in your home or offices to large commercial projects. Regardless of size we believe as your interior decorator in making the home decorating process simple and fun.
Interior Design Consulting

Interior decorating and design consulting services are for clients who need a professional opinion but do not want to hire a designer for the whole project. We can consult on all aspects of the interior design process — paint, tile, countertops, carpet, window treatments — you name it, we can consult with you about it! What to do? Where to start? Urban Aesthetics helps to make the decision process fun and stress free!


FURNITURE RE-UPHOLSTERY

Do you like the shape and style of your existing furniture but you know that the fabric is old and showing signs of wear? Re-upholstery can breathe new life into your aging furniture. Whether you just want to update or change it all to a new color palate, we can offer your project excellent re-upholstery services. Using the “body” of your furniture we can fix what might need fixing and reupholster the body with a new, beautiful and fresh fabric. The result is a one of a kind piece of custom furniture!
CUSTOM WINDOW TREATMENTS

We take pride in creating beautiful window treatments. Selecting window treatments can be confusing and stressful. Urban Aesthetics will make this process simple and fun. We come to your home and consult with you on the various window treatment options available. You can choose from sleek slatted blinds to luxurious drapery. We also offer natural woven wood shades and various sheer shades as well as plantation shutters. And for our clients who love fabric, we can create custom fabric roman shades, valances and drapery panels that will be unique to you and your home. Visit our window treatments on our portfolio page for more information.


Service Name:
Custom Drapes & Window Treatments


Urban Aesthetics offers a full-service, custom window treatments. Trust in our experience. We offer expert advice, color consultations, precise measurements, quality workmanship, and professional installations. We encourage you to take advantage of our free in-home consultation.

A consultation will determine your needs, analyze your personal style, and colours. Most importantly, we take the time to walk you through the process of customizing your windows, making it easy. Avoid costly mistakes and you won’t be disappointed with the wrong treatment, the wrong color, or a drab look. Our goal is to help make your rooms come alive! Urban Aesthetics provides selection and professional installation of many different types of treatments and blinds including: roman shades, draperies, valances, cornices, cellular shades, blinds, shutters, motorized shades, and woven wood shades.



We also carry a variety of window coverings from Hunter Douglas, Maxxmar Shutters, “Sheerweave” Solar Shades, Qmotion shades and shadings from Elite Window Fashions.

Our in-home consultations allow you to view fabric samples next to your furnishings and in your home’s lighting conditions. By having the consultation done in your home it is also easier to identify and solve any issues (i.e., problem-windows) while selecting the perfect window treatment. As window treatments are often the focal point of your room, we match up your special window treatment with the room’s size and scale. As part of our in-home consultation, we will professionally measure your windows to provide an accurate fit for your custom window treatment. Expert color consultations are also available to help you finish your room’s décor.


Service Name:
Commercial Interior Design
At Urban Aesthetics commercial interior design projects showcase your business by creating a space which is environmentally advanced, designed to have a long lasting impact, and be unique to the clients’ needs.

Urban Aesthetics works with all types of businesses and institutions requiring commercial interior design services, such as: medical offices, dental offices, law firms, corporate offices, condominiums, office buildings. We listen to the needs and wants of our commercial clients, and review their company’s mission and objectives. We place a great amount of emphasis on research and planning to help create and design the environments that meet the strategic business objectives, and to ensure that it supports people while they work, live and enjoy their new work environment.



As an interior decorator, it is paramount for us to recognize and concentrate on all of the key elements needed in a commercial space. We have to create a unique design that is functional and relevant to the commercial client´s vision, all while maintaining a balance between functionality and aesthetics.

We follow a systematic approach, including: research, analysis and integration of interior design knowledge into a creative process. We find that this approach best suits the needs and resources of our clients to produce an interior space that fulfills their project goals. Urban Aesthetics always takes care to provide an extensive amount of research before submitting the design proposal for approval to the client, architect, engineer.

We are always happy to carry out any type of commercial interior design projects — from initial planning phase to the final implementation and completion of the project.
