Service Name:
Content Management System
QuickSilk's eMarketing solution is a secure enterprise grade, subscription based, Website Builder & Content Management System (CMS). Distribution channel and SME's use QuickSilk to design, build, deploy, manage & analyze the performance (revenue generation) of their websites for inbound and outbound marketing activities.

The QuickSilk eMarketing platform renders secure, standards-based, feature rich, fully managed and supported, websites, microsites, extranets, and web portals that are responsive and device agnostic. Key features include: - Securely operating and managing all technical aspects for Clients - Allowing Clients to fully manage their sites with no programmer, or programming skills, required - Offering subscription-based product variations - Language manager (supports 70 languages) - Comprehensive user manager - Drag and Drop page manager, and template manager - 50+ modules

We will also offer "migration" services; moving customers from current platform to QuickSilk.

Testimonial:

"QuickSilk has been instrumental in providing a one-stop-shop for all of our web needs. The QuickSilk platform has allowed us to create tailored microsites for partners in developing countries at the click of a button. The support from the QuickSilk technical team has been exceptional and collaborative, and with their efforts we have been able to share quality information to our global audience in real-time."

Libby Williams,
Communications and Program Specialist,
Center for Environmental Risk Assessment, USA

"QuickSilk has been pivotal to ISBR’s ability to provide newsworthy information on events, committee work and resources to our members through it’s SaaS content management system. The platform has also been essential in maintaining our memberships, utilizing the sign-up and renewal processes. Using the microsite capability, we have been able to manage details about our biennial international symposium with ease and efficiency. QuickSilk has helped ISBR raise its visibility, be accessible, and help us serve and grow our membership."

Morven McLean, Ph.D.,
President,
International Society for Biosafety Research (ISBR), USA

"We have worked with QuickSilk since 2010 and have always received exceptional support. Their flexible and easy to use content management system ensures we can both manage our publications and communicate them to our key clients. The extensive features of their User Manager allows us to assign specific rights and permissions to different areas of our website, along with the ability to set Start and Stop publishing dates, as well as full version control over previously published content. QuickSilk also developed and integrated a JAVA based utility that allows us to easily manage the PBO's Integrated Monitoring Database, which is used by parliamentarians, media and the general public.”

Jason Jacques,
Director, Fiscal Analysis ,
Office of the Parliamentary Budget Officer, Canada
