Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Sanipull
a ribbon-profile cleanable vinyl pull system used for over bed light and nurse calls in healthcare facilities. It is easy to see and grab and protects fixtures from damage due to over-force pulls.

Product Name:
SaniSecure
call bell cord positioning strap with plastic clip that has no sharp edges. It protects pull in jacks by keeping cords affixed to linen rather than wrapped around safety rail. It will not come off cord. Guaranteed for 5 years against breakage.

Product Name:
SaniTether
A cleanable 5 foot long ribbon-profile tether that can be attached to special instruments to keep them on station. SaniTether helps prevent theft of instruments and supports infection control by keeping diagnostic instruments tied to a single patient.

Product Name:
SaniKeyfob
short strap with plastic spring clip and key ring often used for holding key, USB sticks, name badges or meds cabinet keys

Product Name:
SaniDoorLeash
Three foot long strap that attached to neck of a door knob with loop on the end to assist people in wheelchairs to close a door behind them for privacy.

Product Name:
SaniStrap retrofit cleanablr emergency lowering ribbon-profilr pull for patient lift
a 2-foot long red ribbon-profile retrofit cleanable strap to replace unsanitary traditional nylon straps and cords emergency straps on patient ceiling-mounted lifts.

Product Name:
SaniPowerCord Strap
8" or 10" long 1/2" wide vinyl strap fitted with a plastic string clip on one end and a ring on the other. Designed to facilitate gathering and supporting power cord(s) up under bed frames to prevent a tripping hazard. Cords are held without the need for nylon tie wraps or special tools for cutting them.

Product Name:
SaniSafetyLanyard
cleanable lanyard made with wipeable vinyl ribbon. Comes in many colours and with two safety breakaway snaps to prevent choking if pulled forward or backward. Great for holding keys, ID pass and name badges.

FSC Code:
G001-Health Care

Alliances:

Strategic alliances:

Sanipull supports infection control strategies, provides a user-friendly pull system for healthcare facilities and significantly reduces maintenance costs by protecting fixtures from over-force damage. A work order cost to replace a broken fixture averages $100. A Sanipull implementation pays for itself in the medium term.

top of page

Sector information

Unique applications:

Sanipull cleanable pulls are used in hospitals, long term care and retirement homes to help reduce the risk on contact spread of infection.

Key / Major clients:

33 out of 36 Greater Toronto area hospitals have implemented Sanipull on over bed light and nurse call fixtures. Over 2000 healthcare facilities use Sanipull products in North America and the UK.

Success stories:

My original client hospital's Infection Control and Health and Safety Manager has written a paper attributing her facility's significant reduction in hospital infection rates to two main reasons. Improved training for housekeeping staff and the hospital-wide implementation of cleanable pulls on over bed lights and nurse calls.

Testimonial:

Testimonials
"These are an absolute perfect answer to a situation that presents itself as a continuous unsanitary health & safety issue. The ribbons are extremely durable (instead of using string or cling) and most importantly are easy and quickly cleaned for the next person's use. The cleaning of the ribbons prevents transmission of in house infections to the next patient. It adds a little class to the hospital environment. Infection control, health & safety committees should be pleased that something so simple could be so effective."

Thank you.

W. Lem, RN
South Muskoka Memorial Hospital
Bracebridge, Ontario

We had been having an issue with cleaning of the light cords in our patient rooms as they were made of a cotton type string material and it was very apparent that they could not be sanitized properly. I was given the Web site for Sanipulls from my supervisor and placed the order. The Sanipulls are easy to attach, and do not come off when forced by the patient, and most importantly easy to wipe clean and sanitize. They have helped us to maintain a cleaner, healthier environment in our patient rooms. I would recommend the Sanipulls to all that are concerned with Health and Safety.

Doug Peter
Environmental Service Working Leader
Daysland Health Centre
Alberta
