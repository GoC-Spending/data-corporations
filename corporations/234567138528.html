Company Profile


Victorious Flooring Sales, Installation and Repair Services is your one stop carpet flooring solution provider across Toronto, Mississauga, Markham, Richmond Hill, Scarborough, Pickering, Ajax, Whitby & Oshawa with no obligation consulting and estimate service! For your satisfaction, we offer unconditional LIFETIME labour warranty on all of our projects.

We offer a wide range of Carpet Flooring services including Sales, Installation, Repair and Stretching Services. Not only supplying with affordable and competitive priced broadloom carpets but also fixing permanent stains and iron burns on wall to wall carpet. Stretching your carpet to remove dangerous buckles, bulges and wrinkles. Repairing snags on Berber carpet, cat and dog chews or damages on stairs and thresholds.


Our services includes but are not limited to:

Warehouse priced, wide range Wool, Wool blend, Nylon, Polypropylene (Olefin) and Polyester fibre wall to wall carpets, broadlooms, commercial carpets and commercial carpet tiles for residential and commercial applications.
Full Installation for Residential, Commercial & Marine carpeting.
(We use Computerized Carpet Flooring Measurement / Estimate Software that minimizes the waste and saves you money)
Custom Carpet bindings such as extra wide cotton tape / fabric binding, invisible hand binding, blind stitching, machine mesh binding and surging.
Environmental Friendly & Solvent FREE, CRI Approved Commercial Carpet Installation & Repair Services for your office and retail store that improves the indoor air quality. We strictly use non-toxic, odourless and non-flammable adhesives, calculated with zero VOCs
Patching discoloured, stained or burned spots. (such as cigarette, iron, charcoal burns & bleach spots on you carpet)
All type of cat, dog and pet damages on your plush carpet, berber carpet or sisal carpet. Whether the carpet damage from your cat or dog is on your threshold, seam, or the stairs / steps. We can repair them all.
Loose or exposed seam repair.
Underpad replacement for water leaks and damages.
Stretching to remove dangerous ripples, buckles and wrinkles from your wall to wall carpet and broadlooms.
Underpadding / Carpet cushion bulge repairs.
