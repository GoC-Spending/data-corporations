Company Profile


Stay Safe (SSIP) is a specialized training firm focussing on the areas of Effective Communications, Crisis Management and Resistance Management systems. SSIP "Resistance Management Systems" is an industry recognized and accepted standards in Use of Force principles, Subject Restraint (Compliant/Active), Intermediate Weapons (Expandable Baton), Defensive Tactics (Sharp-Edged Weapons).

Our Chief facilitator Steve Summerville is a court recognized Expert in the areas noted above. The firm also provides Special Event Security Management having provided those types of services for events as diverse as the SARS concert at Downsview to the AIDS 2006 conference in Toronto, AIDS 2008 in Mexico City, AIDS 2010 in Vienna and AIDS 2012 in Washington, DC.

We provided specialized training and consulting in the areas of Incident Management, Emergency Preparedness and Critical Incident Planning.

We have attained an international reputation in the area of Special Event Security Coordination and Management as a result of our role in successful conferences and events, such as the AIDS conferences in Toronto, Mexico City, Vienna and Washington. We have been able to provide safe and secure events by liaising with the proper authorities, stakeholders, interested parties while developing and delivering both Operational and Deployment plans.
