Company Profile


Metafix, Inc. is an ISO-9002 registered company whose innovative
technology has brought it into the forefront of silver recovery
and photochemical treatment.

Established in 1987, Metafix, Inc. provides systems and service
to the photo imaging industries. Metafix's primary business is
the design, manufacture and marketing of systems to:

reduce processing chemicals and effluent
treat photochemicals and wash water
recover silver from photo processing solutions

Metafix, Inc. has developed its core distinctive competencies
which have led to a predominant position in domestic markets as
well as an increasing presence in international ones.

Since Metafix, Inc.'s inception, the company has grown
substantially in terms of product range and global profile.
Metafix's business spans over 30 countries, with marketing, sales
and service organizations in Canada, the United States, the
United Kingdom, Europe, Asia and Mexico.

Metafix, Inc. is the largest manufacturer of "in-line" chemical
reduction and silver recovery and systems in North America. The
company sells directly to the Quebec and Ottawa markets, while it
is represented outside these markets by a worldwide distribution
network. Metafix, Inc. has also established private label
arrangements with national and multinational companies serving
the different photo imaging industries.

Metafix systems are sold in to the medical (x-ray), non-
destructive testing (industrial x-ray), photofinishing,
microfilm, printed circuit board and graphic arts/printing
industries.

Metafix, Inc. is an environmentally conscious manufacturer whose
products reflect our philosophy to reduce, reuse, and treat on
site.

Product Name:
Metafix photochemical treatment systems
Metafix photochemical treatment systems include:

Wash Control: Metafix introduces a new concept in wash water treatment. Replenished on demand vs. continuous flow-through. Other water recirculation systems circulate water until it becomes unusable, then the system and processor have to be dumped and refilled. The Wash Control continuously circulates water from its holding tank through filters to the processor's wash tank. The wash water is refreshed daily and regulated by the Wash Control's unique control system. It monitors the processor's activity and replenishes the exact amount of fresh water per film necessary for efficient washing and archival stability. The Wash Control controls bio-slime by automatically metering in WashClean(TM), a non-toxic solution that keeps your wash tank virtually spotless.

The Wash Control is programmed to meter out the spent wash water to the innovative 10-2-1TM Wash columns. These are the only columns in the industry capable of consistently and effectively desilvering wash water down to non-detectable levels.

These components form a powerful wash water treatment system which will save you money, maintain a clean processor and keep you in compliance with the most stringent silver discharge regulations.

DevControl: Metafix introduces the DevControl(TM), a real solution for developer reduction. Unlike other developer saver units on the market which are expensive, high-maintenance machines that do little more than play on the "buffer zone" of developer chemistry, the DevControl offers advanced technology, with "hands-off" maintenance at a reasonable price. What makes the DevControl superior is its intuitive design which reduces developer usage, logically and intelligently. It uses a built-in film sensor to keep track of how much developer is being consumed per film. Its two pumps make the unit completely automatic, with no need to keep dumping and refilling the tank. Using a rational feedback system to find the optimum minimum rate of developer usage, the DevControl creates an ideal balance between new and recycled developer to ensure optimal developer performance. This means the developer is always in prime condition. Once the developer has gone through the processor, the DevControl passes it through a particle filter and then through specially formulated resin, which regulates bromide levels and captures oxidized developer by-products which can cause discolouration.

Another unique feature is the DevControl's download capabilities. Using a sophisticated yet easy-to-use monitoring system, the DevControl can print out a record of the processor's activity, indicating the number of films passed and amount of developer circulated in a given time period. Thereby indicating how much developer has been saved. DevControl is a logical choice at a reasonable price.

It's fully-automated which means there's no handling, no storage, and no hauling. After treatment, the neutralized effluent is safely discharged to the drain.

The CTP-pH-Control safely neutrializes the effluent from all plate processing technologies, including thermal, polymer and conventional processors. Product Name:
Fountain Solution Control System
FOUNTAIN SOLUTION CONTROL
Fountain SolutionLike You Have Never Seen It Before!

The Metafix Fountain Solution Control System (FSCS) is a two-part system consisting of the Fountain Solution Control Unit (FSC Unit) and MetaTrax, a proprietary Environmental Management System, designed to expand printers existing dedication to process improvement specifically for their Fountain Solution. Previously, monitoring the pH, temperature and conductivity of fountain solution was a manual process done with varying levels of consistency. With the Metafix FSCS printers can expect improved quality and cost savings associated with the active measurement and management of their fountain solution

The Metafix FSC Unit is connected to the existing dampening/recirculation system. A custom designed sensor array, featuring a Toroidal probe that resists fouling and does not require constant calibration, is used to measure conductivity, pH, temperature and all relevant fluid volumes. A second probe is used to measure the feed water to account for any variations in conductivity of the incoming water. The FSC Unit maintains a consistent, highly stable conductivity during the pressrun essential for optimal and predictable ink/water balance and consistent image quality. The FSC Unit can also measure and dose custom blended additives into the dampening system to treat many common press issues, such as toning, scumming and piling. An optional recycling module can be ordered with the FSC Unit to capture and recover waste fountain solution, eliminating discharge and offering GREEN Printers true Zero Discharge. The result: less waste due to system dumps, make ready and re runs.

MetaTrax captures the data generated by the The FSC Unit via an internet connection and converts it to usable information. Detailed reports are distributed daily documenting the conductivity, pH, temperature and fluid volumes data read by the FSC Unit. This data is constantly compared to user established parameters and MetaTrax issues an alarm to specified site personnel via email or SMS if the operating parameters are no longer in balance, eliminating potentially costly problems before they effect the press run. MetaTrax information can also be used to generate customizable, detailed statistical reports and merged with press data to deliver powerful data models for process improvement. With measurement comes management.

Maintaining proper control of your fountain solution is a critical function in the pressroom and has been called more art than science. Implementing the Metafix Fountain Solution Control System is like having a highly trained chemist and artist managing your dampening/recirculation system. Combining the measurement and management capabilities of the FSC Unit with the reporting tools of MetaTrax gives printers a unique and powerful combination of quality improvement tools and substantial cost savings.


Service Name:
MetaTrax Remote Monitoring Service
TURNING THE DATA WE MEASURE INTO THE INFORMATION YOU NEED

In todays competitive business landscape companies are constantly searching for more and better sources of data to improve their operations, reduce their expenses, or increase their sales. Data is at the heart of most every process improvement program. Data without context can be overwhelming and prove useless. However, when data is put in relevant context, information is born. And information is the key to any process improvement.

Information development is the core of MetaTrax. Metafix designed and developed MetaTrax, a proprietary Environmental Management System, to take the data that our equipment in the field collects and turn it into highly useful, actionable information. Our customers have unparalleled flexibility in determining what data they want reported, when and how often the reports are generated and ultimately what the reports look like. Customers have access to their MetaTrax data via a secure Metafix intranet site.

MetaTrax customers can receive real time alerts when operating conditions vary from acceptable ranges. These alerts can be sent via email and/or SMS message, alerting the company to potential problems before they become severe.
