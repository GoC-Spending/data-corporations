Company Profile


1. Description of the firm

P.A. Services Conseils Inc. is a Canadian company, created in
1986, specializing exclusively in international consulting.
Taking into account the experience of its senior management
group, P.A. Services Conseils Inc. has been active in this field since 1979.

With headquarters in Montreal, P.A. Services Services Inc. is presided over by Nessim Awad, MBA, CA.

P.A. Services Conseils Inc. has adopted a streamlined management structure enabling it to concentrate its resources in the field in order to deliver cost effective, professional and custom designed solutions to its clients.

P.A. Services Conseils Inc. has successfully completed projects covering a broad spectrum of disciplines over the years, for both the private and public sectors. This success is the result from the combined strength of the firm's management team and that of its pool of experts who have been selected for their knowledge, extensive and wide ranging experience, can further be broken down into the following.
