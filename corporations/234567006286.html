Product Name:
ISO 9001 Translated into Plain English
Our publication translates the ISO 9001 quality management system standard into plain English.

Product Name:
ISO 9001 Gap Analysis Tool
Our Gap Analysis Tool will help you to identify the gaps that exist between the ISO 9001 standard and your organization's activities and processes. Once you know exactly where your gaps are, you can take steps to fill them. By using this approach, you will not only meet the ISO 9001 standard, but you will also improve the overall performance of your organization's quality management system.

Product Name:
ISO 9001 Conformance Audit Program
Our ISO 9001 Conformance Audit Program will evaluate how well your quality management system has been implemented, how well it's being maintained, and how well it meets the ISO 9001 standard. Our program will allow you to evaluate the performance of your quality management system. It will help you to identify nonconformities, pinpoint root causes, generate corrective and preventive actions, record results, and track follow-up actions.

Product Name:
ISO 27001 Translated into Plain English

Product Name:
ISO 27002 Translated into Plain English
ISO IEC 17799 (27002) is an Information Security Management Standard. Use it to develop your Information Security Management System (ISMS).

Product Name:
ISO 27002 Information Security Audit Tool
Our Information Security Audit Tool will tell you what you need to do to comply with the ISO/IEC 17799 (27002) Standard. Our tool will pinpoint the security gaps that exist between ISO's security standard and your practices and processes. Once you've filled all the gaps, you can be assured that you've done everything humanly possible to protect your information assets. If you use our Information Security Audit Tool you will not only meet ISO's many security requirements but you will also improve the overall performance of your information security program.

Product Name:
ISO 13485 Translated into Plain English

It translates this difficult material into plain English.

Product Name:

Product Name:

ISO IEC 20000-1 is an IT service management standard. It can be used by both service providers and service recipients. Product Name:
ISO 20000 It Service Management Audit Tool

It can be used by any service provider to demonstrate that it is capable of designing, delivering, and improving its services. Service providers can also use it to evaluate their service practices, processes, policies, and procedures.
