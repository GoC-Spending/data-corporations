Company Profile


We design, develop, manufacture, and distribute BACnet® compliant, Internet-connected building automation systems for monitoring and controlling the HVAC environments within government, commercial, institutional and agricultural buildings. Our controls are simple, flexible and competitvely priced and are backed with an industry best 5-year warranty.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
HVAC controls
Controls for Heating, Ventilation, Air-Condition and Refriguration systems installed in commercial and agricultural buildings.

Product Name:
Building Automation Systems
Direct Digital Controls for the mechanical and electrical systems within commercial and agricultural buildings.

Product Name:
MACH-ProSys
The Reliable Controls® MACH-ProSys is a high-performance, fully programmable BACnet® listed controller. The controller is ideal for large installation projects. Features include: BACnet, RCP and Modus Protocols; 12 Inputs, 8 Outputs and 256 variables; 64 PID Controllers; 32 Weekly Schedules; 8 Annual Schedules; 20 Custom Tables for creating custom input ranges; 64 System Groups; 128 Control-BASIC Programs with 2500 bytes per program; 96 Trend Logs that stores 512 samples and up to 6 points each; 128 Runtime Logs that totals the on time and records the on/off times of a digital point and hold 200 events; 128 User Passwords and each user is assigned a user name and an access level; 16 Custom Units; SMART-Net Port; Real-Time Clock; Industry Best 5-year Warranty

Product Name:
MACH-ProCom
The Reliable Controls® MACH-ProCom is a sleek and powerful 32-bit BACnet® device that delivers unprecedented value in a high performance building controller. Features include: BACnet®, RCP, SMTP and Modbus protocols; Control-BASIC Programs with 2500 bytes per program; Inputs and Outputs (I/O); Variables at selectable standard and custom ranges, as well as fixed or program-driven values; PID Loops; Trend Logs that stores 512 samples and up to 6 points; BACnet® Trend Logs; BACnet® Schedules; Runtime Logs that holds 200 samples; System Groups that allows related points to be grouped on one display at 160 points/group; User Passwords with each user is assigned a user name and an access level; 5 Custom Tables for creating custom scaling functions; 16 Custom Units, SMART-Net Port; Real-Time Clock; Industry Best 5-Year Warranty

Product Name:
MACH-Global
The MACH-Global native BACnet® building controller is a powerful central-point BACnet® controller designed to support multiple BACnet® LAN communication technologies simultaneously and comes with an Industry Best 5-Year Warranty.
This flexible and powerful BACnet® controller also supports and controls up to 128 universal input/output hard points directly on board using a combination of eight modular 16-point expansion cards.
Certification includes BTL listed (B-BC), ISO 16484-5, UL916 Open Energy Management Equipment Certified and UL864 UUKL Certified

Product Name:
MACH-Pro2
The Reliable Controls® MACH-Pro2 is a high-performance, fully programmable and expandable BACnet® listed controller. The controller is ideal for large rooftop or mid-sized mechanical room applications and comes with an Industry Best 5-Year Warranty.
The MACH-Pro2 controller features 12 universal inputs and 8 universal outputs, and can be expanded with 3 additional MACH-ProPoint modules.
Certification includes BTL listed (B-BC), ISO 16484-5, UL916 Open Energy Management Equipment Certified.

Product Name:
MACH-Pro1
The Reliable Controls® MACH-Pro1 is a high-performance, fully programmable BACnet® listed controller. The controller is ideal for mid-sized rooftop equipment or small mechanical room applications and comes with an Industry Best 5-Year Warranty.
Certification includes BTL listed (B-BC ), ISO 16484-5, UL916 Open Energy Management Equipment Certified.

Product Name:
MACHPro-Zone
The Reliable Controls® MACH-Zone is a fully programmable, versatile BACnet® listed controller designed for unitary control applications. The MACH-ProZone controller is ideal for small rooftop and heat pump applications and comes with an Industry Best 5-Year Warranty.
Certifications include BTL listed (B-ASC), ISO 16484-5, UL916 Open Energy Management Equipment Certified, FCC CFR47 Part 15/B.

Product Name:
MACH-Stat
The Reliable Controls® MACH-Stat is a fully programmable, versatile BACnet® controller designed for space control applications where user access is required without the need of a PC. Comes with an Industry Best 5-Year Warranty.
Certifications include BTL listed (B-AAC), ISO 16484-5 (Pending), FCC CFR47 Part 15/B and UL916 Open Energy Management Equipment Certified.

Product Name:
MACH-Stat-ND
The Reliable Controls® MACH-Stat-ND is a fully programmable, versatile BACnet® controller designed for space control applications where an LCD display is not required.
The MACH-Stat-ND can be ordered with optional setpoint slider, humidity sensor, CO2 sensor, and occupancy sensor.
The MACH-Stat-ND comes with an Industry Best 5-Year Warranty and certifications include BTL listed (B-AAC), ISO 16484-5 (Pending), FCC CFR47 Part 15/B and UL916 Open Energy Management Equipment Certified.

Product Name:
MACH-ProAir
The Reliable Controls® MACH-ProAir controller is a fully programmable, versatile BACnet® listed controller designed to meet the needs of variable air volume (VAV) control.
The MACH-Air comes with an Industry Best 5-Year Warranty and certifications include BTL listed (B-BC), ISO 16484-5, UL916 Open Energy Management Equipment Certified.

Product Name:
RC-Studio® Software
RC-Studio® 2.0 is a powerful and intuitive BACnet® operator workstation application that allows building engineers and operators to think outside of the box and create control programs and interfaces that are limited only by the imagination.
Features include: automatic BACnet® discovery ; drag and drop BACnet® points, full priority array control; intuitive database worksheets; cut and paste control strategies using Control-BASIC® editor; full-featured programmability for inputs, outputs, variables, weekly/annual schedules, runtime logs, trend logs, Control-BASIC for sequence of operation, graphical interface annotation and alarm programming with priorities; program an entire system offline using the simulator; programs can be written completely offline without the need of controller hardware; graphic support (BMP, SWF, TIFF, JPG, GIF, PNG, EMF and flash animation); supports VGA/SVGA/ULTRAVGA resolutions; hyperlink support to URLs, Network Management - alarm monitoring and annunciation to printer, paper, and email, global alarm monitoring over an Intranet, wild card sorting and report generating, and automated network backup.

Product Name:
RC-Toolkit Software
RC-Toolkit is a feature-rich Windows-based utility that is loaded with easy-to-use tools that make configuration, set-up, and diagnostics a snap. The software is a must for any technician who starts up, commissions, or expands a system, or for the integrator required to tie together multi-vendor products using an open protocol.
Features include: MSet - configure addresses and baud rates; Panel File Utility - Create duplicate copies of panel files for rapid configuration of large networks; OS Send - update the firmware remotely from the PC to one or more panels on the network; BACset - displays BACnet® points shared between controllers; MACH-Air Calibration - Calibrate the damper position of one or more MACH-Air controllers on a network; Network Diagnostics - view diagnostic data on a network to locate potential problems in panel configuration or wiring; Modbus Setup - configure the MODBUS-Link to communicate with and extract data from a Modbus device; SNMP Setup - integrate using Simple Network Management Protocol; and BBMD Setup - integrate WAN using BACnet®.

Product Name:
RC-Archive Software
The Reliable Controls® RC-Archive 2.0 software is a full-featured, dependable data acquisition application for operators who need to retain and manage trend data over extended periods.
Features include: Wizard guides the operator to provide easy setup; single selection to create archiving of all Trend Logs in a system, network, or controller; browse through multiple panels and create archives from multiple jobs; while logging, RC-Archive 2.0 manages communication resources automatically; graphing with zoom and pan capabilities; seamless graph of archive data with real-time data; export directly to Microsoft Excel; SQL database accessible by third-party applications; log and report data without user intervention; runs as a Microsoft Windows service, or as a user logged in; archive size only limited by SQL licence and hard disk space.

Product Name:
RC-WebView Software
RC-WebView is a server based application that provides a thin-client access solution using Microsoft Internet Explorer in place of proprietary client side software.
Features include: Thin-Client access; unlimited number of simultaneous users; IE 6.0 browser support; easy configuration for new users; secure password setup; able to view, edit, override inputs, outputs, variables, and schedules; able to graph/print Trend Logs, able to view/acknowledge alarms; seamless integration; uses the same graphic files as RC-Studio® 2.0; no custom export of graphics; automatic update between thick-client and thin-client; audit trail; automatically records every change made by a web user, tracks date, time, user, point effected, action, user embedded notes, etc.; and always controlled by password security level.

Product Name:
SMART-Sensor LCD
The Reliable Controls® SMART-Sensor LCD (SSL) is an addressable room sensor and a versatile user interface to the Reliable Controls® MACH-System. Up to 10 system-wide points can be displayed and adjusted using the 3 button interface. The device features a 3 line display to clearly indicate point name, point value, and point icon. All SSL models include an onboard thermistor. Options include additional I/O, humidity and occupancy sensors.
SMART-Sensor LCD includes an Industry Best 5-Year Warranty with a 1-Year Warranty on the humidity sensor and is FCC CFR47 Part 15/B certified.

Product Name:
SETUP-Tool
The SETUP-Tool is a powerful, microcomputer-based interface required to configure the MACH-Air and the MACH-Zone and comes with an Industry Best 5-Year Warranty.

Product Name:
SPACE-Sensor Temperature
The Reliable Controls® SPACE-Sensor Temperature (SST) line of products are attractive, functional sensors designed to compliment SMART-Sensor LCD installations.
SSTs are available in 12 different configurations and comes with an Industry Best 5-Year Warranty and 1-Year Warranty on CO2 sensor.

Product Name:
MACH-ProPoint
The Reliable Controls® MACH-ProPoint is a general purpose expansion module designed for use with the MACH-ProCom and MACH-ProSys BACnet® controllers and comes with an Industry Best 5-Year Warranty.
Features include: superior design with status LED per I/O and glows proportional to voltage level; reversible door flips to conveniently show points list; 12 inputs with universal ranges; 8 outputs with universal ranges and 4 of those outputs able to support either relay or universal modules; conveniently mounts in the same enclosure as the MACH-ProCom and MACH-ProSys or mounted at a distance in separate enclosures in the same space; and requires 24 VAC/VDC power.

Technology profile

All design, development, assembly, service, and repair are housed under one roof in our head office. We resist the trend to outsource any component of our business, and prefer instead to develop our own processes internally and retain full control of our quality.

Our entire suite of software products have been developed with efficient coding and numerous product features by in-house developers and programmers, inspired by loyal customer and dealer input.

Reliable Controls® building control products are tested and listed with the BACnet® Testing Laboratory (BTL). Look for the BTL mark and be confident with your next building automation system integration.
