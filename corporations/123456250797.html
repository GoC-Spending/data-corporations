Company Profile


QPS Canada with their world class,
scientific 3D visualization and analysis software, Fledermaus,
provide innovative, interactive and client-driven solutions and
knowledge for surveying, mapping and research. Fledermaus
presents intuitive insight into massive geographic data sets of
numerous data types promoting professional interaction and
collaboration.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Fledermaus
Fledermaus is a highly sophisticates interactive 3D data
exploration tool. It is very flexible, allowing input
of many data types and interactive exploration of massive,
complex, multidimensional data in a natural and intuitive
manner using a 6-degree of freedom input device (a 3D equivalent
of your computer mouse).

Product Name:
3D submarine cable or pipeline route planning
This product is specially designed for plannning
of cable and pipeline routes in 3D. It has been used by a
number of major telephone and oil companies and has proven
to be a very cost-effective means of route planning. Plan cable
or pipeline routes. View route in 3D with route draped on
surface.

Product Name:
3D Exploration of data
Complex multi-variate data sets (e.g. climate, agricultural data,
etc.) can be interactively explored in 3D space using color,
texture and shading to understand much more quickly and
completely the relationships in your data. Explorations can be
recorded and used to create movies of data exploration sessions.

Product Name:
Hydrographic data processing
Our software package, Fledermaus, allows you to quickly and
easily view and understand huge volumes of hydrographic data in a
3D virtual environment. It is tailored for use with multi-beam
data and is capable of presenting 10's of millions of data points
in a single scene. We allow you to view both surface models
representing the sea floor and all the individual soundings, and
their attributes, that were used to generate the surface. Our
software greately enhances the speed with which you can ascertain
the quality of your data, and allows you to understand the
results in a simple intuitive 3D environment.

Product Name:
3D COMPUTER MOUSE
Product Name:
Visualization Software for Geospatial Data
Allows for the importing of numerous data formats. Surfaces are
color coded by height, depth or any other attribute (such as
imagery) that can be draped over a DTM. (digital terrain model)

Service Name:
Mining/geological engineering
Our software package, Fledermaus, allows you to display and
interactively explore 3D models of underground formations and
structures. Such as: ore bodies (known or predicted) and mine
workings.

Service Name:
Computer software development services
Service Name:
Customized Software Development
While our Fledermaus Software Suite contains multiple
applications, IVS works closely with clients and prospects to
customize the software for their specific needs. These changes
are then implemented into the main package.

Service Name:
Software Training
IVS offers comprehensive 1-5 day training services at the
client's location or ours to improve customer's effective and
appropriate use of the FLedermause Software Suite.
