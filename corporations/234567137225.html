Company Profile


Wave Point Consulting is a bespoke advisory practice for clients with interests in trade and transportation. Our vision is to help organizations thrive by surmounting the obstacles of moving goods and people to market. If you require a combination of innovate solutions, routes and transportation modes then it is a job for our team. As multimodal transportation experts, Wave Point Consulting takes pride in our integrated solutions approach to advance your mobility goals.

We offer clients a distinct value proposition in the provision of the following services:

- Strategy,
- Trade Development,
- Logistics & Supply Chain,
- Transportation,
- Infrastructure Development.

Wave Point Consulting uses our independent research findings, subject matter expertise and partnership network to constantly analyse and interpret market and political developments for clients. As a result, we are a sought after provider of trade and transportation intelligence and advisory services to small cap firms and governments. Our blog Shipping Matters is your chance to see what team members think and ask them questions about how the latest developments are impacting your industry.

Service Name:
Trade Development
he Wave Point Consulting trade development practice is focused on helping firms overcome market intelligence obstacles. For exporters, importers, or governments involved in international commerce, making timely trade development decisions, responding quickly to commercial and economic trends and establishing investment priorities is extremely important. In a fast changing world, small or medium sized importers and exporters face financial, networking, and knowledge challenges when pursuing international trade activities. To address these issues our trade development services include:

- Business Development,
- Feasibility Assessments,
- Market Entry & Diversification Strategy,
- Trade Flow & Route Analysis,
- Trade Policy Development & Monitoring.

Wave Point Consultings aim is to help small cap and government clients obtain and use the right information for their international trade development efforts.

Service Name:
Strategy
The Wave Point Consulting strategy practice is focused on helping firms maximize the value of their business. For companies to be successful, it is necessary to make informed forward-looking decision and then take action to address specific challenges, or opportunities. Shifting economic and competitive conditions and changing customer requirements are constant business pressures. Corporate, or business unit, revenue growth depends on a robust assessment of emerging trends, sources of competitive strength and the creation of a set of goals and actionable tactics that can be embraced and communicated across the entire organization. Whether the goal is to increase revenue, reduce costs, or reshape the organization, Wave Point Consulting can improve and sustain organizational performances by working with clients to conduct, develop, or facilitate:

- Business & Strategic Plans,
- Business Restructuring,
- Change Management Initiatives,
- Competitive Analysis,
- Financial/Economic Analysis
- Scenario Development & Sensitivity Analysis.

Wave Point Consultings aim is to help small cap companies and governments make the right strategy choices for their organization.

Service Name:
Logistics & Supply Chain
The Wave Point Consulting logistics and supply chain practice is focused on getting the right product, to the right market, in the right condition, at the right time. Shippers, carriers and terminal operators are all adapting to the impacts of shifts in economic fundamentals. Rapid change in currency values, capital market fluctuations, volatility in fuel and commodity prices, the uncertain pace of consumer demand and new environmental initiatives are all having a dramatic impact on logistics and supply chain practices. We assist firms seeking to achieve cost reduction targets, access new markets, support increases sales activity, or enhance the environmental, or operating performance of existing assets.

For small and medium enterprises without full-time logistics or traffic departments, companies that are rapidly expanding, or simply dont have the time and resources to deal with crucial logistics functions, the Wave Point Consulting team is an ideal fit. Our multimodal transportation expertise, commercial and market knowledge can be applied quickly to address the following needs:

- Freight Rate Negotiations,
- Logistics Services Request for Quotes,
- Performance Metric Improvements,
- Supply Chain Analysis,
- Supplier Evaluation & Selection.

Wave Point Consultings aim is to help small cap firms make the right logistics and operational choices for their organization.

Service Name:
Transportation
The Wave Point Consulting transportation practice is focused on helping firms, communities and governments adapt to shifting world trade patterns, changing customer requirements, relentless business pressure and sustainability expectations. Uncertainty is a major factor facing transportation clients, and shippers that rely on their surface transport providers. Rapid change in currency values, capital market outlook, volatility in fuel and commodity prices, technology and new environmental initiatives are all having a dramatic impact.

As multimodal transportation experts, we help port authorities, importers, exports, carriers and terminal operators and governments take informed actions to assess the commercial impacts of these changes, improve operational performance and determine infrastructure requirements. Team members with deep industry experience deliver well researched, expertly considered intelligence and advice through the provision of the following services:

- Acquisitions & Merger Due Diligence,
- Cruise Tourism Development,
- Economic Impact Assessments,
- Freight & Goods Movement Analysis,
- Shipping & Environmental Regulatory Law,
- Transportation Socioeconomic Studies.

For firms to be successful, making the best transportation decisions is extremely important. Our aim is to help small cap firms and governments make the right choices for their organization.

Service Name:
Infrastructure Development
The Wave Point Consulting infrastructure development practice is focused on securing both short and long-term client value-creating opportunities. Quality revenue generation, new market access, improved sustainability, and community acceptance of transportation infrastructure (and associated commercial real estate opportunities) are all important factors that influence the nature of the social license required for business investment. Delays in obtaining regulatory approvals can result in significantly increased costs, or a competitor securing a stronger position in the marketplace. The nuances of the Canadian federal and provincial regulatory approval process, transportation legislation, tight time frames and the rigour required for project approval can be a challenge for both executives and technical personnel working on infrastructure development projects. To overcome these challenges our team members can assist project proponents with:

- Community Consultations,
- Employment Land Studies,
- Project Environmental Assessment Process Management,
- Regulatory Strategy,
- Site Selection & Market Access Evaluation,
- Transportation Master Planning Land Use Analysis.

To assure optimal investment and operating decisions work with our team members.
