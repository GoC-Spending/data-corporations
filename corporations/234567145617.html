Company Profile


Welcome to Surrey Digital- Your Wide Format Print Specialists!

Surrey Digital is a family owned printer and a genuine one stop print shop under one roof! We offer quality design services and as wide format printing specialists, we offer excellent Cad Plotting, Blueprinting, Plan Printing, Indoor/Outdoor Signage, & Banners at great prices. Our talented staff also produces an aray of the best, yet cost effective marketing materials and everyday business necessitites such as envelopes, letterhead, business cards and forms at the best possible price. As an Authorisded Large Format Printer dealer (Canon, HP, Seiko, Oce), we are able to sell a full range of Wide Format Printer Equipment, Engineering Plotting Bond Paper and Rolls, Media and Supplies.

The distinctive characteristics of quick turnaround and high quality workmanship are found to provide advantages to the many industries we serve. High speed plan printing, short run/customized colour brochures/pamphlets allow firms to promote their business to highly specific market segments, maximizing their promotion dollar. Large format/short run colour banners, posters and presentation boards accomplish high end image creation for specific customer applications.

The practiced art of job pick-up/delivery, shipping services, and distribution make complete fulfillment a natural extension of the services provided to our clients.

If weve not had the pleasure of serving you before, please give us a try. Contact us via our website or visit us at our shop, conveniently located in central Surrey.

Service Name:
Reprographics
At the heart of our business is our ability to provide high quality, time sensitive reprographic printing (blueprinting) for our engineering, architectural and construction customers. Utilizing the latest in wide format equipment, we have the capability to provide both black and white as well as color Cad prints in a timely manner. We accept a wide variety of file formats for printing and your files can be sent via e-mail, our on-line order form, or uploaded onto our FTP site for fast, convenient file transfer.

We offer the following services:
Digital black and white AEC prints from PLT, PDF, DWG files (Cad Plotting)
Reduction and enlargement of AEC drawings
Color Cad plotting of AEC files
Spec printing up to 11" x 17" in color or black and white
Scanning and Archiving
Blueprinting, now called wide format digital printing
