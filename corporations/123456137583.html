Company Profile


Broadband wireless technology is one of the fastest growing
areas in the high tech industry. An increasingly mobile society
is demanding immediate access to voice, data and other
information anytime, anywhere. Those living or working in remote
locations and in areas without a wired infrastructure are also
demanding the same access to information and high-speed services
available in metropolitan areas. The UBS line of products is
designed to deliver the broadband wireless technology that will
meet these demands.
UBS has maintained a strong focus on the research and
development of new and innovative wireless solutions. The
result has been our ability to deliver the most advanced
wireless solutions for todays complex wireless systems, and to
develop the products and systems that will lead the advancement
of wireless networks into the future.UBS designs, develops and
manufactures high-speed mobile and fixed wireless solutions,
including systems based on COFDM, a robust and efficient RF
modulation technology that provides superior coverage in non-
line of sight situations, and is resistant to multipath
interference even in a mobile environment.
Our solutions target the high growth markets for wireless
Internet, wireless cable TV, digital radio, and other
applications for wireless video, voice and data services. UBS
also manufactures high-quality, reliable, passive components
including custom filters, waveguide assemblies, couplers and
filter combiner assemblies.
Our main design and manufacturing locations are in Concord,
Ontario. The UBS head office, the
main manufacturing plant, and the principal research and
development centre, are all located at our Concord facility,
just north of Toronto, Canada. UBS has
more than 25 years of experience in the design and manufacture
of transmission equipment.
To better serve the changing and emerging needs of our global
customers, in addition to Canada, UBS has offices in
the U.S., Singapore, the U.K, Italy, Russia and South America.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Transmission Equipment
UBS develops and manufactures RF transmission equipment that
delivers the most advanced broadband wireless solutions for
todays complex systems and will continue to develop the
products that will lead the advancement of wireless networks
into the future. Transmission products include, Transmitters
(MMDS, L-Band & S-Band, 18 GHz, receivers (MMDS, L-Band & S-
Band) and transceivers (L-Band & S-Band).

Product Name:
Passive Components (Waveguide, Filters)
Unique Broadband Systems, Inc. designs and manufactures a
complete line of rectangular Waveguide products from WR-28 to WR-
284, and microwave filters up to 40Ghz. All types of microwave
filters can be supplied, e.g. low pass, high pass, band pass,
band stop, direct-coupled and dual mode, directional filters,
and multiplexers

Product Name:
High_Speed Mobile Wireless Systems (DAB, SDARS)
DAB (Digital Audio Broadcast)
Digital systems are the future direction for all forms of
broadcasting. A complete COFDM DAB system from Unique Broadband
Systems will allow for the broadcast of CD quality, interference
free radio, as well as the transmission of a visual component
referred to as program-associated data (PAD). The PAD can
include data such as song lyrics or artist information delivered
in conjunction with music being played. The DAB system can also
transmit data which can be used in applications where data
transmission is required to or from a vehicle moving at high
speeds. This data management capability opens the way for
interactive services over the DAB system in the future. 

SDARS (Satellite Digital Audio Radio Services)
An SDARS system can provide CD quality audio programming to a
broad coverage area. A terrestrial repeater network, such as the
UBS designed COFDM system, is a necessary component in an SDARS
system. Due to the limitations of satellite transmission, the
signal is not able to penetrate buildings, so it cannot
effectively cover dense urban areas. The UBS terrestrial
repeater network extends SDARS coverage and allows providers to
reach the greatest number of subscribers and provide quality
radio coverage.


Product Name:
Modulators and Demodulators (COFDM-based and QAM - QPSK)
Unique Broadband Systems, Inc. is one of the world's leading
manufacturers of COFDM-based and QAM/QPSK modulators, de-
modulators, adapters and monitoring equipment for transmission
of digital TV signals and data. Products include Modulators,
Adapters, Monitor, WEb Link, Regenerating processor.



Product Name:
Digital Video Distribution Systems
TV broadcasters, cable operators, satellite TV providers, and
anyone else in the business of delivering television service
recognize the increasing complexity of the market. With the
increasing number of choices consumers have, competition is
forcing operators to become more innovative in the programming
they deliver, while at the same time controlling costs.  
Unique Broadband Systems Digital Video Distribution System
offers the operator a modular approach to combining programming
from various sources: satellites, local broadcasters, or their
own studios. In addition to re-mapping the content to suit the
operators requirements, the system also has the capability of
decrypting or encrypting a signal and/or applying conditional
access. Output from the DVDS allows for broadcasting over a
wireless or cable system.
