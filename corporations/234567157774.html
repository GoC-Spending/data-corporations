Company Profile


Navigator Multimedia Inc. is a full-service digital agency located in Kelowna, BC. From logo design to website development, search engine optimisation and online marketing to powerful business class hosting, all the work at Navigator happens in-house. Envision a room full of collaborating, creative web pros, working together to execute your project. As a team, we produce memorable online experiences for diverse audiences.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Website Development
Our web specialists are in-house. That means you get to talk to the people who are building your project, and gain confidence in their expertise and care.

We know our stuff. Years of experience, combined with passion and skill, make our dream team your go-to web developers for bringing ideas big and small to life.

Just the facts, jargon-free. We’re not here to confuse you. Ongoing communication with our project manager will keep you in the loop with a clear understanding of your project’s progress.


Service Name:
Online Marketing as a Google Partner, social media and strategy


Put your website in the right place, at the right time, for higher traffic and revenue.

We’ll provide you with clarity first. Strategy and tools, second. Because online marketing is confusing enough. The trends come and go, but at the end of the day you want to put your money where it makes the most sense for growing your business. That’s why we offer our customers a range of flexible solutions, without the technical jargon.

Your website: if you build it, they will not necessarily come. If you want to get the word out about your website, it’s time to look into online marketing.



Effective online marketing connects your website with your targeted audience, making it that much easier to convert prospects into supporters. No doubt your website or Facebook page is fantastic; online marketing helps your targeted audience discover and use it.

Our approach is data-driven. We pull from a dependable range of strategies. From our Google-certified Partner Analytics specialist to our SEO manager, nobody here goes off intuition when putting together your online marketing solution. We use traffic data tools to determine precisely the most effective solution for your goals, and draw from a number of tried-and-true strategies to bring it all together.


Service Name:
Web Apps and programming
From event calendars to reservation systems, there are a number of uses for database-driven applications. We can assist with identifying, planning and building the right solution for your needs.

We employ top programming technologies, including PHP, MySQL, Ajax, JQuery, and Javascript. Our in-house team of coders keep up with the latest programming languages in order to provide applications destined for longevity and scalable growth.

Solutions influenced by user experience. Our database-driven applications are built by humans, for humans. That’s why user experience is a touchstone of how we approach production and presentation. We simplify processes so users can manage data with ease, often integrating our solutions with content management systems including WordPress for ultimate convenience.


Service Name:
e-commerce solutions
You can sell online and not be overwhelmed. Our solutions are user-friendly; we provide one-on-one training so you can transact with confidence.

Safe and secure payment gateways are essential. We work with trusted leaders including PayPal and Moneris to give our clients the most reliable systems available.

Custom, scalable solutions make it easy to grow. With the world as your marketplace, there’s room for growth. At Navigator, we build custom solutions so you can start with what you need, and add to your empire over time.

Service Name:
Content Management Systems
Content Management Systems (CMS) let you edit your website, on your time. Instead of waiting for your web designer to make changes, you can do it yourself with a CMS. Now you’re in control of your website content.

You save money when you manage your own website content. You add, edit and delete your own page content, meaning fewer tasks for your web designer.

Can you create a Word doc? You can create a web page. Our content management systems are built for ease of use, so you don’t need to be a computer whiz to manage your website.


Service Name:
Web hosting
Relax with reliable service & human support. Here’s what you need to know about our web hosting services:

Want your website online? You need web hosting. Every website owner requires a “place” on the Internet to “host” the site. That’s where our services come in.

Not all web hosting services are created equal. There are different types of web hosting services out there, with pricing ranging from free to very expensive, depending on the provider and your hosting needs.

We offer reliable, quality service. Web hosting that’s so good, you forget you even need it.. And if there’s ever an issue, a question, or a change of plans, support is just a phone call away.

Get started with web hosting

Web hosting takes care of the tools, storage, data transfer, bandwidth, and oftentimes the email accounts you need to get your site online. So how do you determine the best web hosting package for your needs? Start here:
1.Consider the size of your site. The amount of web hosting “muscle” required is often dependent on the demands of the site. How much space do you need to store data? How much traffic to the site do you anticipate? These are questions to review with your prospective hosting providers.
2.Customize to suit your needs. Professional web hosting providers offer scalable packages with hosting options you can customize, even over time if required. See our solutions below for more information.
3.Get acquainted with customer service. Save time and stress by working with a hosting provider that offers real-time, human support. Whether you have a question or issue, you should know who to turn to for assistance.
