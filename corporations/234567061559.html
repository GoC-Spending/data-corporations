Company Profile


Symtrex Inc, a Cyber Security Specialist/Solution Provider, is a provider of several distinctly different IT Security Services and products for our clients. Our expertise in the aspect of network security pertaining to evolving regulations, standards, and security compliance is in demand. From our beginnings, over 25 years ago, we make sure everything we do honours a commitment to the highest security standards available to guarantee that peace of mind. Our specialties include system logging and reporting (ELM, SIEM, SEM), malicious traffic detection and advanced persistent threat protection, data protection and encryption, network access controls and network monitoring, end point protection and unified threat management systems.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Sophos SG Unified Threat Management System
The Sophos SG Unified Threat Management System offers complete production with five security subscriptions that can be selected - Network Protection, Email Protection, Web Protection, Wireless Protection (using the Sophos AP) and Web Server Protection. Included in the Network Protection is the new feature detection of Advanced Persistent threats. The product is available as a hardware solution, or software (which can be either virtualized or placed in a private cloud). For large installations, one can cluster up to 10 installations of the firewall.

Product Name:
Network Access Control - SnoopWall NetSHIELD
The SnoopWall NetSHIELD Appliances deliver the ABCs of Network Security for Intrusion Defense and Regulatory Compliance  Alerting, Blocking and Correcting internal security threats, vulnerabilities and network access without being inline, without any agents, without any forklift upgrades. NACwalls combine agent-less RSA SecureID and Active Directory authentication with control, detection, quarantine and blocking of untrusted assets and unmaneagable network equipment such as BlackBerry, iPhone, VoIP phones, Wireless Routers, Barcode Scanners and other devices. NACwalls do this with dynamic agent-less VLAN provisioning using 802.1q NOT 802.1x

Product Name:
Sophos SafeGuard Encryption
Sophos SafeGuard Enterprise Encryption 7 introduces the most complete data protection solution on the market today, protecting data on multiple devices and operating systems. Whether your data resides on a laptop, a mobile device, or being collaborated upon via the cloud or other file sharing method, SafeGuard Encryption is built to match your organizational workflow and processes without slowing down productivity. Couple that with our management of Microsoft’s BitLocker and Apple’s FileVault.

SafeGuard Enterprise automatically and transparently encrypts files on removable media such as USB sticks, memory cards and CDs/DVDs.

Share encrypted data on removable media easily across your organization without impacting your users
Using a portable application and password, easily and securely share encrypted removable media with users not using SafeGuard Enterprise
Removable media whitelisting makes encryption management easier and more flexible


Product Name:
Sophos EndUser Protection
Next-Generation Enduser Protection is the integration of Sophos innovative endpoint, mobile and encryption technologies to deliver better protection and simpler management. From malicious traffic detection integrated into the endpoint to cloud-managed policies that follow users across devices and platforms, we're redefining what it means to provide comprehensive enduser security.

Product Name:
SIEM - LogRhythm
LogRhythm - a next Generation Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) fully integrating Log Management and SIEM capabilities with deep Network and Endpoint Forensics capabilities. LogRhythm’s next generation SIEM analyzes all available log and machine data and combines it with deep forensic data capture at both the server and network level for true enterprise visibility. This insight is leveraged by AI Engine, LogRhythm’s patented Machine Analytics technology, to deliver automated, continuous analysis of all activity observed within the environment. The integrated architecture ensures that when threats and breaches are detected customers can quickly access a global view of activity enabling exceptionable security intelligence and rapid response.

Product Name:
Open Threat Management / UBA - Seceon
Seceon OTM is an automated detection and remediation solution. The products is designed to detect threats, both known and unknown, in minutes using a data driven analytics solution. Combining behavioural analysis and machine learning, with threat intelligence provides a holistic overview of your users, devices and network to pinpoint abnormal and malicious behaviour. Designed to work on as a standalone product or in conjunction with an event logging tool, it will provide the single pane of glass overview, prioritizing alerts and if necessary providing real- time remediation.

Product Name:
Data Erasure - Blancco
Data sanitization is the process of irreversibly removing or destroying data stored on a memory device such as hard drives, flash memory/SSD’s, mobile devices, CDs and DVDs. Simply hitting delete does not necessarily remove the data, and enterprises require a method of “sanitizing’ across every IT asset. Blancco’s data erasure uses a method of overwriting the data with the aim to completely “destroy” or clear the electronic data residing on the hard drive or other digital media. However most enterprises need an audit trail to prove compliance to data retention and sanitization. Blancco Data Eraser works across the entire enterprise and lifecycle of every IT asset, in addition providing confirmation removal.


Product Name:
Security Awareness Training
End users can be your best asset, or the weakest link to your security posture. Testing and training your employees ability to spot phishing emails, which can potentially contain malware, ransomware, trojan is key. Testing will provide the baseline, and then supplement with proven security awareness education – what to look for, where to look for signs, to downloading the attachment or click the link, and then test again.

Depending on your requirements, organizations can choose the level of education and training – from simple phishing, through to vishing ( voicemail from reputable companies ), USB and texting.

Service Name:
IT Security Audits
A comprehensive audit that encompasses both IT Security and Organziational Security Vulnerabilities. Our auditors analyze every phase of your organization and have to produce on a daily basis to ensure that you organization gets a return on your investment.

We have the tools to ensure IT compliancy with PIPEDA, HIPAA, Sarbanes Oxley, NISPOM Chapter 8, Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act and CISP.


Service Name:
Network Security Services
Security is not technology, it is the knowledge on how to use the technology. Our Network Security Consultants have the knowledge on how to implement the correct technology to protect your network.
