Company Profile


MACFAB's strengths are Precision Machining;

- small (within a 20" cube) part precision machining, to micromachining, with tolerances to 2/10's and CMM capability to 1/10.

- single piece to mid-volume quantities

- quick turn, with many materials in stock (metals and plastics)

- excellent quality, on time delivery and customer service - cost effectively

- material include practically all steels, exotic alloys, and are specialists with engineered plastics.

- additional services include laser engraving, parts cleaning, mechanical assembly in a clean room environment

- ISO 9001:2008,

- Canadian Controlled Goods Program (CGP) Registered

- Interested in helping with IRB commitments
