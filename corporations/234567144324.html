Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
SolarBag
The 3 litre SolarBag is the worlds first portable water purifier that meets strict EPA standards without the use of chemicals, consumables, or power to remove micro-organisms, organic contaminants, and heavy metals. It is powered by the natural energy of the sun to activate a patented nanotechnology mesh to purify water in a few hours, even on cloudy days. It is BPA free, lightweight, portable, re-usable hundreds of times, and treats up to 9 litres a day for pennies a day.

Product Name:
The Shield 500
The Shield 500 provides the broadest range of coverage for ground water contaminants, and is extremely effective for drinking water for residences and livestock. Some of the major sources of these contaminants are storage tanks, septic systems, hazardous waste sites, landfills, and the widespread use of fertilizers, pesticides and other chemicals.
Most of the emerging contaminants on the EPA primary, secondary, or contaminant candidate lists are organics, pathogens, or metals that can be eliminated by the Puralytics Process, without creating any hazardous waste.

Unique applications:

SolarBag 3L: Using only sunlight to activate our 5 photochemical purification processes, the SolarBag 3L is the ideal solution for urgent, remote and/or portable water purification; including humanitarian response, emergency preparedness, outdoors recreation and for rural, village situations.
 Simple To Use  Just fill the SolarBag 3L with water and place in the sun for 2-­&#8208;3 hours.
 Broadly Effective  It has the broadest range of contaminant removal available from any purifier, including not just biological pathogens, but also heavy metals and chemical toxins  including pesticides, herbicides, fertilizers, petrochemicals and pharmaceuticals.
 Portable & Storable -­&#8208; Each bag holds 3 Liters, meeting the WHO guideline for daily drinking water, continuously purifying that water until its consumed.
 Very Long Lasting  The SolarBag 3L can be used up to hundreds and hundreds of times over!
 Environmentally Friendly  There are no filters, chemicals or waste water discharge to worry about and SolarBag 3L is fully recyclable.
 Low Cost Per Liter -­&#8208; Over its expected life, SolarBag 3L delivers pure water for just pennies per liter.
 Proven  SolarBag 3L has been independently certified to meet US EPA water purifier standards for delivering 99.9999% pathogen free water. It meets the World Health Organization (WHO) Guidelines and has already been deployed in over 45 countries globally, including use/testing by the Red Cross in Haiti.

The Shield 500:
The Shield 500 uses the same core light- activated Nanotechnology, except it is electrically powered and uses an array of LEDs
to stimulate our 5 photochemical processes. the shield is designed to be a decentralized and single point solution for water purity. installed close to point of use. As such the Shield can allow the user to control their own water purity and replace what otherwise would require multiple systems to accomplish.
Simple Operation and Low Maintenance - The Shield 500 does not have any moving parts and runs continuously in the background, without the ongoing need to add chemicals, replace filters/membranes.
Broadly Effective - It has the broadest range of contaminant removal available from any purifier, including not just biological pathogens, but also heavy metals and chemical toxins - including pesticides, herbicides, fertilizers, petrochemicals and pharmaceuticals.
Replaces Multiple Systems - The Shield is a compact, single pass solution that can replace what otherwise woudl take multiple systems to accomplish for contaminant removal.
Scalable Volumes - Each Shield unit delivers 500 gallons per day and can be combined for 'stacked' operation in 500 gallon per day increments.
Very Efficient - It yields an incredible, 100% of water throughput - unlike the typical less than half yielded via other wasteful, reverse osmosis processes.
Environmentally Friendly - The Shield 500 is uniquely green and clean, with absolutely no waste water discharge and no biohazard consumables to throw away.
Low Cost Per Liter - The Shield 500 can achieve daily operating cost savings of up to 70-80% savings versus a typical reverse osmosis system and is capable of delivering water at less than half the price per litter of other combined systems.
Proven - The Shield 500 is already deployed in 10 Countries now!

The SolarBag and Shield are winners of Clean Tech 2010- Imagine H20 - Zino Society and many other awards.

Success stories:

Testimonial:

EPA Purifier Tests were done at the University of Arizona - Tucson, Arizona - This test demonstrated that the SolarBag exceeded the requirements the EPA set, including the removal of Arsenic.

Cascade Designs Inc. - Seattle, Washington - Cascade Designs Inc (CDI) makes several water treatment and storage products for outdoor recreational and military use. Their microbiological laboratory tests various water purification products against EPA and military guidelines for performance. Over a six month period, CDI performed a series of tests measuring chemical contaminant reduction and microbial inactivation on the SolarBag. The tests were performed outdoors in both the morning and the afternoon sun, under both clear and overcast skies; ambient temperatures ranged from 15 -30 C. CDI also used specially formulated challenged water designed to represent developing world water supplies, as well as challenge water defined in EPA and NSF P248 protocols.

The tests showed that the SolarBag was able to reduce 99.9999% of all bacteria, 99.99% of all virus, and over 90% of the chemical contaminants in just 2 hours on warm and sunny days while taking 4 hours in rainy overcast conditions.

We have many other testimonials; please do not hesitate to ask us for them.

Over a six month period, CDI performed a series of tests measuring chemical contaminant reduction and microbial inactivation on the SolarBag. The tests were performed outdoors in both the morning and the afternoon sun, under both clear and overcast skies; ambient temperatures ranged from 15 -30 C. CDI also used specially formulated challenged water designed to represent developing world water supplies, as well as challenge water defined in EPA and NSF P248 protocols.

The tests showed that the SolarBag was able to reduce 99.9999% of all bacteria, 99.99% of all virus, and over 90% of the chemical contaminants in just 2 hours on warm and sunny days while taking 4 hours in rainy overcast conditions.
