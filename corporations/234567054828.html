Sector information

Unique applications:

Creating Great Customer Service! 70% of lost customers leave because theyve been treated poorly. Learn to maintain and gain your customers satisfaction and ongoing business with our seminar that will put the human back into your job with tools to empower and motivate you all day long. Learn the golden rules to great customer care, changing the mind set and upsell to every caller, to how to deal with difficult customers, develop your verbal power and maximize your role as the voice of your companys service/products and so much more.

Dynamic/Motivational Leadership: Be the Leader you've always wanted to be. You will learn how to: gain respect, find out what motivates you, learn the 6 parts of behaviour and how to change the hats we all wear every day, know the 4 types of employees and how to deal with each one and be the leader that others wish to be!


Coaching to Success! Whether youre a team of 5 or 30, coaching is a key perk to organizations today. Learn the 8 secrets and how to be a great coach (to guide, direct, uplift and get your team to work with you) How to inspire others to go the extra mile and lead them to higher performance.

Superstar Employees: Do others see you as a professional? No matter what your role is, this seminar is for every employee on your team! They will learn: phone/email finesse and etiquette, how to maintain enthusiasm all day, ask clarifying questions so they the job done right the first time, tactics to stay on track, how body language work for you/against you, to how to handle difficult situations/people, keep deadlines and prioritize tasks and much more.

1st impressions-Selling Yourself! Think selling is only for people in sales? Think again! In business you have to sell yourself constantly. No matter what position youre in, you never get a 2nd chance to make a first impression. Imagine if you could radiate and attract the energies that would allow you to be a true living magnet. By developing your charisma you will discover the secrets that allow you to get what you want, your ideas and projects implemented and enrich your life beyond your wildest dreams!


Be the CEO of your life! Work takes up a big chunk of our day and we all need is a survival guide for peoples desire to have a positive work/balance in their lives. Your day affects your personal life and vice versa and what youll learn are the 7 essential traits you need to live an extraordinary life. By developing these traits, blending good old common sense, coupled with being able to shift your old ways of thinking your will live the extraordinary life you desire!

Happily Ever Laughter: Children laugh 300 times daily, adults 15 times or less! You will learn that laughter truly is the best medicine with 10 easy steps that will increase your immune system, counter act anxiety, bring a glow to your face, release endorphins, put you in the moment, and so much more. This seminar is one of our most popular as it allows everyone to truly realize the importance of "laughter' which enriches both your personal/and business life....

Seminars/Workshops can be done in English or French

Other services we offer:

Lunch and Learn: Time is money and your organization doesn't always have the time or the means to let all your staff go to a full day seminar/workshop. Our "lunch and learn offers a 90 minute workshop which focuses on 1 specific topic. The topics can be as varied as what is served on your luncheon menu. This gives your team the opportunity to work in the morning, take time out for an informative workshop and get back to work in the afternoon.

Key / Major clients:

Bell Technical Services, Maple Leaf, ACEF, Innovaderm, Urgences Sante, Caprillon-Thallion, MP2B, Uniboard, Bombardier
