Company Profile


Sanmina, a leading global Electronics Manufacturing Services (EMS) company, makes some of the most complex and valuable electronic and mechanical products in the world. Sanmina not only focuses on engineering and fabricating complex components, but also on providing complete end-to-end supply chain solutions to Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs). With a global footprint to efficiently compete in todays marketplace, Sanmina is the premier technology and most advanced global supply chain solution partner to accomplish your vision.

Sanmina Ottawa has a rich history of manufacturing some of the most complicated optical and RF/Microwave products in the world. We specialize in developing the custom end-to-end supply chain, manufacturing processes, tests and tools to build these products to the highest quality and performance standards. All the while respecting our customers IP. Were specialists in creating custom, highly-manufacturable, cost-effective products that meet our customers specifications. We complement our customers design team with our unique combination of design, process, and test engineering skills and servicesall based on our solutions acceleration IP.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Custom Optical Design and Manufacturing
Sanmina offers a full array of optical, laser and sensor custom manufacturing and collaborative design services  in multiple locations around the world.

Our engineers, designers and test-engineers are able to provide a full spectrum of services that leverage our unique optical design experience  as well as our extensive custom manufacturing expertise. Thats because, for the past 30 years, weve been adding to our optical heritage by creating a unique blend of custom design and manufacturing services  all while protecting our customers IP and driving down their total cost of ownership.

Service Name:
Custom RF/Microwave Design and Manufacturing
Sanmina offers state-of-the-art microelectronic design and custom manufacturing services for a myriad RF/Microwave applications. Because of the unique skill-sets of our engineers, designers and test-engineers, were able to provide a full spectrum of services that leverage our RF Microwave and Microelectronic design experience  as well as our extensive custom manufacturing expertise  especially in products which exploit the L to K bands.

Service Name:
Product Design and Development
Sanmina provides state of the art, end-to-end product design, test and customized manufacturing solutions for complex Optical, RF/Microwave, Lasers and Sensors products worldwide.

Were specialists in creating custom high performance, manufacturable, cost-optimized products. We can also complement our customers design team with our unique skills and services. Both solutions will accelerate our customers market penetration  all the while respecting the customers intellectual property (IP).

Service Name:
New Product Introduction
Hard work, perseverance and exceptional talent may get an Optical, RF/Microwave or micro-electronic product designed. But it doesnt guarantee it can be built. Sanmina realizes the critical importance that New Product Introduction (NPI) plays in a companys product roll-out plans. Everything hinges on the predictable delivery of a working product. Period.

Thats why weve made significant investment in coordinating our internal supply chain, process, test and design teams  to ensure a smooth, custom-built product launch. We know the challenges of building new high-frequency products  be they RF/Microwave or optical. We apply our teams to ensure we anticipate, detect and overcome the specific product challenges that come with creating a custom manufacturing process. And we not only deliver our customers product on time, we provide them with detailed feedback on what can be done to improve future product quality and manufacturability.

With a streamlined prototyping process, 20 years of NPI experience and an impressive delivery record, our dedicated project teams give customers the flexibility to get products to market quickly and on-budget.

Service Name:
Test Development
To ensure industry-leading, superior quality products, Sanmina has developed an End-to-End Test Strategy focused on delivering the lowest total cost of ownership to our customers.

Whether in the New Product Introduction (NPI), volume manufacturing or end-of-life phase, a properly defined test strategy allows our customers to maximize their return on investment and minimize their test-related costs. When Sanmina creates a custom manufacturing processes for a specific optical, RF, Microwave or micro-electronic product, we ensure the test strategy and techniques are an integral  and cost-effective  part of the overall solution.

Test is a tool. Work with a partner that knows how to use its full potential.

Service Name:
Electronics Manufacturing Services
Sanmina Corporation is a leading electronics contract manufacturer serving the fastest-growing segments of the global Electronics Manufacturing Services (EMS) market. Recognized as a technology leader, Sanmina provides end-to-end manufacturing solutions, delivering superior quality and support to OEMs primarily in the communications, defense and aerospace, industrial and semiconductor systems, medical, multimedia, enterprise computing and storage, automotive and clean technology sectors. Sanmina has facilities strategically located in key regions throughout the world.

End-to-End Design Services
At Sanmina, end-to-end design partnerships with our customers are at the core of our value proposition. Weve invested heavily in creating the models and mastering the tools to ensure timely response when tackling RF Microwave challenges. Which means, when we do product design, we warranty it to meet our customers specifications. Our customers own the IP and we assume responsibility for production yields and supply chain issues. And our customers take on a partner who stands behind their product and its success.

Contract Design Services
We are Solution Accelerators. Its our way of complementing our customers existing design team  with the tools, talent and technical expertise they need to go to market quickly. Whats more, our customers can focus on their core competencies while partnering with a world-class RF Microwave design partner.

We also offer a wide range of DFx services, such as Design for Manufacturability and Design for Testability, to help drive cost out of the products we build. Its a significant value-add service thats at the core of a successful business model our customers have come to trust.

Custom Manufacturing Services
Were experts at customizing the correct supply chain, process steps and tooling to build optical products with high quality and high performance. All while respecting and protecting a customers IP.
