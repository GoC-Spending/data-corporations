Company Profile


Shark Marine Technologies Inc. is an industry leader in delivering new and innovative technologies to the underwater industry. Your underwater technology specialists since 1984, Shark Marines founding mandate was to deliver underwater products and services that are innovative, high quality, dependable and cost effective. Shark Marine has delivered on that mandate and has gained global respect for its developments in undersea technology, the expertise it brings to on-site operations, and the creative solutions it delivers to unusual and challenging applications.

Delivering Solutions
Recognized for its expertise and experience, Shark Marine is committed to providing its customers with the right equipment for their needs. In addition to its own product lines, Shark Marine works closely with other industry leading suppliers to deliver complete integrated solutions for its customers. Shark Marines capabilities go well beyond its products and extend to a range of services. Shark Marine has a broad offering that includes the following:
 Diver Held Sonar Imaging and Navigation Systems  Video Systems
 Remote Operated Vehicles  Survey Equipment
 Tether Management Systems  On-site Operational Support
 Consultation  Custom Design and Manufacture
 Training

Our Customers
It is with great pride that Shark Marine serves an ever growing customer base which includes commercial diving, scientific research, survey firms, film production companies, search and recovery organizations, educational institutions, oil, gas and natural resources industries, and some of the most elite military and security forces of governments throughout the world.

Shark Marine continues to work closely with its customers, listen to their needs, and seek new and innovative solutions for the unique challenges they face.

Product Name:
Sea Wolf ROV
The Sea-Wolf ROV manufactured by Shark Marine is multi-purpose
highly adaptable vehicle that packs a lot of thrust into a small

measuring only 36" long X 22.5" wide and 21" high, this unit has
a higher thrust to size ratio than most other vehicles in this
size range.

Product Name:
Barracuda
The Barracuda is a product of more than 30 years of experience in our design of underwater vehicles. Constructed from high-tech plastics, anodized aluminum and syntactic foam, the streamlined, open frame Barracuda has been set for speed and power. The open frame allows for the free flow of water around the powerful 1hp thrusters giving the vehicle the power needed to fight its way across flowing currents. The vehicle has been designed to be slightly larger than most micro ROV’s to provide the frame required for the larger thrusters as well as to support the easy addition of mission specific accessories. Unlike some micros that need to mount accessories like sonars to their lower section, preventing them from resting on the bottom and creating possible entanglement issues, there is plenty of room in Barracuda’s upper frame for these items. This configuration permits the ROV to rest securely on the bottom leaving the sonar with an unobstructed forward view. The larger frame and powerful thrusters also provide greater stability for image taking and video inspection operations while performing station-keeping operations. A “Integrated Total Navigation System” (ITNS)
incorporates a waterproof GPS, attitude sensors and a Doppler sonar for controlling and mapping every underwater movement of the vehicle.

Product Name:
Remote Operated Vehicles
Our ROV technologies have been designed specifically to enable custom designs, sort of a building block process. This allows us to develop a "custom" ROV without the custom work, keeping the cost to the customer reasonable.

Product Name:
NAVIGATOR - Diver Held Sonar System for MCM and SAR operations
The NAVIGATOR Diver Held Sonar system puts the power of a multibeam sonar directly into the hands of the diver. The sonar along with the positioning and navigation capabilities of the Navigator provide the diver with complete situational awareness of his surrounding environment regardless of the visibility of the waters. This modular system can easily be customized for a multitude of underwater tasks from military mine clearing operations and rapid beach profiling to pipeline tracking and archaeological mapping. Additional accessories include underwater communications between units, high definition cameras, radiation detectors, metal detectors, Proton magnetometers and more.

Product Name:
MAKO Diver Delivery System
The MAKO Diver Delivery System represents a completely new concept in diver propulsion devices. Capable of carrying 2 divers and towing an additional 4 the MAKO can travel for several kilometers at a constant speed of 2 knots. Batteries are neutrally buoyant and hot-swappable underwater. This enables extra batteries to be carried along or picked up along the way for extended underwater operations. When interfaced with the NAVIGATOR diver held sonar system, the positioning and navigation capabilities of the NAVIGATOR can be used to control the MAKO's drive system providing it with programmable capabilities such as auto-depth, auto-altitude, station holding, goto target, follow route etc. The stability of the MAKO also makes it a great platform for conducting hydro-graphic surveys or underwater filming.

Technology profile

Shark Marine Technologies Inc. is a world leader in the development and manufacture of new technologies for maritime security. Diver detection and deterrent systems, remotely operated inspection and intercept vehicles and ship hull inspection devices, highlight our focus on security. Our Navigator sonar and diver guidance system, is the only unit that incorporates both multibeam sonar and doppler tracking and puts it into the hands of the diver. Working together with our military and SAR customers, we are listening to their feedback and designing products to improve their situational awareness and increase their underwater capabilites.
