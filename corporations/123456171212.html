Company Profile


Mission: To provide effective solutions for our clients'
business management challenges.

Profile: Founded in March 1993, Visette provides management &
business consulting services, primarily to SMEs involved in
Manufacturing, Distribution, IT, and Services.

Geographic Markets: Visette has extensive experience in several
geographic areas: Canada, EU, Eastern Europe, South America,
and Western Africa.

Management: Visettes shares are owned 100% by Vasile
Zamfirescu, a Canadian citizen residing in Burnaby, BC.
Vasile is a former Professional Engineer (APEGGA) and holds an MBA
from the University of Alberta. He teaches Business Management (Strategy, Operations Management, and International Business) at Kwantlen Polytechnic University, Surrey/Richmon/Langley - B.C. His experience includes profit & loss responsibility for several firms, various executive positions in international trade, management responsibilities in operations and marketing and numerous projects in a consulting or advisory role.

Professional Ethics: Visette abides by the Code of Professional
Conduct of the Canadian Association of Management Consultants
(CAMC) and the Code of Professional Practice of the European
Federation of Management Consulting Associations (FEACO).

What clients are entitled to expect from us:
 Services that are highly effective yet affordable.
 Recommended solutions that are profound and
sophisticated yet practical.
 Expertise that encompasses both strategy and
implementation.

Our pledge: We undertake only assignments for which we can show
accountability to our clients through clear measures that
include quantitative and qualitative data.

Our consulting principles:
1. Even the best run organizations hide potential for
additional growth, enhanced profitability and continual renewal.
2. A challenge, no matter how great, can be solved through
holistic approach, systematic analysis, innovative strategy,
and disciplined implementation of solutions.
3. Failure is not an option!

More information: www.visette.com.

Success stories:

Over the years, Visette and/or its principals
have:
o Conducted negotiations, concluded
contracts and developed business for various
products and services in over 30 countries.
o Led Canadian firms to unprecedented
growth and profitability;
o Assisted overseas clients to several
turn-key contracts for real estate development
projects, including a US$250-Million 5-star
hotel and conference centre;
o Assisted overseas clients to obtain and
execute jobs as general contractors and system
integrators for several banking IT systems,
contract values up to US$17-million ;
o Assisted overseas clients with complex
arrangements for the purchasing, financing and
leasing of a fleet of aircraft, contract value
in excess of US$200-Million;
o Provided the management of foreign
subsidiaries;
o Successfully implemented several
business and banking software systems;
o Recruited, trained and guided teams of
managers for start-up ventures and teams of
specialists in several industries and in several
countries.
