Company Profile


Sportees is a small manufacturer specializing in custom made
active wear. We have a unique selection of both indoor and
outdoor sporting clothing. We also custom make to suit our
customers. We do one of a kind garments and also team or group
order. Sportees manufactures dance and gymnastics outfits,
running tights, cycling jerseys shorts, vests and jackets,
wresting and weightlifting singlets, aerobics outfits,
swimwear, cross country ski outfits......and custom orders. We
also have a large selection of 'Work' clothing and seasonal dress
up fashions, ladies wear and designer fashions at reasonable prices.
Sportees also specializes in fleece clothing. We
manufacture jackets, vests, hats, mittens, headbands, neck
gaiter, balaclavas, scarves, sweaters, pants, turtlenecks, in
all of the latest new tech fabrics....We can build to suit
individuals or teams, and can build clothing to suit the Northern cold climates.
The Sportees Store not only include the Sportees line of products, we also stock dance wear brands Capezio, Bloch, So Danca and Mondor, as well as other brands Spanx, Her Look, Commando, Adria Mode, Neon Buddha, Pure Handknit, Saint James, Gurus, Lifestrength, Strengthape, Bella By Cassie, Daisy Corsets. Muchachomalo, Firma Energywear As with all fashion, it changes with each season, and we are constantly adding new brands.

Sector information

Unique applications:

We are custom manufacturers - making one garment at a time for each customer, to their specifications. For example: We have made swimsuits for young children, with pockets to hold their diabetes pump. And special winter jackets for Photographers with inside extra pockets to hold their camera parts, with small pocket to hold "hot shots" to keep the cameras warm in the winter. We have made items for clients that are big and super tall - as they cannot get items off of a rack in another store, as they just don't fit. We also make dog musher pants, as they need to be super warm, being outside at -40 for up to 2 weeks in some of the races here - we add pockets inside for items that can't freeze and extra warmth and features that would be suited to the event and specifications of the client.
