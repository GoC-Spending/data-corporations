Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
3D
1-20MÉDIA proposes an entirely new way to project your plans. 3D rendering consists of graphically modeling, to scale, a room, a house or a building, with all their details and angles, situated in their expected setting. In architecture and in design, it is often difficult to adequately represent the final result of a project. When one has only a set of drawings or plans on which to rely, errors sometimes creep in, such as in regards lighting or to textures.

With the help of 3D rendering, it is much easier to evaluate various technical and visual choices as well as to fully appreciate the final vision of your projects. Of course, such renderings are highly effective sales and promotional tools for your company What could be better than for your client to have in hand a precise visual reproduction of the final product?

1-20MÉDIA can also develop a multitude of tools for you, from a simple brochure to a complete virtual visit, whether they be for promotional ends or simply for instructional needs. Choose a company that can optimize your investment:

 By accelerating the sales and production calendar;
 By reducing production costs;
 By selling a project, or more precisely, a vision.

With the help of 3D rendering, its simply a matter of ensuring that the result meets expectations, without any regrets or disappointments. Equip yourself with the best tools available to illustrate and/or sell your concepts. Avoid ambiguity and misinterpretation. Project your imagination in three dimensions!

Service Name:
Graphic Design
Every organization, company or coop needs to have its own colour, its own well-defined identity, so that it stands out from the crowd and, especially, so that it can draw people in. The 1-20MÉDIA team possesses the creativity and fresh thinking, as well as the extensive training and experience in graphic design, so important in helping you draw out your companys personality in a way that faithfully reflects your mission, and that will reach your desired audience.

Entrust 1-20MÉDIA with the design of your logo, business cards, brochures, posters or interactive presentations, and give yourself the quality image you deserve.

Service Name:
Website
The web is a window on the world, a tool used by millions of people every day. There is no better way to guarantee unequalled visibility than by equipping oneself with a quality website.

It is indispensable for an organization to possess such a tool. Whether its a simple information page, a dynamic, interactive site, a registration service or even an online sales service, 1-20MÉDIA offers you its expertise through a complete and flexible offer of multimedia tools directly connected to the needs of your organization.

Our team will oversee all stages of the design of your website, from the original concept to launch, including all the details of the visual presentation.

Other services

You want to refine your frame-based website, to adjust it to meet the latest web standards so that it will function in all browsers? We can update your site, while validating the XHTML and CSS style sheets, to ensure that it will correctly display in any browser, even on a PDAor even on a Nintendo DS.

The mission of 1-20MÉDIA is simple Eliminate all doubt and misinterpretation in helping our clients to visualize and make visual their ideas and concepts.

To accomplish this, we rely on our unique team of multidisciplinary creators, all guided by the same passion for visual communication. 1-20MÉDIA worker co-op was born of this common passion, enriched by the unique knowledge and experience of all its members.
