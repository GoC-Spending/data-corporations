Company Profile


Natural immix is a full-service natural source health products producer. Our commitment to first-rate product development, testing, quality and marketing allows us to create vitamins, minerals, botanical herbal extracts, dietary supplements and natural health products. From conception of raw material formulation to semi-finished and finished products, Natural immix has a wide-ranging capability to formulate, process, manufacture and market while adhering to and exceeding the highest local and international quality standards. We can help create a product from formulation to packaging and delivery, or we can implement any combination of steps, through product development and manufacturing. We offer comprehensive client services, including testing, innovative R&D using our own teams of experts and consultants. This winning combination of capabilities allows us to help you create unique, effective and highly marketable products.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Natural Health Products / Nutraceuticals
We offer products that allow people to live healthier. We aspire to change the world through health, one person at a time. Our aspiration is best captured by the idea of Hippocrates: "Let food be thy medicine and medicine be thy food." We followed this path and so the idea of Natural immix has been created along with our motto, "true wealth is good health." Natural immix is not just a product, it is a philosophy; the way to a healthy lifestyle. We believe that nature gives us everything we need. It depends on us how make use of that.

Good health is a valuable asset for all. That is why "true wealth is good health." For some, true wealth can be the pursuit of financial wealth, social status and other forms of personal happiness. The search for true wealth can have different terms, but universally true wealth cannot be attained without good health. Our perspective is established on the basis that happiness lies, first of all, in health. We believe that health is the most important thing in life and that nature, time and patience are vital for it.

Product Name:
Natural Immix
Every Natural immix product is carefully formulated and tested to provide you with the right ingredients to meet your specific health needs. In a perfect world if every person ate a good balanced diet, exercised regularly and lived in a stress-free environment, nutritional supplements would not be required.

Natural immix offers products that help real people live healthier and happier lives.
All our products are premium quality and are manufactured under incredibly strict federal regulations, Health Canada. Our quality control and quality assurance programs guarantee that our products are among the highest quality available in the market

Service Name:
OEM / Private Label
Private Labelling is when you take top quality, time tested and proven formulations and package them with your brand, logo, and label.
OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) is a company that produces products or major components of products that are sold to customers as new. It refers specifically to the act of a company rebranding a product to its own name and offering its own support and marketing of the product.

OEM and private labelling can be an important step in establishing and maintaining a successful account base. Marketing products with your name, logo, design and address on the package helps to ensure that customers identify with your company both for initial and repeat purchases. You may choose to apply your own label to any of our stock products or you may elect to establish your own brand name on one or more custom formulations that are exclusive to your company.

Natural immix Health can provide full turn-key OEM / Private Labeling services as well as Custom Manufacturing, Packaging and Marketing & Design services.
