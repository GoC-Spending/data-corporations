Company Profile


Toronto College of Technology is a leader in private education in Canada. Our multilevel study system with a wide choice of entry points offers a flexible and articulated educational path for students from all backgrounds. We have a team of highly qualified and dedicated academic and administrative staff to provide you with a superior learning experience, a friendly and multicultural college community and a full spectrum of life experiences.

At Toronto College of Technology, you will find all you need to succeed. Toronto College of Technology has three divisions: High School, Professional College & University Transfer Programs, and University & Graduate School. It is a home for the mind, the heart, and the spirit. The school program is based on the inherent belief that all people can continue to improve their capacity for greater creativity and intellectual accomplishment. The staff is committed to providing an enriched curricular and co-curricular program that develops the potential for success for every student throughout life.

Service Name:
Postgraduate Diploma:Enterprise Software Developer(NOC:2174)
Toronto College of Technology bridges the gap between basic programming skills and those with profound understanding of its intricacies. This Enterprise Software Solutions suite is targeted at application developers and architects charged with developing applications using the latest technologies. Students are provided with insight into various connectivity options available, and introduced to the design patterns most suitable for use with these technologies.

The goal of this package courses is to instill the students with a solid understanding of not only the core concepts, but also how all the pieces fit together in complete multi-tier Internet business applications. The tremendous increase of interest in server-side technologies, including J2EE, XML, EJB and Microsoft .NET has shaped these course offerings. Designed for enterprise developers who wish to implement server-side business objects in Java and .NET, the courses address the needs of developers, from those new to Java and .NET and object-oriented programming, to those who need the most advanced training in J2EE and .NET. All these courses emphasize solving real-world problems with the goal of applying solutions right away.



Service Name:
Postgraduate Diploma: Networking Administration(NOC:2281)
The program is intended both for new graduates and for experienced professionals who wish to update their skills or change their area of specialization. It is concerned with the design, management and maintenance of networks in corporations and public networks, or service provision to public and private networks.

The convergence of computing and telecommunications is creating a new kind of networking technology based on IP networks and multimedia applications. Networks today are expected to offer multimedia services anywhere and at any time. Within corporations, intranets support the interaction of people and the linking of these people to information systems. There is an increasing demand for mobility. People increasingly expect seamless access to global networks. IP networking is expected to be everywhere and to offer multimedia services of all types.

The CCNA, CCNP and MCSE certifications are widely recognized and valued in industry. This program also prepares students for the CCNA, MCSE and CCNP certification examinations.


Service Name:
Postgraduate Diploma: Data Warehousing and Business Intelligence(NOC:2172)
This postgraduate program provides the knowledge and skills needed to design and build a data warehouse using North America leading-edge products. The program consists of nine courses;You will gain practical hands-on experience working with Oracle,SQL Server,Erwin, Cognos Impromptu, Transformer and PowerPlay, Crystal Report and Crystal Enterprise,Informatica and SAS

Real-world experiences and Co-op practice are mixed with innovative teaching methods to ensure that you graduate with a comprehensive knowledge of Data Warehouse and Business Intelligence. At the end of the program, you will be able to work as a Data Architect; Data warehouse Analyst, Data Warehouse Specialist, and Business Intelligence Specialist.


Service Name:
Postgraduate Diploma: ERP Information Systems Analyst(NOC:2171)
The ERP Systems Analyst?s role is to supervise and operate the organization?s ERP systems through implementation best practices and to support and manage change to the ERP environment. This includes involvement in installing, configuring, patching, upgrading, and maintaining the company?s investments in proprietary technologies. The ERP Systems Analyst is also responsible for planning and coordinating the change management of processes required for the support of ERP systems necessary for business operations. This individual will map business requirements to standard or custom functionality and partner with the business community to enhance and in support of mission-critical ERP software.



This program is special designed for professionals who want to pursuit career in ERP Systems. Real-world experiences and Co-op practice are mixed with innovative teaching methods to ensure that you graduate with a comprehensive knowledge of ERP systems. At the end of the program, you will be able to work as a ERP systems analyst.


Service Name:
Postgraduate Diploma: Systems Testing Engineer(NOC:2283)
This hands-on program provides the knowledge and skills to lead you to be a SQA Specialist. The program covers software testing and software quality assurance process; testing scripts; automated tests such as Quick Test Professional (QTP), LoadRunner and Quality Center; web application performance and security testing.

Real-world experiences and Co-op practice are mixed with innovative teaching methods to ensure that you graduate with a comprehensive knowledge and skills of Software Testing and Software Quality.

This program also includes software development components which are the essential part of software testing specialist. Graduates of the program also can work as an SQA Analyst, SQA Test Engineer, Software Tester and business/system analyst.


Service Name:
Postgraduate Diploma: Business Analysis and Project Management(NOC:0213)
This hands-on program provides the knowledge and skills to lead you to be a business/system analyst or/and IT project manager. The program covers IT project management, software development mythology and software quality assurance process such as PMP,ITIL, Analysis & Design Using UML, Software Quality Assurance.

Real-world experiences and Co-op practice are mixed with innovative teaching methods to ensure that you graduate with a comprehensive knowledge and skills of Business Analysis and Project Management.


Service Name:
Business Information Technology(NOC:2171)
The Diploma in Business Information Technology aims to meet the growing needs of industry for a new generation of business professionals, who have the twin capabilities of sound business knowledge and skills to develop and manage business systems and e-commerce applications. You will undergo a broad-based and practice-oriented curriculum that will provide you with the appropriate IT and business domain knowledge, management and communication skills.

Foundational modules in Business and IT will be introduced in the 1st and 2nd year, with the focus in the final year on honing the requisite professional skills as well as the integration and application of knowledge and skills. E-business systems, Internet-enabled multimedia, network, servers and database management systems, information and knowledge management systems and enterprise resources planning will form the core curricular coverage in the final year. Experiential learning through programmers such as the six-month Business Industry Practicum in info-communication companies will provide you with ample exposure to the nuances of Internet business and the Knowledge Based Economy workplace.

Working closely with business information technology companies in a myriad range of projects, we ensure that you will be imbued with the latest knowledge and expertise while undergoing our relevant, practical and useful experiential training.


Service Name:
Office Administration and Computerized Accounting(NOC:1453)
The purpose of this program is to provide students with the skills necessary to understand and apply the principles of accounting for proprietorships, partnerships and corporations in a computerized office environment.

This diploma program is instructor-driven, allowing students to navigate quickly through theory and accounting software. The curriculum includes experiential lectures and workshops facilitated by specialized industry.

Who Should Participate?

The Business & Accounting program is designed for those who are interested in areas of accounting, office management, and small business. Students may start their own business and service companies or may move into marketing and sales.


Service Name:
Early Childcare Assistant(NOC:4214)
The Early Childcare Assistant program has been designed to equip childcare workers with the valuable knowledge, skills and experience for employment as Assistants in day care or early learning centres. In addition to covering early childhood development, program planning, communication and other vital topics in childcare, the program also includes the role of computers in a childcare setting.

Early Childcare Assistants lead children in activities to stimulate and develop their intellectual, physical and emotional growth. There is increasing demand in the industry for qualified individuals who have completed formal training such as that offered in this diploma program. Career opportunities exist in childcare centres, kindergartens, nursery schools and centres for exceptional children.

The Early Childcare Assistant (ECA) Diploma is a 945-hour credit program comprised of 455 hours of instructor-led training and 490 hours of volunteer field placement.

Program Duration: 38 weeks


Service Name:
Desktop Support Technician(NOC:2282)
This hands-on program provides the knowledge and skills needed to successfully support end-users and to successfully troubleshoot desktop environments that run on the Windows operating system. Students are required to pass two core exams:

Exam 70-271: Supporting Users and Troubleshooting a Microsoft Windows XP Operating System

Exam 70-272: Supporting Users and Troubleshooting Desktop Applications on a Microsoft Windows XP Operating System


Service Name:
Health Office Administrator(NOC:1243)
In this program, students will study various courses related to health and business. They will learn the proper procedures required to work effectively as a clinical secretary or coordinator in the medical field. Students will study a variety of medical tests and practice making any required written work and documentation. They will work with various software including Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook & Email, and more. This will enable students to perform a diverse number of tasks such as billing. Students will graduate with skills and knowledge in areas of health, business, and technology to provide outstanding administrative support to the medical profession.
