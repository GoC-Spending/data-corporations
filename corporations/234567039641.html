Company Profile


Moveable makes it easy!

Moveable was founded in 1983 and has grown steadily since then,
establishing a reputation for professionalism, thoughtful
attention to detail and a strong commitment to serving its
clients. Today the company operates around the clock out of a
landmark 21,000-square-foot loft space in Torontos downtown
west district, and employs 55 people.

Its prepress and scanning divisions offer a full range of
services, including conventional drum scans, Photo CD scans,
colour editing and image retouching, trapping, imposition and
film separations, and a variety of digital and analog proofing
options. Recent projects include advertising for Smirnoff and
Eli Lilly, sales material for Manulife Financial, magazine
spreads for Wedding Bells and Outpost, point-of-sale promotions
for Revlon and Yves St-Laurent, CD packaging for BMG Music, book
covers for Random House, Harcourt Canada and Key Porter,
catalogues for the Toronto International Film Festival and
Umbra, and annual reports for Bank of Montreal, Andres Wines,
Hydro One and Imax.

The companys digital printing division features a Heidelberg
Quickmaster DI press, two Xerox DocuColors, a DocuPrint black-
and-white printer and an in-house bindery. The poster division
includes three large-format inkjet plotters and offers rapid
production of digital colour posters for indoor and outdoor
point-of-sale, event marketing and signage.

Typesetting is alive and well at Moveable, and the company
offers production services for a wide variety of print projects.
Its innovative proofreading and typographic fine-tuning service
refines designer-formatted pages to ensure that the final
document is error-free and meets the highest typographic
standards. Its rigorous proofreading process has also been used
prior to major Web launches to ensure accuracy and consistency.
This division specializes in corporate communications, and in
the past 12 months alone has handled typesetting, proofreading
and copy editing on more than 40 annual reports, including those
for major corporations like Bank of Montreal, Noranda, Barrick,
Onex,TD Waterhouse, Hydro One and Kimberly-Clark.

Moveable Online, the companys Internet division, offers
everything from HTML coding to custom dynamic application
development and secure hosting services. It collaborates with
its design studio clients to create Web sites and robust
applications marked by high production values and intelligent,
user-friendly functionality.

Moveable is a member of the International Digital Imaging
Association and has won more than 75 international awards for
excellence in the graphic arts. Its work has been cited by the
American Center for Design, the New York Type Directors Club,
the Mead Annual Report Show, The Advertising & Design Club of
Canada and Applied Arts magazine, among others.

WebCell

Service Name:
Web Application Development
Moveable Online offers advanced Web services, including custom
application development and hosting. Our primary role is to
support  not compete with  a graphic design team, allowing
designers to offer the back-end technical expertise of an
experienced development team along with their design, branding
and communications services.

Our staff includes talented web-application programmers, Flash
artists, information architects, database developers and project
managers, all headed up by an experienced IT management team.

Moveables hosting facility is state-of-the-art. Twelve Compaq
enterprise-class Web/application/database servers sit within our
clustered server farm in a secure, climate controlled
environment. Our Internet connection is a massive OC12 fibre-
optic pipe, with an OC3 back-up. The network is fully redundant
and fully switched, and features multiple stages of UPS back-up
to ensure maximum uptime.

We offer complex application hosting for the following
programming environments: Java(J2EE), ASP, Cold Fusion,
Witango2000 and PHP. We house a clustered, enterprise edition MS
SQL Server 2000.

We have focused our expertise on areas in which we can offer
complex logic solutions that simplify the experience of the end
user. Our skills lie primarily in database-driven dynamic
application development coupled with solid HTML coding.

Service Name:
Digital Printing and Bindery
Once upon a time digital printing meant so-so quality for short-
runs  useful when you had no other choice. No longer. Moveable
Print sets the bar very high indeed. Now you dont have to
settle for low-grade reproduction when you need full-colour
printing in small quantities (and with the addition of our
Heidelberg DI, those quantities are no longer so small!).

Our Xerox DocuColor 2045s deliver superior quality and
versatility  dazzling solids with no banding, and sharp,
colourful reproduction  all on stock as heavy as 12 pt. They
handle variable data too, for personalized, targeted promotions,
and are the ideal solution for print runs of 1 to 1,000.

For longer print runs, our Heidelberg Quickmaster DI takes over.
The DI is an automated direct-to-plate offset press that
eliminates the need for film, and enables us to offer fast
turnarounds at competitive rates. While our DocuColors continue
to be the right choice for short runs, the DI is ideally suited
for longer runs of 1,000 to 5,000.

Added to this mix, is a complete in-house bindery to ensure that
we can meet even the most challenging deadlines.

But we offer much more than an impressive array of equipment. We
also have the talent to get the most out of the hardware, and to
ensure that we exceed your expectations on every job. We can
help you choose the best printing and finishing options and find
cost-effective short-cuts. Sometimes that can point to a
conventional printing solution. In these cases our experienced
print consultants can tap into our large network of trade
printer associates, and manage your project for you.

Whichever route we take, well assume complete responsibility
for the finished product, and we will always bend over backwards
to solve problems and meet tough deadlines. Thats been the
Moveable way for more than 18 years.

Service Name:
Prepress
Despite technological advancements over the past few years,
providing reliable prepress services still requires experience
and intelligence. We have lots of great hardware and software,
but we dont let the equipment do the thinking for us. Our
superior track record comes from our superior staff, who delve
into every file to take charge of trapping, imposition and
preflighting. Thats how we are able to avoid the common
problems that continue to plague the prepress process, like text
reflows, font substitutions, inaccurate trapping and colour
shifts.


Our process has been honed to perfection. Add to that lots of
capacity and 24-hour-a-day production, and its no wonder that
we routinely outperform other prepress houses. Our turnarounds
are legendary: five- and six-colour annual reports trapped,
imposed, run to film and proofed overnight.
