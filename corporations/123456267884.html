Company Profile


Windship Advertising has proudly built and supplied world class innovative advertising products from our headquarters in Edmonton, Canada for over 30 years. We produce world class retail & tradeshow displays, event marketing items, a wide variety of printing products and custom inflatable characters, replicas of all shapes and sizes.

Our three product divisions are:

Windship Displays:

Tradeshow: Custom booths and pop ups to drive attention to your exhibit
Retail: Point Of Purchase advertising and creative displays
Bannerstands: Pull up banners in various styles and sizes to fit you needs

Windship Inflatables:

Premier: Our Canadian made premium quality inflatables in any style and size you can imagine
Lite: Our economical answer to budget conscious projects
Helium: Blimps, balloons or shapes, putting your message above the crowd

Windship Printing

Banners: Grand format printing on vinyl, mesh and scrim
Tents: Pop up tents for retail, corporate or public events
Flags: 8to 20 flags for retail awareness or special events
Wraps: Vehicle & Building Wraps for any size and shape

Unique applications:

Solid Foundation
We at Windship Advertising are celebrating our 30th year of excellence in innovative advertising products. Over this time we have become the premier producer of cold air inflatables in North America and have led the way in product research.


Product Quality
Our research has led to the development of our proprietary Windcoat V® vinyl. With unmatched UV protectants, this material resists colour fading unlike anything on the market. All Windship Premier Inflatables use this material along with top quality stitching, components and blowers to ensure a long and effective life. Our commitment to quality can be seen in all Windship products across the board.


Customer Service
The team at Windship Advertising prides themselves on having the best customer service in the business. As a client, you will be allocated a highly trained Account Manager who will ask the right questions and can deliver the best product to fit your marketing needs.


Diverse Product Line
While we are an industry leader in inflatables and helium products, Windship Advertising offers so much more. Our Windship Displays include a comprehensive line of trade show displays, retail fixtures and banner stands. The Windship Printing line features grand format printing, custom flags, pop-up tents and car & window decals. Windship is truly your one stop for all your marketing products.
