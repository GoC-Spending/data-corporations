Company Profile


Enseignes Imperial Signs is a Canadian-owned company
incorporated in New Brunswick since 1963. In September of 1998,
EIS concluded a merger with the Pattison Sign Group, which
has operated as one of the premiere manufacturers of illuminated
signage in the Canadian marketplace, dating back to the turn of
the century. Also, Pattison Sign Group is the world's
largest custom electrical Sign Company, employing over 800
people. With manufacturing facilities in seven (7) locations
across Canada and service offices in a further 24 locations, Pattison Sign Group offers comprehensive coverage across
Canada, with increasing sales in the United States as well as in
Latin America and parts of Europe.

Enseignes Pattison Sign Group (EPSG) is committed to establishing
ourselves as expert-partners in various modes of visual
communication in order to effectively implement our client's
image. This is accomplished through the empowerment of our
employees, continuous training, and the consistent and high
level of quality found in our products and services.

Partnership and quality have been and will remain cornerstones
of our success. Partnerships with clients, suppliers, and
especially employees will continue to be part of our philosophy
and drive our development. Continual research and development
allows us to integrate new techniques and materials in order to
offer our clients the best product at the best value. The firm
also places a great deal of importance on customer service and
customer satisfaction.

We design and manufacture a first-rate product, meeting the
highest expectations of major international accounts, using the
most advanced technology and equipment.

To meet our clientele's growing needs for quality, EIS has been
certified ISO 9001:1994 for quality standards in manufacturing,
a Total Quality Management Program. This certification was
achieved in September 1998, through a valued and successful
partnership of employees, customers and suppliers.

Our values are based on EPSG' pride and satisfaction in a job
well done:
· Customer satisfaction
· Team Work
· Professionalism
· Quality

Pylons

Single-faced signs

it is interior, exterior, illuminated or non-illuminated, the
single-faced sign will be designed and manufactured to fit your
architectural structure and serve as a valuable marketing asset
to your company.

Product Name:
Channel letters
Whether they are illuminated or non-illuminated these highly
visible and diversified channel letters will help you attract
valuable attention to your company.



Product Name:
Awnings
While enhancing the appearance of your establishment, awnings
will contribute in protecting it against the sun and the rain.
Its modern designs and modular constructions will surely meet
all the needs of your enterprise.



Product Name:
Neon
The subtleness of neon, will add a touch of magic to your
enterprise. This medium has already been around for many years
and has never lost its finesse. Its eye-catching features will
allow your customers to see it from a distance.

Product Name:
Canopies
Canopies are almost exclusively used by service stations,
advanced technologies have allowed us to extend the use of new
materials, which enhance performance and allow a lightweight
construction. They provide shelter during inclement weather.

Product Name:
Indoor / outdoor menus
Interior and exterior menu boards are popular items in the
restaurant business. Therefore, we have come-up with a variety
of solidly, adaptable menus, which allow you to interchange your
different menus according to the seasons or simply add an item
or price.

Product Name:
Free-standing Illuminated Signs
Over the past years EIS has designed, manufactured and installed
a large variety of Freestanding Illuminated Signs for several
Canadian cities. Each one is designed and tailored to perfectly
blend in with its surroundings and effectively meet all the
needs of the city.

After moving out of the focus of attention for a time, this
classic display concept is back in full swing. Along with its
magnetic appeal, the great advantage of the urban column is
still the same: it informs people.

These days, through the use of new technologies, we can design
powerful media tools that blend with urban environment. That's
why it's easier to promote cultural events or post ads that
serve the community while informing the general public.

Product Name:
Electronic message center
The electronic message center is a great visual communication
item, which offers a large range of advertising possibilities,
including color to animation.

Product Name:
Wayfinding
Wayfinding systems identify path and place; they are one of the
elements of an environment, which have a direct impact on the
visitors. They are put in place to aid the visitors to find
their intended destination and give the enterprise a
recognizable identity. Effective wayfinding systems consider
the aesthetics of the facility and the corporate brand. Our
consultants will surely recommend a wayfinding solution, which
will suit your particular needs.

Product Name:
Billboards
Our Tri-Vision Billboard is composed of three-dimensional blades
connected to a came shaft, allowing the possibility to display a
maximum of three publicities.



Product Name:
Bus Shelter
Our sturdily tailored Bus shelters are guaranteed to shelter all
of the citizens under any extreme weather condition.
Incorporated on one side of the bus shelters are illuminated
display boxes, which allow advertisers the best and most
effective outdoor medium at daylight or at night.

Product Name:
Spectacular Signs
This spectacular architectural Pepsi Sign located on the Torre
Polar II in the Plaza de Venezuela, Caracas is one of EIS and
Consorcio S&P, their Venezuelan partners, most prestigious sign.
This project was developed with State of the Art technology; the
structure is exclusively constructed out of aluminium and
consists of two elements; the ring, which is 22.50 meters in
diameter (73.8 ft.) and 5.50 meters high, and a sphere of 16.50
meters (54 ft.) in diameter. The illumination of this gigantic
sphere is controlled by a computer system, which regulates the 7
Kilometres of Fibre Optic animation.

Product Name:
electrical sign & visual communication

individual letters, fascia signs, pylon signs, billboards,
awnings, wayfinding systems, street furniture, electronic
message centres and architectural spectaculars

Design
Our Graphic Communication team is committed to providing our

order to help you attract valuable attention to your company, we
will design and create an image, which meets your expectations,
or we will gladly help you enhance your existing one.



Service Name:
Manufacturing
Our manufacturing department takes pride in producing superior
quality products, which are guaranteed to meet your
specifications. The manufacturing of each product is done step
by step according to ISO 9001 International Standards.



Service Name:
Installation
In order to respond to the expanding demands of the National and
International marketplace, EIS has established an installation
network to provide complete and timely service in each of the
regions and countries we serve. Individual companies are
screened for their ability to do proper site surveys, secure
permits, and provide feedback regarding local restrictions and
special conditions. We also make sure that they possess the
right equipment and knowledge to install any of our products and
provide post-installation services. We also verify that they
have the proper insurance, a good safety program, adequate fleet
for the projected work; emergency responsiveness,
professionalism and integrity, and the capacity to support our
client programs within their geographic area. This guarantees
that our clients get a final installation that maintains the
quality of our products and ensures that all work has been done
in accordance with all local regulations and requirements. Each
installation team assigned by EIS is thoroughly screened in
order to meet ISO 9001 standards. Consequently, a list of
criteria must be met with a minimum score of 69%, but an excess
of 80% is preferred.



Service Name:
Customer Service
Our friendly and professional customer service department takes
pleasure in answering, in a timely manner, any of your
questions.

Our various offices, toll-free numbers, and Internet links,
enables EIS to provide its clients with 24-hour access, anywhere
in the world.

Whether you have questions concerning the installation of a new
sign, an existing one or have other requirements, we will
provide you with a quick response.


Service Name:
Leasing
Service excellence, complete customer satisfaction is EIS
number one priority. No matter the source or the importance of
your project, our diverse leasing programs will offer you many
possibilities in order to develop your marketing strategy for
your next signage program.

This leasing program is currently available for clients in North
America.

The benefits and advantages are simple:
· As a monthly advertising cost, this operating expense is 100%
tax deductible.
· Enjoy the benefits of an effective on-premise sign without the
capital expense.
· Maintenance of the sign is our responsibility.
· We carry liability and property damage insurance on the sign.
· At the end of the term, the contract may be renewed of the sign
may be changed to reflect the new marketplace.

Give us a call, it will be more than our pleasure to help you
out!

Technology profile

The main activity of the Company is the manufacture and design
of a complete range of visual communication products. These
include illuminated and non-illuminated signs, using neon, fiber
optic, plastic, steel, aluminum and flexface, and vinyl films.
With fully integrated fabrication capabilities, we can also
produce interior and exterior menu boards, billboards, awnings,
wayfinding systems, street furniture (bus shelters, freestanding
illuminated columns, etc.), electronic message centers and
architectural spectaculars. EIS offers complete design,
manufacture, installation, maintenance and leasing services.
