Company Profile


SunTel Technologies was started in 2007 with a vision to provide advanced technological solutions to the small and medium businesses of Greater Toronto Area. In recent years, SunTel Technologies has developed a major portfolio of Support and Consulting services. We proudly manages entire IT functions as well as component parts for many Legal, Manufacturing and Professional Services businesses. Set of circumstances that drives the prospects to engage with our portfolio of Managed Services is always different. However, the common factor amongst them all is the need for a high performance, best practice and economically efficient delivery of their IT functions. Our customers are our biggest assets and quality service is our motto. With strong customer ties and top notch service we have succeeded to become one of the leading MSPs in Canada.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Managed IT Services
When something goes wrong with your IT, everything grinds to a halt. Managed Services from SunTel Technologies provides you
peace of mind because when there is an IT problem, we already are on call to bring your business back to normal. This way we
help customer to minimize downtime which increases productivity and better predict your IT expenses. Some of our Managed services are : Managed IT support, Managed Anti-Virus, Managed online Backup, Managed Web Protection, Managed Servers, Managed Network services, Managed Workstations, Managed Remote users.


Service Name:
Hosted PBX Solutions
SunTel Technologies’s hosted PBX solutions allow your business to eliminate the cost and complexity of traditional expensive phone systems by taking your communications into the cloud. Hosted PBX is a fully scalable solution that can grow with your business so you only pay for what you need. This solution offers a predictable, per-system pricing model with lower capital and operating expenses, while providing all the features and functionality your business needs to improve communications and enhance productivity. What's more, you’ll always be equipped with all the advanced features it offers, with no extra cost.

Service Name:
Sage 300 ERP Consulting
To stay competitive in today’s global economy, you need more than simple financial accountability and compliance. Sage 300 ERP contains the power and tools to help you see your business in a new way so you can improve productivity, envision success, and accelerate growth. It’s time to turn your business dreams into reality with the real-time visibility, collaboration, agility, and innovation you need to succeed.
At SunTel Technologies, we focus on Sage 300 ERP design and planning. This includes customization of the software to fit your business needs. Sage ERP 300 offers a wide variety of customization features including payroll, employee self service, and inventory management. A consultant from SunTel Technologies can help you determine what features will work best and how they can increase uptime, productivity and promote better customer information management.

Service Name:
Sage CRM Consulting
Suntel Technologies is a Sage Development Partners and we excel in designing and implementing Sage CRM. Our team specializes in implementing Sage CRM. In hiring us, your implementation goes off without a hitch and your staff are trained in all aspects of the software.

Testimonial:

SunTel has been taking care of our computer network for over 5 years with excellent results. An added benefit is that he also looks after our telephone network, so we do not have to deal with another source. Three years ago we moved and downsized, a hair raising experience! SunTel convinced us to switch to a VOIP telephone system. This we did with some trepidation seeing that we are not "high tech'" people. We found that it was virtually trouble free and our telephone costs dropped by more than 80%. How could we not be pleased with that? We also found that staff who live some distance from the office, i.e. Kitchener, Bradford could utilize the VoIP phone system with a handset at their homes which were connected directly to our network, so that they could contact support people with simply an extension number. Wonderful! We have no hesitation whatsoever in recommending SunTel.
Richard Waite
President
Salix Systems Limited

Our company turned over our IT services to SunTel a year ago.

SunTel cleaned up our aging infrastructure, and got our network back to peak performance. A major concern for us was the finicky backup system that was put in place years ago. We now have a rock-solid backup system to protect us from data crashes.

Perhaps the best thing that SunTel has done for us is to introduce us to the world of IP telephony. Not only did we gain access to state-of-the art new features such as integrated e-mail / voice mail / faxing, we now enjoy “follow-me” voice calls to allow clients to contact us no matter where we are physically. Our off-site employees have local extensions so that they not only enjoy all the same features as they would at head office, but all conversations with them are also free!

Our company benefitted financially as well. Our land line and long-distance costs are now a fraction of our previous phone company charges. We were able to pay for the system in only six months with the savings we realized.

We highly recommend SunTel to any business that would like first rate IT service or an upgrade to their existing telephone systems.

Richard Tso
President
Racing Forensics Inc
