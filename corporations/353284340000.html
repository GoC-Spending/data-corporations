Company Profile


Incorporated in Canada in 1978, Nautilus International is the
world leader in the design and manufacture of radio and video
remote control systems, proximity detection systems and underground mine automation.

Nautilus systems are used throughout the world in a wide variety
of industrial environments including the mining, marine,
aviation, offshore and railway industries.

The Nautilus group of Companies includes Computer Aided Design,
The Microwave Research Group, Software Development,
Manufacturing and Video Production & Post Production.

An efficient and friendly administration staff is the final
component of this successful operation, bringing together the
various Nautilus companies and departments into a superb
blending of technologies. The Nautilus commitment does not end
with the sale of a system. The service department is staffed by
experienced technicians who are available for installation, on-
site training and after-sales support.

Whatever your application may be, Nautilus International can
provide a Radio, Video, Communication or Automation System
specifically designed to meet your needs and help you to
increase safety and productivity.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Powercam System
The Nautilus POWERCAM system is a versatile Radio Remote control and TV Monitoring System capable of operating a wide range of mining, construction, and industrial equipment including LHDs, Roof Bolters, Jumbo Drill Rigs, Continuous Miners, Rock Breakers
Locomotives & others.

ADVANTAGES:
. Safe, comfortable and easy operation
. Reliable & efficient remote ore removal
. Remote retrieval of disabled vehicles
. Remote inspection of hazardous areas

PORTABLE UNIT
Since the operator can see into the bucket and the surrounding
area, productivity increases substantially and the LHD no longer
returns from the stope with a half empty bucket. Security access
codes prevent unauthorized use of equipment and eliminates the
need for keys. Security Codes are also used to allow the
supervisor to change the pre-selected parameters.
The Nautilus system can be reprogrammed to give On/Off outputs
instead of Proportional outputs or vice-versa, simply by entering
the appropriate security access code.

REMOTE VEHICLE UNIT
This transmitter and receiver unit is located in the remote vehicle and normally operates from it's 12V or 24V Battery. The tough fiberglass housing is very rugged, impervious to vibration and the lid has a full weather-tight "O" ring seal. A thick rubber pad in the lid holds the "plug-in" circuit boards firmly in place and acts as a giant shock absorber. The 32 character illuminated LCD display provides a variety of diagnostic information. All cards are "keyed" with a special notch to ensure that they can only be inserted in the correct position. "Plug-in" design makes the circuit boards easy to replace, ensuring speedy repair and minimal "downtime".

LIGHT BARS
Nautilus uses Qty 4 x 50 Watt quartz - halogen fully adjustable
spotlights to provide excellent lighting for the color cameras.
Each lamp has an "o" ring seal. The housing is machined from a
single block of aluminum. The base and all other fittings are
made from # 304 stainless steel. Nautilus has made the lights
bars as small as possible so that they may be easily mounted
under the F.O.P.S. canopy for maximum protection. Each spotlight
is fully adjustable in the x and y axes. The lights should be
adjusted so that reflections from the bucket are kept to a minimum. Many companies are now replacing their vehicle's lights with Nautilus Light Bars because of the excellent lighting and long life provided by these units.

CAMERA SYSTEM
Nautilus uses only the finest CCD color cameras which are fitted
with an automatic iris and give an excellent picture even in extremely low light conditions.

Product Name:
VCS-2000
Nautilus designs and manufactures the VCS 2000 leaky feeder communication system for voice, data and video. The standard system provides 4 channels of wideband full colour FM video, 4 voice and 2 data channels simultaneously and it can be expanded to meet custom applications.

Engine monitoring and collection of data from up to 32 sensors (analogue and digital) are incorporated into all Nautilus Systems.

Product Name:
140SSF Radio Remote Control.
This system is used to remotely control underground mining machines such as CAT loaders, Jumbo drills. Blockholers and many other types of machines. This system is used by most of the major mining companies such as BHP, Newmont Mining, Golcorp....

Product Name:
Railmax
The Nautilus Railmax System uses the versatile Chestpack 600 Portable unit and is capable of operating a wide range of industrial equipment including Railway locomotives, car movers, Mining locomotives, cranes and many other types of mobile equipment.

FEATURES:
-Full proportional Control and Monitoring
-Single person operation
-Self Checking diagnostic capabilities
-Excellent operating range
-Full feed back
-Synthesized radios
-Pitch and Catch option
-Large 32 character LCD backlit display

The Railmax System allows easy control of a wide variety of
equipment functions:
-Independent Brake
-Automatic Brake
-Transmission or Reverser
-Battery Field
-Throttle
-Front and Rear Lights
-Horn, Bell and Warning Strobe
-Front and Rear Sander's

Nautilus systems use a 16 bit CRC check combined with unique identification codes to ensure the systems will not malfunction in the presence of extraneous RF signals.

Security access codes prevent unauthorized use of equipment and eliminate the need for keys. Security codes are also used to allow the supervisor to change any pre-selected parameters.

The Nautilus commitment to quality includes building custom test equipment which simulates locomotive operation for in-house testing of all systems to ensure perfect field operation and excellent after sales support.

Product Name:
Chestpack 600 Portable Units
This unit, in its many different configurations, is used to operate equipment such as Yard locomotives, Car movers, underground / surface Mining Locomotives and many other types of mobile equipment.

The Railmax is a full-feedback system capable of monitoring all of the important functions of the R.V.U. and sending this information back to the remote operator to be shown on the Chestpack Unit's illuminated LCD display.

Two snap-in rechargeable batteries provide power for up to 10 hours of continuous operation.

The weatherproof portable unit is made of strong, light weight polyethylene and includes sealed switches and a full "O" ring perimeter seal.

The Portable Unit can be programmed to operate on any pre-approved frequency and identification code.

Safety and operating procedures can easily be changed to suit
customer's requirements.

The transmitter can include a tilt detector which automatically protects the operator but does not respond to normal movements such as bending, climbing or lifting.


Switches, Knobs, and the Back-lit Display have all been designed and arranged for maximum efficiency and ease of operation.

Product Name:
Remote Vehicle Unit (R.V.U.)
Several different models of Remote Vehicle Units are available, capable of operating from 7 to 35 VDC or from 30 to 120 VDC.

The compact size of the R.V.U. makes it very easy to install in the Locomotive or Car Mover.

The microprocessors on the Nautilus R.V.U. continuously monitor all of the required functions and immediately inform the operator as soon as any readings start to move outside of their safe working limits.

Individually fused output relays directly operate even the largest solenoids.

The 32 character illuminated liquid crystal display provides a variety of diagnostic information.

"Plug-in" design makes the circuit boards easy to replace, ensuring speedy repair and minimal "down-time".

All cards are "keyed" with a special notch to ensure that they can only be inserted in the correct position in the R.V.U. Motherboard.

The tough fiberglass enclosure includes a weather - tight "O" ring seal and is fitted with rubber shock mounts.

A thick rubber pad in the lid acts as a giant shock absorber but also holds the "plug-in" circuit boards firmly in place.

Product Name:
Pneumatic Interface
The electro - pneumatic interface provides proportional control of the Automatic and Independent Brakes plus many other functions. The entire interface is mounted on a single plate which is approximately 18" x 24" x 6" deep.

Manifold mounted valves and plug-in solenoids ensure easy maintenance and reliable operation.

Strategically placed gauges make it easy to monitor the pneumatic status of the locomotive.

Product Name:
Cranemax
The Nautilus Cranemax System is a versatile radio remote - control system capable of operating a wide range of industrial equipment including any type of Crane.

CHARACTERISTICS:
-Extremely rugged
-Completely Weatherproof
-Easy to operate
-Very cost-effective
-Safe and reliable
-Explosion proof

Nautilus Cranemax Systems are designed to ensure that both the operator and the equipment are protected for safe operation.

The use of a 16 bit CRC check combined with unique identification codes ensures the System will not malfunction in the presence of extraneous RF signals.

APPLICATIONS:
-Overhead Cranes
-Tower Cranes
-Mobile Cranes
-Telescopic Boom Cranes
-Harbour Quay Cranes
-Railway Cranes
-Many other types of mobile equipment

Nautilus Cranemax Systems are custom engineered to meet each crane's specifications to assure safe and reliable operation.

FEATURES:

The Nautilus Cranemax System will use 1 of 3 Portable Control Units.
The Chestpack 600, the Maxonic 200 or the Powerbase 300.
Each of these Portable Units can control many different types of AC and DC powered cranes.

Security access codes prevent unauthorized use of equipment and eliminate the need for keys. Security codes are also used to allow the supervisor to change pre-selected parameters.

The Portable Unit can be programmed to operate on any pre-approved frequency and identification code. Safety and operating procedures can easily be changed to suit customer's requirements.

A single Portable Unit may control 2 different cranes either individually or simultaneously. This is very useful when two cranes must carry a long steel girder or oversize container between them.

Product Name:
Maxonic 200 Portable Unit
This unique Transmitter meets CLASS I DIV. l / II GROUPS A - F (Explosion Proof) requirements and complies with both North American and European Standards.

This transmitter is designed to meet the customer's needs and can operate 7 motions with each motion having up to 5 speeds and / or:
-Single or dual Bridge
-Up to 3 Trolleys
-Up to 3 Hoists
-Hydraulic Bridge Brake
-Pitch and Catch operation

The Trolley / Hoist selector switch selects the required Trolley and Hoist combination allowing the 3 knobs to easily control up to 7 motions.

The Portable Unit will also remotely control a Crane's hydraulic footbrake (if required).

Product Name:
Powerbase 300 Portable Unit
Large AC cranes and even 250 ton DC cranes are no match for the Nautilus Powerbase 300 Portable Unit.

This System is capable of operating 6 or 7 motion cranes with each motion having up to 6 speeds. Dynamic lowering and proportional control voltages to SCR drivers is not a problem.

The portable unit is light weight and fully weatherproof for use indoors or outdoors in all weather conditions from -30ø C to +60ø C.

Product Name:
Remote Unit (R.U.)
Several different models of receiver are available, capable of operating from 16 to 280 VAC and from 12 to 300 VDC.

A thick rubber pad in the enclosure lid holds the plug-in circuit boards firmly in place.

The tough fiberglass enclosure includes a weather tight "O" ring seal and is fitted with rubber shock mounts.

Plug-in design makes the circuit boards easy to replace, ensuring speedy repair and minimal "down-time".

All cards are "keyed" with a special notch to ensure that they can only be inserted in the correct position in the Remote Unit Motherboard.

The compact size of the Receiver makes it very simple to install anywhere on the Crane Bridge or in the cab.

Although the Unit is small it contains all of the elements required to directly operate the various Crane's AC contactors.

The 32 character illuminated liquid crystal display provides a variety of diagnostic information.
