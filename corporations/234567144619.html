Company Profile


In business since 1994, LaserNett is a sheet metal laser cutting, custom metal fabrication shop. Our philosophy for business success is a simple one. We strive to provide our customers the highest quality of service, a friendly customer service experience, a skilled team of metal fabricators that use the latest in state-of-the-art fabrication equipment.

We are the industry leader in laser cutting, metal fabrication, laser engraving, welding, tube bending, sheet metal bending, powder coating, machining, tube perforation, sheet metal rolling and metal spinning. View our various client testimonials to get a better sense of the high level of service and quality workmanship LaserNett offers.

Located in Mississauga, Ontario, our 55,000 sq ft, state-of-the-art facility allows our dedicated and skilled personnel to tackle projects of all shapes, quantities and sizes. We can take your laser cutting and metal fabrication project from conceptual idea to finished product. We believe in continuous improvement and are constantly investing in equipment and personnel to refine our processes in order to maintain and enhance our overall quality of products and services.

Utilize LaserNett's sheet metal laser cutting and custom metal fabrication services today and see why from design to production, we are your professional one-stop metal shop.

Service Name:
Laser Cutting Services
CO2 laser cutting from LaserNett is a very clean and precise way to cut a wide range of materials. Laser cutting is fast, accurate, requires minimal set-up time and offers significant cost savings.

We operate a 4000 watt CO2 laser cutter and is perfect for your custom metal fabrication projects or your mass production laser-cut components.

Whether you're cutting 1 piece or 1000 pieces of the same item, Laser Nett's CO2 laser has an incredible tolerance range between pieces. On top of our laser being incredibly accurate, we also assure a clean cut and minimal levels of slag.

Our laser cutter is capable of laser cutting the following:
- .750 Mild Steel (Maximum thickness)
- .500 Stainless Steel (Maximum thickness)
- .375 Aluminum (Maximum thickness)

Maximum laser cutting sheet size
- 120" x 60"

Use Laser Nett's laser cutting services today and see why we are your professional one-stop metal fabrication shop.

Service Name:
Welding Services
LaserNett's welding services are second to none. Our state-of-the-art facilities include robotic welding for mass scale projects as well as MIG, TIG, Spot and Stud welding capabilities, where one of our fine craftsmen can weld together the perfect piece with impeccable attention to detail.


Robotic Welding - Very high speed, extremely accurate, excellent repeatability, high strength. For very high volumes, where spot welding is not an option.

MIG Welding- High speed, fairly accurate, high strength. For medium-high volume production welding. Aluminum, mild steel and stainless steel.

TIG Welding - Low speed, extremely accurate, high strength; produces very presentable, high quality weld. For low volume and/or precision parts. Best choice for stainless steel and aluminum.

Spot Welding - Extremely high speed, moderate accuracy, average strength. For very high volumes, but limited to specific applications.

Stud Welding - Specialty welding technique for welding studs to plate in high volumes.


Utilize our welding services today and see why we are your professional one-stop metal fabrication shop.

Service Name:
Tube Bending Services
As the metal fabrication authority, LaserNett is a specialist in tube bending and tube manipulation. LaserNett's knowledge and high standards of metal fabrication excellence combined with our precision machinery, ensures customers receive the finest in tube bending.


Our custom pipe bending process uses a precision mandrel bender that slowly draws a tube over a die. We use this process of metal tube bending because it provides a smooth, aesthetically pleasing appearance while maintaining the full and smooth diameter of the tube throughout the bend.


Use Laser Nett's tube bending services today and see why we are your professional one-stop metal fabrication shop.

Service Name:
Sheet Metal Bending Services
Sheet metal bending from LaserNett is a very clean and precise way to bend a wide range of metals.
Our dedicated and skilled metal forming production staff, utilize state-of-the-art (Accupress Edge) press brakes to produce high quality results.


Using our press brake, LaserNett is able to fabricate complex shapes in a variety of material types, such as mild steel, aluminium and stainless steel to tight tolerances.


Our computer controlled metal bending brake ensures a high degree of continued accuracy for short or long production runs. We can produce volumes as little as one to many thousands with a high level of accuracy and consistency.


Our sheet metal bending capabilities are the following:
- Ram repeat accuracy of +/- 0.004"
- Ram position accuracy of +/- 0.004"
- Programmable ram tilt +/- 0.500


Use Laser Nett's sheet bending services today and see why we are your professional one-stop metal fabrication shop.

Service Name:
Powder Coating Services
Powder coating from LaserNett is a cost effective service that adds a durable and attractive finish to your steel, stainless steel, aluminum, cast aluminum, cast iron, copper and brass items. We offer a powder coating process that is environmentally friendly and emits nearly zero volatile organic compounds (VOC's) into the atmosphere. Best part, our self-contained coating operation ensures any over sprayed powder is collected and recycled for future use.


From start to finish, our trained staff stands behind our high quality powder coating service with fast and affordable production turnarounds. LaserNett can handle a wide range of your powder coating requirements - from one-off prototypes to high volume production runs.


At LaserNett, we understand your need for a variety of powdered paints and offer a large selection of colours, glossiness and textures. Don't see your particular powder coat colour below? Service Name:
Tube Perforation Services
LaserNett's tube perforation services is a cost effective solution to those in various industries like automotive, agricultural, medical and power generation who are looking for tube perforation.


Utilizing tubes upwards of 30" in length, we are able to perforate holes into round tubes with a high degree of speed and accuracy. LaserNett is able to manufacture cut-to-length perforated tubes with a standard range capacity from 1 3/8"(35mm) to 3"(76mm) in stainless steel, mild steel or aluminum tube.


Our tube perforating capabilities include the following:

- Material: Stainless Steel, Mild Steel or Aluminum
- Hole pattern: spiral
- Maximum Length: up to 30
