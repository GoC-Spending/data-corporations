Company Profile


Tanks Direct is an Edmonton based company that serves the Oil and Gas, Mining, Construction, Forestry, and Agriculture industries in Western Canada. We have over 600 double wall rental tanks in our fleet and are continually adding more to better serve our existing and new clients. We pride ourselves on providing top quality products at reasonable prices as well as servicing what we rent. Our service team has over 30 years experience in the industry and are second to none.

Our temporary fuel storage tanks are fully equipped with pumps, meters, filters, low temperature hose, and automatic shut off nozzles. We have recently added high speed pumping equipment to the mix and have those set-ups available on our larger capacity tanks. Our double wall rental tanks comply with ULC regulations for fuel storage in Canada. We also carry a line of double wall transport Canada approved tanks in a 3000 L capacity that can be transported on public roads full of fuel legally. All of our fuel tank rentals are mounted on a heavy duty I-beam skid complete with 5 ½ corner bollards for crash protection and come with nozzle drip containment.

We work in a fast paced and ever changing environment and are always looking for better ways to meet your needs. We keep our double wall tank rental fleet up to date and are confident that when a Tanks Direct double wall tank rental arrives on your site all you have to do is plug in and pump.

Product Name:
Envirotanks (Double wall fuel tanks)
The Envirotank from Tanks Direct provides efficient above ground storage for gasoline or diesel. The 110% contained construction ensures maximum risk management, while the custom pumping set-ups offer ease of use for the operator. Our standard sizes are15,000 L (3300 Gal), 25,000 L (5500 Gal), 50,000 L (11,000 Gal), 61,000 L (13,400 Gal), and 75,000 L(16,500 Gal). All of our fuel tanks comply with the regulations for fuel storage in Canada and are built and labeled in accordance with ULC specifications. These double wall tanks come complete with a heavy-duty skid base with corner bollards for ease of loading and offloading from site to site.

Envirotank Standard Features
-ULC sized primary and secondary normal and emergency venting.
-3 x 4" threaded couplers in primary tank roof.
-72 L man way fill spill box complete with a lockable lid, stainless steel hinge, ¾ drain back port, and camlock connection.
-2 x 2 threaded couplers in primary tank roof.
-2 secondary containment inspection port.
-3 RFWN 4 bolt flange.
-ULC approved lift lugs.
-ULC labeled.
-Saddle base support
-Heavy-duty skid mounted c/w nozzle drip pot and corner bollards.
-Grounding lug.

Product Name:
Double Wall Waste Oil Tank
The Rectangular double wall waste oil storage tank from Tanks Direct provides efficient above ground storage for waste dangerous goods. The 110% contained construction ensures maximum risk management, while the low profile design offers easy access for filling and draining (done at ground level). Our standard sizes are 1250 L (275 Gal) or 2400 L (528 Gal). All of our waste oil tanks comply with the regulations for waste oil storage in Canada and are built and labeled in accordance with ULC specifications. These tanks come complete with a sleeper base, which can be used for forklift pockets to move the tank around your site empty.

Rectangular waste oil tank Standard Features
-ULC sized primary and secondary normal and emergency venting.
-2 camlock and drop tube for emptying tanks contents located in the spill box
-72 L fill/spill box c/w weather-resistant lockable lid. Filters can be placed in the spill box so they can drip dry into the tank and the lid can be closed and locked.
-2 x 2" threaded couplers in primary tank roof. · 2 secondary containment inspection port.
-ULC approved Lift Lugs

Product Name:
Fuel Management Systems/Card Lock Systems
Fuel Management Systems

At Tanks Direct we understand the needs to monitor fuel consumption for your fleet. That is why we offer fuel management systems to compliment our double wall rental tanks that will meet or exceed your needs in keeping track of what is going out of your tank and who is taking it. These systems can be put in your yard site or on remote sites.

Key-lock

- Handles up to 50 users per system.
- Each user/vehicle is assigned a certain key
- Each key has its own odometer which is a continuous counter that cannot be reset to monitor fuel consumption.
- Timeout feature to ensure fuel can only be taken if pulse is received by the fuel management system from the fuel meter.
- Ability to disable any key in the event of the termination of an employee or loss of a key with no downtime.

Card Lock Systems

Whether it is theft prevention, consumption or fleet management, or cost calculating the self contained card lock systems designed and manufactured by Tanks Direct are the answer. Along with our double walled tank rentals we can provide a fully functional and easily portable fuelling station for your job site. We have designed these systems with ease of use and installation in mind so there are no costly professionals needed on site to do the installation of the equipment as everything is factory wired by Journeyman Electricians to quick connect plugs that are clearly marked. Over the past year we have had clients set up these systems themselves (in less than 2 hours) without the visit of a service technician and have been able to poll the system for information anywhere in the world they have an internet connection. All of our horizontal double wall tank rentals can be connected to this system so you have a wide range of tank capacities to choose from for your exact storage needs.

- Can have up to 100,000 users per site controller.
- Wireless/satellite systems available for remote locations.
- Multiple tanks can be set-up on one card-lock (up to 4 tanks as a standard).
- Up to 10,000 transactions can be stored securely in the site controller.
- Each user can be restricted to certain product usage and can be assigned a daily limit.
- Can use proprietary cards, mag strip cards, key fobs, pin numbers for authorization.
- Can do fleet maintenance management to notify operators when they need to have regular scheduled maintenance on their piece of equipment.
