Product Name:
Welding Positioners
Welding Positioners And Welding Turntables – Enhance Productivity

Welding positioners are used for diverse applications ranging from pipe spooling to positioning of very large cumbersome parts such as chassis components or structural steel.

LJ provides top quality welding positioners for a diverse range of weld positioning applications including:

automated gear tilt positioners

Gear Tilt Positioners – gear tilt positioners are used for powered variable speed tilting and rotation, used to automate and increase welding productivity of cumbersome, heavy work pieces

Pipe Turning Positioners – pipe turning positioners are used for extremely cost effective pipe spooling and turning of heavy pipe, elbows and fittings

Powered Height, Tilt & Rotate Positioners
Powered Headstock / Tailstock Positioners

Benchtop Positioners – Small positioners are typically referred to as bench top or table top positioners, used for turning of small pipe or fittings when powerful turning torque, tilting functions are required in a lightweight package

Manual Welding Positioners – ideal for use in applications where power source is not available or limited
specialty positioners

Hard Surfacing Positioners – custom welding positioners for applications ranging from overlay hard surfacing applications and more

Product Name:
Welding Turntables / Welding Rotating Tables
Rotary turntables are used for precise positioning in the flat or horizontal position. High-precision, cost effective method of rotating a workpiece around a vertical axis. Welding turntables, or welding rotating tables, provide a high-precision, low-cost method of rotating a pipe, vessel or industrial work piece around a vertical axis.

Product Name:
Turning Rolls
Turning Rolls
Turning rolls are used for rolling and positioning for applications including small oil and gas pipe, wind towers, enormous pressure vessels and more. All LJ turning rolls can be customized with track mount wheels, or any other custom accessories:

Tank Fit-Up Rolls
Tank Fit-Up Rolls – used for fit-up of vessel shells or to roll thin walled tanks as an alternative to turning rolls

Vessel Turning Rolls
Vessel Turning Rolls – used for turning and rolling large vessels, typically in tandem with boom and column manipulators housing subarc welding equipment

rollers for large pipe
Pipe Alignment Rollers – LJ alignment rolls are designed for specialty applications where pipe or vessels require precise, fast alignment and fine leveling for fit-up and welding

Pipe Turning Rolls
Pipe Rollers – for pipe welding applications ranging from light tubes to extra-heavy walled pipe

Product Name:
Manipulator / Boom Column Manipulator
LJ engineers and manufactures manipulators with boom stiffness and welding head stability, operator controls, and ease of use as key driving factors. Automatic welding heads (typically submerged arc) are designed to be mounted on a stiff and nearly vibrationless boom, and moved to desired height with powered column. The welding head can then be positioned as required for automated welding of large vessels or other workpieces.

Product Name:
Beam Clamp Roller / I-beam Roller / Pipe Rigging Roller
Beam Clamp Rigging Rollers

Pipe rigging rollers are used for a variety of applications, in particular for installation of pipe into racks at refineries, power plants, and other industrial facilities. All LJ pipe rigging rollers are designed to be lightweight, with beam clamps for quick installation and various roller head options so that pipe can be easily, safely, and efficiently be installed.

There are three main styles of pipe rigging rollers:

Multi-directional rollers for pipe rolling in all directions
Conical beam clamp rigging rollers for rolling pipe into racks
Powered rigging rollers- for rolling pipe into racks, equipped with self-locking
geared wheel spacing features and powered roller/driver

Beam clamp rigging rollers are used for installation of pipe in racks and modules where space is limited and cranes cannot access, using rolling elements to enhance productivity and safety.

LJ pipe rigging rollers are designed to clamp onto a range of structural beams for any pipe installation application such as refineries, modules, and many others.

Product Name:
Cable Reel Roller
Cable Reel Rollers are used for rolling large cable spools, for payout, unwinding and take-up of heavy cable. Often used in combination with a tugging or pulling unit, LJ cable reel rollers are equipped with variable speed, bidirectional controls, slip clutches to eliminate over torque and maximize safety, heavy duty rolling components, and easily adjustable diameter range using v-track rollers. LJ engineers and manufactures cable reel rollers to any capacity or specification required.

Product Name:
Welding Pipe Stand
Welding Pipe Stands – Work Safer And Smarter

Welding Pipe Stands are used for rolling pipe, supporting pipe, and handling pipe safely and efficiently. LJ pipe roller stands are typically used as rolling pipe supports for welding positioning applications. LJ offers a wide range of roller stand options for any pipe diameter and weight.

Pipe stands are available in various models and styles, and are a necessary piece of equipment utilized in many applications including:

Shutdown Maintenance Programs
Gas Plant Construction
Pipeline Construction
Fabrication Shops and Module Construction
Rig Welding
LJ offers a large variety of roller and collapsible stands that will satisfy virtually any application.

Product Name:
Tripod Support Stands
available for a wide range of height adjustability and load capacities, complete with your choice of V-support, roller wheels, or multi-directional roller tops. Folding or solid frame optional.

Product Name:
Pipeline Rollers
Pipeline rollers are designed to protect, support, and guide a pipeline section during river and road crossings. Pipe guide rollers help minimize drag forces of pipe-pulling through horizontally drilled or bored holes. LJ manufactures conical or conveyor roller style pipe rollers for applications ranging from 2" light walled HDPE piping up to heavy walled 48" pipe with capacities ranging from 1,000 to 20,000 lbs or greater.

Product Name:
Pipeline Cradles
LJ Welding Pipeline Cradles are used as an effective method for supporting and rolling pipe at the entrance of pipeline crossings. The cradle safely supports, guides, and protects the pipe. Various cradle models for pipe from 2" to 60" (60.3 mm - 1524 mm) in diameter.

Product Name:
Overhead Lifting Equipment
Overhead Lifting Equipment – Check Out Our Turn-key Approach

LJ has built a variety of spreader beams for many custom applications, designed to match our customer's material handling requirements. Specific examples of spreader beam applications to our credit include:

spreader beams1 Ton Pipe Rolling Spreader Beam - used to haul defective pipe out of an oilsands facility in Fort McMurray. Designed, built, inspected, certified, and delivered to site in less than 4 weeks.
