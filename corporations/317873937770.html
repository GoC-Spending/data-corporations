Company Profile


Rosborough Boats is a 52year old company that has been
designing and building vessels of all types and sizes from 18'
to 85'. Building wooden craft up into the late 1970's the
company kept alive the golden age of sail longer than any other
boat company in Atlantic Canada.
Turning to all fiberglass construction of yachts and commercial
vessels in the early 1980' Rosborough has supplied boats to many
and varied clients for many different uses and in many different
places.
Presently the company offers a line of all fiberglass
combination craft of traditional design in power
Configurations. This series the RF series and includes a 22' and 25' production units and several custom models.
Reputed as stable and seaworthy craft the RF series is now a
very popular pleasure craft and the company is busy supplying
many units annually to market in the USA, Canada, Carribbean,
Bermuda, and many other areas. There are still some commercial
and official craft built as demands arise.

All the products built by Rosborough Boats are to high quality
standards and a strong reputation for superior products in
fiberglass and specialty high tech materials has resulted.
The boats are all molded composite components assembled to
ensure ultimate durability and longivety. Features such as 100%
positive floatation, total fire retardency, and exceptional
strength ensure the RF series craft provide the safety, comfort,
and value retention to our clients that keep them coming back
and refering Rosborough Boats to new customers.
Present vessel owners is the primay sales tool the company has
to market new boats and they are always eager to get others
happily involved in Rosborough Boats.

In 2002 Rosborough branched off into the Rigid Hull Inflatable Market with its Rough Water series. The boats were primarily built for official purposes and are now built for Commercial/ Official and Adventure/ Leisure customers alike. These vessels range from 6-9 meters and are available in various configurations.
