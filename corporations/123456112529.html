Company Profile


We are a Crown Corporation that delivers government services and acts as the information utility for the province. We serve the public through 36 service centres, a toll-free call centre and through SNB Online. Our four lines of business include delivering over 200 government services, running the land, personal property and corporate affairs registries, performing the property tax assessment
function for the province, and maintaining the land information/geomatics infrastructure for the province.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Personal Property Registry System
Province-wide registry service where users can serve public
notice of security interests, money judgments, and certain other
claims affecting personal property (movable goods). Anyone can
search the PPR for the existence of securities against personal
property in the Province.
Users who require the service to register notices of securities
must apply for an account. Clients have the option of accessing
the PPR from any Registry Office in New Brunswick or purchasing a
software package which allows remote (on-line) access to the PPR.

ConnectIT Category Description: Municipal Management Land
Assessments & Records

ConnectIT-keywords: security financing, personal property,
registration, notice-based, debth protection, personal property,
movable goods ,on-line registry, New Brunswick

Product Name:
Digital Topography Database
Consists of 1890 digital files which display planimetric features
compiled from aerial imagery at typical 1:10,000 scale standards.
The datum is ATS77 and the projection is Stereographic Double.
A digital terrain model is also available. The DTM grid is
composed of files designed to match the topographic files. There
is a user's guide available to clients purchasing this product.

ConnectIT Category Description: GEOGRAPHICH INFORMATION SYSTEM
(GIS) Geographic Information System / GPS

ConnectIT-keywords: Topography, base maps, digital maps, digital
terrain model, maps of New Brunswick, New Brunswick

Product Name:
Digital Property Database
The land parcel fabric for the entire province is available in
digital format. Clients can purchase a digital property map file
in CARIS or AUTOCAD formats for any geographic window of their
choosing. The Attribute files which can be referenced are: 1)
The Parcel Index Files (providing information about the
ownership, location, extent, registered documents and survey
plans) and the Assessment Roll (providing public information
about the use and value of real property for purposes of
assessment and taxation).
ConnectIT Category Description: GEOGRAPHICH INFORMATION SYSTEM
(GIS) Mapping

ConnectIT-keywords: digital property maps, New Brunswick, land
ownership, cadastre, land information, digital maps

Product Name:
Real Estate property Information Sevice
A commercial Internet based service. Clients with an account
access the service using standard Internet browsers. The
information available consists of three databases which are
linked to each land parcel in the Province of New Brunswick:
1) Digital Property Map;
2) Parcel Index File providing information about the ownership,
location, extent, registered documents and survey plans;
3) Assessment Database provides public information about the use
and value of real property for purposes of assessment and
taxation.
ConnectIT Category Description: Municipal Management Land
Assessments & Records

ConnectIT-keywords: Cadastre, land information, New Brunswick,
land ownership, valuation, assessment, land registration, deeds
registry, digital property mapping

Service Name:
Geographic information services
Service Name:
Government Service Delivery
SNB provides over-the-counter delivery of more than 140
government services through a network of 36 service centers, as
well as over-the-phone and web delivery. This list of services
includes municipal government services as well.

Service Name:
Property Assessment
Assessment services provides assessment of all land, buildings
and associated improvements to provide the basis for property
taxation for municipalities and the Province.

Service Name:
Corporate Registry
The Registry maintains information on registrations at the
Corporate Affairs Branch of Service New Brunswick. These
registrations deal with profit and not-for-profit companies,
partnerships and business names.
