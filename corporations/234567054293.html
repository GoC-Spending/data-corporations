OpinioNation Inc. is recognized nationally and internationally, as a leader in conducting research projects that assist clients with: service standard and program evaluations; performance measurement studies; needs assessments; quality studies; issue, promotion, advertising, awareness surveys; employee studies; along with very sensitive attitude and habit studies. OpinioNation Inc. is a 100% Canadian owned federal incorporation, headquartered in Ottawa, Ontario. OpinioNation Inc. has an office in Europe, USA, plus several associated offices throughout the United States.

Surveying Canadians has never been an easy task and asking anyone to take several minutes out of their busy and hectic day will normally receive a curt "I don't have the time!". Our inter-active high-tech, portable touch-screen computers, loaded with the sophisticated OpinioNation PollMaking software, makes surveying as easy as saying " Thank You!". The TOUCHSCREEN SURVEYCENTRES are totally respondent friendly! Just touch the screen with your finger.

Either you or OpinioNation develop the questions. The questionnaire is then loaded into our interactive TOUCHSCREEN SURVEYCENTRES, and the respondent simply touches answers to questions listed on our specialized computer touch screen. No human interface is necessary, eliminating bias. The respondent can go through a normal 20-minute survey in about 2 minutes.

The TOUCHSCREEN SURVEYCENTRE can be utilized constantly for conducting surveys of all types, and are generally far more appropriate than other methods, such as intercept interview, mail-back, fill in the sheet, pen touch or telephone research. More than 98% of our research is conducted using our TOUCHSCREEN SURVEYCENTRES. Our TOUCHSCREEN SURVEYCENTRES are often used to pick up hard-to-poll demographics, such as high-income earners, seniors, disable persons and professional respondents, due to the ease of surveying.
