Company Profile


WORLD WIDE CONSULTANT TO THE PLASTIC AND RUBBER INDUSTRIES

SOLID TIRES - CASTERS - WHEELS

LARGE SAVING WHEN USING FASTER PRODUCTION METHODS AND LOW COST MATERIALS

New technology for faster rubber production.

Industry average is a 15 minute curing cycle for moulded rubber products, we can reduce cycle time to one minute.

When combining our faster production methods with less costly raw materials (recycled tire crumb) high quality rubber products can be made for a fraction the cost of current methods.

We can offer you new faster production methods to make solid tires - casters - wheels much faster the saving applies to both compression moulding of thermosetting materials and injection moulding of thermoplastic materials.

When also using less costly raw materials such as tire crumbs or recycled thermoplastic engineering materials your savings on production costs are exceptional .

You can count on a fast cycle time of at least one minute on a standard moulding press - for production of moulded rubber products that would usually take 15 minutes to produce.

Example : take 2 kg (4.4 lbs) sold 12 inch diameter tire made from rubber crumb. You will make with our faster production method on one compression moulding press 240 to 480 per hour at the industry average cost of $40.00 per hour your labour cost to make a 2kg tire is only 9 to 18 cents.

A cost to you of 10% on the saving will result in a great difference in your balance sheet at the end of the year. And what do you risk? no saving - no cost.


' eLastoprene ' our Thermoplastic Elastomer

We make from recycled used rubber a truly " Thermoplastic Elastomer " so that anyone with a plastic Molding - Extrusion Machine can make rubber products / sheets without the need to vulcanize the material - reducing production time from minutes to just seconds .

We mold rubber products of up to 75 lbs and up to 4 x 4 ft in size and usually for 50 cents / lbs molded product .

You no longer have to Outsource Rubber Products from countries that pay 100 dollar a month to their workforce - Just contact us and ask for A molded sample plate of our " eLastoprene " Thermoplastic Elastomers and / or prices for this material and molded rubber products .

SOLID TIRES

using new faster production methods make it possible to
mould thermosetting rubber tires that usually take 15 to
20 minutes curing time - in one minute of less on standard
compression moulding presses.

The same much faster production method can be used on standard
injection moulding presses reducing cooling time from 10 minutes
down to one minute.

More output per hour - less cost per piece - more profits -
less overhead - less labour cost - less energy cost - greatly
improved bottom line.

of 240 per hour - with one machine and one operator using
our faster production methods and rubber crumbs from recycled
tires at a cost of Can. $0.60 each tire labour and material
included.

Our new process is applicable for both - Thermosetting and
thermoplastic materials and gives you 10 to 20 times more
production output.

Using low cost raw materials (rubber crumbs - recycled plastics)
to make your products will five you a quality end product no
manufacturer using conventional production methods can compete
with.
