Company Profile


FibreCast is a global provider of high performance specialty products used by many industries, with a diverse group of applications. We search the world for the best quality vacuum formed refractory materials to create innovative products designed to provide substantial improvement in thermal performance. Our products are also designed to reduce costs and environmental impact. We are defined by our experience, imagination and ability to transform ideas into high-performance refractory products and solutions.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Vacuum-Formed Refractories
FibreCast vacuum-formed products are designed to meet specific application requirements in a wide range of industries. Diverse thermal properties can be created by varying the formulation and manufacturing process. These shapes can be a solution for a wide range of needs: metal casting, combustion chambers for boilers, furnaces and stoves, peep sites, tap-out cones, burner blocks, among many other applications.

We design and manufacture our vacuum-formed shapes at our office and manufacturing facility in Burlington, Ontario. We have the expertise technology and equipment to ensure that we effectively meet your specific needs and will work with you to develop the best solution.

Product Name:
Refractory Hardware
FibreCast is a one stop shopping location for everything refractory. Our innovation does not end at blanket and vacuum forming, but extends into refractory anchors and more. We continue to develop refractory anchoring techniques that save time, add value and create increased confidence in the refractory system as a whole.

FibreCast has a complete suite of refractory anchors and hardware:

V and Y anchors with or without crimp
Fibre hardware including twistlock studs, washers, CD pins and spears of all shapes
Brick anchors and hangers of all types and alloys including castings
All common and uncommon alloys from 304 to Inconel
Hex panels and mesh accessories
Studs, nuts, washers for both arc weld and hand weld applications
…and more!

From a simple V to complicated revolutionary assemblies, FibreCast is ready to take on your hardware requirements.

Product Name:
Specialty Refractories
FibreCast Moldables are composed of refractory products that can be applied though a troweling or hand molding and filling. Applications for this product include the protection of alloy studs and fasteners in ceramic fibre furnace linings, gaskets and seals for burner blocks and chimney stacks, and refractory repair. Upon drying, FibreCast Moldables create a smooth and hard refractory material, non-wetting to molten non-ferrous metals.

FibreCast Pumpables are water-based composites of ceramic fibre, organic polymers, inorganic binders, and other proprietary ingredients. Pumpables are used to repair any damaged or cracked refractories or gaps due to shrinkage. Upon drying, FibreCast Pumpables form a durable and lightweight refractory lining. FibreCast Pumpables can be injected using aa standard ARO pump.

FibreCast Rigidizers are inorganic liquid hardeners, with temperature grade ratings of 1649°C (3000°F). They are generally used to increase the durability and surface erosion resistance of ceramic blanket, module, and board products.

FC-Cement provides a range of consistencies for brush spray and towel on applications 1260°C (2300°F) temp. grade. Good adhesion, thermal resistance, dielectric strength, wet gas erosion resistance, and non-wetting properties against non-ferrous molten metals

Product Name:
Refractory Fibre Products
FibreCast offers an extensive line of refractory fibre products and solutions that utilize best in class materials and reap the benefits of our relentless pursuit of innovation. Our Bulk Fibre, Blanket, Paper, Felt and Textiles are just a few examples of our high quality Refractory Fibre Products. We are always looking for new and innovative solutions for our customers that go beyond the status quo.

Our refractory Fibre Products are often used in combination with monolithics to maximize the strength, abrasion, chemical attack and insulating properties for your application. Refractory Fibre Products have superior insulating properties and low thermal conductivity.


Product Name:
Monolithics
Unformed refractories, also known as Monolithics are made and marketed in granulated or plastic forms or as castable, gunning and shotcrete grades, making them available for use in a wide range of applications. Common unformed refractories include monolithic-plastic, ramming, gunning, shotcrete, castables, mortars, and dry vibrating cements. They are manufactured with various techniques.

Product Name:
Custom Precast Shapes
FibreCast Custom Precast refractory shapes are expertly made to exact specifications and can enhance the performance of a refractory lining in many applications. Our facility is fully equipped to cast precast refractory shapes up to 25 tons. Our long standing partnerships with best-in-class materials ensures optimal results for your application. The use of precast shapes can shorten the installation and/or repair time required with your refractory lining.

Product Name:
Refractory Modules
FibreCast modules are designed to reduce the need for controlled start-up after installation. We also believe that current industry dynamics have reduced the demand for a one size fits all approach due to the variety of complex geometrics encountered in various kilns, ovens, incinerators and heaters. We design custom ceramic modules to alleviate the potential for installation and performance shortfalls and disruptions.

Our ceramic fibre modules are designed to deliver maximum energy saving and insulating benefits with low heat storage. Engineered for seamless integration with your specific application, modules can be directly attached to the shell of your industrial furnace with a variety of installation processes that are fast, easy and efficient. FibreCast offers 2300°F -3000°F temperature grades and can be constructed from a variety of high-quality high purity ceramic fibre, zirconia, or polycrystalline fiber blanket.

Our wide range of products allows us to create exactly the right product or engineered system to meet all of your requirements. Our dedicated team of refractory experts and specialists are here to assist you in product selection, system design, and installation techniques and protocols.

Product Name:
Refractory Gaskets and Seals
FibreCast offers a broad variety of High Temperature Gaskets and Seals, made from a variety of refractory materials. Our high temperature gaskets and seals can be customized for specific applications are available for applications up to 1600°C (2912°F).

Product Name:
Firebrick
FibreCast is extremely proud of our high quality Firebrick products. Designed with the highest quality materials, our Firebrick products deliver an effective combination of insulating properties and load bearing ability. Our customers take comfort in knowing that our Firebrick products leverage best in class manufacturing protocols in accordance to specific structure and chemistry requirements with high standards of structural integrity. We continually strive to deliver value in refractory brick with competitive prices and high performance protocols to ensure reliability. Our Firebricks are refractory brick products designed for insulating and structural applications.

Product Name:
Fire Protection
Fire Blankets or duct wraps are a passive fire protection insulation system designed to prevent the spread of fire through the protected ductwork. Fire blanket insulations are used to protect and insulate air ventilation ducts, commercial kitchen exhaust ducts, grease ductwork, and lab chemical fume exhaust ducts.

Fiberfrax blanket and mat products offer superior insulating power. Manufactured from alumina-silica materials for low thermal conductivity, we also carry Fiberfrax products for fire protection applications.

Service Name:
Research and Development
Our Refractory Research and Development Facility is committed to discovering and creating new refractory products and solutions. We search the world for the best in class refractory materials and we are constantly challenging the status quo to discover how we can make refractory products better. We are always looking for ways to increase the service life of existing refractory products and materials.

Our R&D lab can create a custom product or solution that goes beyond the confines of the orthodox to the extraordinary.

Service Name:
Design and Engineering
FibreCast’s highly skilled engineers allow us to offer complete design and engineering services.

FibreCast is proud to offer:
• On-site inspection and failure analysis
• Heat flow analysis
• Energy surveys
• Engineered lining designs (including AutoCAD drawings)
• Cost estimating
• Project Management.

These services allow FibreCast to provide expert analysis of customer processes and design requirements that enables us to offer effective refractory solutions.

Service Name:
Manufacturing and Construction
FibreCast operates a diverse manufacturing facility that enables us to offer a wide range of manufacturing capabilities to meet our customer’s standard and custom refractory requests. Our facility is organized to enable projects that require the manufacturing of a component or a complete product.

Our manufacturing capabilities include but are not limited to the following:
• Vacuum Forming
• Die Cutting
• Module Fabrication
• Precast
• Hot Pressing
• CNC Machining
• Vessel Lining

FibreCast has complete in-house capabilities and refractory materials for a range of construction projects. Our experienced staff and expertise in refractory related construction enable us to offer a total solution that includes project estimates, procurement and project management.

Our experienced tradesmen have installed refractories in many types of industrial applications including:
• Heat treating
• Steelmaking
• Incineration
• Boilers/Steam generation
• Non-ferrous melting applications

Service Name:
Consulting
We offer personalized consulting services to help you to better understand the design, materials, maintenance and operation of your refractory products. FibreCast’s consultants and engineers work closely together through all phases of the project.

Our projects are highly diversified and often include a range of consulting services:
• Custom design
• Repair of a refractory product
• Development and deployment of a new product or solution

We are also committed to performance and reliability through all phases of a refractory repair or capital project to ensure the repaired, replaced or new refractory will meet plant demands.

We are extremely proud of our track record in offering our customers the technical expertise that provides refractory end-users definitive answers to your refractory challenges and opportunities. Our consultants work hand in hand with you to help you decide whether it’s time to build new units or if you should replace or repair the unit.
