Company Profile


LeatherGifts Direct is a 34-year old manufacturer and distributor of high-end leather products. The head-office is in India. We have operations in North America, Europe and Asia.

What makes LeatherGifts Direct unique is that we offer promotional products with a low actual cost but an incredibly high perceived value.

When you gift one of our products to your clients, associates or employees, they perceive the product to have a value that is at least 7 to 9 times the price that you paid for it. You therefore generate goodwill that is 7 to 9 times the cost of the product.

We specialize in promotional products, corporate/employee gifts and incentives. Our prices start at just $1.90 per unit and so we have products that fit into any budget.

We benchmark prestige boutiques like Gucci and Cartier for our leather quality and we use highly skilled artisans who individually handcraft each of our products.

Although our products are truly high-end, our direct from manufacturer business model ensures that you get these high-end leather products at a fraction of the prices that similar products are sold, at prestige boutiques.

We have our own design studio and so can custom design any leather product to client specifications, to meet specific client requirements.

Our clients agree with our slogan which is "With LeatherGifts Direct, you get a Rolex for the price of a Timex!".

Our client list in Canada includes General Electric, ESSO, Royal Bank, TD Canada Trust, Bank of Montreal, Scotiabank, Barclays Bank, AGF, Altamira, Franklin Templeton, Pitney Bowes, Toronto Police, University of Toronto, McMaster University, Durham College, Durham Region, York Region, City of Vaughan, Town of Richmond Hill, AT&T, Ontario Power Generation, Blakes Cassels & Graydon, McCarthy Tetrault, Law Society of Upper Canada, Molsons and many more prestigious corporations and organization.

We provide free high quality, 2-piece gift boxes with our products and emboss your logo on the gift boxes and on the products free of cost. We also pay for the courier cost to ship your orders to you.

We guarantee the lowest prices for all our products and offer a lifetime warranty on any manufacturing defect. If you find any manufacturing defect on any of our products, we will exchange it for a new product, no questions asked.

Sector information

Unique applications:

Leather is not considered by most companies as promotional products because high end leather products are very expensive in Canada. Our clients began using high-end leather items as promotion products on a large scale only after they discovered our range, which truly offers high-end leather items at a fraction of the prices that similar items are sold at in prestige boutiques, department stores and retail outlets. We are one of the few vendors that price our products to include gift boxes, logo embossing on the gift boxes & on the products and courier costs. There are no hidden costs when you do business with us.

Key / Major clients:

General Electric, ESSO, Royal Bank, TD Canada Trust, Scotiabank, Bank of Montreal, Barclays Bank, AGF, Altamira, Franklin Templeton, Synergy Mutual Funds, Brinks, Minacs Worldwide, University of Toronto, McMasters University, Durham College, York Region, Durham Region, City of Vaughan, Town of Richmond Hill, AT&T, Blakes Cassels & Graydon, McCarthy Tetrault, McMillan Binch, Fasken Martineau, Toronto Police, Ontario Power Generation, Molsons, Law Society of Upper Canada, Goodman & Carr, Davies Ward, WeirFoulds, Meloche Monnex, Messier Dowty, Cassels Brock, Borden Ladner Gervais, OMDC, Baker McKenzie, Right Axmith.

Success stories:

In our very first year of operation in Canada, four of the five major banks in Canada became our clients. We have been appointed as Licensed Suppliers to the University of Toronto as, unlike most other vendors, we were able to prove to them that all our products are ethically manufactured, without the use of child labour, sweat shops etc. Given our high-end quality and our value prices, all our clients have been able to reduce their promotional products budgets without having to compromise on the quality and the quantity of promotional products and corporate / employee gifts that they give away.

Testimonial:

"These gifts have put the "WOW" factor back into promotional products. When we give your products to our valuable clients, associates and employees, we well do so with pride.

I am certain that your leather gifts will be very well received and appreciated for their excellent quality and craftsmanship. LeatherGifts Direct prices represent excellent value and are a great vehicle for generating goodwill. I am sure they will help us build many strong and lasting relationships.

- Patrick W. Olive, Commissioner, Durham Region, Department of Economic Development.


After enjoying the reaction of my Branch Managers to their recent gift, I decided I should write to you to express my thanks.

As we discussed, I wanted to present them with something that was different from the typical corporate gifts they frequenty receive. Since we have just formed this new team, my goal was to set a standard, similar to that of my performance expectations and to demonstrate their importance in achieving our business objectives. The leather wallets hit the mark. Having each personalized was a great idea and the subtle embossed logo will provide a constant reminder of our inaugural meeting.

As I have mentioned, I appreciate the excellent service you provided in getting these to me in a timely manner. I have passed your cards and catalogues to a number of my colleagues so that they can consider these gifts for employees and customers.

- Wayne M. Smith, Vice President, TD Canada Trust

International capital projects:

LeatherGifts Direct is committed to helping the underprivileged people in India. We have an apprenticeship program where young underprivileged people in India are provided free training on leather craftsmenship and if they prove themselves, they are offered permanent positions at LeatherGifts Direct.

A significant part of our profits are used to sustain this apprenticeship program, which aims to help underprivileged people gain skills to earn a good living, rather than help them with a one time handout.
