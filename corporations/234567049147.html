Company Profile


Liz Yorke and Associates, established in 1989, is a multi-dimensional health and human services management and organization development consulting firm. LY&A assists clients to make better decisions for the future, based on collection and analysis of significant information, specialized and objective advice built from broad experience and a high level of skill, and workable ideas. The firms portfolio includes project management, business analysis, facilitation, and extensive research, planning and evaluative work in the private, not-for-profit, and broader public sectors.

Liz Yorke and Associates is committed to a collaborative approach in assisting Government, public sector organizations, corporations, and not-for-profit agencies in their search for innovative and practical solutions to new and evolving challenges.

We assist clients with:
- Transformation, restructuring and reshaping in the broader public sector
- Public policy research, development and analysis
- Impact assessment of proposed and existing policies
- In-depth public consultation (interviews, focus groups, meetings, print & electronic) with stakeholders, including multi-cultural outreach
- Facilitation of workshops, planning sessions and high-level meetings
- Evaluation, review, and analysis of policies, planning issues, programs, and service delivery
- Organizational development (governance, board/staff working relationships & accreditation issues) and strategic planning
- Report and proposal preparation
- Particular expertise in the areas of health and human services issues.
