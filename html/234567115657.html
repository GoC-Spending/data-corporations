Company Profile



Launched by ex-Microsoft executive, Sandeep Mittal, Nirvana Canada is the leading Vancouver-based web design and SEO company that offers a range of website development and online marketing services. The company started out with the goal of revolutionizing the interactive marketing industry in Canada by introducing a more affordable alternative to the services offered by interactive agencies without compromising the quality of the services. Nirvana Canada has succeeded in this goal and has a growing number of satisfied clients to show for it.

We continuously invest in advanced up-to-date technology and skilled talents. We emphasize on long term engagement models and innovative partnerships to assist our clients through their business long term growth. We work with our clients to create tailored business solutions that ultimately lead to significant competitive advantages.

We are a leading provider of CRM, ERP, Software Integration and Business solution Services. Our passion is to increase our client's business profitability by our strategic knowledge

Strategic alliances:

We have sales and delivery alliances with companies whose capabilities complement our own business value , either by enhancing a service offering, delivering a new technology or helping us extend our services to new geographies. By combining our alliance partners products and services with our own capabilities and expertise, we create innovative, high-value business solutions for our clients. Some alliances are specifically aligned with one of our service lines, thereby adding skills, technology and insights that are applicable across many of the industries we serve. Other alliances extend and enhance our offerings specific to a single industry group.
Almost all of our alliances are non-exclusive. Although individual alliance agreements do not involve direct payments to us that are material to our business, overall our alliance relationships generate revenues for us from services for implementing our alliance partners products and our related services
