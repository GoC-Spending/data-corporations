Company Profile


SIMAR - DACON inc. engineers, conceives and manufactures various conveying systems for the bulk handling of raw materials, mainly for the aggregates industry (quarries, mines, sand & gravel
plants).

Our companys strength is built on a foundation of 40 years of
hands on experience in the conception, engineering & fabrication
of conveyors systems for bulk handling applications. Our
engineering department designs using AUTOCAD 2000 for Windows.

Our 20,000 ft² manufacturing facility situated on the south shore of Montreal P.Quebec is fully equipped with 4 overhead cranes, shear, press brake, milling machine and several welding machines. Because of our continuous efforts to improve the quality of our products, we are a certified member of the CANADIAN WELDING BUREAU, W47.1 Division 3.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
CONVEYORS
Bulk Handling Belt Conveyor Systems for aggregate materials,
Bucket Elevators, Screw Conveyors, Mobile Crushing Plants, Belt
Feeders, Mobile/Portable Conveyors.


Product Name:
Take-Ups(screw)
SIMAR-DACON makes all steel conveyor belt screw take-ups ruggedly
constructed to give years of trouble free service. The screw is
made of heavy duty Acme thread which is fully protected and is
always in tension regardless of the direction of the load. The
saddles are adjustable for all sizes of pillow blocks (2 or 4
bolt hole type). These units are available in three types: Medium
duty, Heavy duty or Extra heavy duty.

Product Name:
Bins & Hoppers
Steel receiving hoppers to channel materials onto the conveyor
belt. Storage or surge bins to hold bulk materials.

Product Name:
Conveyor, Parts of
Conveyor components such as belt scrapers, hoods, belt screw
take-ups, idlers, pulleys, shafts, bearings, troughing idlers,
speed reducers, motors, walkways, underspeed switches.

Product Name:
STACKER, INDUSTRIAL, NES
Belt type, fixed, mobile or portable

Product Name:
LOADER, CONVEYOR TYPE, INDUSTRIAL, FOR GRAIN
Bucket Elevator

Product Name:
HINGED HOODS OR COVERS
Our standard covers are made from G-90 galvanized North American
Steel and are commercially dust proof & weatherproof. DACON
hoods protect idlers, belt and material and maximize personnel
safety by giving protection from moving parts. We are the ONLY
hood manufacturer offering a design that actually sheds water. In
an effort to minimize installation costs, we now supply
pre-assembled eyebolts as standard hood sheet fasteners.
