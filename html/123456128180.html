PEOPLE SKILLS FOR EFFECTIVE ENGAGEMENT

We provide ONE-ON-ONE Coaching as well as GROUP/TEAM programs.

Synergistic Ideas Inc. is an independent Canadian Training Company. Synergy means that the whole is greater than the sum of its parts. A synergistic team means that when two or more people work together effectively, they are ENGAGED and the benefits & results are greater than the sum of their individual capabilities.

Engaged employees are positive, proactive and creative problem solvers who get more done in less time. Engaged employees are persistent hard workers that make decisions consistent with your departments strategic goals and objectives. An engaged workforce is fun to work with, creating a workplace of choice!

We believe that everyone has tremendous potential for development and growth and that an investment in our training and PERSONAL COACHING programs is one of the most valuable investments any organization can make.

We have delivered workshops to a wide variety of companies and organizations including, Industry Canada, Public Works Government Services Canada, National Parole Board, Health Canada, Royal Canadian Mounted Police, Human Resources Development Canada, Heritage Canada, Manitoba Federal Council, Canada Post Corporation, SaskTel, Royal Bank of Canada, Manitoba Telephone Systems, Air Canada, many Native Communities and many, many others.

Many of the world's finest organizations, corporations, government departments and native communities are using our courses to FAST-TRACK the development of People Skills for Effective Engagement.

This One-Day (or One-On-One Coaching) Diagnostics Based Program of Insights into your SALES SKILLS INDEX will be customized to meet the specific needs of your people.

Sales Skills Index answers these questions and more, while ensuring your sales team handles each sales opportunity with precision and care.

Excel in the Seven Categories of Sales Strategy
