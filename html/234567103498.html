Company Profile


Total Interiors provides customized commercial working environments.
Through a consultative process we help you to create your interior environments that embrace your corporate culture, maximize work flow efficiencies, integrates technology and ergonomically conforms to offer a healthy working environment.

Total Interiors services encompass the creation, supply and installation of furniture, interior walls and architectural components:
Total Office provides innovative working environments
Total Education creates 21st century learning environments
Total Healthcare provide seamless furniture that offers mobility and complies with stringent infection control standards
Total Hospitality provides comfortable, custom furniture to create a memorable experience
Total Installations provides warehousing, inventory control, professional installation and moving services

Product Name:
Total Healthcare
Welcome to Total Health
At Total Health, we understand the demands required to create healthy work environments for your staff and patients. Hospitals, Private Clinics, Physician offices, and Care Facilities, throughout the province have discovered our manufacturers high quality and moderately priced products that offer a perfect solution for all their furniture needs.

We understand that furnishings need to meet and exceed the stringent healthcare infection control standards. Our broad range of lounge and waiting room seating offers dimensional choices, bariatric options, healthcare textiles, and aesthetics that fit with multiple interior designs.

Through our consultative process, we work with you to identify and create your vision with the use of colour boards, space planning and 3D renderings. We analyze the lifecycle cost of quality products to meet budgetary constraints. All of our healthcare furniture is built to last and endure daily heavy physical use in both hospitals and clinics.

At Total Health, we are the experts in healthcare furniture. Our professionally trained staff wants to work with you and your team to help you select the ideal furniture for your specific needs. Let us work with you today.


Product Name:
Total Office
Total Office is the leader in supplying innovative products for your working environment; contract quality, inspired design, adaptable technology that meet your needs now and in the future.

Total Office began 15 years ago in Kelowna, BC and we have earned a reputation for be organized, reliable and professional. We are known as the go-to company that fully comprehends the various needs of company owners and managers forging their way in a competitive and complicated workplace.

We offer highly skilled design and space planning expertise, responsive delivery and installation services with our goal to exceed your expectations every time.

Our clientele include a broad spectrum of business, corporate and government office environments. We offer a wide range of furniture products, including systems furniture, architectural wall systems, seating, storage, filing, free standing desk and ergonomic accessories which embrace todays current trends for mobility and flexibility.

Total Office believes todays modern approach to building a successful business includes creating a healthy, happy, motivating work environment. Analyzing workflow for efficiency, selecting the right furniture components, planning for future growth, embracing the corporate culture and offering life cycle cost effective solutions is imperative.

One contributing factor in our success is the Teknion brand of furniture, Canadas premier line and largest supplier in the Canadian office furniture industry producing the most flexible, cost effective and high quality office environments from furniture to walls to high design accents.

Total Offices philosophy is to provide quality products and exceptional service at the best value. We work with many diverse businesses from small business to chartered banks, credit unions, federal, provincial, and municipal governments, and a wide range of private and public corporations.

As an office furniture dealer our primary competitive advantage is a highly skilled team that works in partnership with our clients, the architectural and design community, and our key suppliers in delivering exceptional products and service.

We strive to ensure our products and services meet the needs of our clients and their evolving workplaces.


Product Name:
Teknion Dealer
Total Office is an Authorized Teknion Dealer for the BC Interior. Teknion is an international designer, manufacturer and marketer of office systems and related products, including storage and filing, seating, casegoods, tables, and ergonomic furniture. Teknions corporate headquarters is based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. The U.S. headquarters is located in Mount Laurel, New Jersey. Since its inception in 1981, Teknion has grown to approximately 3,600 employees worldwide and has approximately 3 million square feet of facilities, including manufacturing plants, showrooms, corporate headquarters and sales offices. The company operates 12 manufacturing facilities in Ontario, five in Quebec, one in Alberta and three in Malaysia. Today, the companys extensive product line is sold in over 50 countries through a network of more than 385 authorized dealers around the globe. This vertically integrated, worldwide network enables Teknion to deliver exceptional value to our clients  regardless of location  an advantage that has resulted in a significant increase in market share over the past decade. Our exceptional results spring from both strong customer partnerships and ongoing product innovation.

Business Philosophy & Mission
Teknion offers a strong portfolio of innovative office furniture products that support people at work. Our business philosophy is geared toward promoting human-centered work environments in which creativity, imagination and ingenuity thrive. It is our collective mission to create, manufacture and market office furniture and related services to provide attractive and pleasing business environments conducive to increased productivity and comfort for users worldwide.
The foundation of our business philosophy lies in one simple concept  we do not try to predict the future, rather we embrace the changes of the technological workplace and apply an innovative, yet practical approach to the development of our products. Teknion operates under an umbrella of collaborative intellect at all levels within our organization. We extend that philosophy to our client relationships, and attribute our collective success to this ability to incorporate the ideas of many into product development strategies, global partnership methodologies and day-to-day business practices. As a result, we are able to provide an outstanding value to our customers who attach importance to long-term partnerships within the contract furniture industry. The bottom line  Teknion will consistently delivery on our value proposition.

Product Name:
Total Education
Your innovative partner in creating inspired learning environments
What does a school look like that encourages learning?
What kind of school inspires children as well as teaching staff?
What kind of learning space spurs creativity, motivation and sense of well-being at the same time?
These are the questions that our education suppliers ask when looking at furniture design. Our flagship sustainable manufacturers are dedicated to providing ergonomic and forward thinking furniture in the education sector and Total Interiors is proud to be the exclusive dealer in British Columbia.

Our specialized education manufacturers are enhancing learning by creating innovative and ground breaking designs that change the way children interact with their environments. They encourage collaborative learning environments with their mobile and functional soft seating and focus on quality, reliability, safety, style, productivity and affordability.

Based on research, and designed by kenetic scientists and ergonomic specialists children need to be in motion to learn. The promotion of movement increases body temperature elevating oxygen to the brain resulting in higher brain productivity. As a result, our products have received endorsement from both physiotherapists and occupational Therapists.

At Total Education, we are the experts. Contact us to find out more about Learning commons, 21st Century Learning environments and Davinci Studios. We would be happy to provide you with information and a demo of these innovative educational products.

Service Name:
Total Installations
Total Installations


Total Installations  because we finish the jobs we start.
At Total Installations we take no chances of having someone else put the icing on our cakes.

In order to best meet the needs of our clients, Total Installation completes the work we are asked to assist with starting. That means we are pleased to deliver and install the products that you have ordered from Total Interiors and our various specific companies such as Total Hospitality, Total Health, Total Education and, of course, Total Office.

We have never understood why many companies drop the ball at the final and perhaps most important step in providing quality service to a client. It matters not if a client has ordered the finest in furniture or products if whoever delivers the order does not treat it with respect, or make sure it fits the customers expectations. As a customer we want you to be assured the products you order from us are installed correct, delivered prompt, safe, and sound, and that the design and planning we did together is competed professionally. We want you to be happy.

With that in mind, we are proud of the fact that Total Installation is the newest addition to our company  and we are thrilled that we have top quality people working for us to ensure that nothing but quality work is applied in completing our commitment to you.

At Total Installation we perform our own installations and also offer product relocation, reconfiguration, repairs, maintenance, service, and storage. In addition we also reupholster and clean furniture and products if desired.

At Total Installation we proudly finish the job which we were originally contracted to begin.
We leave nothing to chance, or rely on others.
At Total Installation, that is our total commitment to you.
