Company Profile


Multi-Power Products Ltd. is a versatile manufacturer of earth drilling rigs and ancillary - related equipment, with a present product offering ranging from their multi-purpose Pioneer ATV class up to their largest reverse circulation Explorer-II in excess of 30 tonnes. Diamond Coring, Air Rotary, conventional and reverse circulation RC, auger, seismic shot hole.

While primarily catering to the Mineral Exploration market with their diamond core and reverse circulation ( RC ) drill rigs, Multi-Power also provides drilling systems and components into the Construction, Deep Foundation, Geothermal, Geotechnical, Geophysical / Seismic, Water Well, and Open Pit drilling markets.

Multi-power is constantly expanding their product range of drilling equipment / components, and are willing to design custom units to meet specific client needs. They offer Heli-portables, man-portables, air/ mud rotary sytems, rotary top drives & chucks, side inlet & RC swivels, hydraulic breakout systems & foot clamps, water supply & pressure pumps, mud pumps, hydraulic mud mixers, Geothermal grouting systems, and various other coring, rotary, and DTH accessories.

They are large enough to compete and deliver their products internationally, yet are small enough for personalized service. From design to production and from sales to service for over 20 years, Multi-Power is totally committed to client satisfaction day after day, year after year.

Success stories:

Success Story # 1. - Gas Hydrate Exploration Geotechnical Survey

Company:
CPE Inc./ Northern Exploration, & Geological Survey of Canada successfully drilled aproximately 50 test holes 4-1/2" dia. to depths of 30 feet in the Mallik gas hydrate fields of the Mackenzie Delta - northwestern Canadian Arctic. Due to the limited access to the area the drilling unit needed to be Heli-portable, easy to manuever once on site, capable of drilling in permafrost, and stay under 500 kg in weight.
Multi-Power worked with the customers and re-designed our Pioneer series drill to accomodate thier requiremnets and was able to produce a small portable system on wheels that would develop about 1,100 lbs of rotary torque and 3,050 lbs. of Pull Back force. The system was equiped with both CRREL Ice core barrels and convention augering systems allowing the client the ability to be flexible in the field.

Success Story #2. - Core Drilling For Gold In Slovakia

Company:
Golden Regent Resources required a smaller exploration drill that was versatile and capable of diffrent types of drilling. Multi-power's Prospector II series drill met their needs, allowing them to enter terrain that the larger exploration drills were not capable of reaching. The Uderina area was the first of many strategic areas that the Prospector II would successfully drill and on the first outing the drillers were able to drill 200M / day making short work of the proposed drilling program in the area.

Success stories:

Success Story # 1. - Gas Hydrate Exploration Geotechnical Survey

Company:
CPE Inc./ Northern Exploration, & Geological Survey of Canada successfully drilled aproximately 50 test holes 4-1/2" dia. to depths of 30 feet in the Mallik gas hydrate fields of the Mackenzie Delta - northwestern Canadian Arctic. Due to the limited access to the area the drilling unit needed to be Heli-portable, easy to manuever once on site, capable of drilling in permafrost, and stay under 500 kg in weight.
Multi-Power worked with the customers and re-designed our Pioneer series drill to accomodate thier requiremnets and was able to produce a small portable system on wheels that would develop about 1,100 lbs of rotary torque and 3,050 lbs. of Pull Back force. The system was equiped with both CRREL Ice core barrels and convention augering systems allowing the client the ability to be flexible in the field.

Success Story #2. - Core Drilling For Gold In Slovakia

Company:
Golden Regent Resources required a smaller exploration drill that was versatile and capable of diffrent types of drilling. Multi-power's Prospector II series drill met their needs, allowing them to enter terrain that the larger exploration drills were not capable of reaching. The Uderina area was the first of many strategic areas that the Prospector II would successfully drill and on the first outing the drillers were able to drill 200M / day making short work of the proposed drilling program in the area.
