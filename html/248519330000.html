Company Profile


PROFILE
Founded in 1903, the company is a long established multi-purpose
machine shop and metal fabricating plant. Ramsay expertise in
machining, fabricating and welding of stainless and carbon steel,
aluminum, and special metals has been used to supply equipment to
the forestry, mining, marine, shipbuilding, transportation,
petrochemical and construction industries. The company has
extensive experience in the design, manufacture and test of
systems using hydraulics, pneumatics and electronic control
systems. Located in Sidney, B.C., adjacent to the Victoria
International Airport and close to ocean loading facilities, the
company can handle has developed, manufactures and supplies an
electrolytic marine grown fouling and corrosion control system.
The company operates from a modern 9,000 square metre plant with
overhead electric travelling cranes and 11,000 square metre paved
external erection/assembly yard.

PRINCIPLE FIELDS OF ACTIVITY
Machining, fabrication and manufacture f equipment ans systems
for the forestry, mining, marine, shipbuilding, transportation,
petrochemical and construction industries.

AREAS OF EXPERTISE
- Manufacture of hydraulically and electrically powered, computer
controlled machinery handling systems in the transportation and
forestry sectors.
- Design and manufacture of pressure vessels, heat exchangers and
process equipment.
- The company is certified by the National Board of ASME
International with "U" and "R" qualifications in accordance with
the ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code. An in-house quality
assurance program is operated in accordance with a documented
quality control manual. The company is Canadian Welding Bureau
certified for pressure vessel welding and manufacture. The
company uses Autocad for CADD and scheduling software for project
schedule control.
- Design and manufacture of electrolytic water treatment and
cathodic protection systems.
- Structural steel and aluminum fabrications and installations.
- Environmental and pollution control fabrications including
tanks.
- Remote generator systems complete with computer control and UPS
backup.
