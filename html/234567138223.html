Company Profile


Keylingo Translations is a professional, business-to-business translation and localization company. Adapting our clients business, marketing and legal communications into new languages helps them sell products and preform services more efficiently around the world. Our unique value proposition combines stellar customer service from a dedicated local office with a sophisticated, centralized operations center. While many firms may provide one or the other, Keylingo is unique in its ability to address both of these critical issues. In summary, Keylingo Translations helps businesses everywhere do business anywhere. 

Key / Major clients:

Komatsu, Coca-Cola, Rubbermaid, Studiocom, Scientific Games International, Special Olympics

Success stories:

One of the worlds largest earth-moving equipment manufacturers came calling with a large backlog of operator manuals requiring translation for expansion into a new market. The Komatsu project consisted of four million words of highly technical text produced in a sophisticated file format with hundreds of descriptive images and tons of specialized fonts. And, oh yeah, they needed the entire project completed in less than six months.

We were among three firms chosen to tackle this Everest of an assignment. As a final verification step, we were put on a thirty-day trial to prove ourselves against the other two vendors . . . a vendor playoff!

When the trial period was over, Keylingo came out as the clear victor! Komatsu consolidated the entire project with us. Not only did we beat the six-month deadline, we completed translation of the four million words, performed all necessary desktop publishing duties, and delivered print ready PDFs to the client thirty days early! Needless to say, the client was very happy and their international expansion a success.

Many things had to come together to pull this off. First, Keylingo provided an Account Manager Komatsu could trust. Someone who:
- Was responsive.
- Understood the scope and detail of the project.
- Could craft and explain the Keylingo solutions in an easily comprehensible manner.
- Maintained open lines of communication throughout the life of the project and served as the clients single point of contact.

Next, Keylingo assigned a Project Manager charged with:
- Facilitating collaboration across all project stakeholders.
- Managing the workflow of numerous large files in multiple formats, while ensuring accountability for project milestones.
- Gathering the team of translators, editors, proofreaders and DTP talent.
- Utilizing the technology that enabled us to reuse previously translated text. (And once we established a translation memory, translation costs dropped by as much as 80%Komatsu REALLY liked that!)

So there you have it. This project was about about as challenging as it gets, and Keylingo delivered results that exceeded the clients expectations. As a matter of fact, our relationship with Komatsu continues to this day. Words translated are now in the tens of millions, manuals produced are in the hundreds, and the smiles all around are countless. But dont take our word for it. If you have a translation project, give us a call or shoot us an email. Well provide a quote and even a sample translation. And heck, if youre really nice, well even let you talk with our friends at Komatsu, so they can tell you themselves how were the cats pajamas.

Testimonial:

"Thank you for giving Special Olympics the best service imaginable. You and your team surpassed all of my expectations. There is always that certain level of the unknown when working with a company for the first time. But Keylingo proved to me and our Board members and staff, that it is a leading company in the translation and interpretation field." - Joanne Maldonado: Global Event Manager, Special Olympics

"As Counsel for Asia for a US public company, I relied upon KeyLingo for translations of a tremendous amount of business and legal documentation in a very tight timeframe. The work was always superior and on time -- and we imposed what would normally be considered to be quite unreasonable time deadlines because of demands of our own customer. The work translated included technical manuals and documentation, business strategic plans, contracts, and other general commercial documents, including correspondence and documents prepared for our customer. The staff was always accessible, even with the time difference of twelve to thirteen hours. The online database of documents was also very useful in that more than one person could manage the translations from our end. My company has also used KeyLingo for translation work throughout our international businesses in several countries. I would highly recommend the services of Keylingo to those looking for top quality, timely and professional translation work." - Reiko Feaver: Counsel for Asia; Assistant General Counsel at Scientific Games International, Inc.

"I was under a tight time line to complete translations for more than 100 operator manuals in less than 3 months. Not only did Keylingo meet our aggressive time line, but also delivered a quality product. I was so impressed that I decided to exclusively use Keylingo for all our translation needs. Keylingo has instilled a culture of professionalism throughout the company that is second to none. I highly recommend Keylingo to anyone that is seeking a vendor that can deliver results." - John McFarland: Director Training & Publications at Komatsu America
