Company Profile


Mathieson Communications is a marketing and communications consulting firm. We provide strategic counsel and develop comprehensive marketing and communications programs for a range of clients, including small and medium-sized businesses, large enterprises and government departments and organizations. We design, plan and execute cost-effective solutions that work.

Our core services are:
 Media and public relations
 Marketing communications
 Lead generation and new business development
 Market research
 Trade show and conference support

We provide writing services to clients that include press releases, case studies for trade media and Website placement, marketing communications materials, proposals, policy papers, speeches, research reports, and business and marketing plans. Our consultative services include public and media relations strategies, market research, new business development plans, distribution channel and partner assessment, and trade show analysis and support.

Service Name:
Market Research
Our range of experience is broad. Our assignments have ranged
from competitive analysis to determining ideal business partner
candidates, to identifying potential prospects with corporate
acquisition goals closely matched to a client's business
objectives. Our clients include private sector and foreign
government trade delegations.

Service Name:
Trade Show and Conferences
We identify opportunities for clients, based on their market
development objectives or business partner relationships. Trade
shows are a tremendous undertaking. To ensure an organization
realizes full value on its investment - both employee time and
exhibit costs - it must choose the right trade shows, then
develop a strategic plan for each event. We help ensure our
clients maximize their effectiveness at trade shows. We assist
clients with:
. Setting clear objectives for trade show participation
. Selecting events most suited to objectives
. Promoting attendance to key customers, prospects and media
. Developing a leads generation campaign
. Analysing the return-on-investment


Service Name:
Market Channel Development
We help clients develop distributor and/or joint venture
relationships with OEMs, VARs and system integrators. Potential
channel partners are identified for the client using a multi-
tier approach focusing on geographic and vertical market
criteria. Coordinated media monitoring provides relevant market
and competitor-related intelligence for incorporation in
marketing plans.

Service Name:
Market Entry Strategies
We help clients understand market opportunities. We select and
define potential markets, calculate risks, calculate returns and
investment requirements, and create unique market positions for
technology offerings. We determine how to reach target customers
and create strategies that align the key tasks needed to reach
prospects with the marketing and sales resources (channels,
media, etc.) best suited for the tasks.

Service Name:
Marketing Plan Development
We assist clients with the preparation of a marketing plan to
ensure adequate attention is given to analysing opportunities.
We assess key market trends, vertical market opportunities,
analyse the client's strengths and weaknesses related to new
market development efforts, then recommend the best product
strategy develop an action plan and set priorities.


Service Name:
Lead Generation and Database Development
We develop integrated marketing programs using direct mail, telemarketing, fax, e mail and personal sales contact approaches to increase the productivity of our clients sales and marketing representatives, and to lower their costs of sales. We develop a viable contact management methodology and database of customers, prospects and business partners to help our clients increase their client retention, generate new business, and ultimately, increase their profitability.

Service Name:
Communications Strategy and Counsel
Media monitoring and regular briefing sessions with our clients to review pending or potential issues as well as opportunities are the core of our strategy and counsel services. We devise and implement results-oriented media management initiatives to ensure a client's position is accurately reported while arguments of opponents are successfully countered.

 Media training
 Research and competitive intelligence reports
 Stakeholder analysis and strategy
 Strategic partnership management
 Arrange speaker opportunities
