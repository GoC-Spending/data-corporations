Company Profile


For over 20 years businesses have trusted Microserve for;

Extensive experience and expertise in providing IT solutions across BC and Alberta.
A commitment to excellence in all of our service offerings ensuring only the highest level of quality and client satisfaction.
A focus on relationships, and dedication to supporting the long-term best interests of our clients.
Microserve is one of the largest independent IT solutions providers in Western Canada. Rooted in a philosophy of long-term partnerships and continuous innovation, our outstanding team has delivered IT solutions to clients across BC and Alberta for more than 20 years. Based in Burnaby BC, we also have branches in Victoria, Edmonton, and Calgary in addition to service networks across both Provinces.

Our local management allows our customers easy access to decision makers and high-level executives within Microserve, including our President Spud Matthews. Microserve endeavours to empower employees through a flat organizational structure, which is deeply entrenched in our corporate culture.

We are committed to excellence in everything that we do. This commitment to maintaining a high level of expertise in the constantly changing IT industry and to developing long-term relationships has allowed us to establish and maintain our strong market position year after year.

Why Hire from Microserve?
Our team at Microserve has worked in the IT industry for over 20 years and we understand the technical and soft skills it takes to see businesses succeed. Microserve is committed to screening all candidates through technical interviews to ensure that our clients are consistently satisfied with the resources we provide.

Microserve offers contract, temp-to-perm, and permanent staffing services across all IT disciplines and all levels. Our focus is on developing the best IT staffing solution and hiring the best staff to ensure that your goals are met. We will present you with a select number of strong candidates whom we have personally interviewed and qualified to match your needs on both a technical and soft skills basis.

Candidate Assessment
Armed with an in-depth understanding and knowledge of our clients business needs, we follow a comprehensive resource pre-screening process which includes as applicable;

Develop a position description and/or profile, which will reflect the responsibilities of the position, as well as the experience and the skills required.
Activate multiple recruitment channels, including but not limited to direct recruiting, referrals, multimedia advertising, searching our internal database and external databases, in addition to other networking.
Schedule an interview with potential candidates to better understand their interests and personality.
Consult with a member of our technical team to validate the candidates skill set, and possibly arrange for separate technical interviews.
Conduct three professional reference checks prior to resource submission to the client.
Guide the candidate through a personality profile evaluation to help ensure that the resource is a good cultural as well as technical fit.
Ensure that the resource has met all the required security clearance for the position.
