Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Matcha powder
Matcha is made from stone-ground, dried and young green tea leaves of Camellia sinensis plants. It is emerald green colour with a refreshing flavor and is rich in antioxidants especially Polyphenol. Main applications in foods include ice-cream, beverages, lattè, shakes, bakery products, cookies, pies, confections, yogurt, cakes, desserts, icing, puddings, cereals, liquors and nutritional bars. Available in 10 kg or 20 kg packing. Kosher certified.

Product Name:
Green lipped mussel powder, defatted
A free-flowing fine tan to green powder derived from Greenshell mussels sourced from the clean and green waters of New Zealand. It is lightly defatted which increases the shelf life and enables better solubility and mixing capability with other ingredients. It is mainly used as an ingredient in pet food products.

Product Name:
Durian powder
Freeze-dried Durian powder from Thailand or Malaysia having a natural sweet Durian fruit flavor and taste. It is yellowish to white-yellowish color.

Product Name:
French Macaroon Base
Natural powder blend designed to create delicious macaroon cookies with just 5 minutes’ prep time. This off-white powder is ground very finely to enable macaroons that are the perfect combination of soft and chewy with a hard-smooth shell. The macaroon base is reconstituted by adding warm water and coloring of choice. The final product will retain a smooth, dome shape.


Product Name:
Organic Matcha
USDA certified organic Matcha powder made from stone-ground, dried and young green tea leaves of Camellia sinensis plants. It is emerald green colour with a refreshing flavor and is rich in antioxidants especially Polyphenol. Main applications in foods include ice-cream, beverages, lattè, shakes, bakery products, cookies, pies, confections, yogurt, cakes, desserts, icing, puddings, cereals, liquors and nutritional bars. Available in 5 kg or 20 kg packing.

Product Name:
Gelatine
LomaNutra's Gelatine (bovine or porcine source) is specifically designed for canned, wet or frozen pet food products. Gelatine and protein flours help to increase the protein content of pet food. Gelatine, together with protein flours, help to increase the protein content of pet food. Another important use of gelatine in pet food is the capacity in stabilizing wet food stuffs, as it reduces the quantity of free water, leading to an increase in stability and quality of the final product. Origin: EU countries.

Product Name:
Hydrolysed Collagen
Sourced from an international leader in the production of Hydrolyzed Collagen and with 50 years of successful activity in the industry, LomaNutra's Hydrolysed Collagen is an indispensable ingredient for the health of our pets - our four-legged friends. Research into animal feeding has established that Hydrolysed Collagen helps them combat the wearing down of bones and joints, and to maintain a healthy and shiny fur. Origin: EU countries.

Service Name:
FORMULATOR™ Software
A Dynamic Windows Software Program for the Food Technologist.
LomaNutra Inc. is the Canadian Reseller for FORMULATOR™ Software.
• All the features and functions to build formulas on an actual basis—% weight, % volume, multi level, multi-analytical.
• Nutrient tables and calculations are defined; Nutrient properties stored by raw material ingredient.
• Identification and filtering of the raw material file. Used for Parve, Kosher and other user defined raw material properties.
• Allows “what if” nutrient target performance.
• Quality Control Method & Procedure Maintenance.
• Costing analysis as you build the recipe—automatically—by the pound, by the gallon, or by any weight or volume measure.
• Formula Tree Viewers with cut and paste, 99 explosion levels, and multiple formula windows.
• Explodes the full intermediate structure of one or two formulas on an actual weights and volumes (or mixed), on a % weight, % volume—user may switch modes with the click of a mouse.
• Allows changing items: Cut and paste from one formula to another—at multiple levels or within a structure
