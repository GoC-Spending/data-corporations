Company Profile


London Machinery Inc. is a manufacturer of truck mounted concrete
mixers. Manufacturing facilities are located in London, Ontario
and sales cover markets worldwide. The company employs 120
people of which 90 are hourly and 40 are salaried. The staff
includes people of many skills including mechanical engineers,
mobile engineering technologists, professional purchasing and
business management skills. The majority of the salaried staff
have been with the company for more than 15 years. Hourly rated
employees belong to C.A.W. Local 27. The company enjoys a good
working relationship with the local union and its
representatives. The union membership also includes a large core
of people with long service with the company.

HISTORY:

London Concrete Machinery Inc. was formed in 1906 to manufacture
and sell a line of concrete handling equipment. The company was
purchased in 1964 by P.W.E. Hodgson. As the product lines
developed and volume increased, a need arose for more space. In
1974, the company moved to it's present location at
485 McCormick Blvd. with 110,000 square feet of space. In
December 1989, the assets of London Machinery Company Limited
were sold to a new company, London Machinery Inc. The
shareholders of this company represent the senior management of
the predescessor company with the addition of one outside
investor with significant mobile equipment manufacturing
experience. In July 1991, the Company acquired mixer inventory,
drawings, customer lists and other related assets of Jaeger
Equipment. Jaeger had been London's primary Canadian competitor
of mixers. As a result of the acquisition, the Company became
the only significant manufacturer of concrete mixers in Canada.
Over the past 30 years the Company has positioned itself as the
dominant Canadian manufacturer of concrete mixers.

In June 1996, we introduced a newly designed combination
dump/spreader to lthe Canadian market. This product has a
capacity to carry 14 cubic yards of payload. This product has
been well received by our customers and we look forward to a
significant increase in market share for this product line.

IMPORTANT PRODUCT FEATURES AND COMPARISON

London Machinery manufactures the highest quality equipment
available on the market today. The Company's goal of exceeding
customer's expectations provides strong motivation for continous
improvement.
