Company Profile


LGL is one of North Americas leading ecological research companies. Founded in 1971, we have built our reputation conducting benchmark environmental studies and providing expert advice. LGL provides biological consulting services to the public and private sectors. LGL maintains offices in King City, Ontario; Cambridge, Ontario; Sidney, British Columbia; St. John's, Newfoundland; Anchorage, Alaska; Bryan Texas; Santa Barbara, California; and Vladivostok, Russia. LGL Limited is owned and managed by its respective employees. From hands-on resource inventories and biological assessments to digital mapping to project design and management, our team of experts can deliver customized products - and solutions - for a wide range of environmental challenges.

Since 1971, LGL Limited has combined proven methodology and state-of-the-art technology to achieve - and exceed - our clients' goals and objectives. We adhere to strict Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) and we're registered with PICS, COMPLYWORKS, and ISN.

LGL maintains an in-house staff of about 100 professionals with expertise in a broad range of disciplines, including birds, mammals (terrestrial and marine) and fish; freshwater, marine and wetland ecology; disturbance effects; environmental assessment; and data analysis. Several staff members are world experts in their fields, regularly participating in international committees and conferences; they also publish regularly in the primary scientific literature.

LGL offers expertise in many disciplines, including ecosystem research in terrestrial, aquatic and marine habitats, environmental planning and assessment, and resource management. LGL has expertise with many field and laboratory methodologies, field logistics, subcontract management, data processing, and analysis. LGL has developed strong expertise in Geographic Information Systems (GIS).

A key to LGL's success has been the stability of the company, resulting in low staff turnover. Several staff members in each
major office have been with LGL for 15-30 years. LGL continues to hire promising new employees to expand the firm's capabilities in areas such as GIS, environmental assessment and fisheries management.
