Company Profile


KOM Networks is the world leader of innovative Storage Management Software solutions and is recognized as the leading authority in the technological advancement of Data Protection & Retention. KOM Networks storage solutions provide Compliance, Archiving, and Information Lifecycle Management (ILM) to customers around the world and our 24/7 support is considered an industry benchmark.

KOMpliance, KOMs flagship product, is a network attached file archive appliance that protects and preserves files using our patented e-WORM technology to create compliant WORM storage volumes on any hard disk or solid state disk storage that may be directly attached, iSCSI attached, or SAN attached. KOMpliance is a true, turn-key solution with everything you need to install, deploy and administer a secure storage environment included right out of the box while leveraging existing infrastructure and expertise.

Since KOMpliance runs on Windows Storage Server 2008 R2, which comes pre-installed, it integrates readily into all Active Directory Services, enforcing all group policies and ACLs for painless deployment and streamlined setup. KOMpliance is easy to configure with intuitive management and so it reduces the administrative overhead associated with data archiving and regulatory compliance such as managing data growth, implementing an ILM strategy or establishing a disaster recovery plan  saving our customers time and money. This lets users immediately get back to business without any time-consuming training and babysitting setup.

KOM Networks has been recognized by Microsoft with an award for Excellence in Advanced Infrastructure for the hard disk based archive solutions powered by Windows Storage Server. Many of the most well known world leading hardware and software technology companies collaborate with KOM Networks to develop innovative enterprise solutions for archiving, compliance and information lifecycle management. KOM Networks products are integrated into solutions offering archiving for SAP, e-mail, SharePoint , Enterprise Content Management, Document Management, Electronic Health Records, PACS Imaging, and Financial Applications, as well as standard files.

Over 10,000 of the world's leading corporations have recognized KOM Networks as the most logical, unobtrusive and secure way to store, access and protect their data. KOM is enabling enterprises large and small a cost effective way to improve productivity and meet compliance requirements without altering their network infrastructure or daily business routines. The industry pioneer holds vital industry patents for electronic file lifecycle management, virtual file management and eWORM fixed content hard disk archiving. Find out how KOM Networks can help you implement a comprehensive information lifecycle solution to optimize your existing storage environment and meet your compliance requirements.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
OptiServer
OptiServer® is a UNIX based storage management software for archiving and accessing mission critical data. Many organizations such as NASA, Merrill Lynch, Federal Election Commission and leading lottery organizations depend on OptiServer to archive and manage their sensitive and mission critical data.

OptiServer enables compliance by writing data directly to a WORM disk for legal admissibility. There is no learning curve as OptiStorm allows use of already familiar system commands and security features of the UNIX operating system to store data on optical media and jukeboxes.

OptiServer operates on the HP-UX, Tru64, SUN Solaris platforms and supports various removable media such as Magneto-Optical (MO), CD and DVD, in both WORM and Re-Writable formats.


Key features and benefits:
Compliant: OptiServer directly writes to WORM media for legal admissibility and helps organizations comply with regulations such as the Sarbanes-Oxley Act, SEC 17a4, HIPAA, DoD 5015.2 among others

Long-term Archiving: Data can be transparently archived to optical media such as WORM, Ablative WORM, CD and DVD.

Transparent: OptiServer emulates optical disks as standard hard disks and allows administrators to use familiar system UNIX commands.

Non-Proprietary: OptiServer operates on a standard UNIX Platform and uses native file systems (UFS, ADVFS) and existing UNIX commands.

Secure
C2 level security and user ID based access control is provided. Files cannot be tampered with or modified.

Portable: OptiServer creates self-contained media volumes. Media can be removed and stored offsite for disaster recovery, if needed.




Product Name:
KOMworx OptiStorm
KOMworx ®, KOM Networks flagship product is a Windows based suite of products that offers storage management software solutions with extensive capabilities such as Compliance, Archiving and Information Lifecycle Management (ILM). KOMworx patented technology extends simplified storage management to third party applications such as E-mail archiving, Healthcare and Financial including compliance and file retention.

KOMworx OptiStorm combines all the benefits of removal storage while emulating the access, convenience and functionality of hard disk drives. OptiStorm supports C2 level security and also extends Windows support to non-standard optical storage technologies.

OptiStorm supports removable media such as UDO, ProDATA, Magneto-Optical (MO), CD and DVD, in both WORM and Re-Writable formats. OptiStorm allows users or applications to save and access files to media in the same way they would to a magnetic disk.

Key features and benefits:

Transparent: Emulates optical disks as standard hard disks making them transparently accessible using native file system commands and APIs.

Easy to Use: OptiStorm is easily managed through user friendly and intuitive KOM Management Console.

Compliant: Direct-Write ability to unalterable WORM media enables compliance and provides organizations with legal admissibility.

Simple: OptiStorm creates Virtual Disks on optical media. Virtual Disks imitate standard hard disks and simplify archiving to all Write-Once Removable media.

Flexible: Virtual Disks are readily accessible as Drive Letters, Shares and Patented Drive-Names. Drive-Names are unique and transportable with the media from one server to another.

Non-Proprietary: Optistorm supports all Windows Native File Systems (NTFS, FAT-32, FAT) and UDF. Full support includes disk formatting, file level security and extended filename.

Media Portability: Fixed Content Data created on self-contained media can be removed and stored offsite for disaster recovery.

Software Toolkit: OptiStorm Software Development Kit is available for ISV and OEMs that need to integrate the functionality into their own solutions.


Product Name:
OptiFile
OptiFile® is a VMS based easy to use storage management software that archives data and enables compliance. OptiFile supports C2 level security and is specifically designed to meet data archival needs for mission critical applications.

In 1983, KOM Networks pioneered the OptiFile technology that enabled an optical media to be managed as standard magnetic hard drive and the ability to Direct-Write to a Magneto-Optical (MO) disk.

OptiFile supports HP VAX based computers, Alpha servers and workstations, WORM, Re-writable, Ablative WORM, Optical Jukeboxes and standalone drives.



Key features and benefits:
Compliant: OptiServer directly writes to WORM media for legal admissibility and helps organizations comply with regulations such as the Sarbanes-Oxley Act, SEC 17a4, HIPAA, DoD 5015.2 among others.

Long-term Archiving: Data can be transparently archived to optical media such as WORM, Ablative WORM, CD and DVD.

Secure: OptiFile supports C2 level security through HP Open VMS. Files can be programmed as Read-Only access to prevent data from being altered or modified.

Flexible: Extends Open VMS to support non-standard optical storage technologies.

Transparent: OptiFile solution is completely transparent as users continue to apply standard Open VMS commands to access data.

Portable: Data created on self-contained media can be removed and stored offsite for disaster recover.

KOMpliance employs patented Hard Disk WORM technology and encryption to meet the most stringent mandates for tamperproof data protection, data authentication and data security in any operating environment using any combination of HDD and SSD iSCSI SAN attached storage.

Its all-inclusive feature set streamlines acquisition, delivers rapid deployment, easy storage management, comprehensive data security, flexible configurations, enterprise-class performance and reliability, and seamless pay-as-you grow expansion.

Everything you need to know to use KOMpliance with any application in any environment to protect and secure files is knowledge and expertise you already have. This lowers the total cost of ownership (TCO) with an all-inclusive turnkey solution that leverages any storage infrastructure.

A business grade NAS (Network Attached Storage server) running Windows Storage Server 2008 R2 and the KOMworx storage management software suite, KOMpliance provides the kind of bulletproof protection, comprehensive compliance, and intuitive policy based storage management interface that small organizations and branch offices need.

This turnkey solution includes RAID 5 storage, either internal or external, is available in base capacities starting at 1TB, and can be set up on your network in as little as 30 minutes. Additional storage capacity can easily be added as needed to accommodate increasing data and application storage needs. As such, it is ideal for SMBs and Enterprises that do not have excess SAN storage capacity but need to add secure, regulatory compliant network storage capacity. Buy only what you need today and easily expand as you grow.

KOMpliance helps businesses save time and money by eliminating the need to manage separate complex and proprietary storage silos. Most businesses have to cope with ongoing budgetary constraints that impact their ability to deploy secure storage solutions and impede compliance. Smaller businesses with limited financial and technical resources can benefit immediately deploying KOMpliance, a Windows NAS file server, that simply works with their existing applications and information systems framework.

KOMpliance SAN Gateway
For customers who already own a SAN, adding a KOMpliance Gateway lets you leverage its full benefits, flexibility and capabilities and adhere to strict compliance and data retention regulations and rules with the security of WORM tamperproof storage volumes. KOMpliance makes it easy to protect your data on the storage you already own. Whether you need a simple secure archive or complex retention, KOMpliance makes it possible with a few clicks in a familiar Windows setting!

Simply deploy KOMpliance alongside any iSCSI SAN array and allocate a LUN to create a secure WORM capable volume that is readily accessible on the network as a standard NAS file server share. Use the SAN storage resources that you are already familiar with and trust to create secure, encrypted, tamperproof volumes protecting valuable digital assets and files to meet your business, industry and regulatory requirements.

KOMpliance Rich Features and Capabilities
KOMpliance was designed to address the widest range of compliance regulations and requirements with minimal effort and impact, and includes:

 File retention enforcement
 Tamperproof WORM data security and protection with flexible file level policies
 Enhanced security with built-in data encryption and secure data shredding

Each KOMpliance volume is Encrypted for data privacy to meet all data-at-rest security requirements and protect data from unauthorized access. Whether your requirements are driven by SEC, HIPAA, PIPEDA, PCI or NIST; KOMpliance ensures you meet them. Managing risks, limiting liability and meeting regulations is easy with KOMpliance.

The KOMpliance Windows NAS file server interface delivers a high-performance, reliable and affordable storage platform that creates secure WORM archive storage volumes on any Hard Disk Drive (HDD) or Solid State Drive (SSD) Storage arrays regardless of whether they are direct or SAN attached.

KOMpliances Management Console uses an intuitive look-and-feel interface that is immediately familiar to Windows Administrators. It leverages the Windows Eco-System with full out-of-the-box pre-configuration for all file sharing standards and operations. Using the wide range of customizable options, administrators can easily create and implement File Lifecycle Management policies that meet all applicable compliance requirements, and apply them to specific classes of files and even individual files. These intelligent, rules-based policies can also be easily modified whenever needed  with the exception of course that retention periods can never be shortened.

Data Privacy is assured with a number of features including AES-256 encryption and wide range of folder and volume management policies that control every aspect of every operation. Every file retained has an SHA-256 digital signature for Data Authentication. Data Security is enforced with standard compliant data shredding that is performed automatically as part of every delete operation - overwriting the contents and shredding sensitive data.

Our regulatory compliant retention and secure storage management software enhances data security for a wide range of enterprise applications such as Content and Document Management Systems, E-mail Archiving, Financial Applications, SharePoint and SAP, as well as Electronic Health Records (EHR) and Medical applications including RIS/PACs, EKG and Fetal Monitoring Systems. Our patented technologies can provide permanent file retention and protection on any storage device (RAID, SAN, iSCSI, Optical and Tape).

Product Name:
KOMworx Enterprise
KOMworx® Enterprise for Windows provides a policy based, dynamically managed ILM solution that responds to an organization's ever changing business and applications requirements. KOMworx Enterprise adapts to the changing IT landscape, enhancing performance, optimizing utilization and consolidating storage. KOMworx aggregates DAS, NAS or SAN devices into a homogenous Virtual Volume, providing a single namespace that is easily accessible by all users and applications.

KOMworx Enterprise extends storage management to healthcare, financial, ECM and E-mail archiving applications and serves the SMB, Healthcare & Life Sciences, Financial Services, Manufacturing, Aerospace, Education, Government, Defense and Retail industries.

Key features and benefits:
Simple Storage Management: KOMworx one pathname across different networks allows for easy access and freedom of naming convention. Disparate storage devices are aggregated into an easy to manage Virtual Volume represented by a single namespace.

Efficient: Administrators can configure data lifecycle policies with easy to use KOMworx Centralized Management Console. Once set, fixed content data is archived, distributed and migrated automatically.

Hard Disk Archiving: Unalterable e-WORM archiving (WORM on magnetic hard disk) is provided for secure long-term data storage.

Flexible Media Usage: Secure duplication and replication is provided on industry leading removable media such as Optical, Tape, CD and DVD.

Maximum Protection: KOMworx Enterprise offers maximum fixed content data protection with features such as e-WORM (Hard Disk protection), ILM enforced retention and enhanced file and directory protection.

Dynamic Capacity Management: KOMworx Enterprise dynamically shrinks and expands capacity based on storage requirements. New storage devices can be phased in while older devices phased out transparently.

Scalable: Using KOMworx Enterprise, customers can archive up to 500 petabytes of data. Hardware devices or storage media are the only limitation.

Cost Effective
Komworx Enterprise optimizes storage capacity by automatically load sharing between storage devices to improve device utilization and performance. This efficient use of available resources results in
reduced total cost of ownership and increased data throughput.

Product Name:
OptiServer
OptiServer® is a UNIX based storage management software for archiving and accessing mission critical data. Many organizations such as NASA, Merrill Lynch, Federal Election Commission and leading lottery organizations depend on OptiServer to archive and manage their sensitive and mission critical data.

OptiServer enables compliance by writing data directly to a WORM disk for legal admissibility. There is no learning curve as OptiStorm allows use of already familiar system commands and security features of the UNIX operating system to store data on optical media and jukeboxes.

OptiServer operates on the HP-UX, Tru64, SUN Solaris platforms and supports various removable media such as Magneto-Optical (MO), CD and DVD, in both WORM and Re-Writable formats.


Key features and benefits:
Compliant: OptiServer directly writes to WORM media for legal admissibility and helps organizations comply with regulations such as the Sarbanes-Oxley Act, SEC 17a4, HIPAA, DoD 5015.2 among others

Long-term Archiving: Data can be transparently archived to optical media such as WORM, Ablative WORM, CD and DVD.

Transparent: OptiServer emulates optical disks as standard hard disks and allows administrators to use familiar system UNIX commands.

Non-Proprietary: OptiServer operates on a standard UNIX Platform and uses native file systems (UFS, ADVFS) and existing UNIX commands.

Secure
C2 level security and user ID based access control is provided. Files cannot be tampered with or modified.

Portable: OptiServer creates self-contained media volumes. Media can be removed and stored offsite for disaster recovery, if needed.




Product Name:
KOMworx OptiStorm
KOMworx ®, KOM Networks flagship product is a Windows based suite of products that offers storage management software solutions with extensive capabilities such as Compliance, Archiving and Information Lifecycle Management (ILM). KOMworx patented technology extends simplified storage management to third party applications such as E-mail archiving, Healthcare and Financial including compliance and file retention.

KOMworx OptiStorm combines all the benefits of removal storage while emulating the access, convenience and functionality of hard disk drives. OptiStorm supports C2 level security and also extends Windows support to non-standard optical storage technologies.

OptiStorm supports removable media such as UDO, ProDATA, Magneto-Optical (MO), CD and DVD, in both WORM and Re-Writable formats. OptiStorm allows users or applications to save and access files to media in the same way they would to a magnetic disk.

Key features and benefits:

Transparent: Emulates optical disks as standard hard disks making them transparently accessible using native file system commands and APIs.

Easy to Use: OptiStorm is easily managed through user friendly and intuitive KOM Management Console.

Compliant: Direct-Write ability to unalterable WORM media enables compliance and provides organizations with legal admissibility.

Simple: OptiStorm creates Virtual Disks on optical media. Virtual Disks imitate standard hard disks and simplify archiving to all Write-Once Removable media.

Flexible: Virtual Disks are readily accessible as Drive Letters, Shares and Patented Drive-Names. Drive-Names are unique and transportable with the media from one server to another.

Non-Proprietary: Optistorm supports all Windows Native File Systems (NTFS, FAT-32, FAT) and UDF. Full support includes disk formatting, file level security and extended filename.

Media Portability: Fixed Content Data created on self-contained media can be removed and stored offsite for disaster recovery.

Software Toolkit: OptiStorm Software Development Kit is available for ISV and OEMs that need to integrate the functionality into their own solutions.


Product Name:
OptiFile
OptiFile® is a VMS based easy to use storage management software that archives data and enables compliance. OptiFile supports C2 level security and is specifically designed to meet data archival needs for mission critical applications.

In 1983, KOM Networks pioneered the OptiFile technology that enabled an optical media to be managed as standard magnetic hard drive and the ability to Direct-Write to a Magneto-Optical (MO) disk.

OptiFile supports HP VAX based computers, Alpha servers and workstations, WORM, Re-writable, Ablative WORM, Optical Jukeboxes and standalone drives.



Key features and benefits:
Compliant: OptiServer directly writes to WORM media for legal admissibility and helps organizations comply with regulations such as the Sarbanes-Oxley Act, SEC 17a4, HIPAA, DoD 5015.2 among others.

Long-term Archiving: Data can be transparently archived to optical media such as WORM, Ablative WORM, CD and DVD.

Secure: OptiFile supports C2 level security through HP Open VMS. Files can be programmed as Read-Only access to prevent data from being altered or modified.

Flexible: Extends Open VMS to support non-standard optical storage technologies.

Transparent: OptiFile solution is completely transparent as users continue to apply standard Open VMS commands to access data.

Portable: Data created on self-contained media can be removed and stored offsite for disaster recover.

Product Name:
KOMworx Enterprise
KOMworx® Enterprise for Windows provides a policy based, dynamically managed ILM solution that responds to an organization's ever changing business and applications requirements. KOMworx Enterprise adapts to the changing IT landscape, enhancing performance, optimizing utilization and consolidating storage. KOMworx aggregates DAS, NAS or SAN devices into a homogenous Virtual Volume, providing a single namespace that is easily accessible by all users and applications.

KOMworx Enterprise extends storage management to healthcare, financial, ECM and E-mail archiving applications and serves the SMB, Healthcare & Life Sciences, Financial Services, Manufacturing, Aerospace, Education, Government, Defense and Retail industries.

Key features and benefits:
Simple Storage Management: KOMworx one pathname across different networks allows for easy access and freedom of naming convention. Disparate storage devices are aggregated into an easy to manage Virtual Volume represented by a single namespace.

Efficient: Administrators can configure data lifecycle policies with easy to use KOMworx Centralized Management Console. Once set, fixed content data is archived, distributed and migrated automatically.

Hard Disk Archiving: Unalterable e-WORM archiving (WORM on magnetic hard disk) is provided for secure long-term data storage.

Flexible Media Usage: Secure duplication and replication is provided on industry leading removable media such as Optical, Tape, CD and DVD.

Maximum Protection: KOMworx Enterprise offers maximum fixed content data protection with features such as e-WORM (Hard Disk protection), ILM enforced retention and enhanced file and directory protection.

Dynamic Capacity Management: KOMworx Enterprise dynamically shrinks and expands capacity based on storage requirements. New storage devices can be phased in while older devices phased out transparently.

Scalable: Using KOMworx Enterprise, customers can archive up to 500 petabytes of data. Hardware devices or storage media are the only limitation.

Cost Effective
Komworx Enterprise optimizes storage capacity by automatically load sharing between storage devices to improve device utilization and performance. This efficient use of available resources results in
reduced total cost of ownership and increased data throughput.
