Company Profile


Since 1997 StepForth has provided web site marketing services to clients in its home town of Victoria BC and all over the world; from the USA to the UK to Bangkok and Australia. The end goal of every one of our services is to increase the visibility and most importantly the profits of our client companies.

Our services cover the spectrum of services required to successfully promote the web sites of small and medium sized businesses anywhere in the English speaking world. Such services include all forms of search engine optimization (local, organic and YouTube), pay-per-click (PPC) marketing, Google Analytics analysis & reporting, blog/site content creation, research and competitor analysis, and most importantly short and long term strategy.

For more information please visit our site and submit a request for a no obligation, free consultation reviewing your business needs.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Web Site Audit
It could be that your web site was optimized at one time for search engine rankings but by todays standard it has fallen short and requires some reworking to bring it up to the level required to compete in the search engines.

When your web site is properly optimized it will have a better chance of obtaining the kind of higher quality visitors that lead to increased sales/conversions.

Our Web Site Audit covers over 120 aspects of your site (and growing) in order to identify ANY issue that may be impeding its success in the search engines.

It is very important to note every one of our web site audits are written to provide you with a take-away list of prioritized tasks which, when completed, will put your web site in a far better position to be found in Google and in-turn increase sales for your business. You do not need us to implement our findings for you - we are here to help if need be but we always write our reports to be as portable as possible.

Contact us for a free, no-obligation consultation.

Service Name:
Web Marketing Consulting
If you would like guidance at any step of your web marketing process then there may not be a need to enter into a long term contract for services. In this case our hourly web marketing consulting services may be a perfect fit for you. These services can include any and all of our services from a consultative standpoint.

Service Name:
Pay Per Click Marketing
StepForth provides extremely high-level pay-per-click marketing services for great value. Our services include Google AdWords setup and management as well as Bing, Facebook, and more. Here is a sampling of what we do for you when we manage your paid marketing campaigns:

> Create and fully set up your pay per click (PPC) accounts.

> Research the most relevant keyword phrase(s) that best describe your products or services.

> Determine possible niche markets that you may be able to tap.

> Create targeted, keyword rich ad copy for your campaigns.

> Management of your PPC account(s) with regular tweaking of your ad campaigns to ensure the best possible results.

> Adjust your cost per click rates and daily budgets to ensure that your placement is not only ranking optimally, but also to keep you on track with your monthly budget.

> Send you detailed, monthly reports with the status of your ad campaign, complete with any information on necessary changes, new initiatives and progress.

If you would like more information please contact us for a free, no-obligation consultation. We even offer a free review of any applicable PPC campaigns to see if we can even offer any benefit - if you are doing everything right you don't need us and we will be the first to admit it!

Service Name:
Competitor Analysis
StepForth will show you why your competitors have higher rankings and we will create a plan to beat them at their own game. This service is only conducted by our most senior staff to provide the most in-depth and insightful search engine marketing strategies.

After we have completed our analysis of your competitors we will provide a detailed report. The size of your Competitor Analysis Report depends on a few factors such as: how many competitors you have us analyze, how large each competitor is, the complexity of the discovered marketing techniques (more space maybe required to explain them) and how much work is required to bring your own website to a place where domination is realistic.

Service Name:
Charity Internet Marketing Services
Your charitable website should be positioned to educate potential supporters on your cause, show your current supporters the direct effect of their support, and assist you in generating donations. By utilizing the options web marketing has to offer, you can see the results, and spend less. In order to assist charities and non-profits with this, we are extending all of our services to these organizations at 40% off their regular price (i.e. $75/hr after discount for most of our professional marketing services)

Service Name:
Social Media Marketing
StepForth offers a full range of social media services. If you want to run your campaigns in-house and just need a little help getting started, we can help you with that. If you want us to manage everything and report back to you once a month, in some cases were equipped to do that, too.

Service Name:
Social Media Consulting and Training
We want to see your social media campaigns succeed. Each platform has its own set of etiquette; navigating your way through these communities can seem overwhelming at the beginning. StepForth will provide you with the necessary resources and training to help you build your brand and drive more traffic to your website.

StepForth offers customized, one-on-one social media consulting and training to fit your needs and level of involvement. We can walk you through the process step-by-step and give you practical tips to engage your audience and grow your business.
