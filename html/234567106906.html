Company Profile


Waterite Technologies Inc. is a national distributor / manufacturer of water treatment components and systems, with offices in Manitoba, British Columbia, Quebec and Ontario. We service retail, commercial, institutional, and municipal markets.
Our products include water softening systems and components, filtration medias, media filter systems, ultraviolet disinfection systems, reverse osmosis components and systems, filters, filtration systems and components.

Filter Housings

Product Name:
Chemical Dosing Pumps
Waterite is an exclusive dealer for Pulsatron and Pulsafeeeder diaghram pumps, featuring many models, capacities and control options. We also stock parts and accessories.

Product Name:
Filter Cartridges
Waterite's own ExcelPure & Hypurion Cartridge Filter brands come in many nominal and absolute micron sizes, and filter cartridge lengths.
Types include String Wound, Polypropylene Spun, Depth and Pleated styles. GAC and PAC, Dual Gradient cartridges and Bag Filters. Available in bulk orders.
Waterite also carries a number of other manufacturers cartridges , Pentek, Flowmatic, Harmsco, and GE.
