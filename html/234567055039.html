Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Static Dispersion Compensation Module
The ClearSpectrum™- Static Dispersion Compensation Module (CS-DCML) is the static chromatic dispersion compensator offering the lowest latency on the market for high-speed DWDM networks. Built in a very small footprint, this module can provide high levels of compensation while maintaining a considerably lower insertion loss than dispersion compensation fiber.

Product Name:
Chromatic Dispersion Emulator
The ClearSpectrum™- Chromatic Dispersion Emulator (CDE) is well-suited for test and measurement. This 1U half 19-inch rack-mountable module is designed to emulate tens of thousands of picoseconds per nanometer in a compact unit while maintaining very low insertion loss. It is a passive, ready-to-use emulator which can be cascaded several times to achieve higher levels of dispersion.

Product Name:
Dispersion Compensator Light
The ClearSpectrum™- Dispersion Compensator Light (CS-DCL) is the smallest passive chromatic dispersion compensator of the industry. Offering low latency and insertion loss, the component is well-suited for integration within compact direct detection transponder environments such as in 4 x 28 Gb/s CFPs. Available in both OEM and module versions, the CS-DCL exhibits the same high level optical performances that have made TeraXion's reputation over the years.

Product Name:
High-Level Dispersion Compensator
The ClearSpectrum™- High-Level Dispersion Compensator (CS-HLDC) is a static chromatic dispersion compensation module for coherent communication ultra long-haul networks. This 1U half-rack module is specially designed to compensate a large amount of dispersion while maintaining a very low insertion loss. TeraXion’s well-established technology in Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) translates into a reliable and compact solution based on a cascade of several Telcordia-qualified gratings. This RoHS compliant solution offers a wide 50 GHz operation bandwidth regardless of the level of dispersion making it perfectly suited for substantial dispersion compensation of 40 and 100 Gb/s coherent detection links.

Product Name:
Slope-Matched Tunable Dispersion Compensator
The ClearSpectrum™ - Slope-Matched Tunable Dispersion Compensator (CS-TDCMX-SM) is a compact, fully integrated optical module that provides accurate and dynamic control of chromatic dispersion for all channels in optical communication networks.
It features a wide bandwidth and a large dispersion tuning range, enabling a reach of up to 80 km in 50 and 100 GHz channel grids. It is a valued solution for data center interconnects, 100 Gb/s direct detection, and in-line dispersion compensation in DWDM agile networks or for fine dispersion trimming at terminals
TeraXion’s CS-TDCMX-SM integrates both circulator and complete electronics in a compact module for increased system flexibility, enabling higher operation speeds and improved design margins.

Product Name:
Benchtop Tunable Dispersion Compensation Module
The ClearSpectrum™ - Benchtop Tunable Dispersion Compensation Module (CS-TDCMB) is a tunable, fully integrated emulator / compensator that provides accurate dynamic control of chromatic dispersion for any channel in high-speed communication networks. It is speciﬁcally designed for R&D labs and production ﬂoor plans in applications such as transponder assembly testing.

Product Name:
Latchable Dispersion Compensator
The ClearSpectrum™- Latchable Dispersion Compensator (CS-LDC) is a compact latchable and adjustable dispersion compensator for dispersion correction of high-speed reconfigurable optical communications.


Product Name:
Reflectors for High-Power Fiber Lasers
The PowerSpectrum™- Reflectors for High-Power Fiber Lasers (PWS-HPR) reflectors are FBG-based mirrors that offer optimized heat dissipation and outstanding reliability for direct integration in industrial high-power fiber lasers. One high reflector (HR) and one output coupler (OC) (or low reflector) are combined as a pair of optical fiber mirrors to create a fiber laser cavity. The PowerSpectrum™-HPR reflectors can be tailored to customer specifications or manufactured from the following standard specifications listed below. PowerSpectrum™-HPRs with these standard specifications are stock at TeraXion.

Product Name:
Tunable Pulse Stretcher for ultrafast lasers
TeraXion’s PowerSpectrum™ - Tunable Pulse Stretcher for ultrafast lasers (PWS-TPSR) is an all-fiber device that provides full control of pulse duration and peak power in ultrafast lasers.
The TPSR lowers operation costs by simplifying the bulk optics alignment of a femtosecond laser’s compression stage and by reducing the need for hardware adjustment. It also greatly improves the robustness of the laser. Its flexible software options make it easy to integrate within any design.
TeraXion’s TPSR is available in two models to accurately match diffraction gratings (Treacy) or volume Bragg grating (VBG) compressors.

Product Name:
Narrowband Stabilizer for Semi-Conductor Lasers
The PowerSpectrum™- Narrowband Stabilizer for Semi-Conductor Lasers (PWS-NLS) is a narrowband stabilizer that can be used to make semiconductor lasers precisely tuned at specific wavelengths. Highly reliable and efficient, it is a display of high-quality manufacturing.

Product Name:
Dispersion Management Reflector
The PowerSpectrum™- Dispersion Management Reflector (PWS-DMR) is an all-fiber, FBG-based chromatic dispersion management device that can provide either anomalous or normal dispersion in a wide wavelength range.
Used as the output coupler (OC) of a mode-locked ultrafast oscillator, the PowerSpectrum™-DMR tailors the overall chromatic dispersion within the laser cavity, allowing control of the operation of the laser and the output pulse duration. The PowerSpectrum™-DMR can be built according to customers specifications or manufactured from the product standard specifications. PowerSpectrum™- DMRs with standard specifications are stock at TeraXion.

Product Name:
High-Accuracy Pulse Stretcher for High-End Ultrafast Lasers
The PowerSpectrum™- High-Accuracy Pulse Stretcher for High-End Ultrafast Lasers (PWS-HPSR) is a high accuracy component used in combination with a Treacy compressor for the chirped pulse amplification stage of ultrafast lasers. With a tighter chirp control, this high-end component improves the laser performance, alowing the amplification of shorter and cleaner pulses as low as 200 fs pulses, often required by medical applications. It also enables larger pulse energy required for faster material processing.
The PWS-HPSR is also available with a gain bandwidth enhancement option. This feature allows the spectral shaping of the input pulse to expand the gain bandwidth of some amplifiers and then improve the pulse duration of the seed laser.

Product Name:
Narrow Linewidth Laser
The PureSpectrum™- Narrow Linewidth Laser (NLL/NLL-E) is a compact, wavelength stabilized, ultra-low-noise and narrow-linewidth DFB semiconductor laser module. Its high wavelength stability optimizes the performance of laser-based sensing systems in applications such as distributed acoustic sensing, coherent OTDR, Oil and Gas - interferometric, phase shift applications: seismic/pressure/Brillouin temperature and strain and LIDAR systems.

Product Name:
Multi Channel Narrow Linewdth Laser System
The PureSpectrum™- Multi Channel Narrow Linewdth Laser System's (PS-MNL) rugged chassis is a fully integrated system comprising up to 10 laser modules. The PureSpectrum™-MNL's low-phase-noise characteristics are made possible through the use of state-of-the-art frequency noise control technology.

Product Name:
DFB Laser Module
The PureSpectrum™- DFB Laser Module (PS-LM) includes a DFB semiconductor laser diode, low-noise laser driver and temperature controller in a compact package suitable for integration in OEM instrumentation.

Product Name:
C-Band Tunable Optical Filter
The C-Band Tunable Optical Filter (TFC) is a narrowband optical filter offering wavelength tunability over the entire C-band with an excellent channel isolation. Based on TeraXion's FBG filter expertise and combined to our reliable wavelength tuning platform, this optical filter module offers a narrow and sharp transmission window which provides an outstanding channel isolation. Narrow band-pass full C-band filtering is required for applications such as signal tracking, sub-band filtering or noise suppression.

Product Name:
Ultra Narrowband Tunable Optical Filter
The Ultra Narrowband Tunable Optical Filter (TFN) combines TeraXion's narrowband FBG filtering capabilities with its compact and reliable wavelength tuning platform to enable high-bandwidth applications for analog RF photonic systems. Through precise dynamic tuning of the center wavelength, the component has been designed to allow matching of different RF modulation frequencies. Aside the extraction of RF sideband signals, the device may additionally achieve functions such as channelized receiver and Brillouin filtering.

Product Name:
Custom Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) Filter
TeraXion provides fibre Bragg grating filters intended to be typically used in fiber laser and semiconductor laser systems. These custom FBG filters can be designed to meet any specific requirement.

Product Name:
Industrial Fiber Sensor
Our unique writing technology outperforms the conventional FBG manufacturing techniques. Therefore, TeraXion’s FBG enables fiber sensing with longer reach, in harsher environments with lower cost interrogators.
Our offer includes FBGs arrays on pure silica fiber for harsh environment. Our sensors have higher reflectivity than draw tower FBGs, greater tensile strength than conventional FBGs as well as narrower bandwidths or complex spectrum for affordable high precision interrogator.

Product Name:
Medical Fiber Sensor
TeraXion has pushed further the Fiber Bragg Grating technology to offer a state-of-the-art solution for a safe, accurate and reliable fiber sensing.
Our technology outperforms the conventional FBG manufacturing techniques. Therefore, FBGs are written through the coating of the fiber, maintaining the fiber integrity. This technique enables a greater tensile strength over conventional FBGs, a better reliability and an easier sterilization. Moreover, our ultra-narrow bandwidth reflectors offer the best sensing precision available today.
