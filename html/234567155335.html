Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
SWEET AMBROSIA®
100% Natural Nutritive Multipurpose Sweet Food Blend as cold water soluble powdered product with excellent flow-ability and highly resistance to aggressive media (high temperature, a wide range of pH and alcohol solutions), with a status of Ready-To-Use Food Additive

No special storage requirements, easily adjustable to the majority of the production processes due to a low viscosity and excellent solubility (after a final solving will be appeared a 100% clear liquid solution and could not be found any cloudiness and precipitations)

Highly effective as a replacer for sugar and for synthetic sugar substitutes with no calories and with no any undesirable aftertaste and bitterness, and also with enhanced and rich harmonic flavor formation of the final product, but without changing its own flavor, and with a stabilization of it during a long shelf life

Improves very well flavor and sweetness of the other kinds of sweeteners

The sweetness level of SWEET AMBROSIA® is much higher than the same parameter of more or less similar products known on the market

The taste of the final products, in which will be used SWEET AMBROSIA®, is more delectable with softer and richer flavor than the same parameter of more or less similar products

Not exposed to fermentation by microorganisms and does not undergo caramelization during heating with the appearance of the finished products being lighter than the same product made with sugar

Especially this product could be successfully used in products that are to have refreshing properties and in products with high and low acidity levels

Excellent non-discoloring effectiveness during a long shelf life

Exerts effect on reduction of the water activity of the food products, which contributes an increasing of their shelf life terms, without a formation of colloidal system

Helps to improve a texture and consistency of the final products

Helps to prevent a crystallization of the final products

Helps to suppress pungency of the sodium chloride

May be added at any processing stage

Has no allergens and artificial chemical agents and accepted to GMO Free Status

Three main Health Benefits in one food blend - Anti-diabetic, Prebiotic (very rich source of two kinds of soluble food dietary fibers) and Antioxidant. If to use 20 grams of SWEET AMBROSIA® daily, we could consume more than 50% of soluble food dietary fibers. At the same time, we could consume at once two very important different kinds of soluble fibers (one kind of fiber is containing the fructo-oligosaccharides). They help our digestive tract function better and healthier because each of them works in different area of our digestive system. Also these fibers have no any side effects.

Effects minimal intake of sugar and avoids artificial ingredients to contribute to a healthy balanced diet

Can be used as part of a calorie reduced diet to achieve weight loss and does not promote tooth decay

Safer for diabetics and can be used to sweeten foods to provide a sugar-free diet.

More detailed food, technological and microbiological properties of the product SWEET AMBROSIA® are described in the Product Specification Sheet, Certificate of Analyses, Nutritional Information Bulletin and Characteristics of the Product. The supporting documentation and the samples of product are available upon request.

3. Especially Useful to be Used:

In products that under sugar effect can change the color or get burned (biscuits, roasted food products)
In products in which usage of sugar might cause reduction of physical properties (ice cream, sherbet, sweet cheese bars and other kinds of sweet dairy products, confectionery, etc.)
In products characterized by high hydroscopic properties and might absorb water (biscuits, dry mixes, dry food and natural health products)
In a wide range of the bakery products
In products that are to have refreshing properties (juices and soft beverages)
In products with high (dietary vinegar, pickles, sauces) and low (milk and milk products, soya-based milk, etc.) pH levels
In products with sodium chloride (to suppress pungency of the sodium chloride)
On production of tooth paste and analogous types of products
On production of chewing candies and chewing gum
On production of products for dietary and functional alimentary products as well as natural health products
In products in which is required a high level of firmed/hard coating because of its excellent agglutinant properties (tablets, capsules, some kinds of hard candies and chocolates, some kinds of bakery products, ice cream, etc.).

The product SWEET AMBROSIA® is intended and could be successfully used for food, pharmaceutical and cosmetic industry companies, catering business (cafes, bars and restaurants, including corporate meals) and for the retail trading network. This product might be sold through departments of healthy diet of the trading network, departments of endocrinology in clinics and SPA institutions.

4. The Examples of Use in Practice:

In bakery products: Danishes, Sweet Buns, “Babka” Dough Products and Puff Pastry Products, Home Made Style Bread, Cakes
In fruit fillings: Cherry, Apple, Strawberry, Raspberry, Plum and Acai Berry
In fruit juices and low alcoholic drinks
In liquid and dry blends: Herbal Teas, Espresso Coffee, Chicory Coffee, Fruit Cocktails (dry), Fresh and Frozen Yogurts

5. Food Safety and Regulatory Affairs. Patenting:

The product mentioned above does have a compliance with the Canadian and US FDA Regulations and Requirements. All ingredients included in the formula of SWEET AMBROSIA® are approved by the Canadian, USA and EU Regulatory Agencies. This product is produced on the production facilities, which are registered by the US FDA and Canadian Food Inspection Agency and accepted to the strictest Food Safety Requirements and Procedures, in accordance with GMP's, HACCP and ISO Programs and Certifications.The product also certified by the Kashruth Council of Canada.

The formula and the name of the product SWEET AMBROSIA® have been filed in the US Patent and Trade Mark Office and in the Canadian Intellectual Property Office and in good standing. SWEET AMBROSIA® is the private Trade Mark and Label of LIVEBROOK, LTD.®
