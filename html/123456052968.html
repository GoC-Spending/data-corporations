Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
SCADAPack Controllers (PLCs/RTUs)
The SCADAPack® line of compact Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC) provides Remote Terminal Unit (RTU) functions for use in SCADA and telemetry applications. SCADAPack controllers are programmable in a variety of programming environments, including Relay Ladder Logic, IEC 61131-3, and multitasking C/C++. The products also provide an unlimited number of PID controllers for feedback control and can be easily expanded for larger I/O counts. Real-time communications using industry-standard Modbus® and DNP3 protocols simplifies integration with SCADA software, MMIs, DCS systems, and intelligent instrumentation. Everything necessary for remote monitoring and control applications is provided.

For more information contact sales@controlmicrosystems or visit www.controlmicrosystems.com.

Product Name:
SCADAPack Gas Flow Computers & Transmitters
The SCADAPack Transmitter sensor-based product line includes Gas Flow Computers with highly integrated PLCs, Modbus multivariable transmitters with integrated sensor technology, Modbus pressure transmitters, and products specifically designed for solar-powered installations, all based on rugged, proven platforms. These popular products are known throughout the industry for their reliability, performance and value.

All Flow and Pressure Measurement products are CSA certified. RTD connections are non-incendive when mounted in Div 2-rated areas. Selected SCADASense products are certified for Single Seal Compliance under ANSI/ISA  12.27.01 as well as ATEX and IECEx to Ex d IIC T6; -50°C &#8804;Ta &#8804;70°C.

For more information contact sales@controlmicrosystems or visit: www.controlmicrosystems.com

Product Name:
SOLARPack 410 Solar Powered Single Run Flow Computer
Low cost, accurate, out-of-the-box EFM is a reality with the SolarPack 410 single run flow computer. Designed for use in installations where solar is the only power source and operator access is difficult, the 410 integrates intelligent battery charging, configurable power management and low power consumption with Bluetooth wireless technology and radio communication. The product is ideal as a cost-effective chart recorder replacement and provides API21.1, BLM Onshore Order #5 and EUB Directive 17 audit trails. The SolarPack 410 is configured with Realflo and supports ClearSCADA's EFM objects. Additional features include low-profile sensor, backlit LCD, RTD input, pulse counter input and gas sampler output. The 410's options package includes an integrated Trio 900MHz spread spectrum radio, third-party-radio, integrated or remote sensor mount and a selection of solar panels and batteries.

The product is backed by a robust 3-year warranty, has cCSAus approval and can operate in a wide range of temperatures. With the internal sensor option, the 410 is rated for use in Class I, Division 2: Groups A, B, C and D Hazardous Locations. With the external sensor option, the sensor alone is rated for use in Class I, Division 1, and the enclosure in Class I, Division 2 locations.

For more information contact sales@controlmicrosystems.com or visit: www.controlmicrosystems.com

Product Name:
RealFLO Multi-run Gas Flow Computer Software
Realflo is an application that enables custody transfer gas flow measurement of up to ten flow runs in our SCADAPack controllers. The application provides EFM functionality independent of, yet concurrent with, the controllers Ladder Logic / IEC6-1131-3 and C/C++ user programmability.

Realflo supports concurrent AGA-3 (orifice plate), V-Cone, Wafer Cone, and AGA-7 (turbine meter) flow measurements with AGA-8 or NX-19 gas density calculations. Meeting the requirements of API 21.1, RealFLO provides 35 days of hourly and daily averages, 700 user changes and events as well as 300 alarms.

The software's user interface offers more efficient well deployment utilising wizards and templates that minimise entry tasks required by operators and measurement technicians. The user also has the ability to export EFM data with customisable file naming conventions for FlowCal Measurement Software, easing file organization.

Realflo now offers functionality to support the gas transmission market.
For more information contact sales@controlmicrosystems.com or visit www.controlmicrosystems.com

Product Name:
Trio Data Radios
The Trio series of state-of-the-art digital serial and Ethernet data radios offers ease-of-use, flexibility, and advanced performance features. With tough environmental specifications and support for industry-standard protocols DNP3, MODBUS, and IEC 60870-5-101, Trio radios are designed for use in the most demanding of SCADA environments. All Trio products are Class I, Div 2-compliant and are backed by an industry-leading 3 year warranty.

The product line encompasses the licensed E and M-Series and the unlicensed J & K-Series, each boasting its own unique set of system configurations and functionality.

For applications requiring the complete range of communication elements, from advanced remote units to full-feature base stations, repeaters and bridges, the Ethernet-based J-Series and serial-based K-Series radios provide all these in a variety of mounting options, utilising secure, unlicensed 900MHz and 2.4GHz spread-spectrum data transmission, innovative collision-avoidance, and multiple data stream technologies.

The E-Series offers Ethernet/IP and serial communication over licensed-band 450MHz half and full-duplex links, with remote, as well as battle-hardened base station/repeater and hot-standby base station controller units. The 450MHz remote M-Series returns high performance at a fraction of the cost of other comparable radios and may also be integrated with E-Series radio networks for added flexibility.

As the most powerful tool in the wireless communication toolbox, TView+ is used to configure all Trio radios (except J-Series) locally or remotely, and to provide useful system diagnostics and communication performance data, ensuring that the radio network is setup and tuned for optimum performance. To obtain a copy of TView+, please contact your local representative or email salessupport@controlmicrosystems.com.

To round out the product line, optional accessories are offered, including Omni and YAGI antennas, antenna duplexer, feed lines and surge suppressor.

For more information on this selection contact sales@controlmicrosystems.com or visit www.controlmicrosystems.com.

Product Name:
ClearSCADA Enterprise Software
ClearSCADA is as a premium SCADA data collection, storage, retrieval and display product. Equally at home in a wide range of industrial and utility environments, ClearSCADA is the most feature-rich product on the market, maintaining the efficiency and ease-of-use that customers expect throughout the entire SCADA system life cycle. Whether you are designing, building, operating or maintaining critical SCADA infrastructure, the ClearSCADA client/server architecture and online object database provide a state-of-the-art, third-generation, SCADA platform.

ClearSCADA has already expanded its comprehensive toolset with powerful new features like RealFLO Electronic Flow Measurement support, IEC60870-5-101 & -104 protocols, advanced SVG graphic import and international language localization, ClearSCADA makes it that much easier for the SCADA system developer and user alike to bring control concepts to life.

ClearSCADA 2007 Edition also provides a number of performance enhancements for existing features, including Context-Sensitive Help (F1), X-Y Plots, improved query processing, alarm handling, trending and security.

For more information contact sales@controlmicrosystems.com or visit www.controlmicrosystems.com.



Product Name:
SCADAPack Series 5000 Expansion and Communication Modules
The SCADAPack Series of controllers are powerful on their own but are also expandable through the Control Microsystems 5000 Series of I/O Expansion and Communication modules. Starting with as few as 11 process I/O points, any SCADAPack controller (except SCADAPack100) can be expanded to over 700 process I/O points, using up to twenty 5000 Series I/O Expansion modules.

For more information contact sales@controlmicrosystems.com or visit: www.controlmicrosystems.com.


Product Name:
Accutech Wireless Instruments
The Accutech line of Wireless Instrumentation products provide cost-effective and easy to install alternatives to traditional, hardwired sensor sites. These rugged field units are designed for the majority of industrial applications and for installations ranging from the plant floor to remote installations with extreme temperature and humidity ranges. All Accutech products offer Class 1, Division 2 Hazardous Area Rating (Class 1, Div 1 on select models) and an industry leading 3 year warranty.

The Accutech line is comprised of self-contained, self-powered field units providing process data to a centralised base radio through an unlicensed band, spread-spectrum, frequency hopping wireless connection. Networks of up to 100 field units (900MHz version) can be created and polled by a single base radio using the secure, proprietary Industrial Wireless protocol, with a typical range between field unit and base radio of up to 3000ft (~1000m). With the capability to scale up to as many as 256 wireless instrumentation LANs, Accutech networks easily accommodate future expansion plans.

A wide selection of field units are available, each equipped with an integrated antenna, long-life battery (up to 10 years) and radio.

For more information vist: www.accutechinstruments.com

Product Name:
Telemetry & Remote SCADA Solutions
As a global specialist in energy management with operations in more than 100 countries, Schneider Electric offers integrated solutions across multiple market segments, including leadership positions in energy and infrastructure, industrial processes, building automation, and data centers/networks, as well as a broad presence in residential applications. Focused on making energy safe, reliable, and efficient, the company's 110,000 plus employees achieved sales of 19.6 billion euros in 2010, through an active commitment to help individuals and organizations Make the most of their energy.
Thanks to its Telemetry and Remote SCADA solutions, Schneider Electric provides integrated, secure and reliable SCADA solutions for water treatment plants and pumping stations, on-shore oil and gas extraction, transportation and storage as well as electrical utilities applications allowing you to maximize system functionality and reduce operational costs. Key solution components include remote process monitoring and control (SCADAPack RTU controllers), long range wireless communication (Trio radios), integrated SCADA host optimized for wide area use (ClearSCADA software) and wireless instrumentation (Accutech sensors). Industry specific solutions include dedicated water pump up and pump down controller (FlowStation), gas flow measurement (Realflo) and gas well automation (ProductionPlus).
For more information, visit www.controlmicrosystems.com.

Technology profile

Focused on making energy safe, reliable, and efficient, the company's 110,000 plus employees achieved sales of 19.6 billion euros in 2010, through an active commitment to help individuals and organizations Make the most of their energy.
Thanks to its Telemetry and Remote SCADA solutions, Schneider Electric provides integrated, secure and reliable SCADA solutions for water treatment plants and pumping stations, on-shore oil and gas extraction, transportation and storage as well as electrical utilities applications allowing you to maximize system functionality and reduce operational costs. Key solution components include remote process monitoring and control (SCADAPack RTU controllers), long range wireless communication (Trio radios), integrated SCADA host optimized for wide area use (ClearSCADA software) and wireless instrumentation (Accutech sensors). Industry specific solutions include dedicated water pump up and pump down controller (FlowStation), gas flow measurement (Realflo) and gas well automation (ProductionPlus).
For more information, visit www.controlmicrosystems.com.
