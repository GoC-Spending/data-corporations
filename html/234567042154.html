Company Profile


Recall Canada Holdings Inc., is a Canadian company and is part of the Recall Global family which today is known as a global leader in lifecycle information management offering Document Management, Digital Solutions, Data Protection and Secure Destruction. Currently, Recall supports approximately 80,000 customers in more than 20 countries on five continents. With a global footprint of nearly 300 dedicated Operations Centers, we apply best-in-class solutions for small proprietorships to large multi-national corporations across a range of industries including: Financial, Banking, Insurance, Healthcare, Legal, Government, Academic, Manufacturing, Business Services, Entertainment as well as Consumer/Packaged Goods. We are a Government cleared and Controlled Goods cleared Company.
Everything we do is geared towards providing our customers peace of mind. We do this on a platform of four key pillars: Security, Efficiency, Customer Satisfaction and Sustainability.
Recall upholds community-driven values and a Corporate Social Responsibility.
Regardless of your companys size or industry  or the format of your information  you can count on Recall to physically and digitally manage your critical business items throughout their lifecycle. Just ask the 80,000-plus customers we serve consistently and reliably worldwide.

Recall provides the solutions and expertise you need:
Document Management Solutions - Physical and digital capturing, indexing, storage, and retrieval of critical documents.

Data Protection and Privacy Solutions - Secure storage, protection, and recovery of media and other valuable items.

Secure Media and Document Destruction Solutions - Confidential destruction of sensitive documents, media, and other business items.

Uniting best practices in document management solutions, data protection, and secure media and document destruction, Recall delivers peace of mind and bottom-line results through Security, Efficiency, Customer Satisfaction, and Sustainability.

Recall provides:
 Decades of experience providing full lifecycle information management solutions from document and data pick up to storage, imaging, tracking, preservation, and destruction
 Optimum security via BABACO-equipped, GPS-monitored vehicles and state-of-the-art Information and Destruction Centers
 Better, smarter, more efficient solutions through innovation and a commitment to continuous improvement
 Approximately 80,000 satisfied customers who take advantage of our Global Footprint, industry best practices and Perfect Order promise
 Sustainable, long term solutions that protect and benefit the community at large and cause zero injuries and zero environmental damage
 Ability to track and manage inventory via ReQuest® Web, Recalls intuitive, web-based platform; and
 RFID-enabled technology that takes inventory audits to an unprecedented level of speed and efficiency.

Mobilshred Inc as a Canadian company is registered with the Industrial Security Program (ISP) and the controlled goods program, operating as a secure and cleared company, all Canadian employees are registered with the Canadian government and all facilities operating within the PWGSC requirements. For further information on the Canadian systems and security please contact the PWGSC, or the Controlled goods division.

Mobilshred Inc is registered with the Controlled Goods Program (CGP) this is a registration and compliance program that regulates access to controlled goods, including International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) items, in Canada. The CGP plays a vital role in the prevention and detection of the unlawful examination, possession or transfer of controlled goods in Canada. Under the authorities of the Defense Production Act (DPA) and the Controlled Goods Regulations, the CGP's mandate is to strengthen Canada's defense trade controls through the mandatory registration and regulation of businesses and individuals who examine, possess and/or transfer controlled goods.

Certifications
Recall is PCI Certified

The PCI Security Standards Council is an open global forum, launched in 2006, that is responsible for the development, management, education, and awareness of the PCI Security Standards, including the Data Security Standard (PCI DSS), Payment Application Data Security Standard (PA-DSS), and PIN Transaction Security (PTS) requirements.
The Council's five founding global payment brands -- American Express, Discover Financial Services, JCB International, MasterCard Worldwide, and Visa Inc. -- have agreed to incorporate the PCI DSS as the technical requirements of each of their data security compliance programs. Each founding member also recognizes the QSAs, PA-QSAs and ASVs certified by the PCI Security Standards Council.
All five payment brands share equally in the Council's governance, have equal input into the PCI Security Standards Council and share responsibility for carrying out the work of the organization. Other industry stakeholders are encouraged to join the Council as Participating Organizations and review proposed additions or modifications to the standards.
In security terms, it means that our business adheres to the PCI DSS requirements for security management, policies, procedures, network architecture, software design and other critical protective measures. In operational terms, it means that you are playing your role to make sure your customers' payment card data is being kept safe throughout every transaction, and that they  and you  can have confidence that they're protected against the pain and cost of data breaches.

Recall is NAID Certification

The NAID Certification Program is offered on a voluntary basis to all NAID member companies providing information destruction services. Through the program, NAID members may seek certification audits for Mobile and/or Plant-based operations in paper or printed media, micro media, computer hard drive destruction and/or computer hard drive sanitization. The NAID Certification Program establishes standards for a secure destruction process including such areas as operational security, employee hiring and screening, the destruction process, responsible disposal and insurance.
Under this program, the certification application and associated fees cover only individual locations. If a NAID member operates in multiple locations, each location must pass the audit to be certified. NAID members who receive certification must specify the location certified in company literature when referencing the NAID Certification Program.

Recall is registered with the Controlled Goods Directorate

The Controlled Goods Program (CGP) is a Federal Government Program administered by the Department of Public Works and Government Services Canada (PWGSC).

The Controlled Goods Directorate (CGD) administers the Controlled Goods Program, a domestic industrial security program that helps strengthen Canada's defence trade controls and prevents the proliferation of tactical and strategic assets including weapons, satellite global positioning systems and communications equipment, military equipment and related intellectual property. This is done by regulating and controlling the examination, possession, and transfer of controlled goods and controlled technology in Canada. Anyone who conducts business in Canada and examines, possesses and/or transfers controlled goods and/or controlled technology in Canada must comply with the Defence Production Act and the Controlled Goods Regulations.
