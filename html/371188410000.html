Company Profile


Trow Global is one of Canadas largest engineering and consulting firms. With over 125 offices across Canada, the U.S. and internationally, and with projects around the globe, Trow Global offers clients in both the private and public sectors a full range of services provided by a dedicated team of more than 3,500 professionals, including specialist engineers and technical experts located throughout the world. Selected as a winner of Canadas 50 Best Managed Companies program for 2001 and 2006, and a requalifier for 2002, 2003, 2007, 2008 and 2009, Trow Global provides integrated solutions in the Buildings; Earth, Environment & Sustainability; Energy; Industrial and Infrastructure markets. Today, the Company continues to enjoy dynamic growth on a global scale.

International capital projects:

Corporate Expertise:
-----------------------
Trow has international experience as project engineers on
various World Bank, EDC, CIDA and other international bank
financed projects. Locations of projects: Colombia,
Venezuela, United Arab Emirates, Guyana, Bahamas, Turks &
Caicos, Jamaica, Dominican Republic, Barbadox, Australia
and
Papua New Guinea.

International work has included environmental, pipeline,
transportation, urban planning, geotechnical and building
science work.


Corporate Language Capabilities:
-----------------------
Chinese, Czech, English or French, Hungarian, Italian, Polish


Proj: Guyana Bridges
Country: Guyana
Contribution:
Trow provided the following services on approximately 256
structures along the main road from Timehri to Rosignol in
north-eastern Guyana: foundation investigation, condition
surveys, pavement design report and aggregate sources list,
QA/QC plan development. This project was IDB funded to the
estimated amount of $30,000,000 US. Work was done in
partnership with FIGG Engineering from the United States.


Proj: TransGas De Occidente
Country: Colombia
Contribution:
Trow, in a strategic alliance with Furgro International of
Houston (now part of the ENSR Group), provided the following
services to the project:
- assistance in the development and review of issues such as
route deviations, shared right-of-way concerns, crossing
designs, mapping and pipe cover depth as requested
- development of right-of-way reinstatement specifications
involving an innovative combination of traditional Colombia
techniques and new technology
- overall oversign of all right-of-way reinstatement work,
utilizing expatriate and national engineers


Proj: Turks & Caicos Cruise Port Development
Country: Turks Caicos Is.
Contribution:
Feasibility study for development of cruise port and resort
on a currently deserted island, for all facilities including
a 13 million cu yd excavation for a harbour.
Responsibilities of Trow: geotechnical engineering,
including foundation recommendations, excavation conditions,
use of excavation material for construction materials,
marketing of excavated calcareous rock for industrial
purposes, dredging parameters, pavement design, service
installation.
