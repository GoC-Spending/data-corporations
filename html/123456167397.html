Company Profile


Centrinity, based in Toronto, Canada, is the industry leader in
developing flexible collaboration and unified communications
software for educational organizations. FirstClass Unified
Communications is the most powerful and effective solution
designed specifically to meet the complex communication
challenges of the educational environment. FirstClass enables
educational organizations worldwide to build online communities
by streamlining communications processes and removing the
barriers of environment and device-dependence. FirstClass
supports educational institutions in communicating, publishing,
sharing information and exchanging ideas in collaborative
forums.

More than 5,000 educational sites around the world create
interactive learning environments with FirstClass' suite of
flexible and intuitive features, making it easy to connect with
their stakeholders using any communication method. The
integration of voice, fax, and text messaging, web publishing,
conferencing, distance learning, database integration and
application development features - accessible anytime, anywhere
via any device - allows educational organizations of any kind to
easily and quickly implement internal or community-wide
communication and collaboration solutions for their online
learning environments. Educators, students, and the community
use FirstClass to share and publish information, multimedia
documents and calendars with one simple tool. Faculty and
administrators benefit from a truly unified, streamlined, and
cost-efficient communications environment.

Centrinity's flexible communication and collaboration solutions
are internationally acclaimed for their reliable, scalable and
robust architecture.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
FirstClass
FirstClass is a client/server workgroup communication system
that integrates advanced e-mail with replicable discussion
databases, remote access, forms processing, enterprise
database access and online information services. The product
offers a number of unique features designed to improve
information communication and management. FirstClass offers
the same ease-of-use, functionality and robust performance
over network or modem, and operates in Windows,
Macintosh, DOS, OS/2 and terminal emulation environments.
The product is available in 12 languages and is used by
businesses, educational institutions, governments and private
individuals at more than 20,000 sites in 30 countries. SoftArc
is supported by a worldwide network of Certified Consultants
and is headquartered in Markham, Ontario, Canada.

Product Name:
FirstClass Intranet Server
FirstClass Intranet Server (FCIS) is the easiest intranet, cross-
platform e-mail and groupware solution for any organization,
large or small. Now, you can get your entire organization
connected, collaborating and published on the Internet without a
full-time Webmaster or a complex mix of hardware and software.
FCIS combines the power of LAN-based messaging with seamless
Internet integration and Web serving so people both inside and
outside your office can work together easily. FCIS even lets
you work the way you wantwith the feature rich FirstClass
client, a Web browser or your favorite e-mail package.

Product Name:
FirstClass Collaborative Classroom
FirstClass Collaborative Classroom (FCCC) is the easiest
intranet, cross-platform e-mail and groupware solution for any
educational organization, large or small. Designed specifically
for education, FCCC creates an interactive learning environment
with powerful, flexible and intuitive features. FCCC will get
your organization connected, collaborating and published on the
Internet.
