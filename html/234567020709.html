Company Profile


Magstar is a leading provider of fully integrated retail management systems to mid-sized specialty hard and soft line operations throughout North America and the Caribbean. Magstar is a Microsoft Certified Partner and IBM Business Partner.

For seven consecutive years, retailers have selected Magstar as a top retail software vendor in the RIS News Leaderboard vendor performance survey.

TOTAL RETAIL® is a fully integrated merchandising and financial management system that allows you to centrally manage multiple locations (stores and warehouses) with multiple channels of distribution: stores, web stores, warehouse/distribution centers

The TOTAL RETAIL® application suite includes inventory control, financials (Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, General Ledger), warehouse / distribution, Customer Relationship Management (CRM), call center order entry, sales analysis and reporting, SQL Server 2005 based Business Intelligence (BI), Open to Buy (OTB), Stock Ledger, Bank Reconciliation, and point of sale (POS) with integrated high speed payment systems.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
TOTAL RETAIL®
TOTAL RETAIL® is a fully integrated merchandising and financial management (ERP) system that allows you to centrally manage multiple locations (stores and warehouses) with multiple channels of distribution: stores, web stores, warehouse/distribution centers and call centers.

TOTAL RETAIL® integrates business processes across operational departments and channels (stores, warehouses, eCommerce, order entry and fulfillment).

It is targeted at small to mid-size specialty retailers with
multiple outlets: specialty hard and softlines

TOTAL RETAIL® supports all aspects of cross-channel merchandising for a variety of Retail business models such as:
· multiple location retail outlets (corporate or franchise)
· catalog, wholesale sales & eCommerce
· warehouse / distribution management
 or any combination

TOTAL RETAIL® is a full featured set of modular applications
that meets the broad requirements of all retailers including:
· full financials
· inventory management and replenishment
- point of sale
· sales analysis and forecasting
- business intelligence (BI)
- Catalog call centre: wholesale/retail order entry, web store fulfillment

Technology profile

TOTAL RETAIL® is a true multi-user, multi-application solution,
with the functionality that you need to manage and integrate
multiple retail-chain outlets, wholesale, catalog sales, warehouses
and eCommerce with real-time stock position and integrated
product fulfillment.

TOTAL RETAIL® is both multi-company and multi-divisional and
provides for multi-location perpetual inventory management with
Just In Time replenishment combined with perpetual financial
management. Modules include TOTAL Store Management, Warehouse Management and SQL Analytics.

Magstar supports industry standard Open Systems hardware and
enterprise operating systems for continuous operation and data
integrity, proven superior reliability, system security, and
performance. Available platforms include single / multi-
processor INTEL, IBM RS/6000 or Compaq Alpha servers with proven
UNIX enterprise operating systems (SCO, NCR, IBM/AIX, Compaq
Tru64, LINUX) for maximum reliability and security.

Magstars founding partners established the company in 1985
specifically to develop retail enterprise solutions. With many
years of retail management, accounting and open system
development experience, the partners recognized the need for an
open, fully integrated retail specific solution with:

· Proven, reliable enterprise operating systems
· True multi&#8209;user, multi-application solution

· An integrated relational database system for fast, efficient
file retrieval

· The flexibility and functionality of a 4GL development
environment to meet ever-changing business requirements

· Speedy and economical 4GL system development with development
time reduced by as much as 10 times over lower level languages

· Over 230 standard reports with user selection criteria plus an
easy to use Query Language for flexible user-designed report generation
