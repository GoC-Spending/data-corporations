Company Profile


MAYA Heat Transfer Technologies Limited is a leading developer of advanced software, technology, and processes for numerical simulation and engineering analysis as well as datacenters. Our software products and services help thousands of engineers, datacenters operators and scientists worldwide to optimize datacenters operations and product design early in the engineering process, enabling them to significantly improve product quality while reducing overall development time and cost. A strategic development partner and reseller for Siemens PLM Software, the market leader in product lifecycle management technology, MAYA develops software applications and technology within the Simcenter platform for engineering design and analysis. Our product line consists of applications for simulation of heat transfer and fluid flow, structural analysis, composites modeling, test data analysis and correlation, durability and datacenters.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Custom System Development
Today’s advanced software tools offer tremendous potential to automate and therefore streamline testing and development – especially for repetitive tasks. As a developer of core products within the Siemens PLM Software family of tools, we’re extremely familiar with NX™, Teamcenter®, combined with our expertise in fluid dynamics, thermal heat transfer, structural mechanics, durability and composite materials, we can deliver advanced numerical algorithms specific to your applications.

Product Name:
IDEAS TMG
IDEAS TMG : Is a comprehensive thermal modelling and analysis software package. The system uses advanced finite difference
control volume technology to efficiently solve non-linear steady-state and transient thermal problems. The system is capable of graphical model building, results post-processing, as well as specialized features to automate thermal modelling and analysis. IDEAS TMG can model heat transfer processes involving conduction, radiation, free and forced convection, advection, fluid flow and phase change.

Product Name:
IDEAS ESC
MAYA's ESC software provides you with a comprehensive set of tools to simulate 3D air flow and thermal behavior in electronic systems. With it's powerful computational fluid dynamics capabilities it is also ideal for many automotive, heating and ventilation, and industrial applications. Electronic component level, board level, and system analysis is performed by seamlessly coupling computational fluid dynamics (CFD) solver technology with thermal solver technology. Together this hybrid coupled-solver can accurately predict fluid flow, convection, conduction and radiation.

Product Name:

Product Name:

Product Name:
Datacenter Clarity LC
Datacenter Clarity LC is Maya HTT’s DCIM solution. Datacenter Clarity LC is built on a foundation that has served as the backbone of engineering departments across the globe in the space, automotive, aerospace, military and defense, and oil and gas industries for several decades. Coupled with the most powerful real-time monitoring engine on the market, Datacenter Clarity LC brings tremendous value to the colocation market.

Product Name:
Simcenter 3D
Simcenter 3D delivers a unified, scalable, open and extensible environment for 3D CAE with connections to design, 1D simulation, test and data management. Simcenter 3D speeds the simulation process by combining best-in-class geometry editing, associative simulation modeling and multi-discipline solutions embedded with industry expertise.

Engineering Solutions

Service Name:
Education and Training
The fastest and most efficient way to become productive using specialized engineering software is to enroll in our training courses. But training isn’t a one-size-fits all proposition. MAYA, a Siemens PLM Software development partner and Official PLM Training Center since 1982, offers a diverse range of training options to meet your organizational, resource, and process needs.

Service Name:
Systems Integration
Service Name:
Mechanical Engineering
MAYA provides a unique combination of engineering expertise, software solutions and industry experience. As specialists in structural, thermal, and fluid flow analysis, we have the skills and know-how to deal with your most challenging engineering problems. We offer a broad range of services and expertise, spanning a variety of engineering disciplines. Using leading-edge CAD and CAE technology, our highly experienced staff can perform the design, analysis, simulation, and testing that you need to shorten your product development cycle, enhance performance, and boost product quality. Our industrial experience is extensive and diverse, encompassing in particular the fields of aerospace, electronics, automotive, and manufacturing.

Service Name:
ENGINEERING SERVICES - FLUID-FLOW (CFD) ANALYSIS
MAYA develops industry-leading computational fluid dynamics (CFD) software, and has extensive expertise using it to solve real-world engineering problems. Our team of specialists can help customers use their software more efficiently to speed product design development and improve the accuracy of results. We can also help jump start your analyses by building robust CFD models and performing the necessary simulations for you. We have a strong record of successful fluid dynamics experience in aerospace, aeronautics, automotive, high tech electronics, heavy industry, and manufacturing applications.

Service Name:
ENGINEERING SERVICES - STRUCTURAL ANALYSIS
Our experience with NX™ Nastran, NX™, Fibersim™, LMS Samcef™ and Femap™ can help you efficiently and effectively model and analyze your mechanical designs from both a dynamic and structural perspective. MAYA has decades of industrial experience meshing complex parts, creating large assemblies, validating finite element models as well as solving and extracting critical information from the solution. We also have experience with vibration, shock simulation and modal testing, as well as with software development of durability and laminates composites simulation products.

Service Name:
ENGINEERING SERVICES - THERMAL ANALYSIS
MAYA develops industry-leading thermal analysis software, and has extensive expertise using it to solve real-world engineering problems. Our team of specialists can help customers use their software more efficiently to speed product design development and improve the accuracy of results. We can also help jump start customer analyses by performing simulations for you. We have a strong record in aerospace, aeronautics, automotive, high tech electronics, heavy industry, and manufacturing applications.
