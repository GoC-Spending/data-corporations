Technology profile

Tire Lyna is a registered trademark of Lyna Manufacturing Incorporated. Tire Lyna is a high quality product, scientifically researched, cost effective alternative to competing tire maintenance programs currently on the market. Developed by professionals for professionals and backed by several years of extensive scientific research and field testing. Tire Lyna exhibits a number of properties not always found in tire maintenance products currently available on the market. These include the fact that it is stable and homogeneous i.e. spreads evenly without settling or separating. It is easily washed out of the casing with water and contains only non-toxic and environmentally-friendly materials (both of which are important for the recapping process). Tire Lyna is conveniently packaged in 5 gallon pails/640ozs and will treat 10 to15 truck/bus (11R 22.5) tires depending on retread grade and make (Asian). Optional application pump is made of high quality aircraft grade aluminum. Once installed, the gel is activated by the tire's normal dynamics, including centrifugal force and heat generated by friction between the tire and road surface and forms a thin protective gel coating over the inner liner, which protects the casing from heat damage and oxidation.
The product is designed to manage air porosity and can operate between -80F to +370F. Its sealant properties stops bead leaks and "O" ring leaks and seals punctures (up to 1/2") in the tread area. The product is specifically designed to reduce the number of casings that end up in the landfills. Tire Lyna maintains air pressure, lowers fuel costs, reduces tire-operating temperature, and significantly reduces tire maintenance costs. It seals punctures while the wheel is in motion, reduces costly downtime, employee down time, frustrations with tires and does not have an effect on the tire manufacturers casing warranty. One treatment is guaranteed to last for the full legal tread life of the tire.
The product is commercially proven and tested with real world customers and endorsements.

All formulations are 100% environmentally safe, non-toxic and are MSDS classified as "Not Controlled".

Tire Lyna is a registered trademark of Lyna Manufacturing Incorporated. Tire Lyna is a high quality product, scientifically researched, cost effective alternative to competing tire maintenance programs currently on the market. Developed by professionals for professionals and backed by several years of extensive scientific research and field testing. Tire Lyna exhibits a number of properties not always found in tire maintenance products currently available on the market. These include the fact that it is stable and homogeneous i.e. spreads evenly without settling or separating. It is easily washed out of the casing with water and contains only non-toxic and environmentally-friendly materials (both of which are important for the recapping process). Tire Lyna is conveniently packaged in 5 gallon pails/640ozs and will treat 10 to15 truck/bus (11R 22.5) tires depending on retread grade and make (Asian). Optional application pump is made of high quality aircraft grade aluminum. Once installed, the gel is activated by the tire's normal dynamics, including centrifugal force and heat generated by friction between the tire and road surface and forms a thin protective gel coating over the inner liner, which protects the casing from heat damage and oxidation.
The product is designed to manage air porosity and can operate between -80F to +370F. Its sealant properties stops bead leaks and "O" ring leaks and seals punctures (up to 1/2") in the tread area. The product is specifically designed to reduce the number of casings that end up in the landfills. Tire Lyna maintains air pressure, lowers fuel costs, reduces tire-operating temperature, and significantly reduces tire maintenance costs. It seals punctures while the wheel is in motion, reduces costly downtime, employee down time, frustrations with tires and does not have an effect on the tire manufacturers casing warranty. One treatment is guaranteed to last for the full legal tread life of the tire.
The product is commercially proven and tested with real world customers and endorsements.

All formulations are 100% environmentally safe, non-toxic and are MSDS classified as "Not Controlled".
