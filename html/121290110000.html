Company Profile


Nautel is a global leader in the design, manufacture, sales and support of transmitters for AM/FM radio stations and TV stations, navigation, industrial and space-based applications. Our outstanding reliability, global presence, efficiency and exceptional support have resulted in more than 12,000 Nautel transmitter deployments in 177 countries.

Nautel builds transmitters that meet stringent quality standards at two ISO-certified manufacturing facilities, one in Canada and one in the United States. Nautel Limited and Nautel Maine Inc. feature CNC fabrication shops, light electronic assembly, final assembly and extensive test facilities. The two facilities also house a full inventory of parts, modules and subassemblies to support customers' maintenance needs.

Nautel is big enough to offer cutting edge technology, but small enough to take care of every customers needs. Understanding customers' requirements allows Nautel to design products they want. In fact, Nautel builds transmitters that suit the exact specifications of each customer.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Antenna Design
Nautel has more than 40 years of experience designing and manufacturing antenna systems, primarily for use with their extensive range of low and medium frequency transmitter systems. Ground-wave propagation studies are routinely conducted to determine system operational range at world-wide locations.

We have the ability to fully evaluate the performance characteristics of new antenna designs using the powerful GNEC-4 modeling software that was developed in the U.S.A. at the Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory. This software can determine important parameters including input impedance, current distribution, ground loss resistance, efficiency, radiation pattern, polarization and field strength at near and far field locations.

Product Name:
Radio Transmitters
Nautel offers the worlds broadest portfolio of solid state digital and analog radio transmitters with models ranging from 1 kW to 2000 kW MW and 300 W to 88 kW FM. We were the first manufacturer to offer commercial DRM+ transmitters and lead the way in HD Radio innovation.

Medium Wave AM radio broadcast transmitters (both analog and digital) in power levels of 1 to 2000 kW.

VHF FM radio broadcast transmitters (both analog and digital) in power levels of and 300 W to 88 kW.


Product Name:
RF Plasma Amplifiers
The Ad Astra Rocket Company of Texas approached Nautel in 2007 to develop a radio-frequency plasma amplifier to be used in a plasma rocket propulsion system. Utilizing high efficiency solid state RF generators made by Nautel, Ad Astras VASIMR® VX-200 rocket project achieved a highly-coveted new power milestone of 200 kW maximum power in September, 2009.

Originally studied by NASA, this technology is expected to revolutionize space travel greatly reducing the operational costs of space exploration and shortening transit times for missions to Mars, and Nautel is playing a key role in its development.

Product Name:
Sonar Amplifiers
Nautel offers high power, low frequency sonar amplifiers which are robust, solid state systems designed specifically for sonar applications in difficult industrial and military environments.

Product Name:
RF Heating, Vacuum Kilns, HF Dielectric Drying
Nautel can provide RF and high voltage experience for RF vacuum kiln system integration including the electrical design of the applicator (electrodes) and vacuum chamber, HF dielectric drying amplifier, matching network and the design of high voltage feed-through insulators.

Product Name:
Navigational Radio Beacons
Nautel has shipped thousands of Non-Directional Radio Beacon (NDB) systems for both land-based airports/airstrips and offshore oil platforms and support vessel applications.

Nautels industry leading quality, reliability and customer support make it the choice of NDB operators worldwide.

Product Name:
Differential Global Positioning Systems
Nautel provides solutions for both coastal and inland Differential Global Positioning System (DGPS) applications to enhance the accuracy of the Global Positioning System (GPS).

Nautels industry leading quality, reliability and customer support make it the choice of DGPS reference station operators worldwide.

Product Name:
LF PNT and and eLORAN Solutions
Nautel provides innovative and industry leading solutions for LF PNT (Low Frequency Position, Navigation and Timing) and eLORAN (Enhanced Long Range Navigation) applications.

Nautels industry leading quality, reliability and customer support make it the choice of LF PNT / eLORAN station operators worldwide.

Product Name:
RF Dielectric Sealing
Nautel can assist companies that wish to do RF heat sealing / plastic welding for plastics, ranging from PVC, polyurethane, nylon, PET, EVA to some ABS types.

Solid state, high power RF amplifiers can replace old tube systems. This gives you a more efficient dielectric sealing system, more modular if any faults occur, and less concern about spares being available when compared to the current tube systems.

Product Name:
NAVTEX Transmission Systems
Nautel provides solutions for shore to ship delivery of navigational and meteorological warnings and forecasts, as well as urgent marine safety notices, via NAVTEX transmission systems.

Nautels industry leading quality, reliability and customer support make it the choice of NAVTEX station operators worldwide.

Product Name:
Weather Radio Solutions
Nautel provides solutions for the broadcast of normal and emergency weather information.

Nautel weather radio systems are used by such organizations as the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) in the USA.

Nautels industry leading quality, reliability and customer support make it the choice of weather broadcast station operators worldwide.

Service Name:
RF System Design
Nautel can assist your design team in any RF related designs, ranging from the high power RF amplifier unit through to the actual load itself, which may be an antenna.

Nautel has diverse RF skills to design and manufacture systems and has the support to stand behind the products. Nautel can easily adapt to whatever methodology is required for the system design with a track record of several engineered systems.

FSC Code:
5820-Radio and Television Communications Equipment, Except Airborne
5825-Radio Navigation Equipment, Except Airborne
