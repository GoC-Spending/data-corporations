Company Profile


Anything is possible with Li-Ning Badminton, North America’s premier wholesale and online destination for badminton clothing and supplies. The choice of world champion Chen Long and the Chinese National Badminton Team, Li-Ning is the preferred badminton racket, clothing and equipment supplier for professional athletes and enthusiasts of all levels and abilities. We offer performance racket options to suit your play style for the ultimate in speed, power and control. Download our Racket Guide to find the best badminton racket for you. Don’t be fooled by cheap imitations online. At Li-Ning Badminton, your badminton racket purchase is backed by our guarantee of authenticity. We are pleased to offer free shipping to Canada and the continental U.S. on most orders of $150 or more. If for any reason you are not completely satisfied with your Li-Ning sporting equipment, badminton shoes or apparel, simply return the item to us in new, unused condition within 30 days of the original purchase date for a hassle-free exchange or refund.
