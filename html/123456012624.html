Company Profile


Roynat Capital is a member of the Scotiabank Group and is Canada's leading merchant bank specializing in innovative long-term capital and business solutions for mid-sized companies: high growth firms with sales between $5 million and $200 millionbut looking for more.

Roynat Capital has been providing innovative financial solutions to businesses since 1962. Today we manage a portfolio in excess of $2 billion including a mezzanine and equity portfolio of $250 million, and provide term debt, sub and mezzanine-debt and equity financing to over 1200 clients across North America.

Roynat Capital's goal is to become the North American mid market merchant bank of choice through delivering "More Than Money" to its clients.

Passion:
Roynat Capital is passionate about your business. Through 21 regional offices across North America we provide over 1200 clients a full range of financing and advisory services.

For example, cross border growth has become a key business strategy for many Canadian mid market companies. Recognizing this, a key dimension to Roynat Capital's expanding merchant banking service is our move into the U.S.

With offices now in Chicago, Charlotte, Cleveland, and Southfield, Michigan, Roynat Capital provides Canadian clients seamless access to long term financing to capitalize on this growth opportunity.

As well through our U.S. offices, Roynat Capital now offers American clients access to a unique and comprehensive suite of solutions for any financing need. US Services

Determination:
From providing traditional and non-traditional financing in the form of term loans, subordinated debt or equity, to providing strategic advisory services, Roynat Capital will work with you to help grow your business, to finance and execute mergers, acquisitions and divestitures, to restructure debt and equity capital or to arrange strategic partnerships.

Whatever the financial challenge, Roynat Capital is determined to deliver more.

Innovation:
Roynat Capital's regional networks and broad industry knowledge are important reasons why we are able to bring meaningful assistance to entrepreneurs and business owners planning long-term growth.

With over 2 Billion dollars invested, Roynat Capital not only has the intellectual capital but the depth to finance new and innovative ideas and business concepts. We provide innovative financial solutions by which mid market companies can evaluate, address and solve unique business challenges.

Leadership:
For over 40 years Roynat Capital has been a market leader because we know the street and have a pulse of the market.

We see opportunity where others cannot.

We believe in the individual when others lose faith. We commit resources when others run for cover.

Roynat Capital looks past the balance sheet and will be there when times are good and more importantly when times are notthat's what leaders do.

Vision:
Roynat Capital looks for those companies and entrepreneurs who have had the foresight to break new ground, those looking for a partner to help navigate growth to build successful companies that realize their full potential .

Partnership:
Our focus is opportunity, not financial ratios. Each relationship is based on a working knowledge of the business, shared goals and most importantly, trust.

Our team of talented and experienced professionals approach every transaction from the perspective of a long-term investor committed to adding value.

Since 1962 Roynat Capital has believed in the strength of companies just like yours and has assembled a unique group of talented professionals who believe in the power of an idea. We are dedicated to providing innovative solutions that help our clients achieve their true growth potential and the means to realize their dreams.

We are Roynat Capital and we are more than money.

ROYNAT... more than money... business financing specialists

Winning in business is about reaching goals... that's where we
come in RoyNat has been helping small and medium size business
realize its goals for more than 35 years...We've met exceptional
people along the way and, time and again, seen companies that
began as little more than ideas turn into robust, competitive
going concerns.

We've participated in mergers and aquisitions that made small
companies serious players in both domestic and international
markets. We've helped managers buy out owners. We've taken equity
positions to help foster growth, and we've built valuable
long-term relationships that have endured through difficult times
as well as good.

Working in this strong and growing sector of the Canadian Economy
has taught us a thing or two over the years, perhaps none more
important than this:
Success is ultimately about the people who manage
businesses...and not simply about balance sheets

Today, RoyNat is the leading company in Canada's private sector
specializing in term financing and merchant banking services. We
provide funding for acquiring fixed assets, refinancing existing
borrowings, working capital, mergers and acquisitions and, most
recently, for succession.

We are proud of differences that separate us from conventional
lenders. In our offices across the country you will find managers
who are first and foremost business people, if not literally,
then certainly in attitude and understanding.

Formula lending is absent. Our managers build financial
strategies that support and strengthen business plans by using
term funds, subordinated debt and/or equity tailored to the best
advantage of our clients. In addition, they look beyond common
financial ratios and factor in more difficult to measure assets
such as management ability and a companies competitive position
within its industry.

Experience and expertise have made RoyNat a specialist. Our
managers understand business. They understand the need to be
prompt, resourceful and flexible. They understand that agreements
made today should not become encumbrances tomorrow. They
understand the importance of standing by their clients.

TERM LOANS:
Term Financing is used to:
-purchase an existing business
-construct, expand, or modernize a plant or other facility
-purchase new equipment, machinery, or vehicles
-provide working capital
-refinance or consolidate debt
-finance a merger or aquisition

Lease Financing:
Leasing should always be an option when new equipment is
required. The question of when to use leasing services, or
whether to use them, requires assessment of a number of financial
variables that are usually company specific and sometimes
complex.

Leases can be fully expensed and are often less costly than a
loan. In addition, a lease might free capital for other, more
productive purposes.

RoyNat's staff, well versed in leasing and experienced in all
aspects of business financing, is able to advise clients about
the wide range of equipment financing opportunities that are
available. They will work with management to put together the
best-priced leasing package relative to financial considerations
and business objectives.

In some instances, a term loan might be the solution. In others,
a lease, a lease line, or a lease in combination with other
financial products, might prove to be the most cost effective.

MERCHANT BANKING:

Subordinated debt and equity - the tools of merchant banking -
are commonly used to facilitate:
- leveraged buyouts
- management buyouts
- aquisitions
- growth and major expansions
- succession financing

Merchant Banking can also be used for bridge financing pending a
public issue. In addition, we will assist clients by evaluating a
target company in an aquisition; by structuring a transaction; by
packaging financing requirements; or by negotiating for
additional funds. We will also have our Mergers and Aquisitions
department help existing clients exit their business by sourcing
a new purchaser or help an existing client find additional target
companies to buy.

Simply put, we start with what's best for the business and work
from there. The end result is a partnership structured in the
present that is well positioned for the future.

SUCCESSION FINANCING:

It is estimated that more than half of Canada's family-owned
businesses will change hands over the next ten to fifteen years
as the leading edge of the baby boom moves into retirement. In
anticipation, RoyNat added Succession Financing to its portfolio
because how well an owner-manager plans succession will determine
not only the price for the business, but also the success of the
transition.

RoyNat is only one component of the succession planning team,
which will include legal, accounting and insurance advisors. The
team will draw up a plan that will profile a buyer or buyers
capable of taking over the business; establish value; deal with
tax, legal and insurance issues; set a transition strategy and
structure appropriate financing.

The advantage of establishing succession well in advance of
retirement is flexibility, particularly how and when an owner can
be paid out. Early planning also protects against unforseen
circumstances, such as illness, disability, divorce or
shareholders disputes.

Experience in succession financing is important because each
case, at core, is unique. When selecting a financial institution,
ask first about experience, because understanding personal and
business needs and crafting the most effective mix of financing
instruments are of paramount importance.

The deepest pitfall is starting too late. Too many owners find it
difficult to acdept retirement and sale of their business, and
delay dealing with the issue, often at severe cost to themselves
and sometimes to the detriment of an orderly transfer of
ownership. Prudent owners begin thinking about succession as long
as ten years in advance.

TWO SOURCES ARE BETTER THAN ONE...

Successful companies typically have more than one source of
funds...long term from a specialist lender, such as RoyNat, and
an operating line of credit from a chartered bank. More than one
source ensures flexibility of supply, creates built-in price
competition and provides a basis for service comparisons.

Fixed and floating interest rates are also available and how each
used is largely a function of economic circumstances and a
client's preference. Sometimes it can be an advantage to choose a
floating rate with an option to fix at a later date. On the other
hand, a fixed rate, which makes money a predictable cost,
enhances management's ability to plan ahead.

We are a businessman's lender providing an array of financial
solutions for today's growing business challenges. With offices
across the country we are able to service business from coast to
coast.

Visit our web site at www.roynat.ca for more detailed information
on our products, client testimonials and locations of all our
branches. Call us. We look forward to serving you.
