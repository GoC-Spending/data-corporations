Company Profile


Targray is a global supplier of raw materials for high-growth manufacturing industries that is currently active in 5 Industries: Solar energy – Photovoltaic, Biofuels, Lithium-Ion Batteries, Optical Media, and Power Trading. Established in 1989 in Beaconsfield, Qc, Targray is a multinational company with global operations in over 50 countries & across 3 continents.


LITHIUM-ION BATTERY MATERIALS – Targray provides the critical raw materials required for Li-ion Battery producers and Researchers to bring new energy storage products to market. The company's international footprint enables it to source the globe with industrial Lithium-ion battery materials. In conjunction with supply partners, Targray delivers competitively-priced high performance battery materials to manufacturers, research institutes and other customers around the world. The fabrication of li-ion batteries requires several material components:

- Lithium Hydroxide, Lithium Carbonate
- Cathode Materials (LFP, LCO, LMO, NMC, NCA, CSG)
- Coated Electrodes
- Anode Materials (Graphite)
- Aluminium Foil, ED Nickel foil, Etched Aluminum foil
- Supercapacitator equipment, Li-ion battery equipment
- PVDF Binders, SBR Binders
- Electrolyte solutions, LiPF6
- Polyethylene Separators, Polypropylene separators
- Packaging Materials - Laminate Pouch, Prismatic Cans and Lids, Battery Tabs

Targray offers a constant and dependable supply of these materials and more.



SOLAR MATERIALS – Targray is a leading supplier to the solar industry specializing in the global distribution of solar grade polysilicon, ingot and wafers. The company helps businesses lower their manufacturing costs and improve product efficiency and performance. Its experience in the photovoltaic industry and expertise with PV materials enables it to help manufacturers through every step of the fabrication process. It supplies solar cell, module and thin film manufacturers with the materials required to produce the most durable & highest efficiency photovoltaic products. Products include:

- Solar Silicon - Polysilicon, Scrap Silicon, Solar-grade Ingot, Solar Wafers, Solar Cells
- Crystalline cell materials - silver paste, aluminum paste, monocrystalline silicon wafers, multicrystalline silicon wafers
- Silicon Recycling services - silicon scrap, broken wafers, broken ingot, broken solar cells
- Junction Boxes



BIOFUELS – Targray is one of the largest biodiesel marketers / biodiesel suppliers in North America, with a fleet of dedicated Biodiesel railcars and Biodiesel storage terminals in California and across the United States. Fully registered with the EPA, CARB, ISCC, and the NBB, Targray's expertise in Biodiesel trading and imports helps Biofuel companies extend their marketing reach and bring efficiency to low carbon markets. The company's Biofuel operations include:

- Biodiesel sales in the US and Canada
- Renewable Diesel
- Biodiesel Marketing
- Biodiesel Trading and Imports



POWER TRADING – Targray's Power Trading Division is focused on energy commodities across global markets. Specialized in creating pricing efficiency across multiple geographic markets, the company is involved in the physical and financial aspects of commodity trading. It's power trading activities include:

- Power Trading in Canadian and US Commodity Markets.
- Power Trading Partnership development.
- Energy Commodity Investing and Trade.



OPTICAL MEDIA MATERIALS – Targray is the world's largest single source supplier of materials to the optical media industry. It delivers a complete range of raw materials to the CD / DVD / and Blue Ray Disc Manufacturing Industry. The company's experience and expertise date back to the inception of the compact disc, and today it provides products and services to more than 500 optical media manufacturers worldwide. Products include:

- Replication - Polycarbonate, Sputtering Targets, Bonding, Lacquer, Gripper Fingers, Filters
- Mastering - Blu-ray Wafers, Mastering Glass, Evaporation Boats, Nickel
- Other - Suction Cups, Nest Pads

Product Name:
Silver Paste
In partnership with Heraeus, Targray offers the HeraSol Ag paste compositions.
These silver metallization pastes are customizable to your process, thus increasing efficiency and fill factor while providing wider processing windows. The HeraSol compositions are a series of screen-printable front and back side silver conductors for crystalline solar cells. The front side formulation is ideal for lightly-doped emitter designs and optimized for high throughput processing to deliver excellent aspect ratios and fine line resolution. For back side solder applications, the new lead-free silver paste significantly lowers material consumption due to high coverage and excellent initial and soldered age adhesion. http://www.targray.com/solar/cystalline-cell-materials/silver-paste.php


Product Name:
Sputtering Targets
Targray offers controlled target composition and design as well as improved supply and timely delivery, at a reduced overall cost. Our integrated solutions will optimize your thin film process and lower your manufacturing cost per watt. Targray is your ideal partner for thin film PVD applications. With over 20 years experience catering to thin film deposition, our expertise will allow you to capture greater overall efficiency gains. http://www.targray.com/solar/thin-film-materials/sputtering-targets.php

Product Name:
Solar Recycling Services

Our Anode Active Materials are compatible with SBR and PVDF binder. http://www.targray.com/li-ion-battery/anode-materials/anode-active-materials.php

Product Name:
Lithium-ion Battery Foils
Targray offers a range of Cu Foils depending on the application of the Li-ion battery. A rolled foil (RA-type), made from wrought Cu is generally used for high-energy, high-power applications such as drivetrain and power tools. An Electro-Deposited (ED) foil is used in high-energy, low-power applications such as Li-ion batteries for cell phones, laptops and consumer electronics. http://www.targray.com/li-ion-battery/anode-materials/foils.php

Product Name:
Cathode Active Materials
Targray offers three cathode active materials: Nickel-Cobalt Manganese Lithium-ion: LiNixMnyCozO2 / Spinel-Based Lithium-ion: LiMn2O4 / Cobalt-Based Lithium-ion: LiCoO2. http://www.targray.com/li-ion-battery/cathode-materials/

Product Name:
Binders
Working in conjunction with its supplier, Targray has added a modified aqueous SBR in its product portfolio that brings new cutting-edge technology to battery manufacturers. The modified SBR can be used for practically all Li-ion cell chemistries  for both the Anode and cathode electrodes. This technology offers distinct advantages for li-ion battery manufacturers. http://www.targray.com/li-ion-battery/cathode-materials/binders.php

Product Name:
Separators
Targray offers an advanced PP (polypropylene) Separator suitable for use in large scale batteries, such as EV batteries. Targray also offers PE Separators (high density polyethylene), both dry stretch and wet-stretch for use in a range of Lithium Ion battery types. http://www.targray.com/li-ion-battery/separators/

Product Name:
Electrolyte Solutions
The most commonly used Electrolyte is comprised of lithium salt, such as LiPF6 in an organic solution. In addition to lithium salt, a range of additives needs to be included in the finished Electrolyte to give the required properties to the Electrolyte solution. These additives improve the stability of the Electrolyte, and prevent dendrite formation and degradation of the solution. The specific Electrolyte formulation will vary depending on the specific Anode and Cathode Materials being used. In addition to the Electrolyte solutions, there is a range of gel polymer Electrolytes available, as well as solid film Electrolytes. http://www.targray.com/li-ion-battery/electrolyte.php

Product Name:
Packaging
Targray, working with multiple suppliers globally has developed its battery packaging product portfolio to suit the unique needs of our customers. Working in collaboration with customers and suppliers, custom enclosure dimensions are made available for all three configurations of the packaging geometries - cylindrical, prismatic as well as pouch cell configurations. http://www.targray.com/li-ion-battery/packaging-materials.php

Product Name:
Lithium Hydroxide
Targray carries a full rang of Lithium Hydroxide materials with different grades of compound purity and concentration for Lithium-ion battery precursor manufacturers. Lithium hydroxide is an inorganic compound with the formula LiOH. It is a white hygroscopic crystalline material. It is soluble in water and slightly soluble in ethanol. It is available commercially in anhydrous form and as the monohydrate (LiOH•H2O), both of which are strong bases.

Product Name:
Lithium Carbonate
Targray supplies high purity, battery grade Lithium Carbonate for manufacturers of Cathode materials. Lithium Carbonate Li2CO3 is polymeric and an ionic compound. Targray has partnered with one of the world's leading mining and chemical industry suppliers to develop its battery grade Lithium Carbonate (Li2CO3) product portfolio. Several grades of Li2CO3 are optimized and made available to Cathode and Electrolyte precursor materials manufacturers.

Product Name:
Electro-deposited Nickel Foil
Targray is a supplier of Electro-deposited (ED) Nickel foil, used as a current collector in the anode electrode production. Supercapacitors require very low internal resistance. Nickel foil inherently has strong anti-corrosive properties; therefore a surface treatment (which is commonly applied to Cu foil and causes an increase resistance) is not required.

Product Name:
Aluminum Foil
Aluminum foil is provided by Targray for use in Supercapacitor applications, acting as the current collector for the electrodes. High surface area and minimal oxide reduces the impedance of the capacitor.

Product Name:
Li-Ion Battery Manufacturing Equipment
Targray offers Commercial and R&D Equipment for li-ion battery and supercapacitor products. We offer customized system and automation solutions for production cell assembly lines, encompassing a full range of product needs to ensure consistent, quality production of large, medium or small format energy storage devices.

Product Name:
Li-ion Battery Pilot Line Equipment
Targray supplies a complete range of Pilot Line Equipment for research and development purposes. Our equipment is state-of-the-art and attuned to the precise needs of li-ion battery developers, encompassing every phase of production

Product Name:
Solar Wafers
Targray offers a full range of monocrystalline and multicrystalline solar wafers for solar cell manufacturing. Through our extensive global partner network, we have access to a reliable supply of high quality solar wafers to support both your short and long term product requirements. Our high quality solar wafers can be manufactured to your exact specifications.

Product Name:
Polysilicon
Targray supplies high-quality virgin polysilicon from major polysilicon manufacturers in a variety of grades and forms to best suit your monocrystalline and multicrystalline solar wafer production requirements.

Product Name:
Solar Ingot
Targray supplies both monocrystalline and multicrystalline solar silicon ingots and bricks.

Monocrystalline wafers generally have a higher efficiency conversion rate of sunlight to electricity. However, in both monocrystalline & multicrystalline crystallization processes, feedstock quality is a critically important issue. Targray assures the highest consistency and purity of polysilicon feedstock for the supply of our solar grade ingots.

Product Name:
Solar Cells
Targray offers a full range of monocrystalline and multicrystalline solar cells for solar module manufacturing. Through our extensive global partner network, we have access to a reliable supply of high quality mono & multi solar cells to support both your short and long term production requirements.

Product Name:
Renewable Diesel
Targray sells Hydrotreated Renewable Diesel, which can be produced from a variety of vegetable oils and waste animal fats. Renewable diesel can be blended in any proportion with petroleum diesel and helps end users, refiners and blenders meet renewable requirements and sustainability goals.

Product Name:
Supercapacitator Materials
Targray sells high-quality Aluminium Foil, Electro-deposited (ED) Nickel foil, Etched Aluminium foil and SBR Binder for supercapacitor applications.

Electric double-layer capacitors (EDLC) are also known as supercapacitors, electrochemical double layer capacitors (EDLCs) or ultracapacitors. Supercapacitors have the highest available capacitance values per unit volume and the greatest energy density of all capacitors.

Service Name:
Biodiesel
Targray is one of the largest North American biodiesel marketing and trading organizations with sales in both the US and Canada. We are committed to supplying quality biodiesel to our customers at a competitive cost. With over 25 years of logistics experience throughout the US we are able to provide our customers with reliable and consistent service and supply their biodiesel needs.

Service Name:
Power Trading
Dealing in both physical and financial aspects of the trade, Targray specializes in creating pricing efficiencies across multiple geographic markets.

Targray’s methods are based on thorough and encompassing risk management strategies, as well as complex quantitative analysis of potential energy markets. We truly understand our trade partners and put an emphasis on foreseeing the complexities of unique energy markets. The result is an insightful approach to power trading and the optimal performance of energy assets, maximizing return of investment for our trade partners.

In conjunction with our understanding of both United States and Canadian energy markets, Targray also holds licenses and permits from both countries, allowing us to do business seamlessly. With an engrained understanding of energy commodities, our seasoned power trading team delivers peace of mind and excellence to every client.
