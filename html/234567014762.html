Company Profile


Wilco Wireline and Swabbing Services Inc. is an Alberta based
oil and gas service company specializing in bottom hole pressure
surveys and other downhole completion requirements. Wilco
Wireline and Swabbing Services Inc. operated successfully for
fifteen years and has continually shown stability in a volatile
market. This is due to quality fieldwork and a professional
sales approach to petroleum engineers for both major and junior
energy companies.

Wilco Wireline and Swabbing Services' objective is to provide
the most professional service possible. We have superior
turn-around of pertinent downhole information and on site
electronic recorder interpretation.

Product Name:
Bottomhole Pressure and Temperature Recorders
The Starburst, Stellar Tech System 2000 and Z-I Probe Electronic
pressure/temperature recorders can be equipped with various
memory sizes ranging from 6000 to 500000 pressure and temperature
readings. It is capable of recording pressures up to 70000 kPa,
and temperatures up to 140O Celsius. Pressure accuracy of +/-
.024% of full range and pressure resolution of +/- .0003% of full
range. Temperature accuracy of +/- .0O C.

The Starburst Ultra Quartz electronic pressure/temperature
recorder has a memory size ranging from 26000 to 52000 pressure
and temperature readings. It is capable of recording pressure up
to 103400 kPa. Pressure accuracy of +/- .02% of full range and
pressure resolutions of +/- .00001% of full range. Temperature
accuracy resolution of +/- .005 C.

In addition to the above mentioned, Wilco Wireline and Swabbing
has additional brands of recorders available to suit all needs

Service Name:
Wireline Services
Wilco Wireline & Swabbing services provides wireline, swabbing
and crane rental services in the oil and gas industry. With
offices located in Calgary, Alberta and Westlock, Alberta we have
wireline units readily available 24 hours/per day 7 days a week.
Our fleet includes 6 wireline units, 2 crane units and 2 brand
new tandem axelled swab rigs. Even the best equipment is only as
good as it's people operating it, and we are proud to employ some
of the best people in the industry. By combining the best people
in the industry with innovative equipment, we get the job done
right. We specialize in bottom hole pressure surveys and downhole
completion work. We also provide fishing and consulting services,
data interpretation, PAS files, and consulting services.

Some of our units are equipped with 0.125 Stainless Steel
slickline for maximum pull power. Wilco also has two 4 X 4 units
which are capable of passing through most road bans and get at
hard to reach leases.
