International capital projects:

Personal Impact International's principle Kimberly Law was the first in Western Canada to receive international recognition as a Certified Image Professional through the Association of Image Consultants International (AICI) and currently serves on AICI's Board of Directors as President-Elect. In her leadership role she works with image and etiquette consultants from all over the world.
