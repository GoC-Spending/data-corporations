Company Profile


MMM Group Limited offers comprehensive consulting services in project management, engineering, planning and geomatics to government and private sector clients across Canada and overseas. MMM Group is an industry leader in program and project management, and the provision of multidisciplinary services for large-scale, complex projects such as international airports, large-scale urban and residential developments, transportation infrastructure, environmental initiatives and advanced geomatics applications.
MMM Group International Inc. (MMM) is a wholly owned subsidiary of MMM Group Limited, providing responsive and innovative consulting engineering services, planning, project management, environmental, surveying and commissioning services to private sector owners, architects, contractors, International Financial Institutions, investors and government clients internationally.
The company has been active internationally since 1964 and has successfully completed projects in more than 50 countries: in the Caribbean, Central and South America, Europe, the Middle East, Asia and Africa.
Over the past decade, the firm has applied its expertise in the private development of infrastructure to implement major airport projects in many countries and is now using that expertise in the development of major institutional buildings, solid waste management facilities, highways, bridges and other infrastructure requirements around the world.
