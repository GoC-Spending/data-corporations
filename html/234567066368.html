Company Profile


Company Background

Established in 2003, Ascenta Health Ltd. is a developer of innovative natural health products unsurpassed in quality and purity. Ascenta is an industry leader in omega-3 products and developer of NutraSea, Canadas #1 selling omega-3 fish oil supplement line. Ascenta is committed to producing supplements that improve health and promote optimal wellness. Its team of scientists and industry experts have a deep understanding of health issues and the expertise to develop high quality research-based, innovative solutions.

Ascenta is headquartered in Dartmouth, Nova Scotia, Canada. Its innovative natural health products are sold throughout North America, Europe and Asia. www.ascentahealth.com

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
NutraSea - Omega-3 fish oil supplements
NutraSea is an award winning, great tasting line of pharmaceutical grade omega-3 fish oils. NutraSea is the preferred brand of leading medical institutions and doctors in North America and the #1 selling omega-3 fish oil supplement in Canada.

o NutraSea Original is a balanced EPA/DHA formula for optimal health and well-being. NutraSea Original with a great lemon taste, and available in liquid and capsules, is suitable for children, adults and seniors. NutraSea Original provides 1500 mg omega-3 per serving and is beneficial for general health enhancement; cardiovascular protection; healthy joints and improved mobility; improved mental function and learning; brain, eye and nervous system health; healthy looking skin and hair; and healthy fetal and nervous system development.

o NutraSea HP high potency is a concentrated high EPA formula with therapeutic benefits. NutraSea HP has a unique 3:1 EPA/DHA ratio that supports a healthy inflammatory and immune response and is based on the most current medical research. NutraSea HP with a delicious lemon taste, provides 2300 mg of omega-3 per serving. NutraSea HP has therapeutic benefits for people suffering from cardiovascular disease; arthritis and joint mobility problems; immune system weakness; pain and inflammatory conditions; mood imbalance; poor fat metabolism.

All NutraSea products are third party tested through Ascenta's Pure Check program where each product batch is independently tested and certified for quality, purity and label claims. Quality test results are posted at www.ascentahealth.com for customers to see.
