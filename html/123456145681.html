Company Profile


Simcoe Block Limited is an independently owned and operated
family enterprise, which has served the construction supply
needs of building projects across Ontario Canada for over 50
years. Simcoe has four divisions, strategically located
throughout central Ontario. Simcoe is well positioned to service
the residential and commercial marketplace due to its
diversified structure. The structure of the company is comprised
of a manufacturing division, and a wholesale, retail, and
distributor supply division. Simcoe manufactures concrete
foundation block, as well as a wide range of colored
architectural units, and lightweight units for both the
residential and commercial markets. To complement our masonry
manufacturing capability, we offer a full range of masonry
products including brick, stone, and precast cement products
just to name a few. Our building center location in Barrie
offers a huge product line to assist contractors as well as
homeowners in their construction requirements. Simcoe has one of
the largest fleet of delivery vehicles in Ontario to service the
needs of our customers. Come and see for yourself why we are the
number one choice of contractor's and builders across Ontario.

Product Name:
BLOCKS, Architectural
Our architectural units are manufactured in Full Face Split,
Random Panel, Split Ledge, Scored Six Rib, 2,3,4 or 6 Rib Split
face styles. We also manufacture a complete line of Verticle
Score concrete units. All masonry units are produced in metric
modular sizes. All concrete block manufactured by Simcoe Block
conforms to CSA Standard A165.1 M94 or latest version.

To highlight your designs, choose from the many different block
colours available at Simcoe Block. Our design group will assist
you with the selection of the right colour for your project.
They will also help you with mortar pigmentation to match or
contrast block colour.
