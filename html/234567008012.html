Testimonial:

MEDIAFORCE has had the opportunity of working with many companies and people throughout the years and we've developed great working relationships.

Here's a little of what they had to say:

In the fall of 2012 we began interviewing over 35 companies Canada wide for the development and maintenance of a new website for the Facial Surgery & Cosmetic Centre of Ottawa, Inc. We were looking for a company that could create a dynamic website, write content, perform SEO and provide solid marketing strategies specific to our industry. Christine, our office administrator first spoke with Joe Bongiorno, President and CEO at MediaForce over the phone, at which time found him to be friendly, approachable and very competent. After just one meeting with Joe we knew we found the company we were looking for. Working with the team at MedicaForce over the past few months has been an extremely positive and enjoyable experience. All members of this talented group exhibit a high level of professionalism, are courteous as well as extremely knowledgeable in their areas of expertise. They offer excellent customer service and are always friendly and prompt to get back to us if we have questions or concerns. We are excited about working with MediaForce and look forward to a long term business relationship.

Bonnie Craig, face.ca
