Company Profile


At the Royal Oak Pet Clinic, we focus on restoring and preserving the health of all your four legged family members. We are veterinarians serving Victoria, providing medical and surgical care for cats, dogs, rabbits, chinchillas, degus, ferrets, guinea pigs, hamsters, hedgehogs, mice, and rats.

Service Name:
Veterinary Clinic
Clinic Services
Wellness and Preventive Medicine
Our focus is to help prevent disease and illness in your familys pets. Our goal is to help you minimize the need to treat and cure preventable diseases. Preventative medicine increases your pets chances of having a long and healthy life. http://www.royaloakpetclinic.com/services/ We accomplish this through routine annual physical examinations of your pet, proper nutrition, parasite control and through the use of a vaccination program based on an individualized risk assessment for your pet.

Nutritional Consultations and Pet Foods
We have a wide selection of veterinary pet foods and treats for both cats and dogs that can help your dog live a long and happy life. From adult maintenance diets to specially formulated diets, such as hypoallergenic and kidney formulations, we have the diet that will suit your pet. http://www.infovet.ca/royaloakpetclinic If we do not carry the specific brand of food that you need, we can often specially order it in for you.

Animal Behavior Consultations
An increasing number of products and training programs are available to help minimize or eliminate inappropriate behaviors in pets. Please feel free to consult with our staff should you have any questions or concerns regarding your pets behavior.

Service Name:
Veterinary Hospital
Hospital Services
24 hour emergency and critical care

The clinic works closely with Central Victoria Veterinary Hospital(CVVH), which provides the laboratory, radiology, surgical and hospitalization facilities that Dr. Chambers uses. Dr. Chambers generally starts her day at CVVH and visits frequently if any of her patients are hospitalized.

The hospital has an on-site veterinarian and emergency team 24 hours a day 7 days a week 365 days a year.
Walk in service for all emergencies.

24 hour veterinarian attended hospitalization

Intensive and critical care monitoring  including:

24 hour doctor attended critical care monitoring
24 hour doctor monitored IV Fluid therapy
Incubator and Baer Hugger thermoregulation
Separation / Isolation ward for contagious conditions
Constant rate medication infusion (CRI)
Oxygen therapy
Blood transfusions

Surgery
Orthopedic Surgery

Three staff veterinarians with over forty years combined experience in fracture repair and joint stabilization.

Soft tissue Surgery

Trauma and emergency surgery

Elective surgery
Spays, neuters and soft tissue procedures are performed at Central Victoria Veterinary Hospital by the primary care veterinarians who have CVVH surgical privileges. Click on our member clinic tab to arrange an appointment at a clinic near you.

Specialist Surgery

Appointments for complex surgical procedures can be arranged with a board certified specialist in surgery on a referral basis.

Dentistry
We do routine dental work such as cleaning and extractions by appointment. A pre-dentistry examination is required before any dental surgery in order to determine what needs to be done and also to evaluate your pets surgical health.
Dental procedures are performed at our hospital under the direction of the primary care veterinarians who have CVVH hospital privileges. Click on our member clinic tab to arrange an appointment at a clinic near you.

On site laboratory

Lasercyte automated complete blood count analyzer
Two Vet test chemistry analyzers
I stat blood gas analyzer
Vet stat blood gas analyzer
Two Vetlyte blood electrolyte analyzers
Vetlab U/A  Urinalysis analyzer
coagDx  blood coagulation analyzer
Cytology
Hematology
Parasitology
Urinalysis

Specialist referral

Board Certified Internal Medicine Specialist available by referral appointment
Board Certified Dental Specialist available by referral appointment.
Board Certified Surgeon available by referral appointment

Diagnostic Imaging

Digital radiography

X-Rays and related aids assist us in quickly assessing any physical injury or anomaly that may be affecting you pets well being. We can then quickly determine the appropriate course of action, medical and/or surgical.

PennHip evaluation
