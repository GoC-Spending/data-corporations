Company Profile


Mandrake is one of Canada's foremost executive search and consulting firms. As a strategic partner to some of the world's leading businesses, our primary goal is to enhance organizations by identifying and attracting outstanding leadership executives.
Our services, however, are not limited to Executive Search. We excel at brokering mergers and acquisitions, forging strategic alliances, and developing organization structures for long-term results. We offer clients a complete range of recruitment, selection, and hiring services through our subsidiary company, wwwork!com. In addition, we provide a full range of outplacement, executive coaching and performance improvement services through our sister company, NEXCareer.

Mandrake is the only Canadian management consulting firm that provides a complete range of services for managing the human capital equation, from intern to CEO, in all phases of your business' growth. We call it the Total Talent Solution.
