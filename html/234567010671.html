Company Profile


MTE Consultants is an employee-owned company that provides services in the fields of civil and structural engineering, building and environmental science, toxicology, and land surveying. Since beginning in 1985, MTE has grown to over 180 employees with offices in Kitchener, Burlington and Stratford. MTE puts our clients first, working together to design the right solution to meet their needs.
