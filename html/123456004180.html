Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Think on Your Feet(r)
Think on Your Feet(R) - Clarity. Brevity. Impact. 

Getting your ideas across clearly, concisely, persuasively (and
being remembered) is vital. Everybody wants to get to the
point. Quickly. Think on Your Feet® introduces the capsules-
of-persuasion concept 10 plans that structure your ideas
quickly  for impact. Answer questions on the spot. Explain
Complex data clearly. Be more Persuasive. Get to the point and
be remembered.

Think on Your Feet® is a practical, intensive workshop on how to
speak spontaneously with clarity and impact. The workshop
provides you simple, step-by-step methods to train your mind in
structured communication. The workshop is filled with practice,
coaching and feedback.

Public or customized on-site training programs are available.
Our accreditation and licensing program will also train members
of your organization to deliver our programs as an on-going part
of your training curriculum.


Product Name:
The Skilled Presenter(tm)
The Skilled Presenter(TM) - Latest techniques for improving your
presentation style.

This workshop examines the mechanics of how to deliver a
presentation. We focus on how listeners perceive the speaker,
and introduce techniques that professional speakers use to
prepare and deliver presentations. Participants deliver stand-
up presentations, and receive feedback from the instructor and
other participants. We will videotape the presentations to
record your progress.

Every participant receives a bound 190-page copy of The Skilled
Presenter manual. The text acts as a workbook during the
program, and as an easy-to-use reference afterwards.

Public or customized on-site training programs are available.
Our accreditation and licensing program will also train members
of your organization to deliver our programs as an on-going part
of your training curriculum.

Product Name:
Writing Dynamics(tm)
Writing Dynamics (TM) - The McLuhan & Davies system for writing
clearly, concisely, persuasively -- and fast.

The Internet has profoundly affected how we write. Shorter
sentences, compressed information, different styles of
paragraphing, lateral organization rather than linear.

This workshop thoroughly reviews the writing process  for
todays digital reader. Writing Dynamics also shows you how to
achieve quality writing and save time  by using a writing
system developed by our team of award-winning professional
writers.

Public or customized on-site training programs are available.
Our accreditation and licensing program will also train members
of your organization to deliver our programs as an on-going part
of your training curriculum.

Product Name:
Email Intelligence (TM)
Email Intelligence - Using Communication
Technology Effectively

Todays communication technology brings us together as never
before, as forecast by Marshal McLuhans Global Village. But
this electronic communication is still imperfect. It needs
the human touch to realize its potential.

Email Intelligence examines what causes
communication problems. Why and how the technology separates
us, although apparently connecting us. This half- or one-day
workshop provides strategies and solutions to meet these new
challenges. It offers ways to communicate more effectively,
using a combination of new and traditional communication
technologies.

This session assumes that e-mail is the number one electronic
communication method used in the workplace. Managing e-mail
therefore is a major focus.

Please note: this session is not a writing-skills workshop but a
strategy session on how to improve communication effectiveness
and productivity  using communication technology.

Customized on-site training workshops available for your group.

Product Name:
Clarity Mountain(TM)
Clarity Mountain - Getting to the top through words

Clarity Mountain is a two- or three-hour experiential game for
20 to 200 people featuring multimedia technology: IMEGG
(Interactive multimedia Experiential Group Game).

Clarity Mountain provides a fun and challenging learning
environment for communication skills and team building. It
helps refresh and stimulate participants professional skills by
reinforcing and expanding knowledge of how to achieve clarity in
the business world. It also shows hot to stay competitive by
exploring the latest trends in business communication.

Designed for your Special Team-Building Events! Questions can
be customized as part of our consulting services.

Product Name:
Mastering Communications(TM)
10 secrets to....

The concepts are based on research McLuhan & Davies
conducted over the last ten years, advising various
organizations on their communication needs.

We developed the workshop to help participants truly understand
the communication process, label its elements and measure its
effectiveness.

Anyone can use the concepts, professionally or personally.

Our research shows that when we communicate, we do so in three
distinct patterns. Recognizing these patterns and using them
consciously allows you to more accurately achieve
communication. Wavelengths also provides new ways to improve
your listening skills, by showing you what to listen for when
people communication in any of these patterns. That way you can
shift to the right wavelength to communicate more effectively.

Customized on-site training programs are available. Our
accreditation and licensing program will also train members of
your organization to deliver our programs as an on-going part of
your training curriculum.


Product Name:
Facilitating Learning(TM)
Facilitating Learning - How to Involve Todays Learner in the
Learning Process

Examining the difference between presenting and facilitating
provides the theme of this two-day workshop. We focus on how to
advance adult learning  through effective facilitation and
communication skills.

Topics include: involving audiences; handling difficult
participants and challenging class situations; managing group
discussions; meeting learning objectives; delivering information
clearly; creating interactive learning. Participants come
prepared to facilitate (not lecture or present) material they
currently deliver.

Customized on-site training programs are available.

Service Name:
Communication-skills training workshops
COMMUNICATION-SKILLS TRAINING WORKSHOPS - Like most people, you
want to continually improve the way you communicate - in person
and on paper. The main challenge you face in today's fast-paced
business environment is getting and holding peoples attention
long enough to get your message across. And how can you improve
your odds of getting your message heard? Learn to communicate
with Clarity, Brevity, and Impact.

At McLuhan & Davies, weve built our training business using
these principles. And we love sharing them with people like
you. People who want to learn proven techniques to persuade
people in person or on paper.

Communication-skill development is our specialty, and our unique
niche.

Public or customized on-site training programs are available.
Our accreditation and licensing program will also train members
of your organization to deliver our programs as an on-going part
of your training curriculum.

Service Name:
HR Project and Performance Management Services
Performance Management and HR Project Services  Improving
Performance through Better Communications

Most management and performance-related skills are
communications related in some way. Also, for learning and
change to take place at any level, communication-related issues
often need addressing, along with the training.

The McLuhan & Davies HR Division assigns expert consultants to
help organizations address these areas:
- Leadership communications
- Performance improvement
- Training and development
- Interpersonal & team communications
- Coaching & mentoring
- Recruiting, interviewing & orientation
- E-communications & people management systems
