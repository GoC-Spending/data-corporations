Company Profile


The Attain Group provides independent telecommunications, IT, data centre, audio-visual, wireless and security system consulting engineering services to both public and private real estate owners, federal government departments, architectural and construction engineering firms, and tenants. Our expertise helps these organizations to better understand, control, and protect their telecommunications assets.
Only The Attain Group specializes in applying its comprehensive communications domain knowledge to deliver both short-term and long-term strategies for the management of wired and wireless infrastructure. Our proven process provides the roadmap, the language, and the tools necessary to ensure that telecommunications services are installed and delivered securely, reliably, and cost-effectively with minimal impact to facilities and building design.

Service Name:
Telecommunications and Computer Network Engineering
We perform design and engineering to telecommunications and computer networks.

Service Name:
Telecom Building Condition Report
Telecommunications infrastructure is a vital asset for property owners. Management of this asset is necessary to retain existing tenants and attract new prospects. Building owners are often unaware of the state of their telecommunications assets, and may not have a clear understanding of which carriers are present in their building, what type of voice and data services they offer, or whether the building infrastructure is sufficient to support tenant technology requirements.
The Attain Group Telecom Building Condition Report is a comprehensive telecommunications audit that ensures property owners have a clear understanding of their telecommunications assets, associated liabilities, and lifecycle planning requirements for telecom and other low-voltage systems.

Service Name:
Telecom Maintenance & Planning
Telecommunications infrastructure is an integral part of the structure and lifecycle of a building. Yet, building owners and managers are often unaware of the state of their telecom infrastructure until a problem arises.

With tenants selecting their communications service providers from a growing array of choices, a wide range of technicians may perform work in telecom rooms, through conduits and in ceiling and wall spaces. A proliferation of technologies, both wired and wireless, also adds to the complexity of telecom property management today.

The Attain Group Telecom Maintenance & Planning service provides building owners and managers with both long-term planning and short-term maintenance. We work to manage telecommunications assets consistent with the goals you have for your property.



Service Name:
Telecom Training for Property Managers
As an unregulated utility, telecommunications is often absent from the education and training available to property managers. Yet, property managers are faced with numerous requests from tenants and carriers related to telecommunications and associated work orders every day.

The Attain Group provides Telecom Property Management Training to building owners and property managers, equipping them with the tools and knowledge necessary to resolve tenant issues and maintain control of their telecommunications assets.

Our Telecom Property Management Training can be accessed in a variety of forms, including:

Privately, through customized telecom training developed specifically for you and delivered either in-house, or at an external location.
Publicly, through workshops and seminars that we offer.
Through industry associations such as BOMA.


Service Name:
Wireless Services
Wireless systems are increasingly prevalent in today's communications systems. Wireless systems require unique planning and proper installation to ensure that neither the building nor the property owner is at risk.

The Attain Group's Wireless Services ensure that wireless communications infrastructure is sufficient to meet tenants' needs, as well as properly installed and maintained to protect the building envelope and abide by applicable building and fire codes.

The Attain Group will:

Conduct a wireless site-survey and threat-risk assessment (TRA).
Provide site planning and budgeting for multi-frequency wireless extension service.
Design and implement wireless access point placement.


Service Name:
IT Cable Plant Lifecycle Management
The Attain Group Lifecycle Management service helps IT and cable plant managers to ensure they have a solid plan  and budget  in place to maintain their telecommunications systems and upgrade them based on their future technology requirements.

The Attain Group will:

Perform a telecommunications cable audit to document all existing telecom assets and to assess the state of those assets.
Check for compliance to all applicable codes and standards.
Provide a detailed lifecycle plan over 5-25 years, with associated tasks and budgets.
Establish an up-to-date record of all low-voltage systems, along with a maintenance process.
Provide records management services and/or software on an ongoing basis as required.

Service Name:
Cable Management
The Attain Group offers a cable management/maintenance service designed to help IT and cable managers get a handle on the telecom assets they oversee. We help manage on-going moves, adds and changes and the associated quality and access issues - all while ensuring adherence to installation standards and codes. By establishing a process that documents activities and ensures building safety, we help managers keep their records up to date  facilitating accurate planning and budgeting.

The Attain Group will:

Audit and document all existing telecommunications assets.
Create a database of assets and a record of ownership, connectivity, and lease details for each asset.
Establish a records management process  including for telecom room access  for the client to follow.
Oversee the management of client telecommunications records and telecom room access.
In addition, clients can choose the Attain Information Management (AIM) database - an online service that provides clients with access to view, update, and maintain their telecommunications records.



Service Name:
Telecom Technical Audit
The Attain Group provides the telecommunications expertise necessary to complete the telecommunications component of a technical audit. By engaging our team, you prevent the need for after-the-fact design changes, and eliminate costly oversights related to tenant connectivity needs.

The Attain Group will:

Consider both inside and outside plant infrastructures, ensuring that telecom rooms are sufficient and appropriately placed.
Assess conduit and tray to ensure that sufficient pathways for low-voltage systems are incorporated into the building design.
Through our Wireless Services, ensure that wireless rooftop telecommunications infrastructure has been considered and sufficiently planned for.
Review proposed telecommunications products, or make recommendations to suit the project goals, budget, and tenant needs.
Our technical audit services can encompass all low-voltage communications systems, including telecommunications, networking, paging systems, security systems, and more. Once the audit is complete, we may be engaged to provide telecommunications design-build services.



Service Name:
Telecom Design-Build
The Attain Group Telecommunications Design-Build services ensure that new buildings, expansions, renovations and re-fits adequately support this critical infrastructure. Our Telecommunications Design-Build service offerings support and complement the design/build deliverables performed by the ACE community.

The Attain Group will:

Work jointly with the architect, engineer, building owner and tenant to determine accurate and detailed telecom requirements.
Based on the requirements, produce a telecom design and detailed cost analysis for discussion with the architect, engineer and property owner.
Provide detailed schematics and all required computer-assisted design (CADD) for engineering review and acceptance.
Develop telecommunications specifications for the procurement and deployment of telecom assets.
Provide tender call services on behalf of the architect and engineer, including tendering bids for all telecommunications-related products and labour.
Monitor contractor implementation of the telecommunications infrastructure and the commissioning of all completed work, ensure compliance with codes and best-practices and receive sign off from the engineer.
Provide our Wireless Services to ensure adherence to wireless hardware construction specifications and rooftop installation best practices.
Our design-build services can encompass all low-voltage communications systems, including telecommunications, networking, paging systems, security systems, and more.



Service Name:
Telecommunications Retro-Fit
The Attain Group telecommunications retrofit services ensure that these projects are well planned and smoothly executed with a focus on meeting time and budget parameters.

The Attain Group will:

Interface with the tenant to determine their needs and develop a telecom functional requirements document.
Produce a telecom design concept, including detailed schematics and any required computer-assisted design (CADD).
Provide technical writing services on behalf of the client, including writing the technical specifications document during the tender process.
Assist IT and facilities managers in the technical evaluation of tender responses.
Represent the client to ensure all telecom requirements and deliverables are addressed during project meetings with the PWGSC Technical Authority, trades and construction primes.
Project-manage the telecommunications infrastructure implementation including inside wiring, outside plant, and wireless rooftop assets.
Verify all completed work to ensure compliance with codes and best-practices.
Obtain manufacturer certification on behalf of the client to ensure the new telecom infrastructure is guaranteed for product and application support.
Our telecommunications retro fit services ensure a smooth transition to new infrastructure that is transparent to your end-user community.


Service Name:
Specialty ACE Consulting
As experts in the field of telecommunications building science, The Attain Group works with design-build professionals through the planning, budgeting, design, construction, management, and maintenance of telecommunications and other low-voltage building infrastructure.

Our services for architects, building consultants, and engineers includes the specialty consulting offerings:

Lifecycle cost analysis - A multi-year plan for the maintenance and upgrade of the specific telecommunications (and other low-voltage) infrastructure.
Tenant technology integration - We work with large tenants to manage infrastructure change; define requirements; tender bids; review products and vendors; design and implement structured cabling systems; design and manage the building and operation of voice and data networks; and to provide training and processes around building policies and telecommunications best-practices.
Integration and convergence - Consulting, design, and project management services for the integration of low-voltage systems into a single maintainable infrastructure.
Infrastructure management system - A process, database, and online service that helps clients to maintain and manage their low-voltage equipment records.

Unique applications:

Attain Services Reduce New Telecom Construction Costs by 25%
Problem/Needs:

A government department had recently purchased a 40,000 square foot building with an existing telecom infrastructure in place. The department wished to assess the existing infrastructure, identify which components were re-usable and of value, and determine whether any additional telecommunications equipment needed to be installed to meet the departments needs.

Process: The Attain Group provided its Telecom Building Conditioning Report service to the client. This service included:

Examination of inside and outside plant infrastructures to ensure that telecom rooms were sufficient and appropriately placed.
Conduit assessments to ensure that sufficient pathways for low-voltage systems were present
Rooftop audits to ensure that the existing wireless rooftop telecommunications infrastructure was sufficient

Work Performed: In delivering the Telecom Building Conditioning Report service, The Attain Group audited and assessed the existing cable pathway infrastructure (both copper and fibre) to determine its inherent asset value and its reusability. Based on its assessment of the existing telecom infrastructure, The Attain Group was able to provide a report detailing further telecommunications equipment that would be required to meet the departments needs.

Results: Based on The Attain Groups review, the department was able to reclaim a large portion of the buildings existing telecom infrastructure. By reusing the existing assets identified by The Attain Group, the department was able to save 25% of the total telecommunications construction budget cost.

Success stories:

Attain Consulting Services Lead to $25-Million Cable Plant Upgrade
Problem/Needs:
A division of the federal government required an audit of its aging telecommunications infrastructure. With more than 150 buildings and seven remote campuses across the province, the group lacked the internal resources to devote to such a project and wanted a third party to inspect and report on all components of its telecommunications infrastructure and provide a 15 year lifecycle budget.

Process: The Attain Group provided its Lifecycle Management service to complete the project. This included:
A comprehensive telecommunications equipment audit to document all existing telecom assets and to assess the state of those assets
Verification of standards-compliant grounding and confirmation that the assets did not breach fire codes
Delivery of a detailed 15-year lifecycle plan with associated tasks and budgets.
An up-to-date equipment record of all low-voltage systems, along with a maintenance process

Work Performed: In delivering the Lifecycle Management service, The Attain Group examined all telecom assets, determined their presen- day value, estimated replacement schedules and projected the existing equipments future costs. Attain also:

Provided a report on immediate deficiencies and associated repair costs
Identified current or potential health and safety violations and methods for their remedy
Delivered a complete 15-year lifecycle management report for predictive costing and budgeting of the organizations telecommunications infrastructure

Results: Based on the recommendations in The Attain Groups report, the client's cable plant manager was able to receive $25 million in funding within one week of submitting his budgetary request.

Process: The Attain Group provided its Telecom Building Conditioning Report service to the client. This service included:

Examination of inside and outside plant infrastructures to ensure that telecom rooms were sufficient and appropriately placed.
Conduit assessments to ensure that sufficient pathways for low-voltage systems were present
Rooftop audits to ensure that the existing wireless rooftop telecommunications infrastructure was sufficient

Work Performed: In delivering the Telecom Building Conditioning Report service, The Attain Group audited and assessed the existing cable pathway infrastructure (both copper and fibre) to determine its inherent asset value and its reusability. Based on its assessment of the existing telecom infrastructure, The Attain Group was able to provide a report detailing further telecommunications equipment that would be required to meet the departments needs.

Results: Based on The Attain Groups review, the department was able to reclaim a large portion of the buildings existing telecom infrastructure. By reusing the existing assets identified by The Attain Group, the department was able to save 25% of the total telecommunications construction budget cost.

Attain Consulting Services Lead to $25-Million Cable Plant Upgrade
Problem/Needs:
A division of the federal government required an audit of its aging telecommunications infrastructure. With more than 150 buildings and seven remote campuses across the province, the group lacked the internal resources to devote to such a project and wanted a third party to inspect and report on all components of its telecommunications infrastructure and provide a 15 year lifecycle budget.

Process: The Attain Group provided its Lifecycle Management service to complete the project. This included:
A comprehensive telecommunications equipment audit to document all existing telecom assets and to assess the state of those assets
Verification of standards-compliant grounding and confirmation that the assets did not breach fire codes
Delivery of a detailed 15-year lifecycle plan with associated tasks and budgets.
An up-to-date equipment record of all low-voltage systems, along with a maintenance process

Work Performed: In delivering the Lifecycle Management service, The Attain Group examined all telecom assets, determined their presen- day value, estimated replacement schedules and projected the existing equipments future costs. Attain also:

Provided a report on immediate deficiencies and associated repair costs
Identified current or potential health and safety violations and methods for their remedy
Delivered a complete 15-year lifecycle management report for predictive costing and budgeting of the organizations telecommunications infrastructure

Results: Based on the recommendations in The Attain Groups report, the client's cable plant manager was able to receive $25 million in funding within one week of submitting his budgetary request.
