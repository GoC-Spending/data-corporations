Company Profile


Pixel Ink Retouching featuring retoucher and prepress professional Mark Veldhoven. Providing one-stop image crafting service to creative agencies, photographers, prepress and more. Beautiful colour and captivating images for advertising, fashion, publishing, prepress, web and custom image work. Calibrated retouching studio with IDEAlliance ISO GRACoL, SWOP and SNAP certified colour proofing, photo printing, prepress and scanning services.
