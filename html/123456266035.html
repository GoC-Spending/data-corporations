Company Profile


For 75 years Spiral Binding has been a leading manufacturer and worldwide distributor of a diverse line of document finishing equipment and supplies, as well as presentation products and custom imprinting services. We serve major corporations, small to mid-sized businesses, printers, facility management organizations worldwide, as well as the Canadian and US government and education markets. Spirals customers enjoy a rich selection of quality binding, laminating and finishing equipment and supplies.

Product Name:
Roll Laminators
Roll Laminators
Seal it in plastic... instantly, permanently and inexpensively.
Anything that is written, typed, printed, photographed or drawn
can be sealed in plastic! Protect and preserve sales materials,
visual aids, menus and documents quickly and easily. Spiral's
line of laminators fills every need from inexpensive pouch
laminators to heavy-duty industrial laminators.

RSL-2702 Roll Laminator
Fast - Laminates thermal and pressure sensitive films up to 27" (685 mm) wide at speeds up to 8.2" (2.5m/min) per minute.
Versatile - Able to mount rigid boards up to 3/16" (6mm) and laminate up to 10mil (250mic) film, including THINFLEX.
Flexible - Designed for laminating, encapsulating and mounting with adjustable nip settings (max. opening 3/16"/6mm).
Easy To Use - Soft touch digital keypad allows easy operation and function control of temperature, speed and stored programs.
Convenient - Swing-out arm film shafts and drop down idler all in a tabletop roll laminator designed for a small working area.

25" Industrial Laminator
Dependable...Productive...Trouble free...Easy to use...Versatile...Easy to maintain.These are the kinds of terms owners of the HD-25 use to describe their laminator. Intelligent Heat Controllers - display both the set temperature and the actual temperature. the microprocessor in the control quickly learns to anticipate the heat demand of each job and keeps the actual temperature within a few degrees of the set temperature.
Laminating and Pull Rollers - are covered with quality silicone rubber for superior release quality, higher lamination quality and easier cleaning. The laminating rollers and pull rollers are interchangeable.
Removable Heat Shoes - are teflon coated to reduce film abrasion. They are easily unplugged and removed to clean or replace the laminating rollers.
Built-in Retractable Slitters - save an enormous amount of cutting time by trimming away side scrap during the lamination process. The two heads can be adjusted independently with great precision.
Dual Tension Knobs - (clutches) for each supply roll mandrel are easy to use and provide as little or as much tension as needed to keep work wrinkle-free.
Forced Air Cooling - is essential when running thicker films, because the lamination needs to be cooled to get good results.
Easy Access Controls - are conveniently located and grouped for effective operation.
A Safety Guard - is built into the feed tray to help insure it will always be in place when laminating.
Both upper and Lower Rollers Are Driven - to minimize the wrinkling or curling that can occur when only one roller is driven
Standard Precision Feed Tray - has both left and right side guides and can be replaced with the power feed tray in seconds.

27" Professor Laminator
The 27" Professor has been engineered to provide years of service with minimal maintenance. It laminates with up to 10 mil film and mounts materials up to 3/16" thick. Its unique self-threading feature reduces the anxiety often associated with threading a laminator. The hot roll design permits simultaneous mounting and laminating using heat activated mounting boards.

Mighty Lam 2700 27" Laminator
The MightyLam 2700 is capable of laminating from 1.5 mil to 10 mil laminating film. Simplified, easy-to-set heat controls, and a variable speed control for operating at any speed up to 10 feet per minute.

25" Premier IV Laminator
Variable Speed - operates at any speed up to 10 feet per minute. Thicker films are generally applied better at mid-range to lower speeds, while 1.5 mil film should be applied at mid-range to higher speeds.
Digital LCD Temperature Display - makes it easy to set and maintain the correct temperature. The indicator light inside the heat switch comes on as the machine warms up; at set temperature the indicator light turns off.
Simple Controls- Conveniently placed switches provide a compact, reliable control pad, icons and international symbols are used for controls and safety warnings.
Slitters - Experts know the value of slitters for laminating a quantity of same-size sheets. The slitters remove the side scrap from both sides of each sheet as it goes through the laminator... this greatly reduces the amount of time required for trimming.
Glitch-proof Threading - Threading can be the most difficult aspect of using a laminator, so the Premier 4 has built in threading aids. The top idler is guarded from the top heat shoe for safety and easy threading. The bottom idler is attached to the feed tray. Once the upper and lower webs of film have been run through the machine, bottom idler threading is automatic when the operator puts the feed tray on the machine.

25" Educator Laminator
Preset lamination pressure and speed - makes the machine easier to use and eliminates many possible sources of problems.
Enclosed laminating and pull rollers - help you avoid time-consuming jams and wrap arounds.
Forced air cooling - allows laminating with 1.5 and 3 mil films, Without a film cooling system, thicker films will warp and ripple because they are still hot and partly melted when they come out the back of the laminator.
Fixed-position lamination rollers - provide a stable platform for uniform roll pressure to provide the highest quality lamination available in a school laminator.
Attached safety shield - the safety shield is fixed to the machine and is placed so that the operator will naturally put it in the guard position before laminating. The shield is made of clear polycarbonate so users can see their work and will not be tempted to try to laminate without the shield in its safe position.
Reverse switch - can be essential at times to help avoid problems or clear misfeeds.

Easylam 27" Laminator
The easy-to-use design makes the operator's job simpler with features such as:
Easy Loading - Plenty of clearance is allowed for film loading. No more burnt knuckles when "hot loading".
Easy-To-Use Controls - Since pressure, speed, and roll tension are preset, the laminator requires very little instruction to use.
Adjustable Heat Knob - Heat is easily adjustable for different materials.
Reverse Switch - Quickly clears any wrap-arounds.

MiniKote 27" Roll Laminator
-User preset saves speed and tempurature settings by defined users.
- Factory presets set a speed and temperature that is optimal for 1.5 mil standard film.
- Usage counter with reset easily tracks film usage.
- Hand held slitter conveniant and handy with storage right on the machine.
- Adjustable feed guide assures exact lamination.
- One inch mandrels standard (2-5/16" supply roll mandrel kit optional.)
- Safety shield prevents accidental contact with heated areas of machine.
- Keyswitch prevents unauthorized use of the laminator
- Stop/Reverse button to reverse the machine.
- Digital display shows tempurature, speed and usage counter.
- Temperature controls increase and decrease the tempature settings in 1° increments.
- UL listed

Signmaster 44 Laminator
The Signmaster 44 combines the features and benefits of the well-known Econocraft pressure-sensitive roll-applicator with the XL44 pouch mounter/laminator.

Xyron 4400 44" Wide PressureSensitive Laminator w/ Stand

Product Name:
Spiral-O® Wire Binding
Spiral-O® Wire Binding
For that Hi-Tech look, use the wire binding that works with your
plastic binding machine!
Spiral-O ® Wire Binding is a unique wire binding -- it's the only
one that conforms to the standard hole-punching patterns of
plastic binding machines. Simply punch your booklet on your
plastic binding machine and bind on our inexpensive E-Z Closer
using Spiral-O ® Wire. Spiral-O ® offers extra strength and
durability. Pages turn more easily, open and lie flat and can
turn 360° degrees back to back. A wide selection of sizes and
colors is available. With Spiral-O ®, the sleek, professional
look of wire binding is now available to plastic binding punch
owners!

Product Name:
Thermal Binding
Thermal Binding
Simple, secure and versatile... Turn loose sheets into perfectly
bound documents! Pages bound by our thermal binding machines are held securely in place with a strong adhesive that enables
documents to withstand the wear and tear of repeated readings.
It's simple to use: just insert pages into the cover, place the
document in the binding machine and wait 30 seconds, and your
document is ready to go! Corrections or revisions are easily made
too!

Coverbind® 101 DFS
The Coverbind® 101 DFS Document Finishing System is a powerful, automatic and flexible thermal binder for moderate to heavy use. This remarkable innovation produces up to 900 bound documents per hour, making the 101 DFS well matched to high-speed printers and copiers.

Capable of producing bound documents with exceptional book-like appearance, the Coverbind® 101 DFS gives all your company's documents a professional impression.

Perfect binding has never been easier, the 101 even tells you which cover size to select. Drop the paper into the cover and the cover into the 101 and within seconds remove the bound document ready for distribution.

Coverbind® 5000
The CB5000 is a table top unit designed for individual and shared use locations with 500 or more documents per year. The CB5000 binds documents up to two inches thick (500 sheets) or it can simultaneously bind 10 small documents. This compact and durable system sets the standard for productivity. The Coverbind 5000 is the fastest table top machine on the market.

Coverbind® Personal Binder
The Personal Binder (PB) is a compact desktop unit designed for individual users with modest document volumes.
The PB binds documents up to 120 20# bond 8½" x 11" sheets. Up to three small documents can be bound simultaneously.

25" Industrial Laminator
Dependable...Productive...Trouble free...Easy to use...Versatile...Easy to maintain.These are the kinds of terms owners of the HD-25 use to describe their laminator. Intelligent Heat Controllers - display both the set temperature and the actual temperature. the microprocessor in the control quickly learns to anticipate the heat demand of each job and keeps the actual temperature within a few degrees of the set temperature.
Laminating and Pull Rollers - are covered with quality silicone rubber for superior release quality, higher lamination quality and easier cleaning. The laminating rollers and pull rollers are interchangeable.
Removable Heat Shoes - are teflon coated to reduce film abrasion. They are easily unplugged and removed to clean or replace the laminating rollers.
Built-in Retractable Slitters - save an enormous amount of cutting time by trimming away side scrap during the lamination process. The two heads can be adjusted independently with great precision.
Dual Tension Knobs - (clutches) for each supply roll mandrel are easy to use and provide as little or as much tension as needed to keep work wrinkle-free.
Forced Air Cooling - is essential when running thicker films, because the lamination needs to be cooled to get good results.
Easy Access Controls - are conveniently located and grouped for effective operation.
A Safety Guard - is built into the feed tray to help insure it will always be in place when laminating.
Both upper and Lower Rollers Are Driven - to minimize the wrinkling or curling that can occur when only one roller is driven
Standard Precision Feed Tray - has both left and right side guides and can be replaced with the power feed tray in seconds.

27" Professor Laminator

Mighty Lam 2700 27" Laminator
The MightyLam 2700 is capable of laminating from 1.5 mil to 10 mil laminating film. Simplified, easy-to-set heat controls, and a variable speed control for operating at any speed up to 10 feet per minute.

25" Premier IV Laminator
Variable Speed - operates at any speed up to 10 feet per minute. Thicker films are generally applied better at mid-range to lower speeds, while 1.5 mil film should be applied at mid-range to higher speeds.
Digital LCD Temperature Display - makes it easy to set and maintain the correct temperature. The indicator light inside the heat switch comes on as the machine warms up; at set temperature the indicator light turns off.
Simple Controls- Conveniently placed switches provide a compact, reliable control pad, icons and international symbols are used for controls and safety warnings.
Slitters - Experts know the value of slitters for laminating a quantity of same-size sheets. The slitters remove the side scrap from both sides of each sheet as it goes through the laminator... this greatly reduces the amount of time required for trimming.
Glitch-proof Threading - Threading can be the most difficult aspect of using a laminator, so the Premier 4 has built in threading aids. The top idler is guarded from the top heat shoe for safety and easy threading. The bottom idler is attached to the feed tray. Once the upper and lower webs of film have been run through the machine, bottom idler threading is automatic when the operator puts the feed tray on the machine.

25" Educator Laminator
Preset lamination pressure and speed - makes the machine easier to use and eliminates many possible sources of problems.
Enclosed laminating and pull rollers - help you avoid time-consuming jams and wrap arounds.
Forced air cooling - allows laminating with 1.5 and 3 mil films, Without a film cooling system, thicker films will warp and ripple because they are still hot and partly melted when they come out the back of the laminator.
Fixed-position lamination rollers - provide a stable platform for uniform roll pressure to provide the highest quality lamination available in a school laminator.
Attached safety shield - the safety shield is fixed to the machine and is placed so that the operator will naturally put it in the guard position before laminating. The shield is made of clear polycarbonate so users can see their work and will not be tempted to try to laminate without the shield in its safe position.
Reverse switch - can be essential at times to help avoid problems or clear misfeeds.
