Company Profile


Legend Power is an electrical energy conservation product provider - we help commercial buildings and high density residential buildings use less electricity. We are the developer and manufacturer of a patented Voltage Optimization solution named the Harmonizer. The Harmonizer reduces a building's electricity usage by up to 8%.

The Harmonizer is installed in a building's electrical room where it regulates and reduces a building's voltage levels. The Harmonizer remains dynamic with the fluctuating power grid, changing reduction settings in real time to ensure voltage levels are always at an optimal level. If Incoming voltage drops too low, the Harmonizer will bypass itself form the electrical system until voltage levels increase.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
North American Owner/Distr. of Electrical Harmonizer-AVR commercial/institutional/light industrial-scale energy conservation device (world patented)
The Electrical Harmonizer-AVR is the world's first proven, sustainable and cost effective solution to address energy conservation in commercial and industrial facilities through voltage optimization.

Energy savings from the Electrical Harmonizer-AVR are based on Conservation Voltage Regulation (CVR). The main concept behind CVR is that customers receiving voltage at higher levels are unwittingly paying high electricity bills, overdriving their equipment and wasting energy.

The Electrical Harmonizer-AVR has proven to achieve the following benefits:

* 7-12% reduction in electricity bills ($)
* 8-12% reduction in peak demand (kW)
* 7-10% reduction in energy consumption (kWh)
* Reduction in Greenhouse Gas emissions
* Reduction of direct and indirect facility maintenance costs
* Extended life-cycles of machinery and equipment
* Improved overall power quality issues
* Swappable under load with no downtime to facility
* Conservation with no lifestyle changes

Technology profile

The Electrical Harmonizer can be used to reduce consumption of
electrical power on a large scale. The larger units will
minimize power consumption for facilities as large as an entire
shopping mall or office tower.

The Harmonizer is installed in series with a facility's main
electrical incomer, at the 480/600 volt level. It requires
only the adaptation of the present 3 phase wiring to that of
the 3 phase wiring on either side of the Electrical Harmonizer
device, along with the installation of the electrical panel box
which contains the Harmonizer device.

The unit is provided for no upfront cost, through a performance
based approach, to all large energy consumers who
meet the qualifications for the Legend Power installation
program. Through this approach, the measured and documented
savings are shared between the Legend and the customer. This
requires no large outlay of capital on the part of the
customer's company, and eliminates risk. Legend Power also
offers a direct sale,lease, and lease to purchase program.

Once a company meets the qualification criteria, a unit will be
ordered for the specific power requirements of the facility,
based on Peak kilowatt Demand and monthly kwh consumed.

It will be built, shipped and installed within 90 days, to
allow customers to begin accumulating their electrical and
maintenance cost savings immediately.

Sector information

Key / Major clients:

LPSI's clients include: BC Hydro, Canada Post, IKEA, Honda and Science World.

Success stories:

Canada Post

Legend Power Systems Inc. (LPSI) and BLJC installed Electrical Harmonizers in the Canada Post facilities in Vancouver and Victoria.

Based on pre-installation audits the Vancouver facility installed two 1,500 amp Electrical Harmonizers, which were installed on the A and B distribution circuits ahead of the distribution panels, and the Victoria facility installed one 400 amp Electrical Harmonizer.

These buildings were 'Power Smarted' by BC Hydro prior to the installation and both buildings still generated significant savings. The results of these installations were verified by a third party consultant.

Since the installation of the Electrical Harmonizer the following results have been achieved and will be persistent into the future:

Vancouver
* 900,802 kWhrs reduction in annual energy consumption (8%)
* 88 kW reduction in peak

Victoria
* 167,055 kWhrs reduction in annual energy consumption (7%)
* 50 kW reduction in peak demand

Since the installation of the Electrical Harmonizers at the Canada Post buildings in Vancouver and Victoria on January 2004 and December 2003 respectively the facilities electrical distribution systems have operated with optimized efficiency.

Testimonial:

"Based on the results observed, it has been concluded that the Harmonizer made significant energy savings and met BC Hydro's Power Smart criteria for energy conservation?"
"The Power Smart program recognizes the above and will accept incentive funding applications from our customers to install the Harmonizer for energy savings."
 Bev Van Ruyven, Senior Vice-President, Distribution BC Hydro

"Even during our summer period with air conditioning loads, we still ended up with the equivalent electrical bill saving results in the range of 8.7% on our entire facility load."

 Doug McCann, General Manager
IKEA Richmond Ltd.
