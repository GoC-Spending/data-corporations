Company Profile


Please note: Tina Nicolopoulos will forward calls from Canada to SGS (GIS) office sales.

At SGS, we provide independent services that touch the lives of millions of people across the globe. We provide you with industry leading inspection, verification, testing and certification services  anywhere in the world.

SGS has 70,000 employees, 1,350 offices, and 150 laboratories worldwide.

Our services enable you to operate in a more sustainable manner by improving quality and productivity, reducing risk, verifying compliance and increasing speed to market.

When it comes to the products and services you produce or purchase, their quality, safety and compliance is vital. Whatever your industry, wherever your operations, we deliver essential information  when you need to be sure.

SGS is entrusted by several public administrations to protect consumers and domestic industries against unsafe, substandard or fake products, promoting trade facilitation and fair competition.

SGS has unique solutions of Product Conformity Assessment progams that can help your company with Certification of Conformity - CoCs that is required for regulated products in the Middle East and African countries. This will help your company comply with specific regulations and international standards for quality, ecology, safety, reliability, and effectiveness for the product being exported.

Countries that SGS governments and instituions (GIS) office can help in the following countries: Algeria, Botswana, Iraq (Kurdistan), Indonesia, Kenya, Kuwait, Mongolia, Nigeria, Russia, Saudi Arabia, Syria, Tanzania(new), Uganda, and Zambia.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
CERTIFICATE OF CONFORMITY (Certificate of Quality Control of Goods)
Certificate of Conformity: SGS will provide CoCs that are needed by Exporters in Canada or U.S. to evaluate their product certifications to International standard requirements so that their product will pass through customs so product can be sold into that country.

BASIC FEATURES
SGS will:

> Help you to provide complete product documentation relevant to the product and technical requirements of the importing country.
> Review the documentation for accuracy.
> Suggest and organize testing or additional reports that may be required.
> Schedule a physical inspection of the goods (if needed) or if necessary, perform a factory audit, in USA and Canada.
> Verify that specifications, quality and quantity match your product documentation;
> Issue a Certificate of Conformity for the products to be shipped

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
SGS will ask you to provide:

> Request for Certification (RFC) form
> Copy of the Proforma invoice (for the final certification, final invoice will be required)
> Product technical specifications, manufacturing records and test reports according to the applicable standards in the country of importation.
> Other documents specific to the importing country.
> Proof of payment for the Certification services.

We invite you to contact SGS if any of the above countries is the final destination of your products.
We will be pleased to assist you in obtaining the mandatory Certificate of Conformity.

Service Name:
Cargo Validations (CVS)
SGS is currently active in Cargo Validation programs on behalf of North American importers all around the globe. Business customers, consumers and end users have expectations about products and services and the delivery of those promises.

No more searching all over the world to find a reputable inspection firm that will protect your interests
against fraud, dishonesty or mistakes. SGS North Americas Miami office will take great pride in representing
your firm at your suppliers location, anywhere in the world, ensuring that what you requested is what you
receive. You can call one central location for all your inspection needs.

OBJECTIVES
SGSs primary objectives for our Cargo Validation Services are;

Elevate the accuracy of your imports by validating the cargo before it ships.
Make sure that your suppliers products match the measure of your order.
To facilitate swift and trouble free transactions between buyers and sellers.
Create a level of certainty beyond blind trust.
Provide proof of shipment content for banks and lending institution.
To stop inaccuracies in shipments before they leave your suppliers warehouse.
To prevent the costly return of goods to your suppliers overseas.
To increase your sales by helping you receive what you ordered without complications.

BASIC FEATURES
SGS will;
Count and visually validate all your purchases at the suppliers location, worldwide.
Validate that the product features match your expectations.
Reduce conflict between buyers and sellers increasing the speed of transactions.
Help you gain and maintain confidence in your supplier.
Support your bank or lending institution and allow your supplier to be paid more swiftly.
Eliminate short or long quantity shipments and the man hours it takes to correct them.
Help you receive what you ordered and place it into distribution quickly.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
SGS will ask you to provide a;
Request for Cargo Validation form.
Copy of your order to the supplier and L/C if applicable.
Copy of any details of the product or packaging that can be validated visually.

SGS WILL SUPPLY
A qualified inspector in the country of origin that will validate your shipment.
A quick response discrepancy report for cargo that does not measure up to your expectations.
An inspection summary outlining our findings.
Photos of your goods before loading.
Photos after loading and container sealing upon request.
