Company Profile


Yardstick is a well-regarded, fast-growing Canadian company founded in Edmonton, Alberta in 2005. Yardstick provides full-solution examination and eLearning solutions, psychometric consulting services and instructional design to certification/licensure, government, corporate and academic clients. Yardstick has developed from a small practice exam company into an award-winning powerhouse that has been featured on Alberta Venture Magazine’s “Fast Growth 50 list” seven times and been listed as one of PROFIT Magazine’s “Canada’s Fastest-Growing Companies” for four consecutive years and was recognized in 2014 as one of Canada’s 10 Most Admired Corporate Cultures by Waterstone Human Capital.

On February 14th, 2014, Yardstick acquired Christie Communications, an award winning, eLearning and testing business. In 2013, Christie Communications was the first company to win three prestigious Canadian Awards for Training Excellence by the Canadian Society for Training and Development (CSTD). With over thirty years of experience in design, development and production, Christie Communications is known for delivering high-end and imaginative learning
products.

Through dedication to excellence in service and technology, Yardstick has successfully secured numerous clients in the regulatory/association industry, such as Alberta College of Pharmacists, the BC College of Pharmacists, College of Licensed Practical Nurses of Alberta and the National Dental Hygiene Certification Board. As well, Yardstick’s platform and service model has also been embraced in the corporate testing industry. Notable Yardstick clients include Microsoft, Enterprise Rent a Car, NEC, Moen, and Science Diet Pet Food.

All of our clients will tell you that Yardstick takes pride in offering a world-class, robust, user-friendly item bank and exam software solution. But, more importantly, they will tell you that we also take pride in the talented individuals that make up the Yardstick team. Our impressive attention to detail and our never-ending dedication to providing the best customer service in the world have offered us the opportunity to achieve success at an award-winning rate.

Product Name:
COMPUTER SOFTWARE AND SERVICES - Constructive Marking Technology (Yardstick RedPen)
RedPen helps you mark constructed response questions, making it easier and cost effective. RedPen helps you mark everything from complex case scenarios to short stems with essay responses.

Integrated with Measure, Subject Matter Experts create the questions and store them in the secure item bank. These questions can then be built into exams and administered to candidates.

RedPen’s user-friendly interface allows administrators to set up marking teams, workflows, and timelines. The robust permission system allows each marker to only see the questions he/she has is permitted to grade. All candidate-identifying information is hidden to ensure ‘blind’ grading and is based solely on the scoring rubric criteria.

RedPen Features

Highlights for Markers
• Easy access from any web browser allowing for de-centralized remote marking of constructed responses
• A simple and intuitive marking interface that guides Markers through constructed response answers provided by candidates
• Progress reporting so Markers can see the progress they’ve made on their current marking assignments
• Ability to write notes within the interface which can be viewed by Marker Team Leads
• Highly flexible scoring system that supports dichotomous and/or polytomous marking scales

Highlights for Team Leads
• Easily able to resolve marker conflicts (aka arbitrations or third reads)
• Items in consensus are greyed out (do not require attention)
• Items in conflict are highlighted red (to be resolved by the arbitrator)

Product Name:
COMPUTER SOFTWARE AND SERVICES - Item Generation Software (Yardstick Itematic)
Yardstick Itematic is a revolutionary way to build multiple questions at once without having to painstakingly write out each question individually. Based on the principles of automated item generation (AIG), Yardstick Itematic is a user-friendly web-based tool where your authors can create hundreds of questions at a time, instead of just one.

Yardstick Itematic works by segmenting a question into multiple components based on its grammatical structure.

Using a model defined by the user, a list of alternative stem components (e.g., procedures, symptoms, patient characteristics, etc.) become easily interchangeable. This allows users to quickly construct unique questions and answer options in a systematic manner that is more efficient than creating questions one by one.

• Easily create question templates
• Specify sets of valid question stems
• Create comprehensive lists of question options
• View various combinations and permutations
• Denote “friend” and “enemy” options

Service Name:
CONSULTING SERVICES - Psychometrics
Psychometrics is the field of science concerned with the theory and application of designing, planning, and implementing defensible assessments.
Psychometricians generally have a graduate degree in a field like education or psychology specializing in statistical methods related to testing. Yardstick’s approach is not to shroud the complexities of this field in a veil of mystery; rather we demystify the processes involved in creating defensible assessments involving our clients every step of the way.
This collaborative approach is just one of the reasons why Yardstick is a cut above the rest when it comes to psychometric services and building defensible examination programs.

Service Name:
TESTING SERVICES - Exam Administration
Yardstick offers the most comprehensive exam delivery centres for computer-based testing in Canada with over 180 locations coast to coast. Annually Yardstick delivers tens of thousands of computer-based certification/licensure exams via our professional and secure exam delivery network.

Yardstick delivers exams ranging from 1 hour to over 6 hours in duration that can include breaks, external resources, special testing conditions, virtually any requirements that your testing program needs. As part of our service offering, Yardstick provides ongoing training to proctors and test centres in our network to ensure that the highest quality of professional proctored, testing services are provided to our clients.

Yardstick and our exam delivery partners strive to provide the highest quality test-taking experience for our client’s candidates. To this end all Yardstick exam delivery centres endeavour to meet or exceed international best practice standards, such as those outlined by the National College Testing Association (NCTA).

Using SCORM, our eLearning programs will automatically handle module prerequisites, accurate tracking of learner data, and bookmarked progress throughout a course

Gamification helps connect learning to on-the-job performance, making the experience credible and memorable.

Responsive development framework, your courses can function on tablets, phones, and desktops. We can also create downloadable versions of eLearning programs to run off-line.

3D immersive technology to run training simulations of hazardous situations, role-play human interactions, or teach the use of complex machinery in a zero risk environment.

Custom media and voiceover narration are useful for providing examples, telling stories, and connecting the training to the learner’s environment. Whether still photos, video clips, graphics, or animations – the key is to provide a visual reference for learning and to personalize programs to the your organization. We can create programs for English speakers, English as a Second Language (ESL) learners, or in other languages such as French, Spanish, or Arabic.

Yardstick Itematic is a revolutionary way to build multiple questions at once without having to painstakingly write out each question individually.
