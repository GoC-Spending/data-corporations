Company Profile


Metanoia International Sales Inc. Is a wholly Canadian owned
manufacturing company supplying engineering, new technology,
training and consulting services for all facets of the water
industry, the transportation and packaging of various food
products, the manufacture of various sizes of bottles, virtually
any size of steel building for industrial, commercial or
agricultural requirements.

We provide equipment, engineering and training, to local business
people who now have the opportunity of establishing various
profitable commercial operations to supply products to their
local area, their own country as well as providing additional
export opportunities.

Local businesses being established could include:

-a complete water bottling operation
-a water cooler assembly plant
-production of clean drinking water from rivers (muddy and murky)
and/or salt water
-a mobile and stationary water treatment and distribution system
to towns and villages
-a low cost water purification plant for towns and cities that
does not depend on the production of chlorine
-a low cost packaging plant for various detergents and germicidal
cleaners where we supply a very concentrated product for
dilution (160:1) and packaging on site
-a packaging plant for cooking oils
-a low cost manufacturing plant to produce bottles for water,
cooking oil, detergents etc.
-supply and installation of any size of steel buildings

Our unique mobile emergency water treatment and bottling system
combined with our waterbag bulk water transportation system can
provide a rapid response to deliver water in bulk in an emergency
situation, or for military use. Routine delivery of potable
water to outlaying areas (town and villages) on scheduled basis.
Tanker trucks not required. A brief product description is
noted below. New and innovative products are continually being
introduced through research and development.

Production of bottled water, juices, and water coolers is now
possible at a cost substantially lower than previous imports,
plus local labour is utilized. Production capacity can meet both
domestic requirements as well as developing export opportunities.

A substantial export or import market of various products is now
possible using the reusable waterbag shipping system. Standard
20 ft. And/or 40 ft. Shipping containers can now be used to ship
virtually any liquid or powder without any "one way" or cleaning
surcharge, or the expensive cost of shipping/returning empty
containers. The Waterbag consists of two parts; an outer heavy
duty liner that is re-usable, (life expectancy is a least 6 years
under normal use) and an inner bag that is normally discarded
after each shipment. As there is no cross contamination, fuel
oil can be shipped in one direction and a food products can be
shipped on the return trip. Of the outer shell is not re-used
for another shipment of any type of product, it can be folded
onto a skid for storage or returned at a fraction of normal
shipping costs. Two way shipping results at minimum cost without
expensive investment in takers or "fixed" volume containers.



Assembly, testing, engineering of new products, parts
manufactured to our specifications by contractors (machine shops
and stainless fabricators).


Products and systems available

The following are for drinking water:

-bottle washers
-bottle fillers
-bottle cappers
-bottle cap tighteners
-complete plant - for producing bottled water
-complete plant - for assembly of water coolers
-complete plant for producing bottles on site
-complete plant for sanitizing water distribution systems for
cities, towns and villages without producing chlorine and using
salt as the base product
-complete plant for packaging cooking oils, detergents and
germicidal cleaners
-mobile, completely self contained, water treatment / bottling
system for emergency or military applications. Optional 20,000
litre mobile water storage system (WATERBAG TRANSPORTATION
SYSTEM)
-stationary, completely self contained, water treatment plant
with optional bottling system and optional 20,000 litre water
storage system (WATERBAG SYSTEM)
-water - bottled and bulk
-re-usable and collapsible shipping and/or storage containers for
liquids/powders etc. 5300 U.S. gal
-bottles: any size, including "hard" and collapsible / foldable
bottles
-cooking oils
-concentrated detergents and germicidal cleaners
-steel buildings virtually any size for industrial, commercial
and agricultural applications.

We have now engineered buildings for high occupancy, residential
housing that are quickly erected, and low cost.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Bottle Washing and Filling Equipment
BOTTLE WASHING AND FILLING EQUIPMENT
A complete and diversified product line of bottle washers,
fillers, and cappers to meet any requirement from small start up
to high volume output suitable for all 3, 5 and 6 U.S. gal
bottles. Semi automatic and completely automatic systems are
available each offering a choice of high to minimum labour
requirements. Collapsible and "hard" bottles are available, as
well as all operating supplies. Filling bottles from 350 ml to
22 litre and 1 gal bottles are also available.


Product Name:
Complete Bottling Plant
A COMPLETE BOTTLING PLANT
An operational plant is available. We can provide all equipment
and material, and use local labour plus our installation
supervisor, and tradesmen, or equipment only is available, for
installation by others. By providing equipment and training, at
an economical and reasonable cost, the needs of the small bottler
as well as the larger established growing bottlers, can be met.


Product Name:
Waterbag
WATERBAG - SHIPPING & STORAGE CONCEPT
A unique, low cost, flexible and versatile solution to shipping &
storage of virtually any liquid or powder product. Shipment of
water, cooking oils, concentrated detergents, juices, flavours,
paint, oil, powders, etc. is now possible in the same container
system, simply by replacing (or re-using if applicable), the
inner bag. There is no product cross contamination, which allows
the WATERBAG to be used as a "two way" shipping container
regardless of the type of products being shipped. The outer
heavy duty liner has a life expectancy of 6 years under normal
use. The MODEL 20K (20,000 litres) folds onto a standard skid
should it be necessary to be returned empty. Note that other
types of returnable containers keep their original shape and
volume resulting in substantial additional costly shipping
charges when returned empty. Stainless steel tankers are very
expensive, not normally available, and must be dedicated for
drinking water. Transporting any other liquid is not normally
possible. As the tanker must return empty, operating costs
become very high. Model 20K is placed into any standard 20 ft.
Container, or 2- in a 40 ft. Container depending on road
restrictions.
By adding a water treatment system to the WATERBAG a permanent

bulk water storage system can also be established at minimum
cost.


Product Name:
Water Treatment Plant
WATER TREATMENT PLANT
Using low cost salt where available to produce a sanitizing
product that is 50 - 100 times more effective than chlorine to
treat a water distribution systems for cities, towns and villages
or permanent water storage tanks.

To fill bottles and pails with germicidal and disinfectant

Product Name:
Water Cooler Assembly Plant
WATER COOLER ASSEMBLY PLANT

A complete "turn key" operational assembly line that will supply
water coolers for "own use", as well as sales in the local area;
the country; and for export to other countries. The system is
available where applicable, in completely assembled modules that
interconnect on site that become a completely enclosed "building"
within the main plant. The result is a completely operational
assembly plant including air conditioning, lighting, all
utilities, (electrical, plumbing, air) conveyors, air compressor,
tables, PLUS all assembly, testing and installation equipment and
tools. The evacuation, (vacuum), refrigerant charging, leak
detection and operational testing of coolers is carried out using
sensitive monitoring equipment. Local labour only is required.
Base system is rated at 200 coolers per shift, (daily output may
vary depending on market requirement and the number of
employees). Output production can be increased in future by
adding modules into the system. Resulting cooler cost is usually
less than ½ of the normal landed price. All tooling can also be
provided whenever local metal fabrication is economically viable
in that particular country.

As an alternative to the above high production operation, we also
provide a low cost assembly system for small production outputs.
All tools, equipment and training would be supplied according to
each situation.

All water cooler component parts are supplied in container loads.


Product Name:
Mobile - Emergency Water Treatment and Bottling System
MOBILE - EMERGENCY WATER TREATMENT AND BOTTLING SYSTEM
An innovative and unique complete self contained, mobile
emergency water treatment and bottling system, fabricated in a
45/48 ft trailer or shipping container and includes its own power
source. Each system is designed to customer's requirement and
can include a filtering system, bottle filling equipment, bottle
washer, ozonator, storage tanks, bottles, (sizes available from
350ml to 22 litres. Both the 1 litre and the 20 litre bottle is
available in both a hard and a collapsible model.). This system
is ideal for both military, and civilian applications to help
solve a water distribution crisis. Mobile unit is operational
within 10 minutes of arriving on site. Local water source (sea
water, lakes, stream, wells) can be treated and resulting potable
water can be bottled, or transferred to larger permanent storage
containers. The 20,000 litre WATERBAG transportation system
placed into a 20 ft. container can also be used to transport the
treated water. In can now be used either as a mobile storage
system accompanying the mobile bottling unit or as an "on site"
storage system. By travelling between sites (towns, villages
etc.) At pre-determined time intervals, clean drinking water is
provided to a large number of people at minimum cost.
