Company Profile


With roots that date back more than 100 years and world-wide pool of nearly 10,000 employees to draw upon, Hatch Mott MacDonald is a North American company which has earned a reputation for technical excellence, innovation and client responsiveness on some of the most prominent and challenging projects. We have an extensive record of major accomplishments in the fields of transportation, tunnels, water conveyance, wastewater/CSO, environmental, gas pipelines, buildings and utilities. Our success is the product of any outstanding group of people dedicated to the goal of providing the absolute best and highest engineering technical service.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Consulting Engineers - Transportation
Hatch Mott MacDonald (HMM) is a leading consulting engineering
firm with a century of world-wide experience. With more than 30
offices in North America, and staff resources exceeding 10,000
worldwide, HMM, including Hatch and Mott MacDonald, can respond
quickly and cost-effectively to any project demand. HMM's focus
on innovation and corporate dedication to quality has been
recognized with numerous industry awards and recognition from
clients. FIELDS OF SPECIALIZATION - HMM has designed and
managed some of the most prominent transit projects in North
America and around the world. The company's history extends
from the beginning of the London Underground over a century ago
to its present day involvement in systems spanning North America.
HMM specializes in the following areas: Railways & Transit,
Airports & Aviation, Highways, Bridges, Tunnels & Underground
Structures, Systems.

Service Name:
Consulting Engineers - Railway & Transit
HMM provides a full range of engineering services for all types
of rail projects such as light rail transit, heavy rail and
subways, freight rail and high speed rail. Multi-discipline
expertise allows one-stop capability encompassing every facet of
railway. HMM capabilities include feasibility studies, route
planning/alignment, condition inspections, design, construction
and program management; design/build, station design, electrical
and mechanical elements and signaling and communications.

Service Name:
Consulting Engineers - Airports & Aviation
Providing a full range of planning, design, project and
construction management services for passenger, FBO and business
jet terminals, airside and landside infrastructure/airfield
lighting as well as aircraft maintenance facilities. HMM also
provides technical expertise for freight and cargo terminal,
integrated airport systems and technologies, intermodal
transportation, facilities management and airport securities.

Service Name:
Consulting Engineers - Highways
Using comprehensive skills in civil, structural and
transportation engineering, HMM highway portfolio includes the
planning, design and construction engineering for major
highways; roadways, rahabilitation/reconstruction widening and
safety improvements; and roadway infrastructure including bridge
and tunnels. HMM undertakes all stages of new and existing
highway projects for major routes using extensive knowledge of
design standards, methodologies, project financing and
construction methods. HMM expertise also covers maintenance,
rehabilitation and improvement. The range of services includes
planning and feasibility studies; specialist consulting; design
and design management; and project and construction management.

Service Name:
Consulting Engineers - Bridges
HMM provides extensive capabilities in both the rehabilitation
of existing structures and in the design and construction of new
structures. HMM has expertise in planning, design, inspection
and supervision of construction for all types of structures for
highway, rail and water crossing.
