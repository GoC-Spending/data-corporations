Company Profile


The Consulting Practice is based in the Greater Toronto Area, and serves clients throughout North America and Internationally with their Software and Technology needs. We also develop software and create targeted products for our clientele.

We provide Subject Matter Expertise with specific areas of technology. We operate as a team in providing services, and our organization is comprised of individuals who have the appropriate education, certification, and experience. We provide our clients with Project Based, Fixed Price, or Per Diem consulting engagements.
