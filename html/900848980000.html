Company Profile


BIOREX: TOP ENVIRONMENTAL EXPERTISE

The first consulting firm in Quebec to specialize in marine
biology, sea fisheries and the ocean environment, BIOREX is now
an acknowledged leader in these fields.

BIOREX has a solid footing in eastern Canada, particularly on
the
Atlantic coast, with a head office in Quebec City and operations
in Gaspé, Quebec, Caraquet, New Brunswick, and Dartmouth, Nova
Scotia.

The dynamics between aquatic organisms and their environment,
the
impact of human activities on aquatic, ocean and coastal
ecosystems and resources, site conservation and restoration,
responsible resource development, and aquaculture, are all among
the challenges that BIOREX has faced daily since its foundation
in 1978.

BIOREX provides services that require up-to-the-minute
information, mainly in the form of laboratory or field
assessments, consulting and expert advice.

Proven Proficiency
BIOREX's record is an eloquent proof of our competence and
credibility. Our teams have gained a reputation for high-
quality
work in hundreds of projects. Our many years in business bear
witness to our proficiency and unfailing reliability.

Our Team Is Our Strength
BIOREX's true strength lies in its team of highly qualified
fisheries, aquatics, oceanography and environmental specialists.
Our team's stability, flexibility and versatility ensure its
success in meeting new challenges.

Efficient Organization
BIOREX aims for high performance, reliability and quality in
every project it undertakes. Our structure and mode of operation
give us a broad view of projects, from start to finish.

Furthermore, BIOREX is proud to be one of the only firms in its
field to enjoy total professional independence. Autonomy
fosters
healthy objectivity, quick decision making and a minimum of
hierarchy, which has a positive impact on the bottom line.

A Progress-Oriented Approach
Our innovative approach is based on the use of state-of-the-art
technology. Project management, digital cartography,
mathematical modeling and statistical analysis tools enhance our
information processing.

Looking Ahead... Globally
Due to its acknowledged competence and a well-established
reputation, BIOREX has penetrated international fishing and
aquaculture. In the coming years, we plan to intensify our
activities abroad in fisheries resources management and marine
environment conservation. BIOREX welcomes the challenges of the
future.
Fishery Protection
Essential for Sustainable Resource Development

In Canada, the government and the fishing industry have
introduced fisheries observer programs to monitor ocean fishing.

BIOREX has been involved in the management of Canadian fisheries
observer programs since 1981, and today it has outstanding
expertise and irrefutable credibility in this area.

Well-Orchestrated Management
In order to manage the fisheries observer programs properly,
BIOREX has created a solid organization on the eastern coast.
The company has regional coordination offices and satellite
bases
of operations, standardized recruiting, training and supervision
procedures for technicians, and proven quality-control,
data-processing and data-transmission systems.

Stringent Quality Criteria
BIOREX has powerful management tools that enable it to provide
fishery surveillance services that have all the essential
features of such programs:
independence from the observed parties;
enhanced operating efficiency;
a very high degree of integrity;
highly-skilled teams of observers;
flexibility to adapt to the changing needs of government and
industry.

Exportable Know-How
Canada's fisheries observer programs are recognized world-wide
for their outstanding quality. A number of countries have based
similar programs on ours. BIOREX has already helped to
implement
such programs in Estonia, Latvia and Lithuania, and has helped
to
train observers from several countries in Europe, Africa and
Asia.

BIOREX's contribution to the development and management of the
Canadian programs, combined with its international experience,
make it a first-rate partner for introducing observer programs
elsewhere in the world.

Overline 1
Who are Fisheries Observers?
Fisheries observers are independent technicians who monitor and
compile detailed data on fishing activities for reports to
governments and industry. These observers work on board
national
and foreign vessels fishing in national or international waters.
The data collected are essential for fishery protection and for
evaluation and management of several major national and
international resources.

Overline 2
Canadian Fisheries Observer Program
In Canada, the fisheries observer program is a large-scale
operation with four regional coordination offices, a dozen
observer bases and over 250 permanent employees.

INTEGRATED MANAGEMENT OF WATERSHEDS AND COASTAL ZONES

An All-Encompassing, Unified Approach
BIOREX's activities in aquatic and marine environments are based
on integrated management of watersheds and of coastal zones.
These new ecosystem-based approaches require the agreement of
local and regional authorities. BIOREX has made a substantial
contribution to the introduction of this type of management in
Quebec.

A True Picture of the Environment
BIOREX's ability to compile a true picture of the status of
freshwater, estuary and ocean ecosystems is, and has always
been,
one of its areas of excellence. The BIOREX team looks at the
biophysical and socioeconomic aspects of the environment with
site inventories, investigations and consultation of all kinds
of
databases.

Once the information has been collected and processed, BIOREX
can
produce an environmental status report, a geographical
information system database or an ecological zoning report. It
can also identify the potentials and constraints of the area
studied. Analysis and integration of data make for fair,
enlightened decision making and pave the way for coordinated
conservation, restoration and development.

Preserving Our Heritage
The first aim of integrated management is to conserve resources
and their supporting environments. BIOREX has developed an
original approach to help local, regional and national groups
recognize conservation needs and develop management plans for
protected areas.

Ecosystem Restoration
The experts at BIOREX can also play a key role in restoring
aquatic and marine environments that have been disturbed by
pollution or physical changes. Feasibility studies, action
plans, and follow-up are some of the methods used.

With its extensive experience, the BIOREX team can quickly
identify problems and find the most effective and profitable
environmental solutions.

Environmentally Responsible Development
Specialized in the design of development plans for aquatic,
coastal and ocean environments, BIOREX contributes to economic
diversification in coastal regions in a context of sustainable
development.

ENVIRONMENTAL ASSESSMENT

To Achieve and Maintain a Healthy Environment
BIOREX has developed outstanding expertise in doing
environmental
impact studies for projects or operations in aquatic and ocean
environments. Its studies provide developers and regulatory
authorities with all the information they need to make wise
decisions.

Preliminary Studies
With the experience acquired since its foundation in 1978,
BIOREX
can handle all facets of environmental assessment and impact
studies for projects in water (lakes, waterways, oceans) and
coastal environments. Studies range from the description of the
project and components of the natural and human environments to
the development of measures to prevent, reduce or eliminate the
probable repercussions of the project.

This expertise can also be used in impact studies of industrial,
mining, hydroelectric and other projects.

Compliance Audits
To assess the compliance of existing facilities, BIOREX uses
various procedures, including environmental site assessment,
compliance audits and environmental monitoring.

These operations are carried out by professionals who are
recognized for their in-depth knowledge of legislation and
environmental issues. In doing this work, BIOREX adheres to the
standards and guidelines developed by regulatory bodies.

Risk Analysis
A field that has made great strides in the past few years, risk
analysis assesses the probability that an accident may occur
that
could have harmful effects on the physical, biological and
socioeconomic components of the environment. Such methods also
enable a more precise appraisal of the scope of the impact.
BIOREX uses several kinds of risk analysis in aquatic and ocean
environments and can tailor its methodology to its customers'
requirements.

Environmental Contingency Plans
Contingency planning for natural catastrophes or accidents helps
to minimize the harm done to natural and human environments.
BIOREX has the essential skills and tools for developing and
implementing contingency plans, including geographic information
system databases on sensitive habitats, natural resources and
usage of aquatic and ocean environments. BIOREX also does site
sampling, which is useful in determining the scope of the damage
caused by such accidents.

SEA FISHERIES AND AQUACULTURE

Unparalleled Expertise in a Rapidly Changing Sector
Over the years, BIOREX has become a top-notch consultant with
regard to sea fisheries and aquaculture. It has played an
important role in helping the fishing industry deal with the
major challenges it is currently facing.

Sustainable Development Requires Better Understanding of
Resources
For twenty years, BIOREX has studied the abundance, distribution
and biology of underdeveloped fisheries resources and their
potential for national and international harvesting, processing
and marketing. This work has contributed to the development of
new fisheries in the Gulf of St. Lawrence and its estuary.

Responsible Fishing
BIOREX has acquired unique research and development skills for
the implementation of responsible fishing. It has tested and
helped to introduce new technologies that can improve the
selectivity of fishing gear, reduce catches of small fish and
accidental fishing, and prevent ghost fishing attributable to
fishing gear lost at sea.

Better Control of Fishing
Since 1981, BIOREX has been heavily involved in monitoring and
follow-up of fishing in eastern Canada. BIOREX has participated
in fisheries observer programs aboard national and foreign
vessels, and has collected and processed statistical data on
catches, ocean dumping, landing and in-plant processing.

Aquaculture: A Viable Alternative
Aquaculture is increasingly recognized as an efficient way to
manage resources and an alternative source of sea products.
BIOREX's expertise in this area lies in mollusk, crustacean and
salmon farming, site identification and assessment, testing of
new technology, and feasibility studies in Canada and abroad.

ENVIRONMENTAL ASSESSMENTS

Ensuring and maintaining a quality environment

BIOREX has developed a remarkable level of competence in
environmental impact assessments pertaining to projects or
activities in aquatic and ocean environments. More specifically,
BIOREX has the expertise to carry out studies and provide
developers and regulatory bodies with all the necessary
information to make a judicious decision.

Final Design

On the strength of its know-how acquired since its creation in
1978, BIOREX can assume responsibility for all aspects of the
assessment and examination procedures of the environmental impact
of projects in aquatic environments (lakes, waterways, offshore
areas) and coastal regions. This responsibility begins with the
description of both the project and the components of the natural
and human environments, and continues up to the formulation of
measures aimed at preventing, reducing or eliminating the
probable repercussions of the project.

BIOREX can also profitably employ its expertise for impact
studies on industrial, mining, hydroelectric and other projects.

Conformability Studies

To assess the conformity of infrastructures in place, BIOREX
proposes various procedures such as an environmental assessment
of the sites, a verification of conformity and environmental
monitoring.

Carried out according to the standards and guidelines enacted by
the regulatory bodies, these activities are undertaken by
professionals who are renowned for their comprehensive knowledge
of the laws and regulations as well as environmental issues.

Risk Analysis

A growth area in recent years, risk analysis takes into account
the probability that an accidental event will take place which
could incur harmful effects on the physical, biological and
socio-economic components of the environment. This method allows
for, among other things, a more precise assessment of the scope
of the impact. BIOREX offers its clients several methods of risk
analysis specifically designed for aquatic and ocean
environments.

Environmental Emergency Plan

Planning for emergency measures in case of natural disasters or
accidents contributes to minimize the harmful repercussions of
such events on natural and human environments. BIOREX has the
necessary knowledge and tools for the formulation and
implementation of emergency plans, namely spatial reference data
bases on sensitive habitats, natural resources and uses in
aquatic and ocean environments. BIOREX also participates in the
formulation and carrying out of field work which helps determine
the scope of damages resulting from these accidents.
