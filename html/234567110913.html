Company Profile


W. B. Robertson Inc. (WBRInc) provides strategic communications expertise through design, branding, image building and the development and implementation of marketing plans. Services also include conceptualization and production of media content for industry. Genres of production include: documentaries, educational / training videos, corporate video magazines, and various types of promotional shorts including music-style videos. Music videos produced for both the Canadian and UK defence industry have won international awards for excellence.

The owner (Bill Robertson) brings over 30 years of experience in the film and video industry as an independent and corporate video producer and, more recently, service branding for small business. Governments and companies engaged in television media, natural resources, structural engineering, precast construction, and refinery infrastructure building and oil refining have repeatedly utilized his services. His expertise includes image building & corporate branding, proposal writing, marketing, video production, creative conceptualization, storyboard and scriptwriting, directing, off-line editing, and directing digital creation and online colorization & master edit finishing sessions. Bill has dual citizenship (UK & Canada) and has extensive foreign location experience that that includes the U.S., U.K., Middle East and Caribbean. Video programs have been produced English, Arabic and French.

Prior to establishing W. B. Robertson Inc. Bill was the president of TMT Productions Ltd., an award-winning production company he had founded and operated for a dozen years in Atlantic Canada. Primary clients included countrys largest oil refining entity and Canada National Defence.

Bill Robertson is a former member of the Academy of Canadian Cinema and Television.
