Company Profile


T.Litzen Sports Ltd., is the leading supplier of team, league and school sporting goods products in Canada. Our 60,000 sq. ft., head office includes a 6,000 sq. ft., school and team showroom with offices and a 50,000 sq. ft., warehouse in Dundas, Ontario, as well as a branch showroom in Mississauga, Ontario.

The past 15 years have seen T.Litzen Sports grow nationally and internationally, with sales representatives and locations across Canada, the United States and abroad. We have over 60 full time employees with offices in Ontario, Quebec, PEI, Nova Scotia and New Brunswick. T.Litzen Sports also has a dealer network  the GTA - set up in Western Canada (BC, Alta, Sask, Manitoba) and Atlantic Canada (Nfld). We are the only National supplier with physical representation in every province.

T.Litzen Sports prides itself in its unique working relationships with all the major name brands in the athletic industry and has an extremely close working relationship with sporting goods manufacturers around the world, thus making us the largest team dealer in Canada.

We are Canadas largest Team Dealer for many major brand names  Tachikara, Wilson, Spalding, Nike Team, Reebok Team, McDavid, Cramer, and Core Tection just to name a few. T.Litzen Sports is also Canadas largest sporting goods importer, bringing in over 30 containers a year from 9 different countries. T.Litzen Sports offers the most comprehensive selection of sporting goods and products IN STOCK in the industry.

To support our client base we supply annual sports specific catalogues in Football, Basketball, Soccer, Baseball, Track & Field, Volleyball, Sports Medicine and most recently a Nike TEAM catalogue. We also distribute a bi-annual institutional catalogue nationally that consists of 200 pages and over 10,000 products to meet all institutional needs.

T.Litzen Sports controls the distribution in Canada of many product lines for School/Team needs including but not limited to;
 Sport Specialty Coatings (Line Marking Paint for Fields)
 Accusplit Stop Watches
 Score Soccer
 Elephant Skin ball products (Spumax, Germany)


Nike: T.Litzen Sports is the exclusive distributor of Nike TEAM in Canada. With exclusive fundamental and team product we are able to supply schools and teams with Nike products that are worn by the best athletes in the world.

Due to our unique relationship with Nike we are able to support the superior demand and quality that is required by University teams and we have a fully comprehensive CIS program.

Schutt: T.Litzen Sports is the Exclusive National Distributor for Schutt Sports in Canada. Schutt is the worlds largest manufacturer of football helmets, and have helped T.Litzen Sports become the largest independent football distributor in Canada. T.Litzen Sports supplies all levels of football from grass roots all the way to CIS and CFL. We also are the official supplier of the Ontario Varsity Football League (OVFL) the largest football league in the world.

Canadian Heart and Stroke Foundation: T.Litzen Sports has been the exclusive supplier for skipping ropes for the Heart and Stroke foundation across Canada for the last 6 years. We also supply and support many of their other initiatives and programs with our products.


Canada Basketball: T Litzen Sports is a corporate partner of Canada Basketball. We are the exclusive supplier of the Steve Nash Youth Basketball Program and we are the exclusive supplier of the Fiba Shot Clock in Canada.
