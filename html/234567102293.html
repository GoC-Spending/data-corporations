Service Name:
1. Managed IT Services
By providing 24x7 Network Monitoring, a fully staffed help desk, and unlimited onsite technical support MBC's Managed IT Service Solution provides all of the benefits of an internal IT department for a fraction of the price of staffing one yourself. The Core Components that comprise our Managed Services package include:

o Unlimited Remote and Onsite Incident Support
o 24x7x365 Network and Critical Device Monitoring
o Dedicated Account Management and Virtual CIO Services
o IT Vendor Management and Procurement

Benefits you will receive as a result of our Proactive Managed Services package include:

o Much Faster Response Time To Trouble Tickets
o Long term strategic IT planning
o Predictive, Proactive IT Support
o Increased Operational Efficiency
o Reduce and Control your Operating Costs
o Cost-Effectively Gain Access to Enterprise-Level Support Staff
o Experience Minimized Downtime
o Regain the Ability to Focus on running your Business, not your Vendors
o Receive Peace of Mind Knowing that your Network is being Monitored 24 Hours a Day, Seven Days a Week

Service Name:
5. Dedicated Server Hosting
Comprehensive solutions equals uptime. Our Dedicated Server Hosting includes all the technology and service that you need to succeed online. Host an array of dynamic and database-driven web-sites such as e-stores, internal applications & customer portals. When downtime equals lost revenue, MBC is the right choice for you.

Service Name:
6. Cloud Hosting
Managed virtual servers often make a wonderful "stepping stone" for those growing Toronto organizations that want to put the focus back on their goals, rather than the constant process of updating, replacing, and maintaining servers.

And with our dedicated managed servers packages, clients who utilize our virtual servers get:

Automatic software and hardware updates performed by our own server technicians
Daily backups and security checks to ensure that your most valuable data isn't at risk
The flexibility to grow your company's technology, or change your server configurations, quickly and easily
Toronto's best service and support team from a fully trained staff

Managed virtual servers offer a unique mix of versatility and pricing, all backed by MBC's service and support  which is focused on building high-trust, long-term relationships with our clients. Don't you want your hardware and communication in the right hands?

Service Name:
2. Managed Backup and Disaster Recovery
MBC's Managed Backup Services protects your data by ensuring secure copies are taken at regular intervals to onsite and office storage systems. We protect all types of data storage systems such as desktops, laptops, servers, blackberry, iPhone, and remote workers. With Managed Backup Services, your data is backed up according to a custom policy that we define together. The MBC Expert Service desk receives daily reports of backup success or failure alerts, allowing them to act immediately on any backup problems. In addition, our Clients can easily request a restore of their data and MBC will quickly recover the last backup on their behalf.

Service Name:
4. Cisco VoIP Implementation & Support
MBC is a Cisco Certifeied Select Partner and Cisco Small Business Specialist. We have succesfully implemented and currently maintain dozens of VoIP phone systems in the Greater Toronto Area. The MBC Sales and Solutions Engineering team works with our Clients to plan, architect, and cost your unique Deployment. MBC Deployments are scheduled to minimize all possible impact to your business and provides onsite personel immediately afterwards to ensure a seamless transition.

Service Name:
3. On Demand IT Projects
The MBC Consulting team is available to help you perform initial planning and discuss business and technology strategies prior to kicking off the project. Once approved, the MBC Sales and Solutions Engineering team works to plan, architect, and cost your specific project needs. If you already have an IT Team and just need additional help, MBC is the perfect fit.

The Project Team provides procurement, planning, scheduling, and implementation services for your IT project. Our Clients are kept up-to-date on the progress of their project via ongoing status updates, meetings, and reporting.
Project Services:

Office Moves
Datacenter Hosting or Migration
Applications selection
Hardware selection
Phone Systems, VOIP, and Access
Virtualization of hardware
Storage Area Networks (SAN)
Cloud Computing (Private, Shared, SaaS)
Network Upgrades and Security Implementations
Backup and Disaster Recovery Systems
High Availability Infrastructures
