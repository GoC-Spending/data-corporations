Company Profile


LNS is an IT Professional Services company providing consulting resources and proven expertise in architecting, modelling, simulating, designing, implementing and managing large-scale, secure, multi-vendor, multi-protocol, complex distributed network infrastructures and next generation networks.

In addition to industry standard IT professional services, LNS brings the following experience and capabilities to your organization :

1. Discrete modeling and simulation of network, systems and applications: using state of the art analysis and optimization tools, we provide our clients with accurate identification of performance and bottleneck issues, including network throughput utilization, server optimization and application deployment issues.

2. Virtualization Initiatives for Servers, Applications and Desktops (VMWare, Citrix and Microsoft)

3. Technology Architecture of Next Generation Networks (such as 1/10Gbps Resilient Core, VoIP, IPv6, Secure/ 802.11n Wireless, MPLS)

4. Enterprise and SMB Storage Area Network (SAN) Architecture design (Fibre Channel SANs, FCIP, FCoE, iSCSI, NAS, etc)

5. Blade Server and Server Cluster Archticture and Implementation (HP c-Class/p-Class, SAN connectivity)

6. Network Architecture design and implementation of complex, multi-vendor, multi-protocol, multi-jurisdictional networks; (Cisco, Aruba, Allied Telesyn, Juniper, IPv4-v6, MPLS, VLAN, OSPF, EIGRP, IEEE standards, multi-caveat networks, firewalls, intrusion detection, IPS)

7. Security Architecture: Design, integration and support of Industry leading Security Systems, including Virtual Private Networks (VPN), Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS), Public Key Infrastructure (PKI), Strong I&A, Firewalls, Authenticated Remote Login, Single Sign-on, and various Encryption solutions

8. Infrastructure and Systems Performance Forecasting: Using our unique approach, we are able to assist our clients in identifying immediate changes to systems and services required to meet service level agreements and future IT objectives.

LNS is an IT Professional Services company providing consulting resources and proven expertise in architecting, modelling, simulating, designing, implementing and managing large-scale, secure, multi-vendor, multi-protocol, complex distributed network infrastructures and next generation networks.

LNS is an approved provider of OPNET Modelling & Simulation Consulting services, and has expertise in using OPNET's core suite of product tools, including ITGuru, SPGuru, ACE and VNE Server.

Using advanced modelling & simulation techniques, LNS is able to provide its clients with advanced forecasting and troubleshooting capabilities in the areas of next generation network designs (such as VOIP, IPv6 migrations, SAN Architectures, 1/10 Gbps LANs, Layer 3 Switching, VPNs, VLANs, IDS), complex multi-protocol network designs (IPv4, IPv6, IPX/SPX, BGP, OSPF, EIGRP, ATM, FR, ADSL, etc), and application requirements forecasting, whiteboarding and troubleshooting services.
