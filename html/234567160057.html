Company Profile


PG PAINT & DESIGN - Ottawa House painters provides Interior & Exterior Painting with 30 years experience, in custom Residential Painting in both Interior and Exterior, where the owner is always on the job site.
We specialize as your personal and personable paint contractor in the Ottawa and surrounding area. Independently (privately) Owned & Operated, Home Painting Company. Our house painting services also include drywall repairs, stipple ceiling removal, small general repairs while on the job site. Learn more about our full Residential House Painting Services in the Ottawa areas by visiting our website www.pgpaintanddesign.com where you can book your next house paint project with us. You'll also find our social listings to join us at such as https://www.facebook.com/PG.PAINT.DESIGN/, https://www.pinterest.com/PGPAINTDESIGN, https://plus.google.com/+PGPAINTDESIGNOttawa, https://twitter.com/PGPAINTDESIGN and http://www.houzz.com/projects/users/pgpaintanddesign_ottawa_painters

My wife and I were introduced to Pino Germano many years ago by a neighbour in the renovation business. We invited him to do some repair and painting in our house, and have never looked back, i.e., have used Mr. Germano for all our paint and related design and repair work since. This includes our home, inside and out, and our cottage. Our projects have varied from minor (e.g., deck re-staining, under $500) to major (e.g., whole home exterior, or main floor interior, involving several thousand dollars). Over more than 15 years we have relied on him to:

- fit us into his schedule with considerable flexibility;
- always work cheerfully, and incredibly efficiently and quickly... we're always surprised how much he accomplishes in a short time frame;
- in spite of the speed, produce impeccable results ... he won't take shortcuts that might compromise quality. Work is always "prepped" with needed repairs, so that the results are first class down to every detail. (We can think of occasions then we thought the work was fine, but he decided it needed redoing/improving!).

Depending on the size and complexity of the job, Mr. Germano brings with him one or two mature, experienced colleagues, who are equally friendly and obviously love their work (and working together).

We've recommended PG Paint & Design to many friends and neighbours over the years, who have thanked us for the excellent and satisfying suggestion. We'll continue to do this. from our Rockcliffe and Rockcliffe Park area loyal clients.
