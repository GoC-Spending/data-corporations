Service Name:
FREIGHT FORWARDER - DUTY DRAWBACK
Of the estimated three billion dollars a year available through
import duty rebates, known as duty drawback refunds, less than
one-third is actually claimed by U.S. exporters. UPS Supply Chain Solutions retrieves tens of millions of dollars annually in import duties on behalf of U.S. companies that subsequently export merchandise originally imported.

Our dedicated drawback representatives specialize in
implementing and managing drawback programs that maximize dollar recoveries and ensure the highest degree of regulatory
compliance. Our proprietary drawback software systems automate
the entire claims filing process, which can significantly reduce
the administrative burden required to support a drawback program.

ASIST

Access to UPS Supply Chain Solutions' Information System by Telephone
(ASIST) provides clients and partners access to UPS Supply Chain Solutions network to easily obtain information about shipments cleared in Canada or at the U.S. northern border.

What is available to you through ASIST?

· List of your shipments
· List of imported goods
· List of your NAFTA certificates of origin
· Statement of account
· Shipment search
· Exchange rates
· Harmonized system
· Bulletin board
· Invoice history
· Electronic mail to the UPS Supply Chain Solutions' personnel

generating reports regarding your customs activity.

What is available to you through DATA MANAGER?

· History of all your shipment accessible all time

Service Name:
FREIGHT FORWARDER - DUTY DRAWBACK
Of the estimated three billion dollars a year available through
import duty rebates, known as duty drawback refunds, less than
one-third is actually claimed by U.S. exporters. UPS Supply Chain Solutions retrieves tens of millions of dollars annually in import duties on behalf of U.S. companies that subsequently export merchandise originally imported.

Our dedicated drawback representatives specialize in
implementing and managing drawback programs that maximize dollar recoveries and ensure the highest degree of regulatory
compliance. Our proprietary drawback software systems automate
the entire claims filing process, which can significantly reduce
the administrative burden required to support a drawback program.
