Company Profile


Pressing Media specializes in USB / BLU-RAY / CD / DVD replication, duplication, eco-friendly paper print, banners, flags, packaging, apparel, digital downloads and an array of custom merchandising for all your business needs.

We pride ourselves on our price match guarantee while maintaining our standards of quality and customer service. We will accept all competitors pricing and special discounts making the choice to work with us simple. We believe that everyone should have access to the highest quality merchandise at the most competitive prices.

It is our goal to continue to build personal relationships with our customers. To focus on green solutions, environmental impact before profits, to provide an effortless manufacturing experience and guarantee our product free of manufacturing defects. Our state of the art facilities located throughout North America give us the exclusive edge to fulfill your expectations where and when you need them.

Call today for an expert opinion regarding Your Pressing Needs

Services Replication

Customers are reporting increased interest in High Definition products and Pressing Media (one of America's first High Definition disc replicators) is now supplying both BD25 and BD50 products.Custom Shaped CD & DVD can be designed and made at Pressing Media. Expert consultation is available from recognized industry leaders. FlexDisc is only half-as-thick as a regular DVD and weighs 40% less. It is flexible and can be mailed at lower postage rates. It plays on all standard DVD players/drives and has a full 4.7GB (DVD-5) capacity.

Customized order management systems ensure that customers have complete production clarity for every project.



Duplication

Pressing Media offers a full compliment of DVD-R and CDR duplication services. Duplication is offered as an alternative for short-run rushes and quick turn needs. Pressing-Media has no minimums on the number duplicated discs you require.



Services Duplication



Duplication is the simple "burning" of your content master onto DVD-R or CDR media using duplication towers and automated duplication stations. Pressing Media offers a variety of on print options for duplicated discs:



Digital Laminate Printing: A photo quality printing and lamination process.
Direct Thermal Print: Single Color printing direct to disc.
Inkjet With Water Shield: High quality printing direct to disc.
Screen Printing: Pantone or Spot screen print
Offset Printing: CMYK direct to plate disc print



All of our duplication products can be packaged in the same packaging offered on replicated projects. With our state of the art in-house printing solutions we can bring your product to the market faster.



Product Name:
Apparel Printing

We carry American Apparel, Gildan, Fruit of the Loom, Hanes, Alstyle Organic, Hemptown, Bella, Coal Harbor, Spun Bamboo, Fairline Bamboo & many more. There are countless print styles and inks to choose from and our quality control department reviews each and every garment as it comes off the press. Custom apparel orders are our specialty.
