Company Profile


As one of the most extraordinary ensembles in the Ottawa, Ontario region, Sax Appeal is transforming the musical landscape in Canada with their distinctive sound, look and feel.

Sax Appeal is a unique professional saxophone ensemble whose specialty is to provide appropriate live background music to enhance the ambiance of sophisticated functions that require the finest of touches.

With our distinctive sound (ONLY saxophones), Sax Appeal provides a unique musical experience by playing jazz music, classical music, or anything in-between for literally any event, ranging from weddings to festivals, to cocktail receptions to Christmas festivities.

When you need a distinctive element for your next event, you need Sax Appeal!

Canada’s Premier Saxophone Ensemble
La saxophonie à son meilleur

Always the Right Feel With Sax Appeal!
Pour un événement qui a du style, donnez-lui une touche de Sax Appeal!
