Company Profile


McInnes Cooper is among the top business law firms in Canada, with more than 200 lawyers in offices throughout the Atlantic region, serving clients across North America and abroad. We are market leaders in business law, litigation, employment, tax, real estate and energy & natural resources. Clients also benefit from our membership with Lex Mundi, the worlds largest association of independent law firms.

Our clients across North America provide consistent, positive feedback on our ability to deliver quality, service, experience and results  with depth and range of legal specialties.

Our clients appreciate our commitment to providing top-notch legal service in a timely, responsive and cost-efficient manner  fully tailored to their needs and budget. By providing junior and senior-level support, along with an ideal mix of service specialties, our clients receive exactly what they need, exactly when they need it.

We take it a step further by offering several flexible pricing options, including project-based fee caps, flat or fixed fees, blended rates, volume discounts and annual rebates. Our clients have helped us create these alternatives, and our clients truly benefit.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Environmental Products and Services
McInnes Cooper provides a full range of environmental law legal
services to assist in the resolution of problems. Many problems
can be resolved through negotiation and allocation of liability
by agreement or through alternative dispute resolution.
Litigation is available as a last resort.

McInnes Cooper can also assist clients in developing
Environmental Management System which meet the test of due
diligence.

Service Name:
Legal
McInnes Cooper is Atlantic Canada's largest single law
partnership. We have offices in all four Atlantic Provinces
and have a number of multidisciplinary Practice Groups. These
teams focus on specific types of law and distinctive industry
segments. We represent major regional, national and
international corporations, yet we take as much pride in
working closely with small owner-managed businesses and
entrepreneurs. Regardless of their size or location, our
clients receive the attention, service and professionalism they
are entitled to expect from a firm that has been helping
clients for over 150 years.

Service Name:
Administrative law service
Members of McInnes Cooper's Administrative Law Group have
participated in various administrative law matters. We
represent clients in administrative proceedings before numerous
administration tribunals, including the Canada-U.S. Free Trade
Dispute Panel, the Canadian Judicial Council and provincial
judicial councils, Workers' Compensation Boards, Human Rights
Tribunals, and Labour Boards.

In addition, a significant portion of the work of our group
involves judicial review, certiorari and injunction
applications. Often these are applications for review of
decisions reached by administrative bodies.

Service Name:
Litigation/defense
Our Litigation Department provides a full range of experienced
service in connection with civil litigation and regulatory
matters. Our lawyers appear before all levels of courts,
including the appellate courts in Atlantic Canada, the Federal
Court of Canada and Supreme Court of Canada. We also represent
clients in administrative proceedings before numerous
administration tribunals, including the Canada-U.S. Free Trade
Dispute Panel, the Canadian Judicial Council and the provincial
judicial councils.

The Litigation Group has as extensive insurance defence
practice, serving insurance companies, mutual companies,
specialized carriers, marine insurers, and bonding companies.
We have acted in major cases throughout the region, involving
construction projects and important marine and environmental
cases. Each of our offices has litigation lawyers who have
considerable experience in all areas. The Litigation Group's
range of general litigation services in virtually unlimited.

The Group has several lawyers trained and experienced in all
aspects of Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR). The firm's
objective is to obtain solutions that best match our clients'
concerns and interests. The ADR process may include
negotiation, arbitration, and mediation.

Service Name:
Contract negotiation
Our Contracts Lawyers have wide experience and expertise in
the timely completion of transactions of all types and in the
preparation and negotiation of contracts and commercial
documents. They have extensive dealings with complex commercial
contracts, purchase and sale of businesses and equipment, long
term leasing of real and personal property, computer hardware
and software agreements, and financing agreements of all
kinds.
