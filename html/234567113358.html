Sector information

Unique applications:

XCZONE.TV independent film studios produce a wide-variety sports specific products for home DVD and broadcast, including: skiing, running, in-line skating, paddling, triathlon, palates, yoga, martial arts, and base-jumping. Captured in high-definition by the World leader's in Clean Oxygen Fed Sport Cinematography and multimedia.


xczone is all about promoting Clean Oxygen Fed Sports and Natural Fitness through instructional and motivational (lifestyle) DVDs, for all levels. The series integrates core fitness with the fresh air experience and a sustainable healthy lifestyle. The program offers a gentle introduction into sports like: nordic walking, core-body strength, yoga, pilates, flexibility, paddling, jogging, biking, skiing, snowshoeing, and martial arts.

For the enthusiastic skier...The Nordic Skiing Project is the most comprehensive reference ever produced for nordic skiing and engages the entire snow-sports industry It is delivered in a progressive series of DVD's for the beginner to expert; capturing the science, art-culture and history of nordic skiing. It is the essential owner's manual for every nordic skier, and an enduring product in a box; for the price of a ski lesson.

The Science of Nordic Skiing DVD series provides an in-depth examination of the sports science. It is tailored to engage the skier the more richly, with clever and concise delivery of: technique, training, equipment and advanced moves; in four volumes. These series are intended for intermediate, advanced and expert level skiers, coaches, instructors and for the certification of nordic ski professionals.

Key / Major clients:

FEATURE FILM We have completed three feature length independent films. Our latetest film, "Unlimited" exposed the subculture of outdoor adventure sports. Showing with best of the Banff Mountain Film Festival World Tour, finalist at the Montreal Adventure Film Festival and became a best seller on DVD in 2003. An action-documentary called "High Velocity," provided unsurpassed creative footage of the most popular winter sport in the World.

MUSIC VIDEO We recently teamed with Dean Batstone "Canada's Best Undiscovered Songwriter" to produce a music video of "You've Still Got It" for a movie soundtrack.

PRODUCT MARKETING We produced the Styrka Berry Drink commercial and promotional video. Our studios have provided product placement in films for a number of products including Subaru, Infinity, Fischer, Madshus, K2, Adidas, Nike etc.

ECO-TOURISM We have produced promotional DVDs clients like Madawaska Canoe Centre, Owl Rafting, Nipika Lodge, Mt. Washington, etc., to play at trade-shows, direct mail and to be used in broadcast.

TV EVENT COVERAGE Our studios produced the television coverage for the National Sprint Skiing Championships for Rogers SportsNet and prime-time National broadcast.

MULTIMEDIA DESIGN Arctic Watch, The World's Northernmost resort, turned to us to design and build a multimedia CD-ROM promotional package. The task involved substantial video editing, narrative, photos enhancement and mutimedia creative authoring.

INSTRUCTIONAL DVD-ROM Cross-country Canada, Biathlon Canada collaberated with our company to create the most comprehensive manual on the sport of Nordic Skiing ever produced. The 5 hour double DVD set the standard for instructional technologies, and was adopted by the National Coaching Certification Program.

PORTFOLIOS We produced video and CD-ROM portfolios for Olympic Medals like Beckie Scott as a marketing instrument for her to gain much needed sponsorship.

WEBSITES We have designed over a hundred web sites since 1991.

Success stories:

Fast-track your development, this season with the xczone masters nordic ski camps. They specialize in the highest quality of instruction for intermediate to elite skiers. The team has degrees in Biomechnical Engineering, Sports Physiology and education. They have competed at multiple World Cups or Olympics, and have taught hundreds of students since. They wrote the ski manual and certification for instructors and coaches.

Testimonial:

"Cross-Country Canada enthusiastically endorses XCZONE.TV as a key resource for beginning and experienced skiers or coaches looking for clear, correct and easily-understood guidance for improving their skiing technique. As the National Ski Team coaches collaborated on the technical narrative, we can confirm that the theory and practice ... are carefully aligned with current and evolving doctrine."
