Company Profile


In August 1991, when Les Textiles Patlin Inc. was founded, it
specialized exclusively in the manufacturing of bedspreads and
comforters. Since 1994, the operations have increased on a
continual basis and we acquired the manufacturing facility
of Décor Jacques ltée. This acquisition adds to our expertise
some 20 additional years of experience in the management team
alone. Since then, our manufacturing facilities have increased
significantly with several additions to the existing plant.

To reach new horizons, Les Textiles Patlin Inc. became the
leader in exclusive fabric distribution. We introduced a vast
selection of high quality decorative braids. Our collection of
fabric is remarkable and diversified. Our product lines range
from sheers to fire retardant fabric thus allowing us to
successfully satisfy our growing number of customers. Our
products can now be found in more than a 1000 stores across
Canada and sales are increasing by more than 15% a year.

The fabrication of bedspreads and quilted comforters holds no
secret to us. Today we produce a large array of accessories
answering our customers every need. Our equipment and
infrastructures allow us to offer quality products while
adhering to rigid manufacturing standards.

Continuously paying attention to novelties and progress, Les
Textiles Patlin Inc. is a must-stop. We are considered a top
of the line company within the hotel industry. Les Textiles
Patlin Inc. is very proud to have participated in various
outstanding hotel realisations. Our diversity is well
established. We are particularly pleased to mention the
production of bedspreads and drapes for LAuberge Godefroy of
Bécancour, Simdar Inn located in St. Alexis des
Monts, LAuberge et Relais Champêtre Le Baluchon /Seigneurie
Volant which currently holds international recognition and with
Sheraton, Holiday Inns, Best Westerns, Days Inns, Delta Hotels
and Quality Inns, just to name a few.

Because of our famous customer service and high quality
products, we have established an envious status among Canadian
customers. In our relentless quest of the North American market,
we continue to reach for challenging new horizons.

Since our debut, Les Textiles Patlin Inc. has shown  Spirit
of Quality . This slogan has become our motor. A dynamic family
enterprise, Les Textiles Patlin Inc. is directed by a young,
progressive and alert team whose sole purpose is to completely
satisfy its customers.

We are a member of the CGTA (Canadian Gift and Tableware
Association)
