Company Profile


Oculus is a functional fusion of advertising sales, human resources and management consulting worlds, creating customized designer solutions that are results driven. By merging business operational fundamentals with creative and strategic execution, we provide up-to-date and a modernized approach to todays business operations. Our solutions are not from a box or book. Our customized consulting experience will be based on research, facts and insights regarding the business and area. No two clients are alike and Oculus will remain focused on your business, creating a personalized and private strategy to make a business competitive.

Service Name:
Mystery Shopping
Oculus Hotel & Business Mystery Shopping Services

Mystery shopping is a perfect way to evaluate & facilitate positive changes from within your hotel or organization. Oculus is pleased to offer telephone, internet and live mystery assessment opportunities for retail, hotel or service based business or charities. Results will be presented based on a pre-set criteria and will include suggested personalized improvement tips. An Oculus Opportunity Map will guide your employees and staff through necessary steps on how to progress in their personal growth plan, ultimately encouraging revenue growth as a result of superior customer service and sales skills. Unlike our competition, our results are personalized for your team and provide individual feedback for increased return on your investment. You can choose between the Oculus IMPRESS training programs or we can design your own assessment and shopping program.

Telephone Mystery Shopping

We are pleased to offer recorded telephone mystery shopping both through traditional mystery shoppers and planned recordings from actual live incoming customers. Each result will receive a personalized opportunity map and can be customized to fit your business factors and regional exceptions. We are able to telephone mystery shop the following:

 Assistant Living & Elderly Communities
 Associations
 Call & Appointment Centers
 Car Dealerships
 Charities
 Concierge
 Front Desk
 Hotel Reservations
 Hotel Housekeeping Departments
 Information Centers and Services
 Medical Centers
 Real Estate
 Restaurants
 Retail
 Sales & Marketing Departments
 Ticket Centers

We can work within any organization to customize a mystery shopping experience.

Service Name:
Customer Service Training
Oculus Customer Service IMPRESS
Oculus Onsite Hotel Consulting & Reservations Training

How and when we provide service is a crucial element in all of our customer interactions. Both the timing and the relevance of that service will dictate how your customers perceive your business. At Oculus, we work with your entire team to ensure they have the most up-to-date and applicable service training tools to deliver and to exceed customer expectations. The IMPRESS program is designed to provide revenue generating training program ideas and to encourage employee development within your hotel or organization. We do not provide cookie-cutter programs. All of our training programs are designed for your specific hotel or organization. You place your companys signature to incorporate your unique brand and operating standards. Some of our customer service training programs include:
Customer Situation Management
Taking complaints, requests and challenges and making them revenue and loyalty opportunities. At the end of this training program your teams members will be able to:

* Effectively manage and solve incoming customer service complaints
* Provide cost effective customer centric solutions
* Create confidence and independence with operations staff
* Track and review opportunities for change management
* Ensure customer satisfaction to encourage positive reviews

Service Performance
Building service lasting memories. At the end of this training program your team members will be able to:

* Build lasting experience memories for customers
* Delivery superior customer service standards with ease
* Establish a personal connection and create the path to customer loyalty
* Increase customer service experience delivery

A Connection that is Personal
How to create loyalty through customer connection. In this training program, we will:

* Encourage customer interaction within a service setting
* Provide a customized and personalized service experience to impress customers
* Increase customer service experience delivery

Do First, Question Later
How to positively present a NO. In this training program, we will:

* Determine positive solutions to present in any challenging situation
* Take ownership of an opportunity
* Demonstrate leadership skills and an ability to serve without delay
* Showcase decision making confidence during customer service decision opportunities.
* Increase customer service experience delivery

Lets take a walk
How to move or relocate a customer. At the end of this training program your team members will be able to:

* Show empathy and care within a challenging opportunity
* Showcase a degree of resistance recovery
* Provide a satisfactory solution for incoming customers
* Establish positive follow up and guest experience

They come and they go
Understand how to deliver IMPRESSive check in and checkout guest experiences. In this training program, we will:

* Understand and meet requirements within a travel rating system
* Accurately receive all customer information and requests
* Encourage and identify revenue generating opportunities
* Identify potential service and experience challenges
* Follow up to ensure customer satisfaction
* Establish data collection process for change management
* Increase customer service experience delivery

The majority of the programs listed above would be suitable, and can be customized to fit the following industries:

- Hotels & Hospitality
- Reservations & Call-Centres
- Restaurants
- Casinos & Gambling Industries
- Charities and Associations
- Destination Training & Tourism

Sales Training
Oculus Sales and Telephone Reservations IMPRESS
Oculus Onsite Hotel Consulting & Reservations Training

Although we have established that we are a customer centric organization, we still need to make money. At Oculus, we have taken proven sales strategy, scientific studies, and customer service knowledge to create a sales training program that is focused on both the customer experience, and revenue generation. All of our sales and reservations training programs are designed with you in mind and allow for complete customization. Using the results from our ongoing mystery shopping, we can target the static and weaker areas of your sales or reservation practices. Some of the sales and reservations training experiences that we can provide are:
IMPRESS the Guest Telephone Reservations Sales
How to generate additional revenue through maximizing sales and service opportunities. In this training program, we will:

* Present hotel or product value and describe accordingly

* Encourage additional revenue generating & sales opportunities
* Encourage and establish special requirements
* Maintain appropriate levels to establish hotel ratings
* Understand the need for cross selling
* Be able to present information and details applicable to customer experience

IMPRESS the Guest Sales Management
How to generate maximum revenue by using proven scientific and research sales strategies. In this training program, we will:

* Establish rapport and a connection with every sales experience
* Encourage additional revenue generating & sales opportunities

* Establish an advancement and follow up sales procedure

* Be able to present information and details applicable to customer experience

The Walk Around

* Present hotel or product value customized to sales experience
* Set up a site experience to impress the incoming customer
* Collect appropriate company and caller data
* Measure and qualify a sales opportunity
* Establish rapport and a connection
* Establish an advancement and follow up sales procedure
* Be able to present information and details applicable to customer experience

Preparing for Take Off
Upselling to your current customers. In this training program, we will:

* Encourage selling options and packages
* Encourage creative thinking and pricing strategy
* Use qualifying techniques to establish customer needs
* Present differences between

IMPRESS for the YES
How to overcome opposition and resistance to product & pricing challenges. In this training program, we will:

* Showcase how to overcome price and product resistance through addressing customer areas of concern and subconscious thought.
* Use qualifying techniques to establish customer needs

Full and Happy Tummy, Empty Wallet
Maximizing the dining experience to increase average customer revenue. In this training program, we will:

* Present hotel or menu value and describe accordingly
* Establish personal level connection with every dining experience
* Encourage additional revenue generating & sales opportunities
* Encourage and establish special requirements
* Maintain appropriate standards levels to establish restaurant ratings
* Actual onsite practice and live demonstration
