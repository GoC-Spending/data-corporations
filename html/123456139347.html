Company Profile


Western Heritage is an environmental services firm based in Saskatoon Saskatchewan Canada. Established in 1990, Western Heritage specializes in archaeology and heritage management services, near surface geophysics including both GPR and Magnetics, geo-archaeology, community engagement and geomatics services (imagery sales and interpretation, GPS mapping). Our primary objective is to work with resource companies and other land developers in order to help them both achieve regulatory compliance and to carry out their work in a sustainable manner. Western Heritage senior management team has decades of experience in providing cost effective solutions for our customers, both within Canada and globally.

Service Name:
Archaeology, Heritage Management
Archaeological and heritage services which adhere to Canadian and international heritage regulations, including Heritage Plans and Heritage Districts, Development Overviews (HRO, Stage 1), Impact Assessments of Heritage Resources (HRIA, Stage 2 and 3), and Archaeological Mitigation studies (Stage 4).

Service Name:
Community Engagement
Western Heritage’s community engagement services align the needs and concerns of developers, communities, and governments to ensure mutually beneficial outcomes for all involved. Our approach to community engagement is based on working with communities and providing them with the knowledge to fully understand developments impacts and to use that knowledge for effective conservation and management planning. We believe in the importance of working with communities to create shared value, and we believe strongly in the importance of building sustainability into any community plans. To do this, Western Heritage uses its own experienced staff, expert associates, and community members

Service Name:
Geoarchaeology
Western Heritage is the first Canadian firm to offer specialized geoarchaeology services. These services ensure the most effective use of resources in archaeological investigations, and deliver more meaningful results than traditional archaeology. They include soil micromorphology, detailed stratagraphic mapping, and OSL profiling.

Service Name:
Geomatics and GIS Services
Western Heritage offers a full range of Geomatics and Geographic Information Systems sales and analysis services to meet any and all satellite imagery and information needs. This includes high and low resolution imaging sales and radar imaging sales. Western Heritage also specializes in developing custom models for our clients and our team of remote sensing scientists can extract information that others can't see. Western Heritage offers custom service solutions to resource development industries, the agriculture industry, and government and infrastructure planning.

Service Name:
Near Surface Geophysics Services
Western Heritage offers near surface geophysics services to compliment out archaeological services. These include non intrusive investigations such as ground penetrating radar, fluxgate gradiometer, and magnetic susceptibility service. These are ideal for investigating and documenting buried features such as cemeteries and burial mounds.

Service Name:
Environmental Footprint Monitoring
The Western Heritage Environmental Footprint Monitoring Platform provides organizations with a management tool to assist in the monitoring and reporting of environmental changes. These changes can effect both environmental health, and the integrity and performance of infrastructure and assets. The platform utilizes satellite imagery and remote sensing technology to generate valuable information about the health, growth, and productivity of vegetation in a project area, as well as other important factors such as surface ground water.
