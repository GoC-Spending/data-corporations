Company Profile


SBC Case has been building unique custom cases since 1984.
We've
encountered all kinds of case problems and solved them all. Our
broad range of capabilities has produced a one-stop case
facility
that allows us the freedom to create any size, shape, style, and
strength of case utilizing all leading materials both inside and
outside of our industry. This means we can offer you the most
innovative and ultimately the best solutions for your individual
needs. SBC Case is one of very few case facilities that offer
such a wide variety of case and container products from under a
single roof. This makes us a valuable resource to our customers
as a single source able to meet many needs.

Quality Assurance - At SBC Case we meet the requirements of ISO
9001:1994. The scope of our QA program is The Design and
Manufacture of Case and Container Products.

This ISO standard covers all aspects of our management system
from design through to production, servicing and installation.

Addition - Besides cases and containers, SBC Case engages in
custom fabrication of many different types using many different
materials as well as build to print work and contract
manufacturing. SBC Case is involved in government, military,
commercial, industrial and private work.. Having so many
capabilities under one roof enables us to come up with some very
innovative solutions to your problems.

MOLDED PLATICS
Our plastic cases offer an attractive, lightweight alternative
for applications needing more protection than a padded bag but
not a full-blown standard or metal case. We have a selection of
stock sizes and styles to choose from. As well, we produce many
component parts for other case types as well as provide a
complete, custom-molding service for our customers.
-Molded carrying cases
-Molded transit cases
-In-house mold making


Product Name:
CUSTOM SHIPPING CASES
The use of state of the art design and production equipment
allows us to provide quick turnaround on all of our case
products. Special attention to our customers needs and
requirements by our experienced design staff produce cases that
constantly meet and exceed our customer's expectations at a
competitive price.

Whether it is a simple stock design or a completely new concept,
we have the experience and the ability to satisfy your needs.
-Transit cases
-Protective carrying cases
-Heavy duty shipping cases
-ATA Cases
-Footlockers & Steamer Trunks
-Re-usable MIL-Spec. Crating
-Many styles & Configurations
-Numerous hardware configurations
-Unlimited interior options


Product Name:
METAL CASES
SBC Case metal cases are the durable answer to most shipping
needs. Our lightweight aluminum construction offers the best
strength to weight ratio going. Metal boxes can be air & water
tight if necessary offering the highest level of environmental
protection for your contents. When shipping is frequent, a high
level of protection is required, and your being charged by
weight, our light aluminum construction should be considered.

We also offer laser cutting, CNC punching and powder painted
finishes to broaden the possibilities in our metal fabrication
department.
-Rugged & lightweight equipment cases
-Transit cases
-Large shipping containers
-Electronics panels & chassis
-military equipment cases-Paramedic cases & medical chests
-Standard equipment racks
-Portable tool chests
-Powder painted finishes
-CNC punching
-Laser cutting


Product Name:
SOFT CASES
SBC Case has been sewing bags and padded cases since our early
beginnings. Our Soft Cases are custom sewn for durability,
strength and flexibility. In addition to bags and cases, our
sewing shop offers and almost unlimited spectrum of products
from pencil cases to parachuting equipment.

We are equipped with the most state-of-the-art equipment
available. Die cutting of parts coupled with computerized sewing
and cutting equipment help ensure that our products are
consistent and of the highest possible quality.
-Padded carrying cases
-Briefcases & portfolios
-Garment bags
-Tote bags
-Equipment bags
-Harness assemblies
-Cargo restraints
-Tool carriers
-Backpacks
-Holsters

DESIGN SERVICES
At SBC Case we harness the creative power of our in-house
engineers to develop creative solutions from the prototype to
final production stages. During all stages of development we
refine our products to provide the finest possible case products
for at a reasonable cost. This translates into better value and
longer, stronger case life, not to mention contents that arrive
at their destination safely, with fewer headaches and reduced
risk.

We make sure we understand your requirements completely so that
our finished product performs the way you need it to.

Our good designs aren't an accident. They are a result of a
controlled, creative process supported by years of experience.
That's part of the SBC package.

Our one-stop shop guarantees the best solutions for your case
problems.
