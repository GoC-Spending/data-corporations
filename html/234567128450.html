Company Profile


We provide a full range of advanced ceramic/polymer maintenance materials made by the Enecon Corporation for the repair of pumps, other machinery, cement structures, flooring, foundations, roofs and to resolve corrosion problems. These products are typically used industrially where other repair methods fail or fail within a short period of time. Some products are Nuclear safety certified (DBA tested to ASTM 3911-89). They are toxin free, safe and simple to use. If desired we can carry out the repairs for you or instruct your personnel in their use.

We market a specialized bio-engineered products for the removal of urea salts, fecal matter, blood and other protein stains and odor control. The non-pathogenic bacterial emulsion can also be used for the regeneration of septic systems, and waste water drains. It is excellent for removing pet stains, controlling pet and other odors

Our zebra mussels control coatings prevent the accumulation of zebra mussels without the use of toxic materials or heavy metals. Nothing is released into the environment. The mussel and other aquatic organisms simply can't adhere to the Fuji Duplex Silicon coating.

The hydrophobic character of the coating reduces viscous friction improving fluid flow. Used on ships, it reduces drag and can result in speed increases or fuel savings of approximately 10%: in power generating facilities increase in generating capacity of 2% are not uncommon: pumping efficiency typically rises 7% when used to repair these items.

Our line of steam cleaning equipment is the best on the market. Designed specifically for the Commercial, Institutional, Manufacturing and Marine industries they are extremely well made. Steam temperature dictates the steam's ability to clean and kill bacteria, fungus, viruses and other microbes. These machines generates the highest steam temperatures of anything in their class making them ideal for infection control in clinical settings. However, temperature settings are fully adjustable. The units are portable and can be used virtually anywhere.

Steam cleaning is an excellent, chemical free, solution to a bedbug (or similar pest) infestation. Since no toxic chemicals are involved, the treated area can be used almost immediately.

We provide both Wet and Dry steam cleaning units. Dry-steam units generate only high temperature "dry" steam that removes dirt, grease and kills pathogenic microbes. Wet steam combines the cleaning power of steam with the scrubbing action of high pressure, superheated water. It is fast and suited to large jobs were 1 to 2 gallons per minute of liquid water can be tolerated.

Sector information

Unique applications:

The technical superiority of the Enecon product line has consistently resulted in these products outperforming other, similar repair materials. The anti-abrasion products outlast hardface welding as well as chromium carbide wear plate. The pump repair materials are regularly used to rebuild pumps and other turbo machinery that would otherwise be scrapped. These same products are used to repair tube-sheets and water boxes on chillers and heat exchangers permanently and prevent further degradation.

Key / Major clients:

Ministry of Transportation of Ontario, University of Waterloo, The Landmark Group, Ont, Bruce Power, Trillium Heath Partners, William Day Group, Pearl Hospitality, Atlas Copco, Motion Mazda, Cambridge Memorial Hospital, Department of National Defense, Facca Construction, Landmark Cinemas

Success stories:

In 2011 the LandMark Group while renovating and converting an old grist mill into one of the most prestigious restaurants in the Kitchener/Waterloo/Cambridge region discovered the water turbines originally used to power the mill. After some investigation it was learned that the turbine was a highly acclaimed Canadian design from the mid 1860's. It had been designed and manufactured in Meaford, Ontario and won a number of awards.

The President of LandMark wanted to preserve this piece of Canadian history and asked Strategic Industrial Technology to repair/restore the turbine. The restored unit now sits in front of the Mill Restaurant in downtown Cambridge.

A multi-year test of our Duplex Silicone coating was conducted by Bruce Power over a 4 year period. In that time no zebra mussels were able to attach to the test plates.

We saved a local truck wash facility more than $500,000 when 2500 gallons of 67% nitric acid was accidentally dumped into the the underground, concrete holding tank. Faced with replacing or relining the tank the owner asked if we could rebuild the tank from the inside. Up to 3 inches of the walls and floor were rebuilt with a polymer/aggregate mix. The tank was then coated with a chemical containment coating.

A local cinema need repairs to their front entrance to address multiple safety concerns. A mix of DuraFill and mortar created an adherent cement repair compound that had the look of concrete but provided greater bonding strength than cement.

Trillium Health Partners had a problem with urea salt staining in one of the wards of one of the hospitals. Strategic Industrial Technology Inc. supplied a specialized bio-engineered bacterial emulsion that dissolved the urea salts and eliminated the the odours associated with it.
