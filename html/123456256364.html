Company Profile


S-MATRIX is a technology consulting, contracting, systems
integration, project management, research and development (R&D)
group.

Our Products and Services categories include: Instrumentation
Systems; Multi-User Computer Systems; Efficient Energy Use
Systems.

Our Operating Paradigm is general contracting applied to
technology projects.

Our Primary Disciplines are: computer science, electronics,
engineering science, industrial design, applied mathematics,
applied physics, software engineering.

Our General Areas of Systems Expertise include: software
development, virtual instrumentation, test and measurement,
business factory industrial laboratory office automation,
instrument calibration, data acquisition analysis presentation,
process monitoring management control.

Our Specific Client / Project Experience includes: aerospace,
avionics, biotechnology, computers, efficient energy use,
electronics, GPS, manufacturing, mineral exploration, photonics,
telecommunications.

Additional Areas of Systems Expertise and Client / Project
Experience include: communications, E-mail, Internet, intranets,
network design installation administration, web site design
development marketing, wireless.

Service Name:
Multi-User Computer Systems
S-MATRIX is an established computer systems integration and
support company. We develop, build and support application
specific multi-user, multi-platform, multi-location computer
systems and software for our clients. The components of a
typical client / project are:

* System requirements analysis and design
* Hardware and software selection and procurement
* Financing
* Hardware and software installation, configuration and testing
* Software engineering, customization and development
* Training including business, system and software processes and
procedures
* Ongoing system management and support
* Maintenance

Service Name:
Efficient Energy Use Systems
S-MATRIX has historically worked on applications in all sectors
of energy use - residential, commercial, industrial and
transportation. Our strategy in all cases is first to identify
and catalogue current usage. Then, using current usage as a
baseline, more efficient energy use options are proposed to our
clients who help us select those options needing further
evaluation. The feasibility studies of each selected option
include design and life cycle cost benefit analyses. The results
of the feasibility studies are the foundation for an
implementation strategy. The following is a partial list of the
types of efficient energy use engineering projects that we have
worked on:

* Computer aided design, analysis and simulation
* Energy audits and conservation
* Energy policy
* Energy storage for mobile and stationary applications
(batteries, flywheels, fuel cells, hydrogen, thermal)
* Renewable energy systems (small hydro, solar, tidal, wave,
wind)
* Patents and inventions
* Training

Technology profile

Our larger clients include Fortune 100 Companies Allied Signal
Aerospace, General Motors and Motorola, global wireless
technology leader Telxon, Utilities, Universities and
Governments
(Federal, Provincial, Municipal).

Our earlier clients and projects include such diverse areas as
the following:

* Electronics, power and energy storage systems for submersibles
and other mobile applications

* Software for efficient energy utilization design including
life cycle costing for: residential, commercial, industrial and
transportation applications including community planning and
renewable energy sources

* Software for energy management and recovery: buildings, heat
pumps, solar energy

* Software for solar energy applications: active, passive, photo-
voltaics, seasonal storage

* Efficient energy utilization design courses and seminars

* Water desalination and purification by reverse osmosis

Our current focus is Data Acquisition and Analysis, Test and
Measurement, Process Monitoring and Control, Business and
Industrial Automation. Our more recent clients and projects
include such diverse areas as the following:

* DOS, Windows, NT, Unix system administration: workstations,
local and wide area networks

* E-mail, information services, the Internet and the World Wide
Web

* Packaged and custom application software: science and
technology, project and information management, mathematics,
communications, office automation

* Heart valves and other biomedical devices and electronics
Science center displays and exhibits

* Magnetic levitation products (world globes, toys, advertising)
and displays

* Automated Test Equipment primarily for aerospace and
telecommunications

* Telecommunications, including mobile data applications (CDPD -
cellular digital packet data)

* GPS (geographical positioning system) computerized survey and
mapping systems for mineral exploration

* Spectral analysis and design enhancements for lighting systems

* Wireless LANs and WANs (local and wide area networks) for data
collection and sales force automation, including the use of PDAs
(personal digital assistants with bar code readers, laser
scanners)

* Vacuum multilayer optical coaters

Market profile

Alliances:

  • Sales/Marketing
  • Technology

Strategic alliances:

S-MATRIX Enterprises Inc. is aggressively looking for high
powered Marketing and Venture Capital Partners to help us
identify, focus on and finance the development of a few of the
many new opportunities in business and industrial automation,
process control, virtual instrumentation and test & measurement.
Aerospace, Telecommunications, Biotechnology, and Mineral
Exploration have been our recent client project focus. We
would like to build on this recent experience and develop less
specialized products which have broader marketplace application
and appeal.

Aerospace - Automated Test Equipment (ATE)

Our S-MATRIX ATE for Sikorsky S-76 helicopters does have a large
existing market in great need of automated test equipment for
repair and overhaul, the United States Military. In order to be
successful in this marketplace, we need capital, personnel and
probably an existing organization experienced in dealing
successfully with the US Military.

Automated Test Equipment for aircraft electronics including
flight controls, recorders, guidance systems, and control
surfaces require further development.


Mineral Exploration - GPS Computer Surveys and Mapping

Some of our projects can be developed quickly into products.
Ground based GPS Computerized Survey & Mapping Systems for
mineral exploration is one.

Technology Transfer

There are also technology transfer opportunities with companies
like Boeing that we are prepared to discuss after
Confidentiality, Non-Disclosure and Non-Competition Agreements
have been signed. Our primary role would be to support and
further develop the products.

Test and Measurement, Automation, Control - Software Based

The potential for the development of new lines of general-
purpose virtual instrumentation that can be easily modified for
more than one application is enormous. The software in this case
defines the instrument. One strategy that indicates the potential
is: analyze the Hewlett Packard catalog; select those hardware
centered dedicated instrumentation systems that represent the
best marketing and business opportunities; replace those systems
with software centered generic portable modular PC-based
instrumentation systems that take advantage of the computational,
display, productivity, connectivity and costs of today's
computers and software development tools and systems.
Development costs of next generation instrumentation products
can be as little as 1/4 to 1/3 of the cost of development of
previous generation dedicated instruments. And at least half of
this cost is software.

Energy - Photovoltaics

The importance of building a worldwide renewable economy is
undeniable. Conservation and renewable energy are at the heart
of a renewable economy. In the field of solar energy, it might
already be possible with economies of scale, mass production and
implementation, and innovative systems engineering to design a
cost effective photovoltaic system for general (residential,
commercial, industrial ...) use without the need of any major
technological breakthroughs.
