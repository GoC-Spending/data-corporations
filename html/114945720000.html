Company Profile


At Norwest Precision Limited, a skilled work force using the
most advanced production techniques makes us a leading machining resource with virtually unlimited capabilities.

As a reliable supplier to worldwide markets we specialize in the supply of precision-machined complex components and assemblies for a broad spectrum of industry sectors that include: automotive,
aircraft and aerospace; defense; steel fabrication; oil processing; hydraulics; and power utilities.

The range of services we offer completely encompasses the
manufacturing of small, medium and large size components -
from the initial acquisition of raw materials to the final
inspection of manufactured parts.

Our computer-integrated facilities incorporate the latest
advancements in automated production technology so that we offer customers innovative solutions to design requirements as well as prompt delivery scheduling. Combined with our advanced
technological prowess, our highly skilled technical personnel
possess a vast wealth of expertise and experience. In
the final analysis, Norwest Precision will continue to achieve
its status of excellence through the efforts of our people and
our collective insistence on perfection.

Utilizing the latest technology in multi-axis CNC Machine
Centers available, Norwest Precision is unrivalled in the
ability to supply precision-machined intricate components to
exceptionally close tolerances. Engineered design information
is transferred directly to CNC machine centers via our fully
integrated CAD/CAM programming network which not only increases
manufacturing accuracy but also accommodates demanding contract schedules. We monitor and carry out computerized inspections at every stage of the production process to guarantee that the final product is exact and consistent. Our expertly trained
quality control inspectors use the most advanced testing
equipment to ensure that manufactured products adhere to
customer specifications.
-----------------------

We are a diversified manufacturer of precision-machined components. We manufacture components for various
industries, aerospace and power generation industries requiring machining on medium to large metal components. Our CNC Machine Centres have capacity load up to 80 ton
with shop clearance of 40 ft under the overhead cranes. We also
have computerized Q.A. reporting available for projects. We
have lathes (including CNC Lathe with 63" swing x 236", carrying
capacity of 20,000 kg), milling machines, vertical milling
machines, vertical turning and boring machines and horizontal
boring machines. A complete machinery list is available upon
request.
