Company Profile


NATURE OF BUSINESS

ROMOR Atlantic Limited is manufacturers' sales representative and
distributor for oceanographic, geophysical, defence and offshore
oil & gas equipment, instrumentation and supplies. We are Canada's leading provider of complete intergration of equipment and services to the marine industry. We offer sales, ocean instrumentation and technology consulting, training, maintenance logistics for our products and service to the Canadian marketplace. Our vendors are industry leaders from the United States, Europe and Canada.

Our major clients include Bedford Institute of Oceanography,
Geological Survey of Canada, Department of National Defence of
Canada, Vemco Limited, Satlantic Inc., Canadian Border Services and major survey and geophysical companies.

COMPANY HISTORY

The company began in 1984 as the east coast office of Romor
Equipment, an Ontario-based manufacturers' sales representative.
In 1992 Mr. Darrin Verge purchased the business and the copany was incorporated as ROMOR Atlantic Limited in 1994. In 2007 the company expanded into a 4800 square foot facility and has plans to increase its staff to provide added value for our existing and future clients.

COMPANY MANAGEMENT

Born and raised in Nova Scotia, Mr. Verge graduated from
Toronto's DeVry Institute of Technology in 1983 with a diploma in
Electronic Technology. Under Mr. Verge's ownership, annual
revenue from company sales has steadily increased from 1992.

TECHNOLOGY FOCUS

We supply Canadian businesses and research institutions with
high quality OEM oceanographic and geophysical equipment and
supplies. ROMOR Atlantic Limited's major applications for the
client base include ocean mapping, seismic profiling, underwater
acoustics, pressure measurements, underwater video, subsea
cable, control umbilical, connectors, cable and pipe tracking,
vessel motion monitoring, noise measurement, positioning and
navigation.

responses to client inquiries. We also lease and rent many of
the instruments that we sell.

Innovative Features

Because the fields of oceanography and geophysics are driven by
advancements in research, they are rapidly changing and
difficult to predict. ROMOR Atlantic Limited has a reputation
for keeping abreast of these changes in the field and in
individual client needs.

From ROMOR Atlantic Limited you can expect a broad selection of
top-quality products and services, timely follow-up and accurate
up-to-date information concerning advances in technology and how
they might affect you.

Competitive Position

Located in the Burnside Industrial Park, with a 4800 square foot office and warehouse, in Dartmouth, Nova Scotia, Romor Atlantic Limited is close to Dalhousie University and the Bedford
Institute of Oceanography; institutions whose names are
respected around the world. We have access to accurate
information, resources and contacts in the oceanographic and
geophysical fields.

ROMOR Atlantic Limited is the exclusive Atlantic Canadian sales
representative for more than 15 internationally known
manufacturers of oceanographic and geophysical equipment.

COMPANY STRATEGY

Having established a solid sales base, ROMOR Atlantic Limited
works with companies integrating systems comprising of products
from several manufacturers. To provide sales, ocean instrumentation and technology consulting, training, maintenance logistics for our products and service to the Canadian marketplace.

Product Name:

Our sonar systems employ high-resolution technology and utilize
fully digital image processing. Sonar processors run
multidiscipline survey systems allowing the addition of other

imagery.

systems and fiber optic systems.

Product Name:

Product Name:
Underwater Lights, Cameras, Pan & Tilt
Products designed to perform in harsh marine environments, from
wet/dry surface applications to full ocean depth deployments.
All products are pressure tested to the rated depth. Products
range from camera control units to lights.

Product Name:
Vessel Motion Monitoring
Products include motion sensing, heading, positioning and pipe
and cable survey. Products are flexible and adaptable--custom
built solutions available.

Product Name:
Pipe and Cable Tracking
High-performance system is a self-contained survey system
providing location, tracking and survey data about the pipeline
or cable's relative position, achieving burial depths that have
previously been unattainable. Can be deployed on a wide range
of subsea vehicles due to it compact size, ease of installation
and use.


Product Name:
Hydrographic Survey Tools
Using a (MBES)Multibeam echo sounder, you are able to map the
complete underwater landscape with significant accuracy. When
combined with other sensors, provides data on underwater terrain
to support numerous survey tasks.

Product Name:
Hydrophones, Arrays, Towed and Bottom Mounted
These products are used for high resolution seismic, engineering,
research and military applications. With superior bandwidth,
lower distortion and considerable durability, our products exceed
the demands of the market. We offer down-hole arrays, bottom
cable hydrophone arrays, high-speed towed arrays and data
acquisition systems.
