Company Profile


As British Columbias finest home staging company, Revamp's Home Stagers create stunning home stylings that wow real estate buyers as soon as they walk up to your front door. From makeovers to fully furnishing vacant homes, in our capable hands your staged home becomes a showpiece designed specifically to appeal to the greatest number of buyers. Revamp have a fantastic collection of furniture, bedding and decorative pieces to complement any home.

Revamp are the Lower Mainland's Home Staging & Interior Redesign leaders servicing Whistler, Vancouver , North Vancouver , West Vancouver , Richmond , Burnaby , Surrey , and the Lower Mainland of British Columbia.
