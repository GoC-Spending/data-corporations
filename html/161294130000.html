Company Profile


Spiral Binding Co. has been in the "word packaging" business
for more than 75 years. We have 250 employees throughout North
America all of whom are committed to providing the best products
and services to our clients. Spiral provides a complete line of
wire and plastic binding equipment and supplies, laminating
systems and supplies, customized looseleaf binders and index
tabs, ID systems and accessories, and more.

Product Name:
Spiralastic® Plastic Coil Binding
Spiralastic® Plastic Coil Binding...
Exciting, unique plastic coil with practical and creative uses!

Spiralastic® is ... Great Looking! Economical! Durable! The
smoth, round plastic coil allows pages to turn a full 360º.
The multiple-hole punch format makes the binding extra strong
and the plastic coil's ``memory`` resists bending, making
Spiralastic® great for mailing!

PFP-100K
Automatic Coil Inserter
Autotmated microprocessor-based coil inserter provides
exceptional productivity and ease-of-use. Binds books up to
7/16`` thick and 12`` in length without adjustments. Available
in either 4:1 or 5:1 patterns.

EP-17
Heavy Duty Electric Punch
Heavy duty desktop, front loading electric punch that punches up
to 35 sheets. Change punching patterns easily with
interchangeable dies that slide out in seconds. Punch pins can
be adusted to different binding lengths.

Electric Coil Inserter
Compact coil inserter that is efficient and easy to use. Binds
3/16`` to 1-1/4`` coil in 4:1 or 5:1 pitch.

PMPC1200
Electric Punch and Inserter
Electric punching with a time-saving coil inserting operation.
Bind 3/16`` to `1-1/4`` coil in 4:1 or 5:1 pitch

HD7000
Heavy Duty Electric Punch
Deluxe tabletop punch has a unique die changing system that
allows you to punch all popular patterns. Punches 25-30 sheets
of 20lb. Paper per lift. The machine includes one die, and your
can order other easy-to-change dies to satisfy all your punching
pattern requirements.

PC2000
Punch and bind with this easy -to-use manual punch and electric
coil inserting machine. Available in 4:1 or 5:1 patterns.

Automatic Crimpers

with our automatic crimpers. Foot pedal operation crimps 5mm to
20mm Spiralastic® plastic coil quickly and easily. Choose form
single or dual heat units.

Punch and bind unit

Product Name:
Spiral-O® Wire Binding
Spiral-O® Wire Binding
For that Hi-Tech look, use the wire binding that works with your
plastic binding machine!
Spiral-O ® Wire Binding is a unique wire binding -- it's the only
one that conforms to the standard hole-punching patterns of
plastic binding machines. Simply punch your booklet on your
plastic binding machine and bind on our inexpensive E-Z Closer
using Spiral-O ® Wire. Spiral-O ® offers extra strength and
durability. Pages turn more easily, open and lie flat and can
turn
360° degrees back to back. A wide selection of sizes and colors
is available. With Spiral-O ®, the sleek, professional look of
wire binding is now available to plastic binding punch owners!


Product Name:
Thermal Binding
Thermal Binding
Simple, secure and versatile... Turn loose sheets into perfectly
bound documents! Pages bound by our thermal binding machines are
held securely in place with a strong adhesive that enables
documents to withstand the wear and tear of repeated readings.
It's simple to use: just insert pages into the cover, place the
document in the binding machine and wait 30 seconds, and your
document is ready to go! Corrections or revisions are easily made
too!

DuraBind 315E
This deluxe desktop binder features an electronic push button
control that automatically times the binding operation and beeps
when complete. Bind documents up to 8½`` x 11`` up to ¾``
thick. Cooling rack conveniently holds finished documents.

Thermotronic 400
The perfect choice when you need fast production and high volume.
Quickly binds documents up to 2`` thick and 25¾`` in length.
Automatic shut off feature turns the machine off after last
binding.
