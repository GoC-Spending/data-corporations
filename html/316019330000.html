Company Profile


COMPANY HISTORY  A Legacy of Innovation

Thought Technology Ltd. is the brainchild of Dr. Hal Myers and
Lawrence Klein, who combined their professional skills with
interests in psychology, biomechanical engineering and athletics
to create biofeedback equipment that was more user-friendly and
portable. Their initial effort, launched nearly a quarter of a
century ago, produced the GSR 2, the worlds first handheld
biofeedback device for the clinician, consumer and athletic
markets. The GSR 2 is based on the principle that stress levels
could be precisely measured electronically by translating tiny,
tension- related changes in the skin pores into a rising or
falling tone. Over a quarter of a million GSR 2s have been sold,
many of which are still in use today.

In 1980, the GSR 2 earned an impressive endorsement from Dr.
Hans Selye, the Father of Stress. Dr. Selye recommended the
use of the GSR 2 in any stress management program. Since then,
numerous leading clinicians from major medical institutions have
developed home training programs designed to work in conjunction
with the GSR 2 . Thought Technologys GSR 2 and other
biofeedback devices have been integrated into the training
programs of thousands of Olympic and professional athletes as a
way to enhance performance and rehabilitation.

Today, Thought Technology designs, manufactures and markets a
full line of biofeedback equipmentfrom sophisticated, multi-
channel computer-based systems to portable stand-alone devices
for muscle assessment and re-education, psychological monitoring
and training.

RESEARCH & DEVELOPMENT  Our Continuing Commitment

To ensure that Thought Technologys history of innovation
continues, we dedicate over 20% of our annual sales revenue
toward R&D, employing engineers, programmers and technicians.
While our primary focus is the creation of products that fill a
need in the marketplace, we also devote a substantial portion of
those resources to improving existing products in order to keep
pace with the latest research and technology as it becomes
available. In addition, we sponsor and assist in the design of
various research projects encompassing a wide array of clinical
applications, simultaneously helping people and obtaining
valuable clinical feedback that further contributes to the
development and improvement of our products.

PRODUCTION  Setting the Bar High...Then Clearing It!

No matter how well-conceived our designs are, ultimately theyre
only as good as our manufacturing processes. Thats why weve
made a significant investment in our production facilities over
the past several years. These actions, accompanied by our own
dedication, have enabled us to achieve quality standards that
meet the levels set by ISO9001, its European Union counterpart
EN46001 and all USA FDA QSR standards... in every operational
phase.

Our design and manufacturing resources and expertise are
available to other companies for private labelling.

Weve also established facilities in both the United States and
Canada, allowing us to bring products to market faster and
service the ones that are currently in use more effectively. The
result of this effort has been to provide our customers with a
higher level of quality.

SALES & MARKETING  Providing The Tools That Make A Difference

Weve made a long term commitment to provide the highest quality
biofeedback to a wide array of clinicianspsychologists,
psychiatrists, physical therapists, primary care physicians,
urologists and OB/Gyns. Because we believe that this rapidly
growing field promises to become a mainstay of medicine in the
21st centurysimilar to the role played by surgery and
pharmaceuticals in the 19th and 20th centuriesweve dedicated
ourselves to shaping the course of that growth through
education, effective marketing and impeccable service.

Toward this end, we sponsor numerous seminars and workshops on
electromyography, EEG and the other modalities in the treatment
of conditions such as incontinence and rehabilitation. Working
with leading clinicians, weve developed a series of training
protocols, as well as other instructional materials to help
facilitate the use of our instrumentation. Were also the
founding corporate member of the Biofeedback Foundation of
Europe, an association of clinicians and researchers dedicated
to disseminating information on biofeedback and its many
applications throughout Europe and the Eastern Hemispere, which
Thought Technology helped establish in 1997.

Our products are sold through a worldwide distribution network
of dealers and retailers in over 80 countries.

WHATs NEXT?  A Hand In the Present, An Eye Toward the Future

In anticipation of future needs, Thought Technology is currently
developing several new products. Whether its a dual-channel EMG
designed to aid in the treatment of incontinence, a telemetry
device able to send data to a PC or more home training products,
Thought Technology will continue to lead the way to the future
with a full line of advanced instruments.

Its all part of whats made Thought Technology a leader:
innovation, a relentless pursuit of quality and, most
importantly, a dedication to improving quality of life.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
FlexComp
The FlexComp uses 8 or 16 channels of input including EMG,
EEG, EKG, Skin Conductance, Temperature, Heart Rate, Blood
Volume Pulse, Respiration, Capable of 2000 samples per second
per channel, providing accurate wave forms up to 500 Hz, Raw
EMG, Median Frequency, fast fourier transforms and Spectral
analyses.


Product Name:
ProComp
ProComp has eight channels of data acquisition including:
electromyograph, heart rate, blood volume pulse, temperature,
skin conductance, respiration. The user has a choice of bar
graphs, polygraphs, visual images and event triggered monitoring,
and biofeedback, as well as multi-channel data acquisition and
record generation.


Product Name:
MyoTrac 2
MyoTrac 2, our dual channel EMG with memorization, has two
rows of 16 LED`s, LCD and tone feedback, MyoTrac 2 can
memorize continuous activity, peak, average and work rest. A
fiber optic link sends memorized data to the PC or printer. It
can
also function on line with sophisticated PC software. For more
information, contact Thought Technology at 1-800-361-3651,
514-489-8251 or Fax at 514-489-8255.


Product Name:
MyoTrac
The MyoTrac electromyogram, from Thought Technology, is a
highly sensitive, portable and affordable device with the
accuracy essential for the clinic, yet ideal for in-home use by
the patient. Eight enhancements include 43% more LED`s, wide and
narrow bandpass, above and below threshold, double the battery
life with rechargables, and 0-2 volt output to connect to
computers.


Product Name:
ProComp +/Biograph
ProComp +/Biograph windows version with powerful multimedia
capability and 2 channels of EEG unfiltered or any chosen
bandwidth. Create Display Images patients can relate to. Import
graphics, animation and morphed imagery. Customize patient
protocols. Modalities include EMG, EEG, Temp, Skin
Conductance, HR/BVP and Respiration.


Product Name:
Exercise / Training Machines & Equipment
Product Name:
U-Control
U-Control ($350.00 US) is the latest portable EMG
continence patient home trainer. A vaginal or rectal sensor,
designed for biofeedback retraining of pelvic floor muscles for
fecal and urinary incontinence, pelvic floor pain, constipation,
or vaginismus is also available. The U-Control is comfortable
to use, light weight & sculpted to fit the hand, it measures
6.5x12.5x2.5 cm. It has 8 Green and 7 Yellow LEDs on its
bargraph with a Green UP LED to signal contract and a Yellow
DOWN LED to signal relax at either 5 or 10 second intervals.



Product Name:
MyoTrac 3
MyoTrac 3 ($3,200.00 US) is a highly sensitive dual  channel
sEMG unit, enables clinicians to customize patient assessment
and training regimens for incontinence. MyoTrac 3 can be
connected to any Window®-compatible PC for easy real-time
monitoring or data analysis with our Continence Software.
Clinicians can customize patient assessments and training
regimens, review and compare previous sessions, and perform
other case management functions. Statistical session data can be
easily printed for comprehensive documentation of patient
outcomes.

Product Name:
ProComp+/Cardiopro

ProComp+/Cardiopro (US $3400.00 US includes encoder and
software. Sensors are at an additional cost between $185.00 -
$350.00) is a specialized biofeedback application that focuses
on the physiology of the cardiovascular and respiratory systems
and takes input from one EKG (electrocardiograph), two
respiration, one temperature and one BVP (blood volume pulse)
sensors. The Respiratory Sinus Arrhythmia and 3D screens have a
variable breathing pacer with independent in/out and hold
phases. CardioPro provides both auditory and visual biofeedback
for any one of three physiological changes: Standard deviation
of the IBI, Heart rate Max-Min (Peak to trough heart rate
changes), and Thoracic / Abdominal ratio. The available
auditory feedback is proportional or inverse-proportional MIDI
tones.
