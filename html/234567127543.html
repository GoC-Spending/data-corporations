Company Profile


Neegan Naynowan Stantec (NNS) LP

Neegan Naynowan Stantec LP is a joint venture Ontario Corporation operating as a Limited partnership NNS LP. NNS LP is an aboriginal majority-owned firm with 51% held by Attawapiskat Development Corporation (ADC) and 49% held by Stantec with the registered head office in Attawapiskat and the branch office in Markham. The firm was formed in August 2006 and qualifies as an Attawapiskat Aboriginal Business. The joint venture partners provide employees to NNS to execute work on a project specific contract basis.

Stantec is a publicly traded Corporation listed on the TSX and NYSE and offering environmental sciences and engineering design, geotechnical and materials engineering, planning and risk management services for infrastructure, industrial, urban planning, natural resources and environmental projects. We are committed to capacity building in Aboriginal Partnerships as an investment in growing our business through engaging aboriginal partners to provide services in Canadas north. As a partner of NNS LP, Stantec is committed to providing training programs to our First Nation and Aboriginal colleagues. NNS provides services in various environmental areas such as environmental site assessments, environmental monitoring and sampling, hydrology/hydrogeological studies, site remediation, permitting and approvals (air, water, ground), water and wastewater treatment, EHS audits, mold assessments, tailings management, mine closure & reclamation, aboriginal consultation and ecological engineering systems to treat municipal and industrial waste water.

ADC is wholly owned by Attawapiskat FN and is responsible for the Attawapiskat community development. Their responsibilities include the establishment of aboriginal joint ventures to foster development and community employment including construction projects. ADC has full time and seconded part-time staff depending on the projects to be addressed. Staff are used from the Band Administration and Technical Services as required.

NNS LP is also recognized as an Attawapiskat Business pursuant to the terms of the IBA between AttFN and De Beers Canada for environmental work at the Victor Diamond Mine site.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Environmental Science and Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, Infrastructure Engineering and Construction Management, Environmental Assessment
Neegan Naynowan Stantec LP provides environmental science, environmental engineering, geotechnical engineering, mine permitting and design, infrastructure engineering, land use planninng and development, and management services to industry and government with focus on Northern Ontario and the James Bay Lowlands. NNS LP also partners with others to undertake work across Ontario and Canada.

NNS LP draws on the scientific, technical, planning and project management expertise of Stantec to deliver environmental site assessment, remediation design and execution, geotechnical investigations, indoor and ambient air quality assessments, terrestrial and aquatic assessments, environmental assessments, permitting & approvals (water, air, land), hydrology/hydrological studies, water and wastewater treatment, infrastructure assessment, engineering design and construction management, climate change, sustainability assessment and planning, mine tailings management, mine closure and reclamation plans, EHS management plans & audits, and environmental monitoring programs, etc.

During our projects aboriginal suppliers, contractors and local technical support staff are used to the extent practical to build local aboriginal capacity to support NNS LP growth and operations.

Service Name:
Consulting Services
NNS provides environmental and infrastructure consulting services including the collection of all necessary assessment and design information. NNS works in all environmenal media (water, air, land). Products comprise scientific and technical reports describing findings, and where appropriate, conclusions, recommendations and designs.

Service Name:
Environmental Services
NNS provides environmental services including: sampling and monitoring of air, land, water and terrestrial and aquatic biota; environmental impact assessments; permitting and approvals (ECA, PTTW) for industrial sites, municpalities and mines; hydrologic and hydrogeologic (groundwater) studies for water management planning; water and wastewater treatment assessment and design; site assessment (Phase 1 and Phase 2 ESA) and remediation; tailings and waste rock management; mine closure and reclaimation planning; environmental and human health risk assessment; EH&S management planning and audits; ambient air and indoor air quality studies (including mold);aboriginal consultation; archaelogy and traditional knowledge assessment; sustainability, GHG-climate change and ecological assessments.

FSC Code:
R005-Technical Assistance
R008-Engineering and Technical Services (incl. Mechanical, Electrical, Chemical, Electronic Engineering)

Sector information

Distribution of employees:

ADC Inc has about 20 First nation employees based in Attawapiskat Ontario. Stantec has over 11,000 employees based principally in Canada and the US. Stantec is the Managing Partner of NNS LP and operates and manages all projects executed by NNS LP. Both ADC Inc. and Stantec second staff to projects carried out by NNS LP. First Nation contractor and suppliers are utilized to the maximum extent possible for all projects.
