Company Profile


Founded in 1946, Morrison Hershfield continues to uphold its reputation as a prominent multi-disciplinary consulting
engineering and management firm. Our focus is on our clients by providing them with expertise in telecommunications
infrastructure, civil and structural engineering, building science, facilities management, fire & life safety, transportation infrastructure, project & construction management and specialized services in management consulting. Our commitment to international initiatives keeps us at the leading edge of technology, worldwide. MH operates eleven offices throughout Canada and the US.

During the firms history, MH has worked on all kinds of buildings for diverse clients, built highways, railroads and
bridges, helped owners retrofit their buildings/structures and plan funds for future maintenance/capital work, and provided
decades of service to the Telecommunications Industry. Our contribution to the Hibernia project is an example of, and
testimonial to, our reputation in offshore structural engineering. Be it industrial, commercial, institutional, residential, public or private, domestic or international, our mandate is to provide services and solutions which will assist our clients in achieving their objectives in a cost effective, efficient, professional and friendly manner.

Following the lead of Carson Morrison and Charlie Hershfield, two of the founding partners, our engineers continue to be active members of professional organizations, contributing to the formulation of many major codes and standards. They are committed to maintaining Morrison Hershfields leadership position by initiating and contributing to conferences, seminars and technical journals. The result? Our professionals are looked to for their ability to offer outstanding technical capabilities and the ability to offer clients an objective, strategic, and logical solution to their problems.

Morrison Hershfield recognizes that the increased scope of many major projects, both domestic and international, requires wide
ranging resources from engineering and construction to finance and strategic planning. As a result, we continue to stress the
importance of partnerships where our skills compliment those of our partners and where regional expertise brings an advantage to
our clients.

Ensuring that we are continually in the lead, we seek out, evaluate and ultimately participate in opportunities which will
lead us into the 21st century. As an employee-owned organization, Morrison Hershfield is committed to the excellence of its employees and the recognition of these employees by their peers. In 1994, the Awards of Excellence program honoring Carson Morrison was introduced. The Awards signify yet another step towards maintaining our standards of excellence.

Over our 60 year history, Morrison Hershfield has earned a reputation for excellence, professionalism, innovation and
ethics. It is with this culture that we approach each project to ensure our clients success.
