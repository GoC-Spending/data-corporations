Company Profile


Specialists in structural design, the key personnel of Valron
Engineers Inc. have a wealth of experience in all aspects of
civil engineering, with hundreds of successfully completed
projects to their credit. The firm's expertise in structural
engineering is reflected through design in structural steel,
reinforced and prestressed concrete, masonry, timber and also in
aluminum.

Valron Engineers Inc. currently employs a staff of 12, comprised
of 2 Engineers, 1 Engineer-in-Training, 8 Technicians and 1
Clerical. The firm is wholly owned by its professional staff.
A recent change in management and the addition of senior
professional staff has allowed the company to broaden the scope
of services offered, and enhance its ability to provide top
quality engineering for its clients.

The firm prides itself on its ability to work towards a project
objective as a team player with professionals from related
disciplines, and provide clients with the desired results in an
innovative and cost effective manner. The firm places a strong
emphasis on professional development and strives to keep up-to-
date with the latest technology in order to bring both expertise
and value to its clients.

The firm provides a full range of engineering services from
preliminary and feasibility studies, project planning,
conceptual development, life-cycle costing, preliminary design
and costing, to detailed design, specification writing, contract
and tender document preparation, tender evaluations, project
management, contract administration, scheduling, cost control,
construction supervision, and final project certification.

The firm offers expertise in the area of building, bridge,
marine and related structural evaluations. Key personnel have
experience in restoration and renovation of existing buildings
and historic structures, failure investigations and evaluation,
building condition assessments, appraisals, and insurance claim
investigations.

The firm also offers shop fabrication drawing detailing services
to fabricators in the steel industry, the pre-cast concrete
culvert and pre-cast building panel sectors, as well as
suppliers of light steel metal framing (metal stud walls).

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
steel detailing
The firm of Valron Engineers Inc. has been in the detailing
business for more than 10 years. We are located in Moncton, New
Brunswick, Canada. We are a structural design and detailing
company with a full time in-house technical staff of 12. They
are comprised of the following; 3 engineers, 2 senior
technicians, 5 detailers and 2 checkers. The experience of the
individual detailers ranges from 20 to 2 years. We are familiar
with connection design in accordance with both AISC and CISC
code requirements. Two of our engineers provide connection
design on a regular basis.

Our firm provides a full range of engineering services from One
of the more senior detailers has expertise in 3-D modeling of
hip and valley roof framing and curved beam and girder
structures. Our senior checker also has significant experience
in this area. Currently our 3-D modeling is done on an in-house
CADKEY software program with many customized macros that make
detailing efficient and accurate. In the upcoming months we are
going to be phasing in the powerful automated 3-D detailing
software package, X-STEEL, into our operations. This software
package will allow us to produce detailing jobs more cheaply and
in shorter timeframes. An added feature is the ability to
automatically generate auxiliary reports, bills of materials,
etc. that can be of benefit to the client.

Based in Moncton NB Canada, Valron Engineers Inc. Our experience
as detailers lies in structures for buildings, churches,
schools, hospitals, cinemas, industrial buildings, miscellaneous
mine sites, refineries and powerplant work such as pipe racks,
equipment platforms, stairs, handrails, ladders, as well as
marine structures, turbine and generator lagging.

We find that our capacity is such that 200 to 300 ton jobs
running concurrently with smaller projects works well for us.

We are currently setting up our web site as an on-line resource,
with the capability of password protected data access and viewer
integration. In other words, this system will allow for
extremely easy document transfer between ourselves and our
clients via the internet. This will provide very smooth
communication of drawings.

Service Name:
structural design
The key personnel of Valron Engineers Inc. have many years of
experience in numerous sectors of the civil and structural
engineering fields. The firm's expertise lies in both the
design of new construction and the evaluation, restoration, and
renovation of existing structures. Including bridges, airports,
industrial buildings, correctional facilities, health care
facilities, marine engineering, mining, office complexes,
institutions, schools and special care centers.

Technology profile

At Valron Engineers Inc. the latest in state-of-the-art computer
systems and software are used to enhance design optimization and
to prepare design drawings.
Our library of up-to-date computer software includes programs
such as AUTOCAD 2000 and CADKEY '98 for computer drafting.
AUTOCAD and CADKEY development languages are used for
customizing these drawing packages to further improve our
drafting capabilities. For structural design, programs such as
STAAD III are employed. This powerful design program is the
most popular and widely used structural engineering software in
the world. Other very important software packages which are
also used by the firm's designers include WOODWORKS for design
in timber, MASONRY '95 for design in masonry, LEAP SOFTWARE for
designing bridges and ADOSS for designing concrete slabs. Out-of-
the ordinary structures such as domes, irregular flat plates,
curvilinear structures etc., are engineered using finite element
programs, to provide precise designs. In-house programs are also
developed as required to solve specific problems or to optimize
design of repetitive elements.

The engineering design and drafting produced by our staff is
prepared with care and attention to detail in order to provide
the most cost effective solution for our clients' projects. Our
technicians are experienced in AUTOCAD, MICROSTATION and CADKEY
and will complete the drawings in the format requested by the
client. Manual drafting is still offered to those clients who
require it.
We recently have made the integration of XSTEEL into our
automated detailing capabilities for those clients that prefer
it.
