Company Profile


Magellan Aerospace is a global aerospace company that provides complex assemblies and systems solutions to aircraft and engine manufacturers, and defence and space agencies worldwide. Magellan's operations are organized into key business groups: Aeroengines, Aerostructures, Rockets and Space, Sand Castings, Repair and Overhaul, and Specialty Products.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Aircraft engine manufacture and repair
design, manufacture and repair of aeroengine products including
hot section components and sheet metal fabrication such as
combustors, augmentors/afterburners and casings which require a
combination of machining, fusion welding, resistance welding,
forming, brazing, milling, turning, drilling, riveting, painting
and plasma spray.

Product Name:
Aircraft structure
extensive expertise in the manufacture of components including
composite and metal to metal bonding, sheet metal detail
fabrication, forming, milling and aerostructure assemblies.



Product Name:
WIRE STRIKE PROTECTION SYSTEM
Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) and designer of the
patented Wire Strike Protection System (WSPS). Designed to
provide a measure of protection for a helicopter's frontal area
in the event of inadvertent flight into horizontally strung
wires. Now available for most military and commercial
helicopters worldwide and continues to be adapted to new models.



Product Name:
Black Brant Sounding Rockets
Black Brant is a solid propellant rocket system in single and
multi-stage configurations that can carry payloads of 70-840 kg
to altitiudes from 150km to more than 1500km. Provides up to
20 minutes of useful time for micro-gravity experiments, auroral
studies, deep space observations and other extra-terrestrial
reseach.

Product Name:
CRV7 Rocket
70mm air-to-ground rocket weapon system. High impulse motor
using hydroxyl-terminated polybutadiene (HTPB) propellants which
are unmatched in the field of 2.75 inch rockets.
