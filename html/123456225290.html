Company Profile


As voice and data technologies continue to converge and evolve into the Internet of Things, the enterprise world has become one of multiple platforms, a variety of applications and several delivery methods. SOFTEL has spent over 20 years developing the unmatched scope of highly specialized expertise and experience that is necessary for real world implementations of technology innovations realizing core business objectives of leading organizations across the globe.
 Contact Center Solutions
 Universal Communications/ Mobility
 Cloud Centric Application delivery and Security
 Big Data, Social and Business Intelligence
 Integrating new solutions with existing applications
o Microsoft Lync
o WebRTC
o Data Center Virtualization
o Voice/Facial Biometrics

Over 200 of the Fortune 500 firms worldwide have benefited from SOFTELs expertise and experience over the years. SOFTEL holds some of the highest accreditations and certifications from Avaya, Cisco, Genesys, Microsoft, and other key companies in the technology/communication world. This multiplatform expertise has led these same technology and communication companies to call on SOFTEL Communications to help them deliver complex solutions to their own customers as well drive innovation to enhance their product portfolios and market offers.
