Company Profile


Five Winds International is a management-consulting firm that helps organizations improve the economic, environmental and social performance  the sustainability  of their operations, products and services. Our strength is helping integrate environmental and social considerations into the core business activities of organizations.

 For private sector companies these activities include strategy development, product design, operations, supply chain management, sales and marketing and capital investment.

 In the public sector, core business activities include policy and program development, performance measurement, service delivery, operations and procurement.

The Five Winds International team has expertise in business strategy, engineering and material science, industrial design, environmental science, resource economics, social performance measurement and government policy. The firm provides an international perspective through its offices in the United States, Germany, Canada and the Netherlands. Our client base is primarily comprised of large multi-national companies, but we also work with national, state and local governments, industry associations and multi-lateral organizations such as the UNEP and the OECD.

Five Winds International has four main consulting service lines:
 Sustainability and Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)
 Management Systems for Continuous Improvement
 Climate Change
 Product Sustainability

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
GaBi4 Life Cycle Engineering Software
GaBi 4 is a Life Cycle Engineering tool that supports users in the systematic preparation and analysis of product- and company-related energy, resource and environmental decisions. It does this by helping the user integrate technical, economic and ecological information to support the optimization of material and energy use, production processes, product design and product marketing. It can be used for much more than traditional Life Cycle Assessment work, including: support for ISO 14001 management systems, greenhouse gas analysis and monitoring, capital project, process, and technology evaluations, Eco-Efficiency projects, etc.

The software supports a systems analysis process modeling methodology that can help emissions offsets gain more value, both in economic and political terms. It reduces uncertainty in study results due to its lifecycle perspective, thereby allowing companies to be more thorough in evaluating trade-offs during the decision-making process. The software presents values for environmental impacts such as toxicity, ozone depletion, acidification potential, global warming etc. The approach also fits into an environmental management system framework and can be verified using the ISO 14040 standards. Users have found that the software can assist in the following fields:

§ Greenhouse Gas Accounting
§ Life Cycle Assessment
§ Life Cycle Engineering
§ Design for Environment
§ Energy Efficiency Studies
§ Substance Flow Analysis
§ Company EcoBalances
§ Environmental Reporting
§ Sustainability Reporting
§ Strategic Risk Management
§ Total Cost Accounting


Current users of GaBi range from government, materials, chemicals, energy, automotive and electronics sectors.

Service Name:
Managing Continuous Improvement
With expertise ranging from energy and transportation to infrastructure, chemicals and consumer products, we work with clients to design and implement management systems that meet their own unique requirements. We have a firm grasp of the realities in managing environmental issues in business (such as regulatory compliance), and have helped many organizations achieve ISO 14001 certification.

We have an in-depth understanding of the ways information technologies can be used to facilitate the integration of management systems into your existing workflow. We have created tools, programs and data-collection techniques that support the implementation of management systems and in many cases enhance its effectiveness.

Five Winds delivers a full range of EMS design, training, and implementation services aimed at leveraging existing management programs to develop a strong yet flexible system. Five Winds has access to a wide range of individuals with excellent expertise, to assist clients with EMS activities such as:

Service Name:
Product Innovation & Life Cycle Management Services
Is the changing landscape of corporate governance, corporate social responsibility, customer expectations, and new regulations affecting your business? These changes signal an opportunity to gain competitive advantage by designing better products and services, to reduce costs, improve supplier relationships and fulfill new market segments. Our Product Innovation & Life Cycle Management Services help clients respond to product related policies, sustainable production and consumption, and implement CSR while reducing risk and stimulating innovation.

Service Name:
Climate Change Services
Five Winds International has experience in providing and implementing solutions that help to manage the business risks and maximize the business opportunities associated with climate change.
To meet international environmental commitments such as the Kyoto Protocol, governments are in the process of implementing policies and measures to restrict greenhouse gas emissions. As these come into effect, they will increase the price of fossil fuel based energy, affect the structure of world energy markets, and influence markets to favor less greenhouse gas-intensive industries, companies, technologies, products and services. Businesses are likely to experience shifting demand (both growth and decline) for various technologies, products and services, depending on their relative greenhouse gas intensities.

Over the past three years, Five Winds International has been active in benchmarking and implementing leading edge strategies and practices related to the management and mitigation of greenhouse gases. Our experience includes measuring GHG emissions from industrial systems and technology projects, a review of leading industrial practice, and involvement in the development of leading GHG standards and protocols.

We can help your organization with our range of services, including:

Climate Change Strategy Development & Implementation
Benchmarking your current situation
Assessing your key risks and opportunities
Developing or realigning strategy
Implementing appropriate responses, systems and programs
Managing Supply Chain Carbon Risk
Greenhouse Gas Measurement and Reporting
