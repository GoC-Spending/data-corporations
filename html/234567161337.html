Company Profile


LDC Solutions is a proud member of the Canadian Council of Aboriginal Business (CCAB) as a Certified Aboriginal Business.

We offer solutions to Talent Search, Inclusion & Diversity, RPO (Recruitment Process Outsourcing), Outplacement, and Career Counselling. Our number one objective is to help our clients achieve success by having the right people and programs in place to address their needs for today and for the future to give them the advantage they need.

LDC Solutions is unique in our Industry because of our people. Our Team is comprised of people bringing knowledge and experience from Industries such as Software Development, Aerospace, Automotive, Oil & Gas, and Pharma to name a few. They have enjoyed roles in Management, Engineering, Finance, and Project Management.

It is that wealth of knowledge that allows us the ability to have the insight into the needs of our clients and provide solutions to those objectives.

WHAT MAKES US UNIQUE, ALLOWS US TO SUCCEED

We have had great successes helping both Employers and Job Seekers. As Founder and Senior Consultant, I invite you to explore our website and learn more about the Industries and People we can help.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Recruitment, Inclusion & Diversity, Recruitment Process Outsourcing, Outplacement, and Career Councilling
Recruitment - Our team is unique in the sense that they have all come from careers with Benchmarked companies in the Industry that they service. We are all about quality, not quantity. Our team will assess the requirements with our clients as well as the means of assessment to align and better serve those needs. We perform a search and generate responses from both passive and active recruitment. Candidates are screened for fit and the top 3 - 5 candidates are presented for each opening.

Each candidate will be presented with notes from our preliminary interview to help you understand a little more beyond the resume.

Our services continue throughout the hiring process to aid in scheduling and presentation of offers to help our clients build stronger teams to give them advantages they need for today and into the future.

Inclusion & Diversity - Inclusion is more about helping Organizations and Communities to come together and provide means for mutual success.

While Aboriginal Inclusion Initiatives are a Specialty, we do have the ability to work with all Organizations and Communities to help facilitate Mutual Economic Growth in a meaningful way.


Developing a variety of perspectives in your Organization can be effective in aiding in more effective Managerial decision making and problem solving abilities. Among the many benefits, Diversity in the workplace can aid in aligning to Diverse Customers. The added engagement and involvement of all employees can be very effective in retaining members of your team.

LDC Solutions is a 100% Aboriginal Owned Business that specializes in helping Organizations with Inclusion and Diversity Initiatives. Specifically, we have been instrumental in the success of Aboriginal Inclusion initiatives that help bring Organizations and Communities together for mutual sustainable success.

Diversity and Inclusion reflects Senior Management’s vision of creating an innovative organization that works well. Key steps include:

 Having a workforce that is broadly reflective of the community
 Identifying and addressing barriers within organizational systems

 Attracting and retaining a talented workforce skilled at working in an inclusive and respectful manner with one another and with the community

 Creating processes, policies, plans, practices, programs and services that meet the diverse needs of those we serve.

It is our desire to help your team to work with the Diverse Communities to develop common interests and goals for sustained mutual benefit. We invite you to talk with us so that we can understand the solutions that you need.

LDC Solutions is committed to working with our Clients and Communities to help in building in those relationships. We will donate a portion of proceeds to helping develop Cultural events in Aboriginal communities.

RPO - Recruitment Process Outsourcing - Simply said, it is a great way to help Organizations hire the people they need at a fraction of the cost while having the ability to source candidates from the available Labour Market, being both active and passive candidates. Our team can work on site to better manage the initiative giving you first hand access to both the recruiter and the candidates.

Outplacement and Career Training - Our goal is to provide a service that facilitates an easier transition for both parties. We will be instrumental in that continued growth by aiding Employers with the Transition from old to new. Employees will be provided the key skills that they need to continue to move forward and grow their career paths while reducing their gaps in employment

Career Training – Action Plan
Below, please find a breakdown of the course which describes how we will gain understanding of candidate career needs and tailor a training package to fit those needs.

 First Phase - Review and Revisions of Resume
• Meeting 1 – listen to career history and future objectives.
• Meeting 2 – Review updated resume and teach how to understand and use the ABC structure for every job applied for
o Review and Assess – Review jobs applied for and resume used for application process and offer feedback

 Second Phase - Coaching for Interview process and Skills development
• First meeting – introduce a solutions based type interview…not a sales pitch
• Second Meeting – Develop ability to answer Behavioral and Technical Questions and provide a solution

 Third Phase –Job hunting
• Meeting 1 - Review activities of job searches and interviews. Offer further feedback for improvement or encouragement. Review activities for the following if applicable

o Networking
o Internet and newspapers
o Door to door
o Recruitment firms and Contract firms
o Include them in our job bank

• Meeting 2 - Career planning (short and long term) – Discuss current goals or if there is a change in objectives.
