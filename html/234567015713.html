Company Profile


Schroeder & Schroeder Inc. is a firm of experienced professional program and project managers, management consultants, and corporate managers. Our core purpose is to help organizations help people. By providing Transformation Management consulting services through an "art and science" framework, we help public and private organizations reduce the risk and uncertainty in launching, accelerating and maintaining their transformation initiatives.

Senior level professional experience is provided using a holistic approach; applying both deep and broadly based functional knowledge and experience. Project management skills help with managing the complexity of programs and projects. Consulting subject matter knowledge provides the depth of insight required for particular types of transformations. Corporate experience provides the deeper organizational and industry understanding.

Service Name:
Implementation Management
Transformation programs and projects are increasingly becoming the norm for many organizations as they are looking to remain and increase their ability to be competitive. Schroeder & Schroeder can help your organization with any of its transformation initiatives. We specialize in the art & science of transformation; helping organizations transform smoothly with lasting results.

Our services include:

1. Transformation Program and Project Management
- Transformation Strategy, Analysis, and Planning
- Program and Project Management and Governance
- Business Results and Performance Management

2. Program and Project Recovery
- Project Turnaround
- Program and Project Alignment
- Risk Management

3. Program and Project Optimization
- Project Management Assessment System (PMAS)
- Organizational Change Readiness Assessment System (OCRAS)
- Project Team Optimization

Service Name:
Business Solution Design
Successful implementation and business results starts with strategy. Our expert consultants help organizations with the challenges they are facing by working alongside them to develop robust and sustainable business solutions. We understand the importance of realistic and implementable strategies and will work with your culture and processes to develop a custom strategy and solution for you.

We design custom business solutions on a range of topics and challenges including:

1. Strategy and Competitiveness
- Innovation and Entrepreneurship
- Program Design and Evaluation
- Business Planning and Feasibility Analysis

2. Productivity and Efficiency Enhancement
- Cost Reduction / Lean
- Operations Planning and Management
- Business Turnarounds

3. Enablement Strategies
- Information management and Technology
- Human Resource Management
- M&A, Partner Management, and Procurement
