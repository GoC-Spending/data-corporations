Company Profile


Academy of Learning Career & Business College specializes in the design and delivery of post-secondary education in Healthcare Administration, Accounting, Business, Graphic and Web Design, Information Technology, Sales & Customer Service and Office Administration.
All courses and diploma programs may be completed on a
full or part-time basis. Academy of Learning corporate clients
include employees of large and small business, non-profit
organizations and Federal and Provincial government.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Computer Service Technician Diploma
As the information technology field and the computerization of
business expands, Computer Service Technicians are in high
demand. Every computer will, at one time of another, require the
skills of a qualified service technician. This program will
prepare students to get their A+ Certification - an
internationally recognized standard.

Service Name:
Network Systems Administrator
The information technology field and computerization has
experienced tremendous growth as witnessed by the proliferation
of computer-based technology into all employment sectors.
Computers are used to run home-based business, small, medium and
large scale companies, governments etc. The installation,
maintenance and networking support for these computers requires
trained Network Support Specialists.

Service Name:
Call Centre Customer Representative Diploma
The Call Centre Representative Program develops skills related
to the call centre industry. The program gives students the
foundation to work in a call centre customer representative
position by developing competencies and skills in telephone
communications, team dynamics, personal development, call centre
functions, benefits, technologies and computer software programs.

Service Name:
Accounting Clerk
The Accounting Clerk Program offers students the opportunity to acquire skills in the most popular manual and computerized accoutning systems as well as other software programs and business-related functions.

Service Name:
Computerized Accounting Program
The Computerized Accounting Program offers students the opportunity to acquire skills in the most popular manual and computerized accounting systems as well as other administrative software programs and business related functions.

Service Name:
Medical Administrative Assistant
The Medical Administrative Assistant program develops a full range of comprehensive skills related to the medical/health profession in clerical or administrative support. The program gives students the foundation to work in a medical administrative support position by developing competencies and skills in medical office procedures, keyboarding, interpersonal skills, communications, medical/machine transcription, medical terminology and a variety of computer software programs.

Service Name:
Administrative Assistant
The Administrative Assistant program gives students the foundation to work in an office environment by developing personal competencies and skills in computerized office functions. The program provides students with well-developed secretarial skills and a full range of supervisory and administrative skills. it also covers note-taking, effective communication and leadership skills.

Service Name:
Business Administration
The Business Administration program is designed to prepare students for the demands of today's fast-paced business environment. The objective of the programis to expose students to a vast array of business knowledge, management, administration and practical tools that will assist them in dealing with situations within a business-related career.

Service Name:
Business & Office Accounting Clerk
The Business and Office Accounting Clerk program focuses on common business administration functions as well as bookkeeping and accounting skills. The program offer students the opportunity to acquire skills in the most popular manual and accounting systems as well as other software programs and business-related functions.

Service Name:
Entreprenuerial Business Applications
The Entrepreneurial Business Applications program focuses on starting and running your own business. The program explores the big picture of business planning, entrepreneurial characteristics, consumer demands, global competition and the most popular business software applications.

Service Name:
Help Desk Analyst
The help Desk Analyst program provides students with the necessary skill and knowledge to support end users of computer systems and software applications. It provides in-depth knowledge on evaluating, configuring and troubleshooting communications hardware, software and operating systems. This program also introduces the skills necessary to effectively provide remote customer service support and deal with issures Help Desk Analysts may encounter on the job when addressing customers.
