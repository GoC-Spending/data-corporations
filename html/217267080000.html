Company Profile


As a leader in engineering solutions since in 1958, Presentey provides research, engineering, design, manufacturing, and systems integration of technology-based solutions for government and industry. The company has established a broad customer base and enjoys a high degree of customer acceptance as a manufacturer of custom designed products. As a well-integrated organization, Presentey quickly responds to customer requirements while providing strong project management and life cycle support. We have extensive background and experience in developing and delivering complete solutions both as a prime contractor and as a sub-contractor.

Our headquarters and manufacturing operations occupy a 22,000 square-foot modern and secure facility, located in the National Capital Region of Canada, just minutes from Ottawa International Airport.

As a dedicated team providing innovative design, rigid quality assurance, and competitive deliveries, Presentey prides itself as a critical resource in delivering solutions well within your financial and time budgets. At Presentey we strive to develop cutting edge solutions.

Government - Defence - Security

The requirements of today's aerospace, military and security sectors pose challenging demands on technologies, often requiring 24/7 performance in harsh and remote environments. Presentey's integrated solutions offer reliable high speed communications and are built and tested to meet these challenging requirements. Presentey provides custom and off the shelf ruggedized solutions for various applications. Whether your application is desktop, mobile, or deployable our IT, video conferencing systems, airborne qualified, and network or satellite communications solutions are designed to withstand the challenges associated with harsh and unpredictable environments. We supply both secure and non-secure solutions for land, sea, and air. Our solutions go with you, keeping you connected.

Public Safety and Emergency Response

At Presentey we understand the critical needs of public safety organizations for reliable communication, environmental monitoring, ruggedness and wireless versatility. We partner with leading manufacturers and communication service providers to integrate quick deployment solutions that can be trusted to keep you informed and connected. Our mobile suites are custom configured for optimum performance in changing situations.

Utilities and Field Service

Service technicians in the utility market are constantly faced with difficult challenges, needing reliable equipment and accurate information. As well, field service personnel have the task of collecting and checking customer data quickly and continuously. A lightweight rugged solution can help technicians in these situations to receive necessary data to complete their jobs effectively. Powerful wireless access through WWAN replaces the need for situations where WLAN becomes inaccessible. Using WLAN, WWAN, SATCOM and GPS, technicians are quickly connected and deployed to the proper location. Presentey offers sealed shell, IP54 compliant, solutions that can be decontaminated quickly in the event of contact with questionable foreign matter. Additionally, our lightweight and small form factors design makes it easy to be transported anywhere. In order to keep personal information confidential, optional security mechanisms such as fingerprint identification and SmartCard reader are available. These systems have the ability to connect to multiple networks allowing user-friendly interfacing and communications with or without wire.

Aviation and Meteorological

Presentey specializes in the development, manufacture, systems integration and supply of a line of meteorological and aviation products and systems including Automated Weather Observation Systems (AWOS) and components, Runway Visual Range (RVR) Systems, Wind Altimeter Digital Display Systems (WADDS), and transmissometers. The company's aviation weather products and systems are used extensively throughout Canada by NAV CANADA and DND. These systems and products are backed by extensive knowledge and experience and are designed in strict accordance with WMO and ICAO standards and specifications. The company's activities in this area include:
•Manufacture, distribution, sales and support of meteorological systems and products
•The design and integration of meteorological and aviation systems
•Engineering consulting services relating to the design, installation and testing of meteorological and aviation systems
