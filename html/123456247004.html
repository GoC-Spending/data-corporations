Company Profile


MMC Ontario Ltd.(MOL) is an investment holding company servicing various sectors such as energy, environment, transportation, telecom, advance material and related enabling technologies. We specialize in concept engineering and process management support services for concept to commerce (C2C) pilot projects, resulting growth through partnership.

Nuworld R&D is a non-profit division of MOL, accelerating deployment of C2C pilot projects as a market entry method for SMB, resulting growth through partnership. C2C pilot projects results in scenarios with in-country economics providing the basis for design decisions, value proposition and options for MOL to be an equity investment partner from Ontario, Canada.
