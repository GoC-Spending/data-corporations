Testimonial:

"Approximately 3 years ago, I attended one of your 2-day Flexible Thinker workshops and it was by far the best professional course I have ever taken!

I have the 7 concepts written on a yellow sticky that affixed to my monitor and the orange magnet is attached to my fridge at home.

Although I found the "Yes and because" concept difficult at first, it has recently dawned on me that my use of "Yes, but" has diminised and replaced with "Yes and because". It's incredibly powerful for keeping the momentum of a brain-storming session. In fact, I was in a collaboration session recently and one of my colleagues kept cutting off one of the other members with a, "Yes, but...." Eventually, the cut-off member stopped contributing. I jumped in with, "....and to build on what so and so was saying, consider......" This prompted the recently silenced member to re-join the conversation. By the time we were finished we had collectively created an impressive list of options and an action plan for each.
Thank you for introducing me and members of my team to the Flexible Thinker program. It is a profoundly worthwhile investment in professional development.
Mary Mayo
Manager, Information Technology
City of Mississauga

"The Flexible Thinker® is designed to demonstrate through hands-on exercises how new ideas are communicated within a team  (this program) expanded our thinking and was quite useful.

"I would encourage you to evaluate if it can help you in your Six Sigma deployment."
