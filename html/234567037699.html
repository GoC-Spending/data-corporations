Company Profile


New Trend Environmental Services Ltd. is an Atlantic Canada owned and operated consulting firm. New Trend Environmental Services Ltd. has conducted over 2000 indoor air quality (IAQ) investigations and has pioneered a new method for tacking Ultra-fine particles to their source using condensation particle counting.

New Trend has provided training for over 1000 IAQ professionals throughout NorthAmerica and Europe on this new method. The services offered by this company include both IAQ testing and training. Major hospitals, school boards and private enterprises in North America and Europe have used this service.

This firm also provides Quantitative Fit Testing for respirators, including the N95 respirators used in hospitals.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Les services gestion du nettoyage de New Trend Environmental.
Le nettoyage des sites environnementaux est une question majeure, autant du point vue des responsabilités régulatrices que civiles. Il est essentiel que ces sites soient nettoyés convenablement dès la première fois. Afin d'aider les entreprises à effectuer leurs tâches de nettoyage, New Trend Environmental Services offre un service sur place de gestion du nettoyage, depuis la phase de planification jusqu'à la fermeture finale. Les services comprennent l'essai et l'évaluation, la gestion de l'élimination ou du recyclage des déchets, la tenue de dossier, la liaison réglementaire et plus.


New Trend offre aussi un excellent programme d'échange des déchets, dans le cadre duquel les produits de déchets des clients peuvent être réorientés de façon à servir d'intrants dans les autres procédés de l'entreprise. Pour terminer, cette société peut accomplir une surveillance indépendante des actions de l'entrepreneur général qui effectue le nettoyage.

Product Name:
New Trend Environmental's occupational health evaluations and audits.
In an era of increasing attention directed at occupational health and safety (OH&S), many
companies are recognizing the staggering liabilities from both workers, through the Workers
Compensation Boards (WCB), and from customers, through product liability claims. New Trend
Environmental Services offers a complete range of services in the occupational health (OH) area
to help clients manage OH risks, reduce the number of illnesses, meet their legal obligations and
minimize their WCB and associated costs.


These services include: A complete range of
training programs, from orientation/introductory training to advanced asbestos and hazardous
waste training Development of material data safety sheets (MSDS), supplier labels and
workplace labels Evaluations of WHMIS programs against legislated and government policy
objectives Full range of testing services to quantify concentrations and exposures Help in the
development of OH policies and procedures On-site audits and evaluations of all aspects of OH
programs, comparing to relevant Canadian and International standards Research on OH issues
to address management or employee concerns Through associated companies, complete design
and engineering services for OH issues.

Product Name:
New Trend Environmental's process to track down and identify the sources of indoor pollutants.
The use of traditional occupational hygiene methods has proven increasingly inadequate in coping
with the subjective symptoms such as headache, eye irritation and odours that are typical of
indoor air quality (IAQ) problems, sick building syndrome (SBS) and tight building syndrome
(TBS). New Trend Environmental offers a new propriety system with which to deal with these
problems. Using highly specialized, real-time equipment, New Trend Environmental personnel
are trained to track down the sources of contaminants within buildings quickly and accurately.
This allows for prompt and focused remediation, with the maximum probability of success.


The advantages of this system include: An excellent track record.

The vast majority of New Trend's clients report the problems solved soon or immediately after their recommendations
were implemented Lower consulting fees, as the investigations typically take only a few hours
Quick action. Since their equipment usually gives results in real-time, there are no time consuming
laboratory tests. This allows for remediation to start immediately - often before the investigation is
officially completed The methods are simple and the results are easy to understand The test
results are focused. Rather than supplying vague instructions, the specific source is identified.
This allows any modifications to be highly focused, thus saving money while maximizing impact.


Examples of IAQ problems that have been identified by this method are: Contamination by
copiers, laminators and other office machines Infiltration of construction emissions Infiltration of
garbage gases and fumes Infiltration of sewer gases Leaking furnace exhaust systems
Mammalian infestations Off gassing of building products Reentrainment of building exhausts.
