Company Profile


MedMira is the developer of Rapid Vertical Flow (RVF) Technology, a patented platform for rapid testing applications. Diagnostics for HIV and hepatitis built on RVF Technology have earned numerous regulatory approvals around the globe. Used by hospitals, laboratories, clinics, military medical units, tissue and eye banks, and public health agencies, MedMira rapid diagnostics deliver multiplexed results in just three minutes.
The Company is headquartered in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada. medmira.com.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Multiplo Rapid HCV/HIV Antibody Test
Multiplo Rapid HIV/HCV Antibody Test is a 3-minute rapid test for simultaneously detecting HIV and/or the hepatitis C virus (HCV) in whole blood, serum and plasma. HIV and hepatitis C share common risk factors and similar transmission routes. Oftentimes, patients and medical professionals are not aware that co-infection exists. Yet co-infection is common, reading rates of 30-35% for HIV and HCV. By testing for these diseases at the same time, patient outcomes can be improved since more complete therapeutic action plans can be initiated.

Product Name:
Reveal Rapid HIV Antibody Test
Get easy-to-read results in minutes. Reveal HIV is the only rapid test for HIV-1/2 using MedMira's patented Rapid Vertical Flow Technology. You will go from sample to results in three simple steps. Instant results can help prevent and control the spread of HIV. Patients do not have to wait for answers, consultations can happen in a timely manner and costs are reduced.

Product Name:
Multiplo HBc/HIV/HCV Rapid Antibody Test
Multiplo Rapid HBc/HIV/HCV Antibody Test simultaneously detects antibodies for HIV-1/2, hepatitis B, and hepatitis C in a single test. It is the only rapid test for these diseases that uses MedMira's patented Rapid Vertical Flow Technology. In three simple steps, you get an easy-to-read answer, going to from sample collection to results in minutes. Testing for three diseases at the same time can lead to speedier, more effective treatment. Patients do not have to wait for answers, consultations can happen in a timely manner and costs are reduced.

Product Name:
Multiplo Rapid Syphilis/HIV Antibody Test
Multiplo Rapid TP/HIV Antibody Test simultaneously detects antibodies to Treponema pallidum bateria (TP), the causative agent of syphilis, and HIV-1/2 in a single test. Using MedMira's patented Rapid Vertical Flow Technology, it takes you just three steps and a few minutes to go from sample to results. Instant answers are important in a variety of public health applications, especially maternal and prenatal care. Early diagnosis allows therapeutic action to prevent the transmission of syphilis or HIV from mother to child.

Product Name:
Miriad Rapid Tests for Tissue & Eye Banks
Miriad rapid antibody tests for HIV, hepatitis B, hepatitis C, and syphilis add another valuable layer of information in the donor procurement process. These multiplex tests, built on MedMira's patented Rapid Vertical Flow Technology, provide instant, easy-to-read results. Tissue and eye banks are using Miriad tests to achieve a relative discard reduction rate of 40%, simultaneously boosting safety and cutting costs.

Product Name:
Miriad RVF Toolkit
An off-the-shelf package of components and protocols, the RVF Toolkit provides the building blocks needed to create and optimize assays whose performance can rival much more complex laboratory-based systems.

Using MedMira's RVF Toolkit along with basic materials and supplies in your lab, you can create a rapid analytical tool in three easy steps: 1) Spot your antigen 2) Let it dry 3) Run your test.

Applications include: infectious disease biomarkers, vaccine research, industrial processes, contamination identification, and ELISA and Western Blot alternatives.



Out-License (Offering):
MedMira Rapid Vertical Flow Technology Platform
MedMira's patented Rapid Vertical Flow Technology is a platform offering speed, accuracy, and multiplexing. This technology enables targets of interest in a wide variety of sample types to be captured and visualized on a unique membrane. MedMira's InstantGold Cap simplifies the visualization procedure, as well as the handling and storage of the detection agent. Rapid Vertical Flow Technology is a highly scalable solution that is transforming the development and commercialization of rapid testing applications across a broad range of life sciences and biotech sectors.
