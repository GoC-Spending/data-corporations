Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Trade Show Products
When you want your brand identity and your message to have a special impact, it’s vital that you act strategically, creatively, and courageously.

Trade shows can be cluttered, chaotic, and crazy. But for many industries, they are at the heart of business development – one of the best ways to promote your brand and drive sales. So how do you get a harried trade show pedestrian to stop at your booth and give you a few minutes of attention? As a Winnipeg digital marketing agency, we will help you strategize about how to assemble the best possible booth for your business. From the standing display, to banner stands, to the printed material on the table, we will deliver a winning trade show presence that builds your brand.

Product Name:
Premium Promotional Products
If you want to give your booth visitors a memorable memento of their interaction with you, we can produce specialty products featuring your logo or message. Pens, coffee mugs, water bottles, watches, and much more. Let us help you understand what premium promotional products will give you the best bang for the buck, and introduce you to the latest product options. Make a powerful impression at a trade show, at golf tournaments, at the holidays, and at special events. And with our production savvy, you can rest assured that the process will be smooth.

As a Winnipeg digital marketing agency, we don’t just create premium promotional products and trade show booths. We create brand experiences that help you build relationships with the audiences you need to reach.

Product Name:
Indoor and Outdoor Signs
As a Winnipeg digital marketing agency, we can produce signs and banners that reflect your strategy and your corporate identity with a little bit of flair and a heck of a lot of wow. We can produce signage for your storefront, special event, or indoor office and retail needs.

Product Name:
Vehicle Graphics and Wraps
Wrapping your car or truck in corporate messaging is a very effective way to raise your profile. Depending on where you drive and where you park – whether one car or an entire fleet – we can deck out your ride in a way that attracts positive attention. The keys are bold design, simple and strong messages, and seamless technical execution. As a Winnipeg digital marketing agency specializing in vehicle graphics and wraps, we know how to do that. Call us today to discuss how far your dollar can go in having thousands of motorists see your logo and get your message every day.

Service Name:
Brand Strategy
Your logo is not your brand. Your brand is not your logo. Your brand is everything you want your audience to know about you. What you look like; what you sound like; what you feel like. Your smell, your taste, your groove, your soul. Your brand is your personality, and a logo is just one piece of that.

At ViewSource Media, located in Winnipeg, we’ll work with you to truly understand who you are and where you want to be as a brand. Then every piece of work we produce with you reflects that discovery. The purpose of a brand is to set audience expectations and then surpass them. That’s not random; that’s strategic.

Service Name:
Graphic Design
Graphic design is all about delivering logos, websites, brochures, trade show displays and other visual stuff you need in a way that inspires your audiences to buy, join, attend, donate, or simply care. Bottom line for us, graphic design doesn't just have to look good, it needs to amplify your message strategically; it needs to get a job done. Graphic design is about the client, not about the designer.

As a Winnipeg graphic design company, we have the expertise and track record to connect your objectives to your look.

Service Name:
Logo Design
Your brand identity -- your logo -- is often the first thing your audiences encounter when they get to know your business or organization. That logo should make a stunning impression – one that gives people a strong sense of who you are, what you do, and why you matter. Based out of Winnipeg, we can help you do that through colour, typography, smart tag lines, and vivid graphics. The total package will reflect your business strategy and have strong recognition in the marketplace. It can't just look good; it has to DO good.

Service Name:
Website Design and Development
When it comes to the web, we're living in an increasingly templated world. That's not for you. You deserve something fresh; something unique; something that impresses your audiences and gets them to do the stuff you want them to do.

As a Winnipeg website design company our custom creative and technical expertise can produce a site that is easy to find, easy to use, easy to update, and easy to love. From e-commerce, to ongoing web maintenance, to hosting and domain registration, we can provide complete service – from start to finish, from cradle to rave.

Service Name:
Online Marketing Strategy
If you’re getting click and tired of figuring out what you should do online to get the biggest bang for your buck, give us a call. As a Winnipeg based digital marketing agency we will help you come to some solid decisions about how your website, emails, blogs, and social media can work together to drive interest in your company or organization. And we will help you navigate the ever-changing landscape of online advertising.

Can advertising on Google, LinkedIn, and Facebook work for you? Let’s find out!

Service Name:
Commercial Printing
No, printing is not dead. It is different, customized, and done in a way that is more strategic than ever before. Based out of Winnipeg, we provide customized design and printing services that work on their own or in conjunction with an online campaign.

Service Name:
Online Learning Platforms
The quality of your online teaching and training platform will have a direct impact on your students’ experiences.

Whether you are teaching a distance education course or training your staff and volunteers, ViewSource Media from Winnipeg can help you organize and present your content in a way that inspires student engagement and promotes retention.

By incorporating video, audio, interactive tasks, and seamless assessment tools, we can help you reach your students in a meaningful and memorable way – and in a way that is easy for you to manage and maintain for the long term.
