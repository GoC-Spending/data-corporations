Company Profile


YRH's expertise is in telecommunications and broadcasting primarily in wireless systems (Cellular, PCS, 3G, 4G, LTE, communication radio, Wi-Fi, WiMax, broadcasting system, mobile and fixed broadband systems, paging, etc.), microwave, telecommunications infrastructure, computer networks, IP Phone, FTTH and fibre optics. Service Name:
Consultant Telecommunication
We are a team of consulting engineers providing professional engineering services in broadcasting and telecommunications. We serve both the private and public sectors. We have successfully completed projects in more than 30 countries and on 4 continents, in every kind of terrain and climate extreme. YRH is not new, we have been in business for more than 45 years. But we strive to bring fresh, new thinking to every challenge, giving added value through innovative solutions and a willingness to field-test our ideas.

YRH's expertise is primarily in broadcasting and wireless systems (LMDS, MMDS, Cellular, PCS, 3G, 4G, LTE, UMTS, etc.), microwave, telecommunications infrastucture, computer networks, IP Phone, FTTH and fibre optics. We offer the following range of services:
