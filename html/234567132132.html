Company Profile


Our bright and welcoming Parrsboro retail store has a dazzling array of strikingly coloured TeeShirts. All are printed with either a Parrsboro theme or funny phrase, you are sure to find exactly what you're looking for. We also custom print shirts.

Imagine your own custom printed TeeShirts, these are ideal for your club, business, church, fund raiser, schools or pub crawl, in fact any type of organization or activity. Exclusively yours and irresistibly priced.


So what do you actually do at TeeShirtery and Sign? Excellent question... We screen print our own Teeshirts. Who else can say that? We sell our own Teeshirts in our retail store right here in Parrsboro, they are also printed right here. Thats right our Parrsboro shirts are printed right here in Parrsboro. We offer the largest selection of Parrsboro TeeShirts anywhere in the world! How many other screen printers are confident enough to have their own retail store. Most are working out of basements or garages. They also tend to disappear as quickly as they appear. Not us, we are here for the long term. So as the name implies we also make Signs, Signage billboards, decals, window graphics, vinyl letterying, Windsigns, just about anything. Call email or drop in, We are confident we can help you.

So why printed TeeShirts? These are a terrific way to bring people together, make the staff, or club have a sense of belonging. The additional business you will generate is obviously a bonus as well.

Located in Parrsboro, Nova Scotia TeeShirtery & Signs is a family owned business. Using excellent machines and techniques we are able to offer you great value for money signs. In the uncertain times we are currently in when most people are cutting back. We have found that the customers that push them self's when the going gets tough even to the point of expanding are the ones that are more likely to survive. We are told repeatedly that a good sign is a sign of a good business and the customers seem to instinctively know this. If the company is looking tired and seems to be hanging on by the skin of its teeth, it seems to scare customers away. But the company proudly displaying new signage, and helping the customer find them is the company that hasn't given up. They still have something to offer. They have realized that in the hard times that's when you need to push hardest. We sincerely hope to be able to work with you on your upcoming projects. Even if your drawing a blank on how to proceed, we can help and advise on best way to promote yourself. Either click the email below or phone the number above. Do it now.

General Signage
Quite simply a Sign is an unparalleled way to attract customers. It&#146;s an incredibly effective, yet inexpensive form of advertising for any size business. Customers notice the good ones. Remember your sign will be viewed each time a customer passes it. They may pass it on the way to work, or on the way home, on weekends, during lunch. A person viewing your sign becomes a daily thing. Potentially an hourly thing and its ongoing again and again. If the sign is declaring the right message this will benefit you and your business. if your sign is ill conceived, badly designed or simply has no structure, your sign could actually be costing you money. Product Name:
Free Salesperson 24 hours a day
Now your signage when well placed is going to be working for you 24 hours a day. seven days a week, so you had better ensure that its the right kind of signage thats out there. Think of your signage as a sales person, Yes a salesperson standing out in all weathers working tirelessly. Never late! Never takes a vacation. Never asks for a pay rise. In fact after the initial costs they are working for you for free!

To put that into perspective imagine paying for training that would ensure that this sales person only keeps to your script never deviating. They will be assigned a specific area to cover and they will cover it very well. And then when they pass the training they actually work for you for free! FOR FREE! No time off, No wages. No vacation, no excuses. How great would that be! Thats exactly what your signage should be doing for you. Declaring your message loud and clear to all that reads it, 24 hours a day 7 days a week.

Look around at your signage is that what its doing for you now?

Unfortunately the bad signage, the flaking signs, the signage thats falling over or thats missing letters, or that you cant read is also working for you 24 hours a day 7 days a week. They are also giving out a message Is it the right one? Im guessing no.
