Company Profile


Plastiques Moore Inc. is an innovative leader in managing complex injection molding projects. Plastiques Moore goes beyond best practices in design, molding and assembly to deliver high quality plastics solutions in North America.
Plastiques Moore distinctive advantage lies in creative value for it's customer by it's partnership and customer intimacy program.
Registered as ISO 9001 and QS 9000 compliant.
Plastiques Moore is located in the Plastic Vally in Quebec.
Our Market is very diversified in order to deliver the optimal product.
Medical, Appliances, Recreational, Electronic, Military, Security, Automotive and Environnement.
