Service Name:
24 Hour Emergency Plumbing
o what is Emergency Plumbing? Well it's what you would expect it to be, a plumbing or a drain problem that requires and urgent care or else it can lead to great amounts of damage, be it from sewage backed up or a leaking water pipe flooding your home. And an Emergency Plumbing service is a basic service offered by many plumbing companies, it's a way we as plumbers can help out homeowners in the time of need.


Things break all the time. No matter if its a expensive sports car or something like a drain or a toilet. As homeowners and plumbing professionals we understand the stress a plumbing emergency can cause, and because of this make it one of our top priorities when helping out our customers to provide a stress free plumbing emergency service. A small plumbing emergency can lead to massive damages, and it can lead to this damages very fast. So when we get a emergency plumbing call we handle it as fast as possible, to quickly assess if its an emergency or something that can wait till next morning (if you are calling in a middle of the night).


When you call about an emergency plumbing situation our dispatcher will put you truth to a plumber who will assist you from that point on. Our plumbers are highly skilled and trained people with many years of experience behind them. Our plumbers ask few questions that helps them to assist the urgency of the call and if they can do something about it. In most cases our plumbers are able to tackle a emergency plumbing call, but there are times when we may not have the needed parts or hardware. But do not worry, our plumbers will make sure to temporarily isolate the emergency problem while they look for the needed parts or wait till morning to pick up what they need from plumbing supply stores.

Service Name:
Drain Services and Drain Cleaning
Drain is a complex highway of pipes. Drains are the core of any home, business or industry. They deliver and remove water and waste and are essential in getting anything done. Sometimes there is a problem with your drain, and your drain requires to be serviced in order for continuation of proper drain functions. We offer a wide range of drain services from small to big. Here is a list of some or the Drain Services that we offer.

Drain Cleaning
Drain Rooting
Drain Camera Inspection
Drain Clearance
High Pressure Drain Water Jet Cleaning
Drain Unclogging
Drain Maintenance
Drain Repair
Drain Installation

Over time drains become clogged up by greas, hair, trash, cooking excrements and other things that are flushed down the drain on daily bases. Sometimes drains are clogged by little things that can be easily cleared with a drain cleaning machine, while other times they require more time and effort. Drain services vary in complexity and pricing. For example clearing a clogged kitchen drain would be cheaper as compared to having to remove the toilet and clear the drain do to someone flushing paper towel or worse a diaper down the toilet.

Service Name:
Shower Installation, Repair and Upgrade Services
When it comes to showers there are plenty to choose from, from fancy and luxury showers to basic stock put in by builders. We deal with most if not all shower and brands. Our plumbers are skilled with repair and installation of brands like Moen, PricePfister, Kohler, American Standard, Delta and many more. We do complete shower installation of the shower set and also offer water service connection if your doing some construction and want your shower in a different place we are the guys for the task. On top of offering shower repair and installation services we also offer upgrade services if you choose to get something different or more luxuries. We also provide shower waterproofed membrane installation and removal of the old membrane and installing a brand new one. And lastly we perform shower drain cleaning, removing all of the trash buildup in the drain so you can perfect water removal from the shower drain.

Laundry Room Drain

I take pleasure in recommending the excellent--and personable--service of Alex and assistant Terry of The Toronto Plumber.
They arrived punctually, explained the process of replacing leaking hose bibs and water lines for the washing machine, and performed the task with proficiency. Alex pointed out that the replacement parts he chose were Canadian products because he was more confident of their quality.
I asked Alex if he had a suggestion for eliminating a persistent foul odour in the laundry room. His first thought was that the washer drain hose set-up was incorrect and that was indeed the case. They remedied the situation by installing a p-trap which allows waste water to create a seal preventing the inflow of sewer gasses.
I finally have a fresh-smelling laundry room again. Thank you Alex and Terry.
And they left the work area spic and span.
And their prices are very reasonable.
