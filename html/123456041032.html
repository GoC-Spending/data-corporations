Sector information

Key / Major clients:

The need for sustainable infrastructure is one of the most significant challenges facing the North American continent today. Organizations are challenged with aging facilities, environmental concerns and rising energy costs, along with shrinking capital and operating budgets, while still needing to focus on and advance their own missions.
As an industry leading strategic facility asset manager, The Maricor Group can provide defensible multi-level analysis to address facility issues, financial capabilities, business direction, and overall client vision to guarantee that the best decisions are made. We are part of a family of companies that brings over 100 years of commitment to sustainable practices and to ensuring that the clients needs are always met.
Through a solid vision and determined attitude, The Maricor Group has assembled one of the best strategic asset management organizations in North America. It is this team that brings the experience that makes The Maricor Group the right solution for your needs.

The Maricor Group is a subsidiary of Maine & Maritimes Corporation, a publicly traded holding company. Maine & Maritimes Corporation is also the parent company of Maricor Properties Ltd., Maine Public Service, and Maricor Technologies. Maine Public Service will soon celebrate 104 years of business.

With our leading facility lifecycle management and engineering services, combined with our financial and development offerings, The Maricor Group assists clients in improving their facilities operational and financial performance, while complying with public policy mandates and addressing both economic and environmental sustainability issues.

We are committed, in everything we do, to be strategic leaders in the development and renewal of societys facility infrastructure through the integration of facility engineering, energy efficiency services, and lifecycle asset management technologies and processes.


What follows is our story.


OUR COMMITMENT TO YOU
The Maricor Group is committed to helping its clients, as their strategic facility partner, meet their needs by creating strategic and sustainable asset lifecycle solutions that deliver immediate and long-term, bottom-line results. We seek opportunities to provide a valuable service to, and make a positive difference for, organizations and their communities across North America.

OUR VALUE TO YOU
The Maricor Groups value proposition offers its clients a sound return on investment:

Innovation in strategic asset management...
By integrating engineering, energy, and asset lifecycle analysis, we partner with our clients to innovate a strategic plan for capital renewal and operating budget solutions.

Expertise in sustainable performance...
By applying our facility engineering, energy analysis, and lifecycle management expertise, we improve operating efficiencies and environmental performance and reduce lifecycle cost.

Investment in sustainable facilities...
By defining opportunities to optimize capital investment, we deploy our financial resources to make investments in advancing and sustaining our clients mission.

SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY
Social responsibility is the core of our business. In North America, most of the public infrastructure was built between 1945 and the early 1970s. With this aging infrastructure comes an opportunity and a responsibility that happens only once in a lifetime: to renew and develop our economic infrastructure and social and environmental principles for generations to come.

Our great-grandchildren will inherit the infrastructure we build today. They will learn in the classrooms, work in the offices, play in the fields, and heal in the hospitals. Accordingly, our infrastructure renewal efforts must reflect true stewardship for societys built environment. Our legacy depends on it. Thats why we operate our company according to sustainable economic, social, and environmental principles.

With the formation of the Maine & Maritimes Corporation, the holding company for The Maricor Group, we will conform to the Global Reporting Initiatives Sustainability Reporting Guidelines. It is important to us that all of our stakeholders know how we are performing in the areas of economic, social, and environmental sustainability.



Our Services

The Maricor Group has developed a unique service offering that is designed to be specifically implemented to meet the individual needs of our clients. Each service line is defined by specific products and deliverables. We can provide a total turnkey solution or specific al a cart services. This allows us to best integrate our solutions within our clients organizations by leveraging their existing knowledge, capabilities, and resources for a sound return on investment.

Strategic Asset Management
Controlling the total cost of facility ownership requires comprehensive lifecycle management. To solve current challenges and build a solid foundation for the future, you need accurate and concise knowledge and trustworthy advice. We know that peace of mind comes from a credible capital planning process built on detailed information and reasoned analysis, and on a facility management process built on best practices. With our Strategic & Sustainable Asset Management services and technology, the promises you make today can hold true into the future. Our strategic asset management services include:

 Condition Assessments
 Strategic Planning
 Sustainable Facilities Planning
 Capital Renewal Planning
 Information Management
 Asset Management Solutions

Energy Consulting
Energy efficiency is a key ingredient of environmental sustainability. Our engineers are LEED certified experts in identifying energy and environmental opportunities that will significantly improve the performance of your facilities. We offer performance contracts as well as ownership of energy systems. Environmental stewardship really can be affordable. Our energy consulting services include:

 Energy Engineering
 Energy Performance Analysis
 Energy Conservation
 Sustainable Design
 Commissioning



Asset Development Services
Whether you are considering facility renovation, modernization, or new development projects, we begin by working with you to explore various planning scenarios to get your projects funded and on the ground. Once decisions are made and a plan is in place, you can look to us to manage your complete project. And, if capital constraints are a cause for delay, talk to us about our ownership of facilities and energy systems. We want to be your strategic facility planner, today and tomorrow. Our asset development services include:

 Facility Development Planning
 Project Management
 Design Build
 Energy Systems Development
 Facility Operations
 Facility Ownership

Engineering Services
With offices located throughout North America, our mechanical and electrical engineering divisions, in addition to our strategic partnerships, means that our experts are local to your region. They understand your needs, both as a business partner and a technical expert. Our engineers have deep expertise and experience in many building types and emerging green building technologies. Energy is often the largest lifecycle cost for facilities, in both financial and environmental terms, so we specialize in energy engineering. Our many years of experience, innovative culture, and engineering talent combine to provide the perfect energy efficient solution for your renewal or development project. You will find our LEED certified engineers at the leading intersection of environmentally friendly technologies, application design, and operations and maintenance practices. Our engineering services include:
 HVAC Engineering
 Electrical Engineering
 Plumbing Engineering
 Fire Protection Engineering
