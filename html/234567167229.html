Company Profile


New United Goderich Inc. is a one-stop aircraft heavy maintenance and modification shop. We have the capability to perform almost any task within the realm of aircraft maintenance, repair, paint, refurbishment and modifications. Our modern multi-hangered facility in Centralia Ontario Canada (ICAO: CYCE, IATA: YCE) contains two large paint bays, an interior refurbishment workshop, a composite/structures repair workshop, a custom cabinetry workshop, an avionics diagnostic department, and an engineering department. One of our hangars is even large enough to accommodate an aircraft up to the size of a Boeing 737 or Airbus A320 series.

Over the years, our company has serviced a large variety of makes and models of aircraft. Our past experience with Grumman, Boeing, Bombardier, Embraer, Gulfstream and Dornier aircraft speak for themselves. Over 20 years of industry experience has taught us to provide a quality crafted product with an unrivaled focus on aviation safety.

Product / Service / Licensing

Service Name:
Heavy Maintenance
New United Goderich Inc. takes pride in the quality of our heavy maintenance capabilities; our technicians are highly skilled in both sheet metal and composite aircraft repairs.

Just some of the tasks we can perform are:

Corrosion Control and Prevention
Accident Re-building and Repair
Restoration of the Structural Integrity of an Aircraft
Aircraft Life Extension
Major and Minor Aircraft Modifications
Composite Repair of All Major Components and Control Surfaces
All Major Component Refurbishments (Wings, Horizontal or Vertical Stabilizer)
Fairing Repair
Welding
FAA/EASA Certified Repair
Repairs Requiring Replacement by Service Bulletins or Airworthiness Directives
Non-Destructive Testing

Service Name:
Maintenance Inspections
As a fully authorized Transport Canada Approved Maintenance Organization,as well as an FAA, ANAC, EASA and CAAC approved MRO. New United Goderich Inc. is fully capable to perform A, B, C, or D inspections, phase inspections, cycle inspections, function checks, structural and complete pre-purchase inspections.

We follow the aircraft manufacturer’s manuals and our highly trained staff can perform any type of inspection they call for.

Service Name:
Composites
As part of our heavy maintenance capabilities; New United Goderich Technicians are able to perform a broad range of composite modifications and repairs.

Including:

Custom plug/mold and part fabrication
Rapid prototyping
“Floor to Ceiling,” Interior Composite Molding and Fabrication
Vacuum assisted sandwich or monolithic part repair/fabrication
Aircraft shell kits
Hot Bonding repairs to primary and secondary composite and metal bonded parts
Lightning protection installation and repairs

Service Name:
Avionics Installation
New United Goderich is a dealer for Honeywell, Bendix King, DAC International, Gogo Business Aviation and Flight Display Systems. We have in-depth product knowledge to install all forms of In-Flight Entertainment, Cabin Management and onboard Wi-Fi Systems. Our Avionics Team will outfit your aircraft with the most up-to-date products on the market.

Our experts in the field of Avionics are able to customize the installation of your entertainment system, to improve your in-flight experience or troubleshoot the most difficult avionics issues and bring your current equipment back into service.

Inside the flight deck, the New United Goderich team is skilled beyond the normal Pitot /Static system, VHF radios, ADFs, VORs and DMEs. The Avionics Team is fully prepared to install any manner of navigation, communication, aircraft monitoring and flight management system. As well as upgrade your aircraft’s existing avionics components.

Service Name:
Exterior Refinishing
At New United Goderich Inc., we utilize a state of the art climate controlled paint booth exclusively for priming and painting which assures you that no outside contaminants will affect the brilliance of the finished product. The booth features a downdraft exhaust system that ensures both safety and the highest regard for environmental concerns.

Our highly knowledgeable team of professionals at New United Goderich Inc. is able to work with a customer supplied scheme or one created with your interests in mind. With a wide variety of paint products we are able to provide you with a beautiful, elaborate and creative design that will last for years.

With an incredible record of repeat clients, we know that we can not only meet but exceed any client expectation.

Service Name:
Engineering & Modifications
Expectations can be met with our comprehensive proven processes and pioneered project management system. The ability to convert regional and narrow body airliners to fulfill VIP missions worldwide takes planning, organization and a drive that is not for most.

Our Engineering department is proud to employ experienced aeronautical engineers that can complete any level of modification within your aircraft. From bringing a new look to your floorplan to completing an entire special mission modification.

Our team can design any repair or modification. Provide certification for all designs up to and including the aircraft test flight, Instructions for Continued Airworthiness (ICA) as well as Supplemental Type Certificate (STC) support. In fact, New United Goderich has developed over 90 STCs and currently owns over 30 of them.

Our Transport Canada, FAA , EASA, ANAC and CAAC approvals allow us to achieve certifications and acceptance to virtually all importing countries.

Service Name:
Interior Refurbishment
New United Goderich Inc. has set the standard for aviation interiors. Our tailor-made details make all our interiors unique to the roles of each specific aircraft. We can provide a variety of finishes and wood species to choose from, you can be assured that you will have a one of a kind cabin interior. No matter if you need custom cabinetry, hydrographics, VIP seating or Airline seating our Interiors Team will make it spectacular.

Some additional services we can provide:
• Repair of Plastics
• Carpet & Loncoin Replacement
• Interior Painting
• Solid Wood Work
• Seat & Panel Upholstery (Leather, Vinyl and Sheepskin)
• VIP Seat Repair and Refurbishment including Foam
