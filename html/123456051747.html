Company Profile


Established in 1974 and based in Saint John, New Brunswick,
The DPL Group is a technology based company.

Highly flexible in its activities and always on the edge of new
technologies and developments, The DPL Group uses a strong
technical background and innovative research and development to
maintain a reputation as a leader and solution provider.

The DPL Research & Development Department has provided new
wireless monitoring products for heavy equipment (off road), trucking and the ATM industries.

The current customer base consists of major telcos,
RBOCs , and telecommunication service providers, Construction Companies, Equipment Rental Companies and ATM ISOs across Canada and the USA.

DPL specializes in wireless solutions for cellular remote
monitoring.

Cellular technology, multi-platform programming and the Internet
are combined to deliver remote monitoring to the desktop.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
SBI-145 Telephone Test Set
The DPL butt-in is the best in the Telecom Industry. The most
cost effective, light weight test set available, it has 3
modes:
Touch Tone, Pulse Dial and EBS. It is a tough product that
will survive a 30 ft. drop test. The SBI-145 is available in
black or yellow with different cord options depending on your
requirements.

Product Name:
CPI-101 Computer P-Phone Interface
The CPI-101 uses Caller Line ID to activate screen POPS. It acts
as a stand alone unit connected to a PC (or connected to a PC
and
an EBS set) and it works off an EBS line from a Nortel DMS-100
Switch. It has two modes of operation:
1. DPL CPI-101 protocol (with or without a beta TAPI driver)
2. "DEES" mode where it acts as a DEES box In either mode,
monitoring CLID on an EBS line is established by plugging your
line into the CPI-101 and hooking the CPI-101 to your business
set and/or PC.

As mentioned above, the CPI-101 is proven to work with FastCall
in "DEES" mode. This gives you the ability to provide your
customers with a "DEES" box and software that works without added
development costs. CPI-101 specific software is available upon
request.


Product Name:
TTS-100 Telephone Test System
The TTS-100 is a test system used in the refurbishment of
telephones, or the verification of telephone functionality. The
TTS-100 tests EBS and analog sets with audio and visual cues that
alert the operator of test failures. The included software for
the TTS-100 tests a wide range of telephone operations.

Product Name:
Titan Heavy Equipment Anti-theft
The Titan uses cellular technologies to monitor
equipment for anti-theft, maintenance, tracking and messaging.

Product Name:
SkyHawk Vehicle Tracking
Vehicle Tracking and Communicating

Product Name:
Hercules+ Wireless for ATMs
Provides two-way cellular and internet communications service for ATMs with an optional anti-theft alerts/monitoring

Product Name:
Hercules 1000 Vehicle Tracking
Tracks and Communicates with Vehicles using GPRS

Product Name:
Genermon
The DPL Genermon wirelessly monitors your generator set 24/7. Data is presented on a secure web site for your convenience. All your critical data is available to you. When an alarm condition occurs, you can have the alarm routed to you via e-mail to your computer and cell phone. One model will perform all monitoring functions regardless of the type of generator that you own. Never run out of fuel or miss scheduled maintenance.
