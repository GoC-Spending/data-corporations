Company Profile


Korem has been at the forefront of geospatial technology and business intelligence since 1993. We create and implement integrated geospatial solutions based on your business needs, and we enable you to better utilize your data and improve your business decision-making capabilities. Whether you need strategic consulting, integration, production, R&D, training or location-based software and data, Korem is your proven partner of choice. Through our key partnerships with the industrys top players such as PBBI and Google (Earth & Maps) and a commitment to excellence and adherence to the highest standards, including our ISO 9001:2008 certification, our solutions deliver measurable, replicable, valuable results. Our people, innovative technology and bold vision make us a leader in the business geospatial technology sector.
Korem was named «Best New Partner for North America" by MapInfo Corporation (PBBI) in 2000, and has been awarded Canadian Business Partner of the Year from 2001 to 2008. Korem was also awarded « VAR Business Partner of the Year » in North America for 2009 by PBBI.

As a certified Google Enterprise Partner, Korem offers Google Maps custom solutions development and integration, Google Earth Enterprise custom solutions development and integration and Google Earth Enterprise Implementation & Training.

For more information, please visit www.korem.com.

Product / Service / Licensing

Product Name:
Korem Mapping Studio (KMS)
Korem mapping studio (KMS) is a turnkey solution allowing businesses and organizations to design and deploy multiple mapping applications on the Internet, on an intranet, or an extranet.




KMSs intuitive environment eases the creation, design, and deployment of mapping applications without any programming knowledge requirements. This visual studio provides GIS specialists with all the necessary tools to share maps created with MapInfo Professional®.


For those looking to develop more advanced applications, KMS also offers powerful customization capabilities. As a rapid application development (RAD) tool, KMS provides IT developers all the required flexibility to develop, deploy, and maintain powerful mapping applications.


Providing organizations with simple and efficient means to share geographic information within the organization or with the public, KMS is an essential toolbox for leveraging and enhancing geographic information within an organization.


Product Name:
mapworkspace
mapworkspace is a solution dedicated to MapInfo Professional® users to publish and share their maps.

mapworkspaces accessibility and ease-of-use are truly unique. Although solutions currently exist allowing users to add simple geographic information to free online map services, mapworkspace stands out as it leaves the mapping professional with complete latitude to create maps in MapInfo Professional, and then use mapworkspace to easily share them with their peers or any other user. Public access allows non-GIS professionals to consult published information and interact with the authors in a user-friendly environment.

Delivered under the approach of a continuously evolving environment, new features are regularly added in order to improve the service. Among the hot new features are the support for MapInfo Professional 10.5 new stacked styles and multiple overrides, the support for WMS connections, the links to Google Maps and Earth, KML files export for use in Google Earth Desktop, and much more. To learn more about the recently added features, see the whats new section.

mapworkspace is available as an online service (SaaS, with free or paid subscription packages) or as an on-premise solution.

Service / Solution options

1 - A FREE subscription to mapworkspace as a SaaS provides users with an access to all of the services functionalities and sharing options. They are granted with 10 Mb of space and the capacity to publish and share up to 5 maps. Sharing options include the use of the embedding code for integration within a private Web environment.

2 - PAID subscription packages for mapworkspace as a SaaS are also available, providing more space and a wider range of map views. Depending on their subscription package, paying users can publish an unlimited number of maps within the disk space provided. Paid subscription package prices are mainly defined by hard drive space and number of maps viewed. An Enterprise package provides the additional capacity to share maps within a private group of users. The Enterprise package allows the administrator to create a group and share maps within this private group only.

3 - mapworkspace is also available as an ON-PREMISE solution. The complete application can be purchased for internal use. The application will then be installed on an internal server for private use. In this context, the person in charge of the system for the organization will have all the flexibility to manage all users, groups of users, maps, and usage statistics. The interface can also be customized for better integration into the corporate environment. To know more about this option, please contact us at sales@mapworkspace.com or at 1 888 440 1627 (Canada and USA only) or at +1 418 647 1555 (International).

Service Name:
Traitement de données
Le Groupe KOREM excelle dans l'acquisition, l'exploitation et la
diffusion de bases de données provenant de sources et de formats
divers. Les professionnels de KOREM maîtrisent l'ensemble des
logiciels et méthodes de traitement applicables aux systèmes
d'information géographique et des technologies de l'information.
De plus, ils entretiennent des relations avec les principaux
fournisseurs de données du domaine public et privé afin de vous
offrir des produits adaptés à vos besoins.

Logiciels et technologies utilisés :

- SIG : MapInfo, Vertical Mapper, ArcView, ArcInfo;
- CAD : Micro Station, Autocad;
- Formats utilisés : .tab, .mif, .shp, .E00, .dgn, .dxf, .dwg,
etc.;
- Bases de données : Access, SQL Server, Oracle, etc.;
- Environnements : Win95-98, Windows NT, UNIX.
Types de services :

- Numérisation à partir de documents papier ou fichiers " raster
" (image satellite, photo aérienne, scans);
- Traduction, importation (changement de format, de projection);
- Épuration (remise à niveaux, par thème et type d'entité,
symbolisation);
- Structuration topologique (nettoyage de fichiers de lignes et
construction de polygones);
- Intégration multi-sources, multi-échelles (UTM, MTM, NAD27,
NAD83, 1:20k, 1:50k, 1:250k);
- Modélisation 3D de fichiers avec valeur X, Y, Z;
- Géocodage sur numéro matricule, code postal et adresse civique.
